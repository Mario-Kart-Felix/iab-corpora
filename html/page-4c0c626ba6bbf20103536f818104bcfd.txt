		<p>Members of the automotive press&nbsp;like the 2012 Audi A4 wagon for its upscale interior and balanced performance.</p>
	

	<p>The 2012 Audi A4 Avant (which is Audi-speak for wagon) doesn&rsquo;t excel in any one area, but according to most test drivers, it&rsquo;s a well-rounded package that most <a href="/cars-trucks/rankings/Luxury-Wagons/">luxury wagon</a> shoppers should take a look at. Though it&rsquo;s not the sportiest wagon in the class,&nbsp;test drivers&nbsp;note that it does offer a pleasing balance of power and fuel economy, as well as,&nbsp;a sporty yet comfortable ride. The A4 Avant also earns&nbsp;applause for offering features like all-wheel drive and leather upholstery standard; most&nbsp;rivals charge extra for those features.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Like most Audi models,&nbsp;automotive journalists&nbsp;say the 2012 A4 Avant features a well-built cabin with top-notch materials.&nbsp; However, they complain that some of the electronics are confusing, the backseat is cramped for adults and that the cargo space isn&rsquo;t as large as what some competitors offer. If you&rsquo;re looking for a luxury wagon and want something that does lots of things well, but can deal with it not being a standout in any one area, the A4 Avant deserves a look.</p>
<h3>Other Wagons to Consider</h3>
<p>If you want a luxury wagon with better performance, the <a href="/cars-trucks/BMW_3-Series-Wagon/">BMW 3-Series Wagon</a> is your best bet. Though it doesn&rsquo;t have standard all-wheel drive, and some reviewers caution that its ride can be rough, they also say it sets the performance standard for the class. Another good performer is the <a href="/cars-trucks/Cadillac_CTS-Sport-Wagon/">Cadillac CTS Sport Wagon</a>. Just keep in mind that its styling is flashier than the conservative A4&rsquo;s.</p>
<p>Reviewers also recommend the <a href="/cars-trucks/Acura_TSX-Sport-Wagon/">Acura TSX Sport Wagon</a>.&nbsp; Though its interior isn&rsquo;t quite as upscale as the interior on the A4 Avant, reviewers say its electronics are a lot easier to use.</p>
<h3>Details: Audi A4 Wagon</h3>
<p>There are no major changes to the Audi A4 wagon for 2012. The 2012 Audi A4 Avant is available in three trims: Premium, Premium Plus, and Prestige.</p>
