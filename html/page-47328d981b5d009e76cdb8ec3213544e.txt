			<h1 class="title">Welcome to the Eastern Electric Vehicle  Club Website</h1>
          <p class="byline">founded in 1980, serving Eastern Pennsylvania &amp; New Jersey</p>
            <p>The <acronym title="Eastern Electric Vehicle Club">EEVC</acronym> is a grass-roots network of people from all   walks of life interested in the potential of alternative powered vehicles. As   the need for alternative energy grows, the so does the importance of exploring   alternative energy resources. Maybe you've heard all about &quot;going green&quot; and   want to see what you can do. Maybe you own a Prius and want to share your   experience. The <acronym title="Eastern Electric Vehicle Club">EEVC</acronym> is the perfect forum to learn and share as we move   forward into an age where vehicles don't depend on gas		</p>
