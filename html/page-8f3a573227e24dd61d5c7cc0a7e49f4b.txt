    <p>Don't let your adventures get cut short because you didn't get the right coverage for your ride! Your homeowners or auto policy may not adequately cover your ORV. Lucky for you, <a href="/agency-locator/index.asp?product=Off-Road+Vehicle&submit=+Go+">an agent</a> can help you choose the right Foremost policy for your ORV, so you can go out and enjoy the trail while someone else takes care of the paperwork!</p>
    <p><span class="h2">We've got you  covered:</span></p>
    <ul class="yellow-bulleted_list">
	<li><strong>Safety Apparel Coverage.</strong> <strong>This provides up to $1,500 in coverage</strong> for damage to any clothing designed to minimize damage from an accident, including helmets and goggles.</li>
	<li><strong>Optional Equipment Coverage.</strong> <strong>We include $500 for optional equipment coverage</strong>, depending on your policy. This covers any "extras" added to your ride, including towable trailers or sleds made for use with your ORV.</li>
	<li><strong>Annual Policies.</strong> If the trail is calling, you don't want to miss it. No calls, no hassle with our annual policy you can insure your ORV even in the "off-season."</li>
	<li><strong>Medical Payments Coverage.</strong> Hopefully never needed, but always important with off-road vehicle insurance, this coverage pays for the necessary medical care you receive as a result of an off-road vehicle accident.</li>
	<li><strong>Liability Coverage.</strong> This broad coverage is meant for accidents involving your ORV (the coverage is for third-party injuries and property damage).</li>
	<li><strong>Increased Limits.</strong> You can choose the limits that you want on your liability.</li>
	<li><strong>Collision Coverage.</strong> Regardless of who is at fault, this covers your ORV in the event of damage caused by collision with other vehicles and trees or utility poles.</li>
	<li><strong>Other than Collision Coverage.</strong> Provides coverage for out of the ordinary damages, such as fire, theft, flood or vandalism.</li>
	<li><strong>Trailer Coverage.</strong> Provides coverage for a specifically designed trailer to transport your ORV (valued up to $7,500).</li>
    </ul>
    <div class="take_advantage">Take  advantage of the knowledge at hand, <a href="/agency-locator/index.asp?product=Off-Road+Vehicle&submit=+Go+">locate an agent</a> to help you choose the right insurance today, so you can ride the trail tomorrow.</div>
