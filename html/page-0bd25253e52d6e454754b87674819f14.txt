<h1>Automotive</h1>
<div class="subheadlinecontent"><p>No matter where you&rsquo;re going, we can take you there.</p></div>
</div>
</div>
</div>
<img border="0" src="/resources/images/banner-images/2.3-Automotive.jpg" alt="Automotive"></li>

</div></div></div></div><div class="ls-row-clr"></div></div></div><div class="ls-row" id="emptyrpw"><div class="ls-fxr" id="ls-gen10-ls-fxr"><div class="ls-area contentleftcontainer" id="ls-row-3-col-2-row-2-area-1"><div class="ls-area-body" id="ls-gen11-ls-area-body"><div class="ls-cmp-wrap ls-1st" id="w1300274048373"><div id="c1300274048373" class="iw_component"><div class="contenttxt mgt_list"><p><span style="font-size: 10pt;"><strong><span style="font-size: 11pt;">In 1947, we introduced India to the utility vehicle. </span></strong>&nbsp;More than 65 years later, we're still India's premier utility vehicle (UV) company, but we've also grown quite a bit. In addition to making groundbreaking UVs like the <a title="Scorpio" href="http://www.mahindra.com/What-We-Do/Automotive/Products/Scorpio">Scorpio</a> and <a title="Bolero" href="http://www.mahindra.com/What-We-Do/Automotive/Products/Bolero">Bolero</a>, Mahindra offers cars, electric vehicles, <a title="Pickup Trucks" href="http://www.mahindra.com/What-We-Do/Automotive/Products/Mahindra-Navistar-Trucks">pickups</a>, and commercial vehicles that are rugged, reliable, environmentally friendly, and fuel-efficient. <span style="font-size: 10pt;">The acquisition of the Ssangyong Motor Company in 2011, brought a major South Korean utility manufacturer with a presense in more than 90 coutries into the Mahindra fold. </span>Our global presence means you can find Mahindra vehicles on the roads&mdash;both paved and unpaved&mdash;of Australia, Europe, Latin America, Malaysia, South Korea, and South Africa. And we're seeking out new terrain every day.</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 10pt;"><span style="font-size: 10pt;">Mahindra is also there for you through the construction of excellent components, provision of spares, and commitment to superior service.&nbsp; Our automotive businesses&nbsp;cover all your transportation needs thanks to strategic synergies between our expertise in design, manufacturing, and service.</span></span></p></div><div class="greyline">&nbsp;</div><div id="tabbed_box_1" style="margin-top: 13px;">
