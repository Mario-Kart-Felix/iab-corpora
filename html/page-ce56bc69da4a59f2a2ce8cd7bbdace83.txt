<h3 id="ABDY101"><strong>ABDY 101 - Collision Repair Fundamentals and Estimating</strong></h3>
<p>This lecture/lab course includes an overview of the collision industry, instruction in safe shop procedures, measurement, vehicle disassembly, and estimating software and techniques. Successful students will earn nine I-CAR certification points. 4 Credits.</p>

Prerequisite: ABDY 120 or instructor approval. 4 Credits.</p>
