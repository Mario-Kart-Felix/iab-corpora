The luxury car has greater performance, construction precision, design ingenuity, greater equipments, comfort, technological innovation, and other discretionary features. The article describes the luxury car brands in America.</p><p>Luxury cars include sedan, coupe, hatchback, station wagon and convertible body style vehicles. Luxury cars are available in any size and any price range. The luxury cars emphasize more on comfort, appearance and amenities in the car rather than economy, performance and utility.</p><p>Today cars have not just remained a means of transport, but have become a luxurious, comfortable and swift medium of travel. The total sale of this luxury car in May 2010 was around 17859.</p><p>Mercedes-Benz followed BMW and was listed second in the list of luxury car brands. The sales of Mercedes increased to 25.4% in this particular year. The C-class Mercedes-Benz model was the best-selling model of Mercedes in May 2010. The total sale of this brand in 2010 was around 19,976.</p><p>The best-selling model of this brand was Lexus RX. In the month of May 2010, the total sales of Lexus reached to about 22,216.</p><p>Cadillac CTS remained the best-selling model of this brand in 2010.
