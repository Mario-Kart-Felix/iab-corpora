				<h1 class="entry-title">Auto Review: 2011 Honda Odyssey Minivan EX-L</h1>
		
		<div class="post-info"><span class="date time published" title="2011-01-27T19:23:06+0000">2011/01/27</span>  By <span class="author vcard"><span class="fn"><a href="http://blog.vinart.com/author/acreportergirl/" title="Posts by Stacy Jo" rel="author">Stacy Jo</a></span></span> <span class="post-comments"><a href="http://blog.vinart.com/featured-vehicles/featured-honda-vehicles/auto-review-2011-honda-odyssey-minivan-ex-l/#comments">Leave a Comment</a></span> </div>		<div class="entry-content">
			<p>The <a href="http://blog.vinart.com/vehicles/honda/" >2011 Honda Odyssey Minivan EX-L</a> lives up to its name like no other vehicle. The engine block type is V6 aluminum alloy with 24 valves. It puts out 248 horsepower at 5,700 rpm and 250 pound-feet of torque at 4,800 rpm. Other state-of-the-art technology at work in the engine includes:</p>
<ul>
<li>Multi-point fuel injection</li>
<li>Drive-by-Wire throttle</li>
<li>Variable cylinder management</li>
<li>ULEV-2 emissions rating</li>
<li>Direct Ignition System with immobilizer</li>
</ul>
<p>The standard transmission on the EX-L is a 5-speed automatic. The chassis consists of a MacPherson strut front suspension and Multi-Link Double Wishbone rear suspension. It also features a Variable Power Assist Rack-and-Pinion steering with a 24.0 mm rear stabilizer bar.</p>
<p>The EPA fuel efficiency rating is 18 mpg in the city, 27 mpg on the highway, and 21 mpg combined.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-2383" title="honda-odyssey-minivan-ex-l-2010" src="http://blog.vinart.com/files/2011/01/honda-odyssey-minivan-ex-l-2010.jpg" alt="" width="340" height="215" /></p>
<p>Standard features include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Bluetooth HandsFreeLink</li>
<li>Honda Satellite-Linked Navigation System with voice recognition</li>
<li>Multi-view rear camera</li>
<li>Song By Voice for radio tuning</li>
<li>Removable front center console</li>
<li>HID headlights</li>
<li>Power sliding doors and tailgate</li>
<li>Rear roofline spoiler with integrated brakelight</li>
</ul>
<p>The Odyssey minivan also contains some outstanding safety features.</p>
<ul>
<li>Tire pressure monitoring</li>
<li>Anti-lock braking system</li>
<li>Electronic Brake Distribution</li>
<li>LATCH system for children</li>
<li>State-of-the-art seatbelt and airbag systems</li>
<li>Vehicle Stability Assist with Traction Control</li>
</ul>
<p>The <a href="http://blog.vinart.com/vehicles/honda/" >2011 Honda Odyssey Minivan EX-L</a> features the following standard audio features:</p>
<ul>
<li>AM/FM CD player</li>
<li>MP3/WMA playback</li>
<li>7 speakers</li>
<li>USB audio interface</li>
<li>XM radio</li>
</ul>
<p>The Honda Odyssey is as classy and comfortable as it is beautiful to look at.</p>
