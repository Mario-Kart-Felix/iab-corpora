   <blockquote> <h1>
    LEER  </h1>
  <p style="text-align: left;">LEER truck caps, also called truck toppers and camper shells, are constructed of strong, resilient fiberglass or tough non-corrosive aluminum.&nbsp; Most are the same height as the truck cab; some models are somewhat taller.&nbsp; Most can accommodate a roof rack, adding to their cargo-carrying capacity.&nbsp; Fiberglass camper shells are painted using formulas that match those originally used by the truck manufacturer, and preferred by residential customers.&nbsp; Aluminum caps are preferred by contractors, service technicians, and others working in rugged environments.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Tonneau covers (tonnos, lids and cargo covers) retain the characteristic profile of pickup trucks, covering and securing the truck bed at the top of the cargo box.&nbsp; LEER tonneau covers are made of fiberglass and provide attractive, secure storage, transportation and security, turning the truck bed into a huge lockable "trunk," enhancing the finished look of the vehicle, and improving gas mileage by reducing aerodynamic drag.&nbsp;</p>
