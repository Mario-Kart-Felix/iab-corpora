		<h1>
		Autocross	</h1>
	
	
		<div class="category-desc">
							<p> </p>
<p>Autocross (also known as Solo II or Autoslalom) is a low-cost, "grassroots" Motorsport that allows anyone to enjoy the thrill of automotive competition in a safe, controlled and legal environment. Autocross has been an important part of Ontario's Motorsport history for almost fifty years.</p>
<p>In an autocross event, competitors drive through a ‘race’ course that is laid out with pylons on a large parking lot or other paved surface (in winter, it’s called snow cross . Vehicles race independently of each other, thus reducing risks and allowing drivers to concentrate on threshold braking and precision cornering while having their run electronically timed to thousandths of a second. The course design emphasizes car handling skills and maneuverability  Vehicle speeds rarely exceed normal legal highway speeds.<br /><br />You don't have to be a professional race car driver to participate! In fact, the series is committed to welcoming the participation of beginners who feel a need to see what they and their cars can do. Most competitors compete using their 'daily driver' (a.k.a. street car) against vehicles of similar abilities.</p>
<p>Come join us at the Scotia Bank Place parking lot and test your skills against other drivers and their cars. And don't think the action stops when the snow flies! During the winter months, the snow cross series runs at Capital City Speedway.</p>
<p>For the latest news and everything you need to get going in the current season, please visit the <a href="/cms/../phpbb/viewforum.php?f=3" target="_blank" title="Autocross Forum">Autocross Section of the Forums</a>.</p>
