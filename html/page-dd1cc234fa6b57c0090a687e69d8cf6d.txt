<div><b>Definition: </b>SAE is an acronym for Society of Automotive Engineers. The SAE is a nonprofit engineering and scientific organization dedicated to the advancement of mobility technology. </div><div><b>Examples: </b></div>For manufacturers and engineers, SAE standards are used exclusively; in particular, hardware such as nuts and bolts, and oil specifications, all carry SAE codes. For example, engine oil such as Mobil&#8217;s Mobil 1 fully synthetic brand is available in a number of SAE specifications. A common SAE <a href="http://classicmotorcycles.about.com/od/serviceandrepair/a/MotorcycleOilAdvice.htm">motorcycle oil</a> specification is 10W-30; however, this oil may also carry country specific specifications such as the US&#8217;s API (American Petroleum Institute) code.<!--/gc-->
