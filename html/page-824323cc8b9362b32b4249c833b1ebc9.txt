As time went on, many of our regular service customers who saw these cars being serviced asked if we could service their Classic cars as well. Of course the answer was yes! As a result, the Classic Auto Service business has grown to be a major part of our service operation at the Premier Collection facility.<br /> <br />

<h3>ASSURANCE</h3>

<h2>Classic Auto Service Team</h2>

Ray transferred to Galpin Auto Sports where he worked in our late model customizing center performing all types of customizing work and accessory installation. When we approached Ray with the idea, he could not wait to get started. <br /> <br /> <strong> Service Consultant John Alavazo</strong> has been with the Galpin Team since 2002.
