<h1>KTM Auto Celebrates 8 years in Business</h1>
<p>Plymouth, NH - KTM Auto in downtown Plymouth is celebrating 8 years
          in business. KTM Auto was started by Kevin Maass in 2001 and the
          business has grown to include a GM factory trained mechanic, Cadillac
          specialist, Subaru expert and advance diagnostic technology.</p>
<p>Mechanics include Gary who is GM factory trained and has over 30
          years automotive experience, Eric, a domestic and light truck
          specialist with 25 years automotive experience and Jay a Subaru
          specialist with over 15 years automotive experience who has been
          trained in Chrysler, VW, GM, ford and Toyota diagnostics. Jay also has
          experience and a proficient use of GM Tech 2, Ford IDS, ESAE, Launch &amp;
          Mastertech as well as Toyota Scan Tools.. Kevin is a graduate of a 2
          year NY State automotive excellence program as well as passing
          multiple ASE Certification tests and has over 18 years experience in
          the automotive industry.</p>
<p>Also within KTM Auto you will find Tyred Iron Motorcycle and
          snowmobile repair run by an MMI graduate and master of technology as a
          Harley Davidson Mechanic, offering service and repair on ATV?s,
          snowmobiles, metric bikes and Harleys of all shapes and sizes.</p>
<p>KTM Auto recently updated their automotive diagnostic tolls and
          owns a VW Audi factory specific diagnostic tool to fix your pesky
          check engine light. KTM Auto is a full service general repair facility
          offering NH state inspections, alignments, or anything your vehicle
          needs to be safe, they also offer the cheapest oil change price in
          town at just $19.95 for most vehicles.</p>
<p>With over 70 years combined experience and mechanics you can trust,
