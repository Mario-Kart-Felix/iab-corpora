                <h1>Choose ATC for advanced Hybrid Technology Training in Vancouver</h1>
<p>Hybrid vehicles technology and service is one of many new automotive fields &#8212; and Automotive Training Centre is the ideal place to get the hybrid technology training you need to enter this exciting field.</p>
<h2>Here’s what you can expect to learn:</h2>
<ul>
<li>how to service hybrids</li>
<li>safety issues</li>
<li>general maintenance</li>
<li>subsystems, control systems and hardware choices, including control and communication circuits, 12 volts and high volts circuits, power accessories (power steering, air conditioning, and charging system) and electric motors operation</li>
<li>energy storage systems</li>
<li>designs of battery packs for energy storage capacity, accessibility, location, environmental protection and safety</li>
<li>Transmission and drive train modules, including regenerative braking, planetary gear sets and launch control.</li>
</ul>
<p>Even with the serious warnings about high voltage components, you will be surprised to learn that many of these complex Hybrid drive systems can be repaired in aftermarket shops.</p>
<p>This is a 16-hour course that can be taken over two Saturdays.   Applicants must be 19 years old or be a high school graduate. You also need a Hybrid Technology and Service &#8211; Student Guide, which is provided to all ATC students who take this course.</p>
<p>Tuition Fees: $259 + HST (includes Student Guide).</p>
