                    <h2>RECENT PUBLICATIONS</h2><ul class="items"><li><img src="/~/media/GIAWB/Doing Business/Images/Covers/DB13-G7-cover.jpg" alt="" width="100" height="150" /><h3>Doing Business in the g7 + 2013</h3><p><p>
This is a special report that draws on the global <em>Doing Business</em> project and the findings of <em>Doing Business 2013</em>. The report compares business regulations and identifies good practices in the economies of the g7+ group, a country-owned and country-led global mechanism to monitor, report and draw attention to the unique challenges faced by fragile states.</p></p><a href="/reports/regional-reports/g7plus">Read more »</a></li><li><img src="/~/media/GIAWB/Doing Business/Images/Covers/DB13-report.gif" alt="" width="100" height="150" /><h3>Doing Business 2013</h3><p><p><em>
<a href="/reports/global-reports/doing-business-2013">Doing Business 2013: Smarter Regulations for Small and Medium-Size Enterprises</a></em> assesses regulations affecting domestic firms in 185 economies and ranks the economies in 10 areas of business regulation. This year's report offers four new datasets: <a href="/data/distance-to-frontier">Distance to frontier</a>, <a href="/data/good-practice">good practices</a>, <a href="/data/exploretopics/Entrepreneurship">entrepreneurship</a>, and <a href="/data/Transparency">transparency in business regulations</a>.</p></p><a href="/reports/global-reports/doing-business-2013">Read more »</a></li></ul><h2>Subnational Reports</h2><div><ul class="items">

    <h3>
    <img alt="" width="100" height="151" src="/~/media/GIAWB/Doing Business/Images/Covers/DB13-Italy-cover.gif" />Doing Business in Italy 2013</h3>
    <p>
    </p>
    <p>
    This report provides micro-level data for the business regulatory framework for domestic small and medium businesses in 13 Italian cities and 7 ports. The report examines 5 areas as measured by Doing Business: starting a business, dealing with construction permits, registering property, enforcing contracts and trading across borders.</p>
