        <p>Hybrids and electric vehicles (EVs) are a promising growth area for the global automotive industry. The emergence of the sector has been accompanied by the entry of new participants, as well as new technologies. It is likely to be a significant factor in the long-term growth and rebalancing of the industry after the economic crisis. The development of this sector will be determined by political, technological and economic forces.</p>
					<p>The IHS Automotive dedicated website, <a href="http://www.hybrid-ev.com">Hybrid-ev.com</a>, provides a technical and business perspective of the changing developments for a range of propulsion and charging options. <br /></strong> Hybrid-EV Monthly newsletter looks to map the changing pattern of development of the range of propulsion and charging options, from a technical and business perspective. It includes in-depth analysis of business models, location reports, supplier and OEM profiles and much more.</p>
