		<h2 id="anchor-top"></h2><h2>Road Service Benefits Guide</h2><p>To use this guide: select a topic from the menu below, or for a complete overview of all Road Service benefits scroll down the page. You may also Print a Copy to keep in your glove compartment or in your home for quick reference.</p><div class="subcol1"><ul class="bullet gradient1"><li><a href="#anchor-gethelp">How to Get Help When You Need It Most</a></li><li><a href="#anchor-covered">Vehicles and services covered under Basic and Plus Membership</a></li><li><a href="#anchor-restrict">Restrictions</a></li></ul></div><div class="subcol2"><ul class="bullet gradient1"><li><a href="#anchor-points">Additional Points</a></li><li><a href="#anchor-liability">AAA Liability</a></li><li><a href="#anchor-benefit">Additional Benefits</a></li><li><a href="#anchor-take">Contact Us</a></li></ul></div><h2 id="anchor-gethelp">How to Get Help When You Need It Most</h2><div class="bordered"><p class="right"><img alt="Get Help" class="bordered" height="100" src="/_resources/www/aaasjersey/_system/content/car_rsa_guide_gethelp_120x100.jpg" width="120" /></p></div><p><strong>1) Request Service:</strong></p><ul class="bullet"><li>Call 1-800-AAA-HELP (1-800-222-4357). Wherever you are in the U.S. and Canada, your call will be dispatched to a nearby AAA/CAA-affiliated service facility that can help you — 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.</li><li>Use <a href="/CarDriving/RoadServiceOnline" id="no_link" onclick="window.open(this.href);return false;" onkeypress="window.open(this.href);return false;" title="Road Service Online">Road Service Online</a> to request Roadside Assistance online.</li></ul><p><strong>2) Provide the necessary information:</strong></p><p>To help us and the service facility assist you faster, be ready to give:</p><ul class="bullet"><li>Your name and membership number.</li><li>Your exact location and nearest intersecting street.</li><li>The make, model, color and license number of the vehicle.</li><li>Whatever you can tell us about the trouble you’re having.</li><li>If possible, give us a phone number where you can be reached.</li></ul><p>You should remain with the vehicle to receive service. However, exceptions can be made under extenuating circumstances. If you are able to start or move your vehicle before AAA arrives, please be sure to call us and cancel your request. If you can’t contact or reach AAA Roadside Assistance, call the nearest available towing or repair facility. Pay for the service and get an itemized receipt that includes the printed name, address and phone number of the facility. Submit the bill to AAA Reimbursement Services using the appropriate <a class="autohead" href="/FetchFile.ashx?id=337e6caf-9350-4e4b-8d8f-477d158b2c1b" onclick="window.open(this.href);return false;" onkeypress="window.open(this.href);return false;" title="Emergency Road Service Reimbursement Form">ERS Refund Form</a>.</p><p><a href="#anchor-top">Return to Top</a></p><p> </p><h2 id="anchor-covered"><strong>Vehicles and services covered under Basic and Plus Membership</strong></h2><div class="bordered"><p class="right"><img alt="Basic and Plus Coverage" class="bordered" height="100" src="/_resources/www/aaasjersey/_system/content/car_rsa_guide_basicplus_120x100.jpg" width="120" /></p></div><p>Roadside Assistance means 24-hour assistance for minor mechanical first aid; battery service, tire change, out of fuel, lockout service, extricating and towing. Roadside Assistance will be provided to you when the vehicle you are either driving or riding in becomes disabled. The following types of vehicles are eligible for service:</p><ul class="bullet"><li>Four-wheel motor-driven passenger vehicles (i.e. cars, vans and pick up trucks, including rented passenger vehicles) if those services can be safely delivered without using heavy duty equipment.</li><li>Dual rear wheel unloaded pickup trucks are provided all services except tire service.</li><li>Dual rear wheel campers and motor homes are covered for lockout, battery service and out of fuel services only.</li></ul><p><a href="#anchor-top">Return to Top</a></p><p> </p><h2 id="anchor-restrict"><strong>Some restrictions apply</strong></h2><div class="bordered"><p class="right"><img alt="Some restrictions apply" class="bordered" height="100" limousines, step vans or truck bodies other than regular pickup trucks.</li><li>Services to vehicles which AAA has determined to be unsafe due to overloading or which require heavy duty equipment to
