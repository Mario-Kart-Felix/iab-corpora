<div class="ccsftitle section"><h2><p>Welcome!<br>
</p>
</h2>
</div>
<div class="text parbase section"><p>We offer unique training programs, driven by the passion and craftsmanship of our trade, to our diverse community. Our faculty are well equipped to provide learning and repair experience for a large spectrum of automobile, motorcycle, building maintenance and construction-related areas. Entry-level novices and seasoned veterans find certificate and degree programs that meet their needs with us at the Evans Campus.</p>
</div>
<div class="ccsftitle section"><h2><p><a href="/ccsf/en/educational-programs/school-and-departments/school-of-science-and-mathematics/automotive_construction_maintenance/autotech.html">Automotive Technology</a><br>
</p>
</h2>
</div>
<div class="parbase textimage section">
 
 
 
      <!-- Begin Image Text -->
      <div class="para-block">      
        <div class="rimage"><img title="autobody work in progress" height="160" alt="autobody work in progress" width="160" class="cq-dd-image" src="/content/ccsf/en/educational-programs/school-and-departments/school-of-science-and-mathematics/automotive_construction_maintenance/_jcr_content/contentparsys/textimage_0/image.img.jpg/1317404358782.jpg"></div>
        <p><p><a href="http://www.ccsf.edu/Departments/Automotive_Technology/">Automotive Technology</a> offers entry level and advanced credit and noncredit courses in the Automotive, Auto Body and Motorcycle fields. We also offer four certificate / degree programs: Automotive Technology, Auto Body and Fender, Motorcycle Technology and Custom Motorcycle Design. Our program has served our community for more than 30 years, and our faculty have more than 400 years of combined experience in the field.<br>
</p>

<div class="ccsftitle section"><h2><p><a href="/ccsf/en/educational-programs/school-and-departments/school-of-science-and-mathematics/automotive_construction_maintenance/const-tech.html">Construction Technology</a><br>
</p>
</h2>
</div>
<div class="parbase textimage section">
 
 
 
      <!-- Begin Image Text -->
      <div class="para-block">      
        <div class="rimage"><img title="Construction tools on a desk" height="200" alt="Construction tools on a desk" width="150" class="cq-dd-image" src="/content/ccsf/en/educational-programs/school-and-departments/school-of-science-and-mathematics/automotive_construction_maintenance/_jcr_content/contentparsys/textimage/image.img.jpg/1347989677576.jpg"></div>
        <p><p><a href="http://www.ccsf.edu/Departments/Construction_Technology_Department/">Construction Technology</a> offers entry level credit and non-credit courses in a variety of construction fields. We offer certificate programs with three specializations: Electrical, Carpentry, and Plumbing. We are in the process of adding more green technology courses and certificates. Our mission is to offer opportunities for students to gain the knowledge, practice the skills and develop the abilities required to attain entry-level positions in a variety of construction-related fields.<br>
</p>
