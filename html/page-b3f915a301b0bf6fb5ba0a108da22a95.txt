<h3 class='post-title entry-title' itemprop='name'>
Ford Escape Hybrid - 2009
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-762196245002164341' itemprop='description articleBody'>
<div align="center"><a href="http://www.freeautomechanic.com/newcars.html"><img id="BLOGGER_PHOTO_ID_5305668498765249186" style="DISPLAY: block; MARGIN: 0px auto 10px; WIDTH: 320px; CURSOR: hand; HEIGHT: 160px; TEXT-ALIGN: center" alt="" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/_YN8iBBL6lrw/SaGFY5Th-qI/AAAAAAAAA2Y/efkrv7V8CjQ/s320/ford_escape_hybrid_2009.jpg" border="0" /></a> 2009 Ford Escape Hybrid</div><div align="center"></div><div align="left">MSRP : $29,645 - $33,725
Invoice : $27,422 - $31,135</div><div align="left"></div><div align="left">
Engine : 2.5L Atkinson-Cycle I-4 Engine, Horsepower : 153 @ 6,000 rpm Torque : 136 @ 4,500</div><div align="left"></div><div align="left">The 2009 Escape Hybrid is a 4-door, 5-passenger sport-utility, available in 4 trims, ranging from the FWD to the Limited 4WD. </div><div align="left"></div><div align="left">Upon introduction, the FWD is equipped with a standard 2.5-liter, I4 , 153-horsepower, hybrid engine that achieves 34-mpg in the city and 31-mpg on the highway. The Limited 4WD is equipped with a standard 2.5-liter, I4 , 153-horsepower, hybrid engine that achieves 29-mpg in the city and 27-mpg on the highway. A variable speed automatic transmission with overdrive is standard on both trims. </div><div align="left"></div><div align="left">The <a href="http://www.fordvehicles.com/suvs/escapehybrid/index.asp">2009 Escape Hybrid</a> is a carryover from 2008.
