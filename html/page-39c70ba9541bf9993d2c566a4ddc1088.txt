<p style="font-size: 0.9em;">Virtually every major auto manufacturer in the world, along with numerous smaller outfits, is developing a plug-in vehicle and Plug In America is tracking their progress. Our list below represents highway-capable cars and trucks, 2- and 3-wheeled, and commercial vehicles. To be included on this list, legitimate companies must have completed a concept, demonstration, or mule vehicle, and we list these at our discretion.&nbsp;<a href="http://www.eaaev.org/eaalinks.html#NEVs">Low-speed electric vehicles</a> are not included. Vehicle prices listed do not include&nbsp;<a href="/node/20/" style="text-decoration: none; outline-style: none; outline-width: initial; outline-color: initial;">rebates</a>.</p>
<p style="font-size: 0.9em;">If you have an update to this list,&nbsp;<a href="/node/141/" style="text-decoration: none; outline-style: none; outline-width: initial; outline-color: initial;">please fill us in</a>. Plug In America needs your support to keep this list current. Please&nbsp;<a href="https://salsa.democracyinaction.org/o/2711/t/11294/shop/shop.jsp?storefront_KEY=524" style="text-decoration: none; outline-style: none; outline-width: initial; outline-color: initial;">become a member</a>&nbsp;or&nbsp;<a href="https://salsa.democracyinaction.org/o/2711/t/11294/shop/custom.jsp?donate_page_KEY=3570" style="text-decoration: none; outline-style: none; outline-width: initial; outline-color: initial;">make a donation</a>&nbsp;today!</p>
<p style="font-size: 0.9em;"><strong>Key terms:</strong>&nbsp;EV: Electric Vehicle,&nbsp;<span>PHEV</span>: Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicle (includes&nbsp;Extended Range Electric Vehicles),&nbsp;<span>AER</span>: All-electric range,&nbsp;<span>SOC</span>: state-of-charge,&nbsp;<a href="/accessory-tracker?type=EVSE">EVSE</a>: Electric Vehicle Supply Equipment (Charging Station), SAE J1772™: US charging connector standard,&nbsp;<span>CVRP</span>:&nbsp;<a href="http://energycenter.org/index.php/incentive-programs/clean-vehicle-rebate-project/cvrp-eligible-vehicles" style="text-decoration: none; outline-style: none; outline-width: initial; outline-color: initial;">Clean Vehicle Rebate Project</a></p>
