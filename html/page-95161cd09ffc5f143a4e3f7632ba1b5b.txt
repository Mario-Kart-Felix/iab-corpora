All models will be offered with a revised exterior styling, an enhanced interior and upgraded technologies.</p><p>The 2010 <a

For the 2010 model year, the G37 receives two new exterior colors, Smoky Quartz and Graphite Shadow and Maple Accent Wood Trim on the interior. The new G will also be available with several packages and options like the Premium Package, Navigation Package, Technology Package, separate Sport Packages for the G37 Journey and G37x AWD, as well as a Performance Tire &amp; Wheel Package.</p><p><i>Press release after the jump.</i></p></div><div
class="article_container"><h2><p><em>Press release</em></p></h2><p>Since Infiniti first burst on the automotive scene 20 years ago, it has maintained a reputation as having not only one of the most dynamic product lineups in the industry but also one of the freshest &ndash; with a constant stream of new or enhanced models. The 2010 calendar year is no exception with a greatly refined <a
class='spip_auto' href='http://www.topspeed.com/cars/infiniti-g/ke1264.html' title='Infiniti G'>Infiniti G</a>37 Sedan debuting prior the scheduled launches of all-new, next generations of the Infiniti M and Infiniti QX.<br
/><br
/></p><div
class="article_image_"> <a
href="http://www.topspeed.com/cars/infiniti/2010-infiniti-g37-sedan-ar82324/picture336570.html" type="image/jpeg" title='Infiniti G37 Sedan'> <noscript> <img
src="http://pictures.topspeed.com/IMG/crop/200912/2010-infiniti-g37-sedan-3_600x0w.jpg" width="100%" height="451" alt="Infiniti G37 Sedan" /> </noscript> <img
class="lazyload" src="http://pictures00.topspeed.com/images/spacer.gif" data-original-width="2000" data-original= "http://pictures.topspeed.com/IMG/crop/200912/2010-infiniti-g37-sedan-3_600x0w.jpg" width="100%" height="451" alt="Infiniti G37 Sedan" /> </a></div><p>For 2010, the G Sedan, Infiniti&rsquo;s top-selling model, features revised exterior styling, an enhanced interior treatment and upgraded technology features. Most of the enhancements, except for the exterior refinements, are also available on the 2010 G37 Coupe and recently introduced G37 Convertible. 2010 G buyers have a range of four well-equipped models to choose from &ndash; G37 Sedan, G37 Sedan Journey, G37S Sedan Sport 6MT and G37x Sedan AWD.<br
/><br
/>All 2010 G Sedans are equipped with a refined 328-horsepower 3.7-liter VVEL (Variable Valve Event &amp; Lift) V6. A smooth, efficient 7-speed automatic transmission is standard on all models except the G37 Sport 6MT, which, as appropriate for a cutting-edge performance sports sedan, comes with a close-ratio 6-speed manual transmission. The G37x Sedan AWD is equipped with an advanced Infiniti Intelligent All-Wheel Drive system for enhanced all-weather performance.<br
/><br
/></p><div
class="quote_right"><p>3.7-liter VVEL 24-valve DOHC V6 engine; 328 hp and 269 lb-ft of torque; 7,600 rpm redline</p></div><p>&ldquo;The Infiniti G is a vehicle for people who never slow down or settle for the status quo, so our goal has always been to keep the design fresh, the performance engaging and the equipment perfectly in-tune with the needs of these active owners,&rdquo; said Ben Poore, vice president, Infiniti Business Unit.<br
/></p><h3>Refreshed, Sporty Styling For 2010</h3><div
class="article_image_"> <a
href="http://www.topspeed.com/cars/infiniti/2010-infiniti-g37-sedan-ar82324/picture336572.html" type="image/jpeg" title='Infiniti G37 Sedan'> <noscript> <img
src="http://pictures.topspeed.com/IMG/crop/200912/2010-infiniti-g37-sedan-5_600x0w.jpg" width="100%" height="434" alt="Infiniti G37 Sedan" /> </noscript> <img
class="lazyload" src="http://pictures00.topspeed.com/images/spacer.gif" data-original-width="2000" data-original= "http://pictures.topspeed.com/IMG/crop/200912/2010-infiniti-g37-sedan-5_600x0w.jpg" width="100%" height="434" alt="Infiniti G37 Sedan" /> </a></div><p>For 2010, the G Sedan retains its visually striking exterior design, with its long, wave-like hood, raked A-pillars and athletic stance projecting a balance of power and grace that is suggestive of the performance potential within.<br
/><br
/>The front of the G Sedan is highlighted by its new headlights and grille, which offer a crisp form and high quality feel. The G&rsquo;s standard fog lights have been moved from their previous location in the headlight cluster to the lower fascia where they help provide a more upscale and distinctive appearance. Every G offers standard High Intensity Discharge (HID) bi-functional Xenon headlights. G37 Sport models also receive a new, distinctive front fascia and separate fog lights. The silhouette offers a wedged roofline and a sweeping bodyside character line that emphasizes both the rich shoulder surfaces and the G&rsquo;s rear-wheel drive orientation.<br
/><br
/>In the rear, the refined, evolutional L-shaped LED taillights express the G heritage and the <a
class='spip_auto' href='http://www.topspeed.com/cars/infiniti/index45.html' title='Infiniti'>Infiniti</a> design identity. The trunk lid is accented by an integrated spoiler, while prominent dual exhaust outlets complete the sporty appearance. All 2010 models receive a revised rear fascia with an improved exhaust finisher treatment. G37, G37 Journey and G37x AWD models feature a new 17-inch 5-spoke &ldquo;triple forks&rdquo; aluminum-alloy wheel design.<br
/><br
/></p><div
class="article_image_"> <a
href="http://www.topspeed.com/cars/infiniti/2010-infiniti-g37-sedan-ar82324/picture336576.html" type="image/jpeg" title='Infiniti G37 Sedan'> <noscript> <img

class="lazyload" src="http://pictures00.topspeed.com/images/spacer.gif" data-original-width="2000" data-original= "http://pictures.topspeed.com/IMG/crop/200912/2010-infiniti-g37-sedan-9_600x0w.jpg" width="100%" height="451" alt="Infiniti G37 Sedan" /> </a></div><p>Two new exterior colors are offered for 2010, Smoky Quartz and Graphite Shadow, along with carryover colors Vibrant Red, Lakeshore Slate, Liquid Platinum, Blue Slate, Black Obsidian and Moonlight White.<br
/></p><h3>Interior Refinements Add to Crafted, Quality Feel</h3><p>Like the exterior, the 2010 G Sedan&rsquo;s interior has been given a fresh look and feel for the new model year, along with upgrades to the navigation system and revised optional package content. Every G37 Sedan offers a crafted interior environment and luxury features such as standard leather-appointed seating and an available audiophile-quality Infiniti Studio on Wheels&reg; by Bose&reg; premium audio system, which combines Bose&rsquo;&reg;s 3-way front door speaker array using 10-inch woofers with a parcel shelf-mounted subwoofer.<br
/><br
/>A 2GB Music Box Flash Memory Music Server is available with the Premium Package. Other enhancements for 2010 include a new, predominately white color scheme for the gauges and LCD instrument display, improving both legibility and cohesiveness of design. There is also a new center console design (7AT models) and the previous &ldquo;Washi&rdquo; aluminum trim has been replaced by &ldquo;Shodo&rdquo; aluminum trim inspired by a style of Japanese calligraphy. The Wheat and Stone leather-appointed interior colors are now lighter (Graphite color also available), and the available maple interior accents are now high gloss.<br
/><br
/></p><div
class="article_image_"> <a
href="http://www.topspeed.com/cars/infiniti/2010-infiniti-g37-sedan-ar82324/picture336573.html" type="image/jpeg" title='Infiniti G37 Sedan'> <noscript> <img
src="http://pictures.topspeed.com/IMG/crop/200912/2010-infiniti-g37-sedan-6_600x0w.jpg" width="100%" height="451" alt="Infiniti G37 Sedan" /> </noscript> <img
class="lazyload" src="http://pictures00.topspeed.com/images/spacer.gif" data-original-width="2000" data-original= "http://pictures.topspeed.com/IMG/crop/200912/2010-infiniti-g37-sedan-6_600x0w.jpg" width="100%" height="451" alt="Infiniti G37 Sedan" /> </a></div><p>Unchanged is the G&rsquo;s harmony between the dedicated driver&rsquo;s cockpit and comfortable passenger space &ndash; and between modern technology and traditional Japanese design cues. The result is a &ldquo;modern warmth&rdquo; that is unique in the sport sedan segment. The driver&rsquo;s area has a simple, intuitive layout with easy-to-read electroluminescent gauges. A center information display, located between the tachometer and speedometer, conveys updates from the drive computer (average fuel consumption, instantaneous fuel economy, average speed, elapsed time, running distance, distance to empty, outside air temperature, warning displays, odometer and automatic transmission shift indicator).<br
/><br
/>The G&rsquo;s available Infiniti Navigation System has been enhanced with 08IT technology and a new display screen with higher resolution than the previous generation system&rsquo;s screen. Along with the previously available XM NavTraffic&reg; Real-Time Traffic Information, the navigation system adds NavWeather&trade; with Real-Time Weather and 3-day forecast (XM&reg; subscription required, sold separately), Streaming Audio via Bluetooth&reg; and Zagat&reg; Restaurant Guide. The system also includes a touch screen, Voice Recognition and a 9.3GB Music Box Hard Drive.<br
/><br
/>Other technologies include standard Infiniti Intelligent Key with Push Button Ignition, standard 7.0-inch color monitor display, standard XM&reg; Satellite Radio (XM&reg; subscription required, sold separately), available RearView Monitor, Bluetooth&reg; Hands-free Phone System and Intelligent Cruise Control with Preview Braking.<br
/></p><h3>Powerful, Responsive 3.7-liter V6 Performance</h3><div
class="article_image_"> <a
href="http://www.topspeed.com/cars/infiniti/2010-infiniti-g37-sedan-ar82324/picture336567.html" type="image/jpeg" title='Infiniti G37 Sedan'> <noscript> <img
src="http://pictures.topspeed.com/IMG/crop/200912/2010-infiniti-g37-sedan_600x0w.jpg" width="100%" height="450" alt="Infiniti G37 Sedan" /> </noscript> <img
class="lazyload" src="http://pictures00.topspeed.com/images/spacer.gif" data-original-width="2000" data-original= "http://pictures.topspeed.com/IMG/crop/200912/2010-infiniti-g37-sedan_600x0w.jpg" width="100%" height="450" alt="Infiniti G37 Sedan" /> </a></div><p>As stunning as the G Sedan looks from the outside, its real beauty is best experienced from behind the wheel. The G&rsquo;s balanced dynamic performance starts with its advanced 3.7-liter VQ37HR V6 with VVEL (Variable Valve Event &amp; Lift). Rated at 328 horsepower and 269 lb-ft of torque, the engine&rsquo;s VVEL system combines hydraulic-controlled variable valve timing and electronically controlled variable valve lift on the intake side to help improve performance and response. The VVEL system also offers improved emissions and fuel efficiency over the non-VVEL designs. The VVEL intake camshafts, featuring continuously variable lift control teamed with the Continuously Variable Valve Timing Control System (CVTCS), allow for a 7,600 rpm redline.<br
/><br
/>The engine also features rigid ladder frame construction, large crank journal and pin diameters, asymmetric piston skirts, twin knock sensors, an electronically controlled &ldquo;drive-by-wire&rdquo; throttle system, coated lightweight pistons, Iridium spark plugs and a symmetric twin air intake system. The specially tuned dual exhaust system features equal length exhaust manifolds and low muffler exhaust backpressure to help improve engine breathing. Both intake and exhaust enhance driving pleasure with their authentic performance notes.<br
/><br
/>To help take full advantage of the refined engine design, the G37 Sedan comes with a standard (except for the G37S Sport 6MT model) 7-speed automatic transmission with Downshift Rev Matching, Drive Sport mode and ASC (Adaptive Shift Control) that allows for sportier shifts in performance driving situations. Solid magnesium paddle shifters are standard on G37 Special Edition models and as part of the Sport Package for G37 Journey and G37x AWD models. The G37S Sport 6MT features a smooth-shifting, close-ratio 6-speed manual transmission.<br
/><br
