		of applying patterns, (such as camouflage) using state of the 
		art Hydrographic equipment, to the surface of whatever you think needs 
		to be camouflaged/decorated. Let your imagination run wild!&nbsp; In addition 
		to camouflage, we carry other patterns such as Burled Wood, straight 
		wood, carbon fiber, metals, marbles, stones, Snake Skin, animal print and other 
		unique designs.</p>
