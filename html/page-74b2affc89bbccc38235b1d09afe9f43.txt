                                    <p>&nbsp;<font face="Comic Sans MS"><b>Welcome to the MN Chapter, EAA Website! 
   MNEAA</b></font></p>
<p style="margin-right:10; margin-left:10;"><font face="Verdana" size="2">The Electric Auto Association is a non-profit educational
organization dedicated to the promotion of clean, quiet, energy
efficient personal electric vehicle transportation.</font></p>
<p style="margin-right:10; margin-left:10;"><font face="Verdana" size="2">We meet on a monthly basis and discuss a range of topics including
do-it-yourself electric car conversions, current Electric Vehicle (EV) and Plug-In
Hybrid Electric Vehicle (PHEV) news, event organization and much more.</font></p>
<p style="margin-right:10; margin-left:10;"><font face="Verdana" size="2">Check out the links on the  menu above to see the  latest EV

         forms. How about any special tools that you want to share or sell. We have a new <a href="http://www.mneaa.com/toolshare_form.htm">tool 
         share/sell</a> form for that too. Are there Books collecting dust on your shelves,
