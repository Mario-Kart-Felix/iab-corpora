  <h1>Hi-Lift Jacks</h1>
  <h2>Don't Settle for an Imitation &#8212; The Difference is Quality</h2>
      <div id="right"><img width="214" height="242" src="../media/images/hi-lift-jacks.jpg" title="Hi-Lift Jacks: Cast/Steel, All-Cast, and X-Treme" alt="Hi-Lift Jacks: Cast/Steel, All-Cast, and X-Treme" />
	  	<div class="right-impact-box">
        <a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1ezumvHnvQ0" target="_blank"><img src="../media/images/video_hi-lift.jpg" width="97" height="78"></a><br /><a href="http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1ezumvHnvQ0" target="_blank">Watch a video</a><br /> about the benefits of the various types of Hi-Lift Jacks.</div>
	  </div>
        <p>With over 100 years of quality, the Hi-Lift Jack is a rugged, highly versatile jack that puts you in command of situations requiring lifting, pushing, pulling, winching, and clamping. Although light in weight and easy to maneuver, the Hi-Lift Jack offers a rated capacity of 4,660 lbs (2114 kg) and a tested capacity of 7,000 lbs (3175 kg).</p>
        <p>Only Hi-Lift Jacks are made with a two-piece handle and socket design that is built for extended life. Cheaper imitations use a one-piece design that bends easily if lateral pressure is applied while lifting a load.</p>

        <p>Our jacks are designed to help you survive in the most demanding situations &#8211; whether you are in the Moab desert canyons, the Welsh Mountains, the Amazon jungle, or the farmlands of Indiana. With a full-range of specially designed accessories, the Hi-Lift jack is just about the most versatile piece of off-roading/farm/auto recovery equipment you can buy.</p>
        <p>When it's a heavy-duty situation, the tool had better be a Hi-Lift!</p>

      <h3>Hi-Lift Jack Specifications</h3>
      <ul>
        <li>Approximate weight: 30lbs (14 kg)</li>
        <li>4,660 lbs (2113.74 kg) rated capacity</li>
		<li>Tested capacity 7,000 lbs.</li>
        <li>Climbing pins of specially processed steel with 125,000 PSI tensile strength and 100,000 PSI yield.</li>
        <li>Steel bar is manufactured of specially rolled extra high carbon steel with 80,000 pound minimum tensile &amp; carbon .69 to .82.</li>

        <li>Steel handle of 14gauge high-yield structural tubing with minimum yield of 55,000 PSI. 1 5/16&quot; diameter x 30&quot; long.</li>
      </ul>
      <h3>Hi-Lift Jack Features</h3>
      <ul>
        <li>Every Jack comes complete with an adjustable top clamp/clevis for use in clamping and winching.</li>

        <li>Safety bolt is designed to shear at 7,000 lbs. (3175 kg)</li>
        <li>For speedy disengaging, lifting unit automatically drops away when load is removed.</li>
        <li>4 1/2&quot; (11cm) long lifting nose for positive contact with load.</li>
        <li>Steel bar can be reversed for extra long life.</li>
        <li>Low pickup of 4 1/2&quot; (11cm).</li>

        <li>28 square inch base plate.</li>
      </ul>
