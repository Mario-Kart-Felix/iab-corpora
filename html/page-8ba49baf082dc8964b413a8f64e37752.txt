<h1 class="center">Welcome to The Guild of Automotive Restorers</h1>
<p class="center margin5">The Guild is one of the largest and most experienced antique and classic car restoration companies in North America. <br>With well over 1,000 vehicle restorations under its belt ranging from early Brass Era Cars, Grand Classics to the Fifties showboats and muscle cars of the Sixties, The Guild's expertise is unquestioned.</p>

<h4 class="center">Appraisal Services</h4>
<p class="margin20">Do you wonder what your classic is worth? Need that appraisal value for insurance purposes? Contact us for a professional appraisal. Call The Guild of Automotive Restorers at <strong>905-775-0499</strong> or <a href="contact.html" class="" title="Contact Us">Email us</a>.</p>
<p class="bar"></p>
<p class="center margintop10"><img src="img/the-guild-of-automotive-restorers-1929-hupmobile.jpg" alt="The Guild of Automotive Restorers Hupmobile" /></p>
<h4 class="center">One of Our Proud Restorations</h4>
<p>The Guild restored this car over nineteen years ago and its owner, Hugh Montgomery still uses it almost every day in the summer. It is very satisfying for the restorer to know and have constant contact with the owner of a car as the restoration matures. This is a car of which we have always been very proud of.</p>
<p class="margin20"><a href="1925hupmobile.html" title="The Guild's Love for Hupmobiles">Read  More &raquo;</a> about our love for Hupmobiles.</p>

<h4 class="center capitalize">Specialized restoration Services</h4>
<p><strong>The Guild of Automotive Restorers</strong> restores antique and classic cars for clients worldwide.
<!--<p> currently offers specialized services to those wishing to restore their own projects.</p>-->
<h4 class="margin2">Some services we offer are:</h4>
<ul class="margin5 bullet capitalize">
  <li>Custom Paint and Airbrushing</li>
  <li>Specialty Sandblasting - Individual Parts or Lots</li>
  <li>Fuel, Oil, Water, etc. pump rebuilding</li>
  <li>Electric Motor rewinding</li>
  <li>Remanufacture/Repouring of Babbitt Bearings</li>
  <li>Hard to find parts locating</li>
  <li>Aluminum and Magnesium Welding</li>
  <li>Custom Chroming</li>
  <li>Custom Polishing</li>
  <li>Custom Fabrication</li>
  <li>Specialty Machining</li>
</ul>
