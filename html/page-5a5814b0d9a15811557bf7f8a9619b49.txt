			var nameEQ = name + "=";
			var ca = document.cookie.split(';');

				<h1>Wagon</h1>
			</div>
				<p class="module-description">
					A wagon is a car body style variant of a sedan with its roof extended rearward over a shared passenger/cargo volume with access at the back via a third or fifth door (the lift gate or tailgate), instead of a trunk lid. The body style transforms a standard three-box design into a two-box design to include A, B &#038; C pillars, as well as a D pillar. Wagons usually have fold-down rear seats to allow flexibility in allocating space to either passengers or cargo.				</p>
