<h3>Lincoln College of Technology &#8211; Nashville has been an authority in automotive education for over 90 years.</h3>
<p><img src="/images/nadc250x100_14.gif" alt="Nashville Auto-Diesel College" hspace="6" vspace="6" width="250" height="100" align="right" />Lincoln College of Technology &#8211; Nashville was established in 1919 providing strictly auto-mechanic training. Since then Lincoln College of Technology &#8211; Nashville has expanded to cover all areas of automotive and diesel repair and maintenance. Lincoln College of Technology &#8211; Nashville also offers courses in collision repair and refinishing technology as well. LCT students come from all over the country and interact both in class and out. Lincoln College of Technology &#8211; Nashville also boasts two on-campus dormitories, a full-service cafeteria, and off campus apartments for student housing.Â <a title="Nashville Auto-Diesel College - NADC" rel="nofollow" href="http://www.mechanicschoolsdirectory.com/nashville-auto-diesel-college/nadc-contact/"></a></p>
<p>Students at Lincoln College of Technology &#8211; Nashville have access to the fully equipped auto and diesel shop facilities as well as the collision repair and refinishing facilities. LCT &#8211; Nashville also provides hands on training from ASE Certified instructors and offers the NATEF Master Program and Custom Paints Program.</p>
