This course will provide individuals with the knowledge, skills and tools needed to safely repair and maintain hybrid and electric vehicles.</p>
<p><a href="#DatesAndLocations"><img onmouseover="this.src='/images/button 60.png';" onmouseout="this.src='/images/button 59.png';" src="/images/button%2059.png" alt="button 59" height="39" width="273" /></a></p>

<ul>
<li>There are over two million hybrid cars on the road today.</li>
<li>It is important that every after-market auto shop be confident in their ability to service these fundamentally different vehicles.</li>
<li>Research has shown that every time a technician purchases a new component of hybrid vehicles instead of repairing it, <strong>they are losing $1000</strong>.</li>
<li>This course will prepare mechanics to accurately diagnose and troubleshoot maintenance issues with hybrid and electric vehicles.</li>
</ul>
<p><a name="DatesAndLocations"></a></p>
</ul><ul id="what_youll_learn" class="hide ct"></h3>
<p>CleanEdison's 5-day intensive hands-on course provides participants with a working knowledge of advanced vehicle technologies found in hybrid and electric vehicles.</p>

<li>“Real world problems that technicians face with hybrid electric vehicles</li>
<li>Safety equipment and procedures</li>
<li>Energy management</li>
<li>Power converters</li>
<li>Dc-dc converters</li>
<li>Electric machines</li>
<li>Battery packs</li>
</ul>
