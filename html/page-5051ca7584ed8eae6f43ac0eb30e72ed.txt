<p><span class="inline inline-left"><img src="http://www.autospectator.com/cars/files/images/2012-Nissan-Versa-Hatchback-011.jpg" alt="2012 Nissan Versa Hatchback photo -" title="2012 Nissan Versa Hatchback photo -"  class="image image-_original" width="500" height="333" /><span class="caption" style="width: 498px;"><strong><a href="/cars/models/2012-nissan-versa-sedan-review-official-photos-features-specs-pricing" class="alinks-link" title="">2012 Nissan Versa</a> Hatchback photo -</strong></span></span><em>Find more <a href="http://www.autospectator.com/cars/image/tid/6935">new 2012 Nissan Versa Hatchback pictures</a> in our <a href="http://www.autospectator.com/cars/image/tid/6935">2012 Nissan Versa Hatchback photo gallery</a></em></p>
<p>For 2012, the Versa Hatchback is offered in 1.8 S and 1.8 SL models, each equipped with a 122-horsepower 1.8-liter <a href="/cars/reference/car-dictionary-glossary-automotive-termsd/double-overhead-camshaft-dohc" class="alinks-link" title="DOHC - glossary definition">DOHC</a> 4-cylinder engine. The Versa Hatchback S is available with a choice of 6-speed manual or 4-speed automatic, while the Hatchback 1.8 SL is offered exclusively with Nissan’s advanced Xtronic CVT® transmission.</p>
<p><span class="inline inline-left"><img src="http://www.autospectator.com/cars/files/images/2012-Nissan-Versa-Hatchback-017.jpg" alt="2012 Nissan Versa Hatchback photo" title="2012 Nissan Versa Hatchback photo"  class="image image-_original" width="500" height="333" /><span class="caption" style="width: 498px;"><strong>2012 Nissan Versa Hatchback photo</strong></span></span></p>
<p>Refinements for the 2012 model year include the addition of the previous Premium Package content (Nissan Intelligent Key®, leather-wrapped steering wheel, Bluetooth® Hands-free Phone System, 16-inch aluminum-alloy wheels) as standard equipment on the SL model and four new packages for the S model – Convenience Package, 15-inch Aluminum-Alloy Wheels, “Special Edition” Package and Nissan Navigation Package. Also, an auto-dimming rearview mirror, aluminum appearance kickplates and illuminated visor vanity mirrors are now standard on SL models, and Vehicle Dynamic Control (VDC) is standard on all models (previously SL only). </p>
<p><span class="inline inline-left"><img src="http://www.autospectator.com/cars/files/images/2012-Nissan-Versa-Hatchback-013.jpg" alt="2012 Nissan Versa Hatchback photo" title="2012 Nissan Versa Hatchback photo"  class="image image-_original" width="500" height="396" /><span class="caption" style="width: 498px;"><strong>2012 Nissan Versa Hatchback photo</strong></span></span></p>
<p>“The Versa Hatchback again provides a great combination of roominess, style, performance, value and safety – all in a high quality, low cost of ownership package,” said Al Castignetti, vice president and general manager, Nissan Division, Nissan North America, Inc. “With its range of available features such as Nissan Navigation System, Nissan Intelligent Key® and Bluetooth® Hands-free Phone System, Versa is not your typical entry-level vehicle. It’s a great choice for buyers looking for big enjoyment in a compact hatchback.” </p>
<p><span class="inline inline-left"><img src="http://www.autospectator.com/cars/files/images/2012-Nissan-Versa-Hatchback-012.jpg" alt="2012 Nissan Versa Hatchback photo" title="2012 Nissan Versa Hatchback photo"  class="image image-_original" width="500" height="411" /><span class="caption" style="width: 498px;"><strong>2012 Nissan Versa Hatchback photo</strong></span></span></p>
<p>Distinctive Styling, Responsive Performance<br />
The 2012 Nissan Versa Hatchback’s crisp, youthful body form and long roofline suggest both the roominess inside and Versa’s performance capabilities. Its long 102.4-inch wheelbase and short front and rear overhangs create a sporty dynamic look. The Versa Hatchback exterior also features body-color front and rear bumpers and body-color dual remote-controlled outside mirrors. Versa Hatchback has a coefficient of drag of 0.31. Versa Hatchback 1.8 SL models add a higher level of performance styling with a roof-mounted rear spoiler, lower body side sill extensions, unique front and rear fascia and standard fog lights.</p>
<p><span class="inline inline-left"><img src="http://www.autospectator.com/cars/files/images/2012-Nissan-Versa-Hatchback-007.jpg" alt="2012 Nissan Versa Hatchback photo" title="2012 Nissan Versa Hatchback photo"  class="image image-_original" width="500" height="333" /><span class="caption" style="width: 498px;"><strong>2012 Nissan Versa Hatchback photo</strong></span></span></p>
<p>The 2012 Versa Hatchback is available in nine exterior colors: Red Alert, Metallic Blue, Blue Onyx, Espresso Black, Brilliant Silver, Magnetic Gray, Super Black, Fresh Powder and Arctic Blue.</p>
<p>Every 2012 Versa Hatchback comes equipped with a standard 1.8-liter DOHC 4-cylinder engine rated at 122 horsepower and 127 lb-ft of torque. Three transmissions are offered – a 6-speed manual (1.8 S), an electronically controlled 4-speed automatic with overdrive (optional 1.8 S), and Xtronic CVT™ transmission (standard on 1.8 SL).</p>
<p><span class="inline inline-left"><img src="http://www.autospectator.com/cars/files/images/2012-Nissan-Versa-Hatchback-022.jpg" alt="2012 Nissan Versa Hatchback photo" title="2012 Nissan Versa Hatchback photo"  class="image image-_original" width="500" height="333" /><span class="caption" style="width: 498px;"><strong>2012 Nissan Versa Hatchback photo</strong></span></span></p>
<p>Fuel economy for the 2012 Versa Hatchback is rated at 28 mpg City/34 mpg Highway with the Continuously Variable Transmission; 24 mpg City/32 mpg highway with the 4-speed automatic transmission; and 26 mpg City/31 mpg Highway with the 6-speed manual transmission.</p>
<p>All 2012 Versa Hatchbacks are equipped with an independent MacPherson strut front suspension and torsion beam rear suspension, with front and rear stabilizer bars. Other standard equipment includes Electric Power Steering and power-assisted front disc/rear drum brakes and Anti-lock Braking System (<a href="/cars/reference/car-dictionary-glossary-automotive-termsa/abs-antilock-brake-system" class="alinks-link" title="ABS (Antilock Brake System): glossary definition">ABS</a>) with Electronic Brake force Distribution (EBD) and Brake Assist (BA). Vehicle Dynamic Control (VDC) is also standard.</p>
<p><span class="inline inline-left"><img src="http://www.autospectator.com/cars/files/images/2012-Nissan-Versa-Hatchback-024.jpg" alt="2012 Nissan Versa Hatchback photo" title="2012 Nissan Versa Hatchback photo"  class="image image-_original" width="500" height="333" /><span class="caption" style="width: 498px;"><strong>2012 Nissan Versa Hatchback photo</strong></span></span></p>
<p>Versa Hatchback 1.8 S models feature 15-inch styled steel wheels with covers and P185/65HR15 Continental all-season tires. Versa Hatchback 1.8 SL models offer standard 16-inch aluminum-alloy wheels with Continental P195/55R16 all-season tires, while 6-spoke 15-inch aluminum-alloy wheels are optional on 1.8 S models equipped with the 4-speed automatic transmission.</p>
<p>A Comfortable, Flexible Interior<br />
In Versa, Nissan’s design and engineering teams found a way to fit a large interior in a compact car. Interior length, front to back, is more than six feet, while the 60/40 split fold-down rear bench and large cargo area add versatility for owners with highly active lives. </p>
<p><span class="inline inline-left"><img src="http://www.autospectator.com/cars/files/images/2012-Nissan-Versa-Hatchback-021.jpg" alt="2012 Nissan Versa Hatchback photo" title="2012 Nissan Versa Hatchback photo"  class="image image-_original" width="500" height="297" /><span class="caption" style="width: 498px;"><strong>2012 Nissan Versa Hatchback photo</strong></span></span></p>
<p>Along with a spacious cabin, Versa offers a clean, simple, high-quality interior design and excellent craftsmanship. Comfort is a priority for all passengers. The wide, laterally oriented instrument panel underscores Versa’s width, while the low center console flows freely toward the rear, giving a sense of the interior’s length. Versa gauges feature an attractive “dimensional” appearance and white illumination. Interior illumination is a welcoming reddish orange color. The interior design also integrates convenient storage solutions, including in the center console, glove compartment, front doors and an in-dash storage compartment. </p>
<p>The 2012 Versa Hatchback comes with a long list of available technology, including available Nissan Intelligent Key®, Bluetooth® Hands-free Phone System, XM® Satellite Radio (XM® subscription required, sold separately) and Nissan Navigation System with XM NavTraffic® Real-Time Traffic Information (XM® subscription required, sold separately). </p>
<p><span class="inline inline-left"><img src="http://www.autospectator.com/cars/files/images/2012-Nissan-Versa-Hatchback-015.jpg" alt="2012 Nissan Versa Hatchback photo" title="2012 Nissan Versa Hatchback photo"  class="image image-_original" width="500" height="333" /><span class="caption" style="width: 498px;"><strong>2012 Nissan Versa Hatchback photo</strong></span></span></p>
<p>Standard Safety Features<br />
Safety features include the Nissan Advanced Air Bag System (AABS) (includes dual-stage front supplemental air bags and front seat side-impact supplemental air bags for chest protection), and standard roof-mounted curtain side-impact air bags for front and rear outboard occupant head protection.</p>
<p>Additional safety features include standard LATCH (Lower Anchors and Tethers for CHildren) system, child safety rear door locks, child seat upper tether anchors, front seat Active Head Restraints, Vehicle Dynamic Control (VDC), Traction Control System (VDC) and Tire Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS). </p>
<p><span class="inline inline-left"><img src="http://www.autospectator.com/cars/files/images/2012-Nissan-Versa-Hatchback-019.jpg" alt="2012 Nissan Versa Hatchback photo" title="2012 Nissan Versa Hatchback photo"  class="image image-_original" width="500" height="333" /><span class="caption" style="width: 498px;"><strong>2012 Nissan Versa Hatchback photo</strong></span></span></p>
<p>Models, Options and Manufacturing<br />
The 2012 Nissan Versa Hatchback is offered in 1.8 S and 1.8 SL models. Versa 1.8 S includes standard 4-speaker 90-watt AM/FM/CD audio system with auxiliary audio input jack, power windows and door locks, power rear hatch release, ABS and more. Versa Hatchback 1.8 SL models include sport front and rear fascias, rear decklid spoiler, fog lights, 16-inch aluminum-alloy wheels, woven cloth seats, front center armrest with soft pad, Nissan Intelligent Key®, Interface System for iPod®, tonneau cover and much more.</p>
