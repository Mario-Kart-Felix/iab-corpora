					<h3 class="entry-header"><a href="http://blog.getitnext.com/automotive/2008/08/how-to-find-dea.html">How to Find Deals on eBay Motors Easily</a></h3>
		
		<div class="entry-content">
                <input type="hidden" name="zemanta-related" val="" />
			<div class="entry-body">
				<p><a href="http://blog.getitnext.com/photos/uncategorized/2008/08/18/save_money_ebay_2.jpg"><img width="480" height="378" border="0" src="http://blog.getitnext.com/automotive/images/2008/08/18/save_money_ebay_2.jpg" title="Save_money_ebay_2" alt="Save_money_ebay_2" style="margin: 0px 5px 5px 0px; float: left;" /></a>
 I know a lot of people that have successfully bought and sold autos via eBay motors.&nbsp; Most have very favorable experiences but they have all cited the amount of time it takes to find the exact vehicle they are searching for.&nbsp; For those of you looking to find cars and/or parts there is an easier way to find deals and bargains on eBay motors.&nbsp; As you know eBay is a terrific site but in order to find things it takes alot of time or knowledge of advanced search techniques.&nbsp; </p>

<p>The next time you're searching for a vehicle or car parts on eBay Motors try <a href="http://www.getitnext.com">GetItNext</a>.&nbsp; GetItNext is a free site that has simple to use search queries that allows you to search for exactly what you're looking for on eBay and eBay Motors.&nbsp; Once you find the item on GetItNext you will be connected with eBay to bid and or buy the item.&nbsp; In order to the understand GetItNext's searching capabilities let's try an example by searching for a 2007 Honda Accord LX.</p>

I’m sure you all read about the ridiculous prices people are getting for fuel sippers these days.&nbsp; Its been widely reported lately that <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Geo_Metro">Geo Metros</a> are going for 5 to 10 times the <a href="http://www.kbb.com/">blue book</a> value on eBay. </p>

<p>Why you ask?&nbsp; These once laughed at 3 cyclinder engine cars are now all the rage because they get the same mileage as a <a href="http://search.getitnext.com/beta/toyota-prius/find/ebay-deals/toyota+prius">Toyota Prius Hybrid</a>.&nbsp; For people who are looking for simple transportation they are a cheap alternative to get from point A to point B.&nbsp; The <a href="http://search.getitnext.com/beta/1996-geo-metro/find/ebay-deals/1996+geo+metro">1996 Geo Metro</a> average of 40 MPG is close to the <a href="http://search.getitnext.com/beta/toyota-prius-2008/find/ebay-deals/2008+toyota+prius">2008 Toyota Prius</a> and older models like the <a href="http://search.getitnext.com/beta/geo-metro-1991/find/ebay-deals/geo+metro+1991">1991 Geo Metro</a> and the <a href="http://search.getitnext.com/beta/geo-metro-xfi/find/ebay-deals/geo+metro+xfi">Geo Metro XFi</a> edition, have the same average as the <a href="http://search.getitnext.com/beta/hybrid/find/ebay-deals/hybrid">hybrid</a>.</p>



<p>Now here is a fun fact that will make you think.&nbsp; If you look at closing bids on eBay <a href="http://search.getitnext.com/beta/geo-metros/find/ebay-deals/geo+metros">Geo Metros</a> they have been selling in the $200 – $7000 dollar range.&nbsp; Often times the bidding has been fierce with over 30+ bids per auction.&nbsp; Keep in mind that some of these selling prices are approaching the $9,000 MSRP that the <a href="http://search.getitnext.com/beta/geo-metro-1995/find/ebay-deals/geo+metro+1995">1995 Geo Metro</a> sold for.&nbsp; Its staggering to think that a once laughed after car has now been resurrected into the chic car of 2008. I wouldn’t be surprised to find out that <a href="http://search.getitnext.com/beta/general-motors/find/ebay-deals/general+motors">General Motors</a> has some forgotten warehouse full of these cars that they could barely give away.</p>

<p>Too find your own fuel sipping solution check out these hot deals on these forgotten fuel efficient relics: <a href="http://search.getitnext.com/beta/geo-metro/find/ebay-deals/geo+metro">Geo Metros</a>, <a href="http://search.getitnext.com/beta/ford-festiva/find/ebay-deals/ford+festiva">Ford Festivas</a> and you’ll probably need some parts check out these deals <a href="http://search.getitnext.com/beta/geo-metro-parts/find/ebay-deals/geo+metro+parts">Geo Metro parts</a>, <a href="http://search.getitnext.com/beta/ford-festiva-parts/find/ebay-deals/ford+festiva+parts">Ford Festiva parts</a>.</p>

<p>Continue along with me as my quest for a greener and more fuel efficient solution continues in the upcoming weeks.&nbsp; I will post regularly on how my search is going and any interesting facts that I uncover.&nbsp; Feel free to share any insight and experience you have on the subject.&nbsp; <br />Check out my previous <a href="http://blog.getitnext.com/automotive/hybrid/index.html">hybrid blogs</a> to get caught up on any previous hybrid posts. </p>
