<tr><td valign="top" class="text" width="474">USAA, an American insurance/mortgage company offers a wonderful roadside assistance program to its clients as part of its auto insurance policy. 
<br><br>
Its assistance includes changing flat tires, emergency gasoline delivery along with lost keys and lockouts. United Services Automobile Association tries to get its members back on the road as soon as possible. 
<br><br>
The exceptional company's roadside assistance coverage has been highly appreciated. It provides towing service for those members whose vehicles have broken down on the roads. 
<br><br>
Most people compare insurance companies before getting their policies and this is the same things with USAA. Majority of interested persons make a comparison in terms of its products and services with AAA (another company) in the format, USAA vs. Aaa.
<br><br>
Both offer nearly the same services but the products, services and rates offered by USAA are competitive and rated well in the reviews.

The organization was initially started by a group of military officers in the 1920s who wanted auto insurance for their vehicles. 
<br><br>
Now USAA's coverage has expanded to home, property, life, health insurance, mortgage, financial planning and investment as well as banking services.
<br><br>
The company with its headquarters in San Antonio Texas also has overseas offices in Frankfurt, Germany, London in England. 

<h2>USAA Roadside Assistance Cost</h2>
USAA Roadside Assistance is cost free. The road service is optional with auto insurance coverage. 
<br><br>
If a member experiences a breakdown on the road, they can call the roadside assistance for a towing truck. This service is free as the consumer does not have to pay the tow truck or the tow company for its services as it gets covered by United Services Automobile Association.  

<h2>USAA Roadside Assistance Phone Number</h2>
The USAA Roadside Assistance Phone Number is very much important and handy to the members who are under the program. 
<br><br>
The number or contact comes in use when there is an emergency such when the car has broken down and the concerned individual is in need of immediate assistance. 
<br><br>
Given below is the numbers which the members can phone or call on and the operation hours:<br>
<br>
Roadside Assistance<br>
Phone Number: 1-800-531-8555<br>
Operation Hours: 24/7<br>
<br>
Auto Glass Repair and Replacement<br>
Phone Number: 1-888-452-7754<br>
Operation Hours: 24/7<br>
<br>
Customers can also call on county mutual insurance phone number: 1-800-531-8722.
