          <h5>
          Covered Members
          </h5>
          
        <ul>
          <li>Individual memberships only cover the primary member for roadside assistance. </li>
          <li>Household (<a href="#plus">PLUS</a>) membership covers up to four members including the primary member, a spouse or domestic partner, and children or dependents of the primary member who are between 16-25, are a licensed drivers, are students, and have the same principal residence as the primary member. </li>
          <li>Roadside Assistance coverage follows each individual member. Better World Club covers you in any eligible vehicle that you are driving or in which you are a passenger at the time the vehicle is disabled. We cannot provide service to unattended vehicles. You must be present with the disabled vehicle when a service provider arrives to render service. </li>
        </ul>
        <h5>Emergency Roadside Assistance Service Calls for 
          Auto Membership
          	
          </h5>
        <p>Each covered member is entitled to a total of four 
          service calls, per year. Service calls are defined as 
          one of the following: </p>
        <ul>
          <li>emergency towing</li>
          <li>battery assistance</li>
          <li>flat tire assistance</li>
          <li>gasoline delivery </li>
          <li>extrication (winching)</li>
          <li>lockout services (four calls per household per year)
          </li>
        </ul>
