<h1>New York Automotive &amp; Diesel Institute</h1>
<h3>Jamaica, New York</h3>

<p><span class="blurb">Are you looking for superior training that can allow for quick entry into the field of automotive, diesel, and collision repair? If so, New York Automotive &amp; Diesel Institute (NYADI) could be the perfect choice for your post-secondary education by providing a range of courses to facilitate employment in this rewarding area of the skilled trades industry.</span></p>

<p><span class="blurb">Located in Jamaica, New York, this licensed and accredited school is easily accessible by public transportation, and features instruction using the industry-current equipment that GM, Ford, Chrysler, and Caterpillar utilize. Your instructors ensure that you become skilled in cutting-edge factory procedures, not just "parts changing."</span></p>

<p><span class="blurb">Your choice of courses allows great flexibility in length of program, and area of interest. Full-time classes begin every three weeks, and part-time every five weeks. In as little as eight months you could be starting your career. There is also potential for recruitment to graduate studies with prestigious firms, such as Audi, BMW, Jaguar, International, Mercedes or Volvo. In addition, NYADI employs a career placement department, which has daily interaction with industry employers, allowing for the considerate matching of graduates with available positions.</span></p>

<p><span class="blurb">Whether you see yourself working in an established small business, new car dealership, or want to explore entrepreneurship opportunities, your time at NYADI can provide the cornerstone to vocational success and fulfillment. Find out more by requesting information today!</span></p>
