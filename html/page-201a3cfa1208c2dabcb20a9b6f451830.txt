<h3 style="background: none; margin-bottom: 0px;">Roadside Assistance</h3>
<p class="para">Dodge Roadside Assistance is a dedicated roadside assistance program which provides 24 hour roadside assistance. It only takes a flat tyre in driving rain to make you swear never to be without a roadside assist service. And if you are stranded in the middle of nowhere, you'll be relieved help is just a phone call away.</p>
<p>Dodge Roadside Assistance is provided free of charge to the owner for the duration of the manufacturer's warranty period. Owners can continue to enjoy the benefits of Dodge Roadside Assistance after the warranty period by purchasing annual coverage. When you subscribe for $143 p.a. (including GST) to Dodge Roadside Assistance, you can get help* with:</p>
<ul>
<li>Tele-assist</li>
<li>Roadside assistance</li>
<li>Flat or faulty batteries</li>
<li>Emergency vehicle access</li>
<li>Flat tyres</li>
<li>Emergency fuel</li>
<li>Emergency message relay</li>
<li>Taxi</li>
<li>Accommodation (100km or more from home, up to $150 - one night)</li>
<li>Rental vehicle (100km or more from home, 5 days)</li>
<li>Alternative travel assistance</li>
<li>Vehicle recovery after a breakdown</li>
<li>Personalised holiday planner</li>
<li>Cancellation/re-booking travel arrangements</li>
<li>Emergency parts co-ordination</li>
<li>Medical advice/assistance</li>
<li>Motoring legal advice</li>
</ul>
<p>To subscribe to Dodge Roadside Assistance, please phone 1800 436 343.<br /> Dodge Roadside Assistance... It's a small price to pay for peace of mind.</p>
