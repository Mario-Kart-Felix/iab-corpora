<p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-63088" src="http://www.automobilesreview.com/uploads/2013/04/Mitsubishi-G4-Compact-Sedan-Concept-01.jpg" alt="" width="651" height="488" /></p>
<p><strong><a href="http://www.automobilesreview.com/category/car-makes/mitsubishi/">Mitsubishi</a></strong> has released some photos of the <strong>G4 Compact Sedan Concept</strong>, which will make its official debut at the <strong>2013 Auto Shanghai</strong>.</p>
<p>The manufacturer has published a teaser nearly a month ago, but now there are several photos showing the exterior details. Actually, the <strong>G4 </strong>is a preview for an upcoming affordable small sedan.</p>
<p>The G4 Compact Sedan has a Reinforced Impact Safety Evolution (RISE) body, which Mitsubishi claims it is the lightest in its class thanks to an extensive use of high-tensile steel.</p>
<p>The engine is the well-known 1.2 liter MIVEC with a CVT (Continuous Variable Transmission).</p>
