				            <h2 class="orangeBig1">Information and Benefits</h2>
                            <div style="clear: both" class="autoKnightInfo">
                                
The ShieldMe Auto Club roadside assistance services are provided by the <a class="greenlink" href="http://www.autoknight.com/?refID=SMAC" target="_blank">Auto Knight Motor Club</a>, a proud member of the ShieldMe family of auto club service providers. All membership information, billing, and claims services will be handled by the Auto Knight Motor Club customer service center. Experience the ShieldMe difference - similar roadside assistance service to AAA without the high cost.
                            </div>
                            <h3 class="orangeBig2">Individual and Family Plan</h3>
				            <p><strong>Mechanical First Aid:</strong> Any service requiring a minor adjustment, exclusive of parts; to enable a disabled vehicle.</p>
				            <p><strong>Lockout Services:</strong> If your keys are locked inside the vehicle ShieldMe will send an authorized contractor to unlock your car.</p>
				            <p><strong>Flat tire:</strong> Shield Me will dispatch a service person to change your scheduled vehicle's damaged tire with a usable inflatable spare.</p>
				            <p><strong>Delivery Service:</strong> Delivering needed fuel or fluid to the scheduled vehicle (the cost of fuel or fluids is the customer's responsibility) to get you back on the road.</p>
				            <p><strong>Battery Service:</strong> ShieldMe will deliver needed fuel or fluid to your vehicle to get underway again. (Cost of fuel/fluids is not covered).</p>
				            <p><strong>Towing Service:</strong> ShieldMe's service will tow your vehicle with one tow up to 20 miles to the nearest repair facility.  Additional mileage is available and can be negotiated prior to service dispatch.  Accident towing is included.</p>
                            
				            <p><strong>Trip Continuation:</strong> If Member is more than 100 miles away from home and his or her automobile becomes disabled, ShieldMe will reimburse member up to $200 of actual expenses of continuing journey by car rental or commercial transportation and/or room and board while waiting on auto repairs.</p>
                            
				            <p><strong>Trip Itinerary and Concierge:</strong> Customer road trip vacations will be more carefree and economical when using ShieldMe's Trip Routing Service. Simply tell us where you want to go and we'll find the best way for you to get there. There is no additional cost for this valuable service. We encourage customers to call us any time they are planning a driving trip in the U.S. or Canada. Every Trip Routing Kit will be customized to your special travel plans. Trip Routing Kits are usually sent within 48 hours of request. Discount coupons are also available to registered customers upon request.  Call Customer Service toll-free at 1-800-451-0459, and tell us your desired destination and any cities or states along the way.</p>
                            
                            <h3 class="orangeBig2">Family Plus with Key Replacement</h3>
                            <p>All of the benefits listed above plus...</p>
                            <p><strong>Key Replacement:</strong> In the event your key/remote is lost, stolen or destroyed, we will pay for a replacement key for an amount up to $250.</p>
                            
                            <h3 class="orangeBig2">Coverage Limitations</h3>
                            <p>Roadside Assistance is limited to (1.) a ShieldMe Auto Club Member's vehicle weighing less than 12,000 lbs/5,540 kg, (2.) up to 20 miles of combined tow service on-hook and off-hook mileage and up $100 per occurence, (3.) up to $100 benefit for lock out service, (4.) disablements within the 50 United States, Canada, and Puerto Rico. , and (5.) up to $200 benefit for Trip Continuation, Road Side Assistance is valid three hundred sixty five (365) days from the effective date.</p>
                            <p>Emergency Roadside Assistance is intended to assist members whose vehicles are disabled by providing a toll-free Emergency Dispatch Service number to get help sent to them.  This service may not be used as a substitute for regular maintenance necessary to keep a vehicle in good condition.  Excessive claims may result in no-renewal or cancellation of membership. Pre-existing conditions prior to the membership effective date are not covered.</p>
