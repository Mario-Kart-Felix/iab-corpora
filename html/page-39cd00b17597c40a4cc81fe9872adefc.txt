<strong>Types of Automotive Primary Wire</strong>

<p>Automotive Primary Wire is made with either PVC or cross-linked insulation. The biggest difference between the two <a title= "Automotive Wire" href= "http://www.awcwire.com/ProductTOC.aspx?id=Automotive-Primary-Wire">automotive wire</a> options is temperature range. Cross-linked automotive wire can withstand much higher temperatures than PVC automotive wire.</p>

<strong>PVC Automotive Wire</strong>

<p><strong>GPT</strong> - <a title= "GPT Wire" href= "http://www.awcwire.com/ProductSpec.aspx?id=Type-GPT">Type GPT automotive wire</a> is intended for use whenever SAE J-1128 specifies general circuit wiring. GPT automotive primary wire features extruded insulation, and has a recommended temperature range of -40Â°C to 80Â°C.</p>

<p><strong>TWP</strong> - <a title= "TWP Cable" href= "http://www.awcwire.com/ProductSpec.aspx?id=Type-TWP">Type TWP automotive wire</a> is a lead-free PVC option. It is <strong>thin-walled</strong>, lightweight, and small, so it is a good choice for cramped automotive applications. TWP automotive primary wire has a maximum temperature rating of 105Â°C.</p>

<p><strong>HDT</strong> - <a title= "HDT Wire" href= "http://www.awcwire.com/ProductSpec.aspx?id=Type-HDT">HDT automotive primary wire</a> is general circuitry wire, but it is less common than the other types of automotive wire. However, it offers greater physical protection than those types because it has the <strong>thickest wall</strong>. HDT automotive primary wire meets the requirements of SAE J-1128, and can be used in cars, boats, buses and RVs.

<strong>Cross-Linked Automotive Wire</strong>

<p><strong>GXL</strong> - <a title= "GXL Wire" href= "http://www.awcwire.com/ProductSpec.aspx?id=Type-GXL"> Type GXL automotive primary wire</a> is the most common type. It works with most standard automotive wire connectors and may be applied in engine compartments where higher heat resistance is required according to SAE J-1128. GXL automotive wire is <strong>thin-walled</strong>, giving it a small diameter for hard-to-fit areas in trucks and trailers. Its recommended temperature range is -51Â°C to 125Â°C.</p>
