		<font class="title">BUICK ESTATE WAGON</font>
	</legend>
	<div style="margin-top:10px;">
	
<br><br>Buick used the Estate name on their full-size station wagons.

<br><br>The first estate wagon was first offered on the 1940 Super model. It used a wooden body and was available on Buick's larger "C" series (Super and Roadmaster) in the 1946-53 model years. Then it was offered the smaller "B" body Special, Century, LeSabre and Invicta with all steel bodies from 1954 t- 1964.

<br><br>The model was reintroduced on the "B" body in 1970 and was simply called Estate Wagon.

<br><br>From 1971 t- 1976, the Estate Wagon was built on the "C" body platform and shared the innovative "clam shell" tailgate used on all full size wagons from General Motors.

<br><br>In 1977, the Estate Wagon was downsized and relaunched on General Motor's "B" platform. In 1979 an Estate Wagon Limited was offered with many extra cost options included as standard. T- further differentiate the Limited model, fenders included four venta ports (up from three) and the interior had loose pillow designed seating. In 1980 body changes made the wagon more aerodynamic for better fuel efficiency. Als- in 1980, the Electra Estate Wagon was introduced and replaced the Estate Wagon Limited. The 'base' model was called the LeSabre Estate Wagon.

<br><br>A Buick Electra Estate Wagon was central t- story in the 1987 film Adventures in Babysitting.

<br><br>The last Estates were rear wheel drive B-bodies with a 115.9 in wheelbase. The Electra Estate model name was used in 1988 and 1989 even though the Electra sedan used the front wheel drive C platform. The final year of Estate production, 1990, just used the Estate model name. The Estate was replaced with the B-body Roadmaster Estate Wagon for 1991. Though it used the "Estate" name, it is more commonly referred to, simply as the "Roadmaster".

<br><br>The Estate used the Buick 455 from 1970 t- 1976, and the Buick 350 from 1977 t- 1980. After the Buick V8's discontinuation, the Estate came with either the Olds 307 or Olds 350 diesel.
