<p style="margin-bottom: 0in"><font size="2" face="verdana,geneva">Welcome to the world of classic car automotive restoration. </font></p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in"><font size="2" face="verdana,geneva">Our 72-week, all-inclusive Classic Car Restoration program is for students with a passion for restoring classic cars and vintage automobiles. This program emphasizes the historical significance and traditional time-honored methods of classic car restoration, while integrating the modern technology utilized in both automotive technician training and auto body technician training. </font></p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in"><font size="2" face="verdana,geneva">By combining both automotive and auto body technologies, this comprehensive program focuses on: </font></p>
<div class="training_list_red">
<ul><font size="2" face="verdana,geneva">	</font></p>
<li><font size="2" face="verdana,geneva">Engine and drive train restoration</font></li>
<li><font size="2" face="verdana,geneva">Ignition, fuel and exhaust system tuning</font></li>
<li><font size="2" face="verdana,geneva">Metalworking</font></li>
<li><font size="2" face="verdana,geneva">Upholstery and trim restoration</font></li>
<li><font size="2" face="verdana,geneva">Welding</font></li>
<li><font size="2" face="verdana,geneva">Damage repair</font></li>
<li><font size="2" face="verdana,geneva">Suspension and chassis restoration</font></li>
<li><font size="2" face="verdana,geneva">Painting and refinishing</font></li>
<li><font size="2" face="verdana,geneva">Final assembly and testing</font></li>
</ul>
</div>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in"><font size="2" face="verdana,geneva">Classic and vintage car restoration is a growing career field, and those who complete this automotive restoration training will have a solid understanding of this dynamic field. </font></p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in"><font size="2" face="verdana,geneva">Several late-model cars are currently used in the program: a &rsquo;57 Chevy, &rsquo;64 Pontiac GTO, &rsquo;54 Dodge Royale, &rsquo;70 Dodge Charger, &rsquo;72 Dodge Challenger and a &rsquo;68 Mercury Cougar. </font></p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in"><font size="2" face="verdana,geneva">This course is tailored to prepare you for entry into the rapidly growing auto restoration industry, as well provide you with the special skills needed to work on classic automobiles. </font></p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0in"><font size="2" face="verdana,geneva">If you have a passion for classic cars, this unique program is for you. At OTC, we&rsquo;re more than a classic car restoration school; we can provide the training you need for your future. </font></p>
