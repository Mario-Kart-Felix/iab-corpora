        A good quality mechanic school can provide you with the skills necessary to get a good job as an automotive, truck, aircraft, motorcycle, boat mechanic or auto body repair specialist. <br />
        <a href="body.html"><img src="car-lift.jpg" alt="Getting some car work done at the shop" width="233" height="287" hspace="5" vspace="3" align="left" /></a><br />
        If you're good with your hands and you like taking big things apart and then putting them back together again, you should be able to find a technical training program to prepare you for a new and rewarding career. <br />
<br /> 
You can find a mechanic training school in most larger cities.   Besides automobile and diesel truck service technician programs,  you may find a few institutions that offer either a marine, aviation or motorcycle repair and maintenance class or maybe even a complete degree program. <br />

<span class="style4">Featured Community Colleges</span>:<br />
<strong>South Seattle Community College</strong> is a community college that offers a variety of programs including <em>Auto Body Collision Repair</em>, <em>Automotive Technology</em>, <em>General Service Technician</em>, <em>Aviation  Maintenance</em>, <em>Aeronautical Technology</em> and <em>Diesel & Heavy Equipment</em>. <a href="schools-southseattle.html">Go learn more about this school</a>. <br />
<br />
<strong>Contra Costa College</strong> is another public community college in San Pablo, California. Their <em>Automotive Services</em> program provides their students with training in gas and hybrid vehicle repair along with vehicle collision repair. Program graduates can get certified through ICAR or ASE. <a href="schools-contracosta.html">Go learn more</a>. <br />
<br />
<strong>Barton County Community College</strong> is a junior college in Great Bend, Kansas.  Barton offers certificate and associate degree options in automotive technology featuring NATEF certified courses in  brakes, electrical, steering/suspension, engines, engine performance, air conditioning, automatic transmissions, manual drivetrains and hybrid vehicle technology and auto body. <a href="schools-bartoncounty.html">Go learn more</a>. <br />
