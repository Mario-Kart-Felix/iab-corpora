    <td height="132" colspan="7" valign="top" bgcolor="#FFFFF0"><font color="#000000" size="2" face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">A 
      hybrid car features a small fuel-efficient gas engine combined with an 
      electric motor that assists the engine when accelerating. The electric 
      motor is powered by batteries that recharge automatically while you drive. 
      <br>
      <br>
      There are two types of gasoline-electric hybrid cars: the parallel hybrid and   the series hybrid. In a parallel hybrid car, a gasoline engine and an electric motor work together   to move the car forward, while in a series hybrid the gasoline engine either directly powers an electric motor   that powers the vehicle or charges batteries that will power the motor. <br>
