<h2>Classic and Antique Car Insurance</h2>
Whether you are a hobbyist restoring antique cars, or a collector amassing your favorite models from throughout the years, Auto-Owners can provide a policy to protect your prized possessions. Due to the increased value and special care given to classic and antique autos, Auto-Owners has developed a special rating class for both types of vehicles that recognize these distinctions. <br />
<br />
<h3>Antique Auto Insurance Program</h3>
Auto-Owners offers an Antique Auto Program for qualified vehicles. Antique vehicles are 25 years old or older, must be maintained and licensed as an antique automobile, and used only in parades or exhibitions. Program members will receive substantial&nbsp;<a href="http://www.auto-owners.com/our-products/car-insurance/discounts">discounts</a> due to the limited use of these types of vehicles. <br />
<br />
<h3classic />
<p>Auto-Owners Classic Automobile program provides coverage for restored automobiles that typically appreciate in value. Classic Automobiles are insured on a stated amount basis, and receive discounted rates on most coverages.</p>
<p>Antique and Classic Cars can be insured with your other personal automobiles on a single, convenient policy. <br />
<br />
