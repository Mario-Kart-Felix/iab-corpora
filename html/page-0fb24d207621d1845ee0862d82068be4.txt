                  <p>Hybrid vehicles are becoming more popular; particularly with the high gas prices of today. Hybrids are much more environmentally friendly than standard vehicles,  and they are far more fuel-efficient. You can save quite a bit on annual fuel expenses when you drive a hybrid  vehicle. You can also save money on your  auto insurance.</p>
				   <p><a href="php/exit.php">Get  an affordable car insurance quote on your hybrid >></a></p>
				   <br>

                  <h2>Car Insurance Coverage Options for Hybrid Vehicles </h2>
                  <p>Auto coverage available on hybrid vehicles is the same as  for other cars and trucks. You can  select the following types of coverage:</p>
                  <ul>
                    <li><a href="coverage_liability.php">Liability</a></li>
                    <li><a href="coverage_collision.php">Collision</a></li>
                    <li><a href="coverage_comprehensive.php">Comprehensive</a></li>
                    <li>Bodily injury</li>
                    <li><a href="coverage_property_damage_liability.php">Property damage</a></li>
                    <li><a href="coverage_medical_payments.php">Medical payments</a></li>
                    <li><a href="coverage_underinsured_uninsured.php">Uninsured or underinsured motorist</a> coverage</li>
                    </ul>
                  <p>The only difference is the savings you'll  enjoy when the insurance company gives you a discounted rate for driving a  hybrid. For example, both <a href="companies_travelers.php">Travelers Insurance</a> and <a href="companies_farmers.php">Farmers Insurance</a>  hybrid drivers receive a 10% discount.</p>
                  <br>

                  <h2>Discounted Auto Insurance Premiums for Hybrid Car Owners</h2>
                  <p>More and more auto insurance companies are offering  discounted premiums to drivers of hybrid vehicles. The federal government even offers tax  incentives when you purchase a new hybrid vehicle, and some states permit  hybrid drivers to use the carpool lanes even when driving solo. There are a lot of good reasons to buy a hybrid vehicle. The most popular hybrid  models on the road today include: </p>
                  <ul>
                    <li>Toyota Prius, which is the best selling  hybrid worldwide</li>
                    <li>Honda Civic Hybrid</li>
                    <li>Toyota Highlander Hybrid</li>
                    <li>Ford Escape Hybrid</li>
                    <li>Honda Insight</li>
                    <li>Lexus RX 400h</li>
                    <li>Honda Accord  Hybrid</li>
                    <li>Toyota Camry Hybrid</li>
                    <li>Mercury Mariner Hybrid</li>
                    <li>Chevy  Silverado Hybrid Pickup </li>
                  </ul>
                  <p>Another reason insurance companies are beginning to offer  hybrid drivers discounted insurance rates is because these drivers typically  fall into the &ldquo;preferred insured&rdquo; group anyway.&nbsp;  These drivers are much more likely to have good driving records than those that do not own hybrid vehicles. In addition, sales of hybrid vehicles are continuing to grow and  insurance companies want to be ready to serve this growing consumer  market.&nbsp;</p>
