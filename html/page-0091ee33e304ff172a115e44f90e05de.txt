		<h2>SVT &#038; Hennessey Performance Vehicles</h2>
<p align="left"> <img src="/wp-content/themes/bennettdunlopford/images/GT500-938x280.jpg" alt="" height="280" width="938"></p>
<p> <strong>Performance Division<br />
</strong>Bennett Dunlop Ford is proud to be   southern Saskatchewan&#8217;s only SVT dealer. Our group of dealerships is   also the exclusive dealer for Hennessey Performance upgrades for Ford   vehicles in Canada. If you&#8217;re looking for a vehicle with performance on a   different level, you&#8217;ve come to the right place. </p>
<p> <strong>SVT</strong><br />
  SVT or Special Vehicle Team is a performance division of Ford that   specializes in their highest-performing vehicles. From the heritage-rich   and massively-powered Shelby GT500 to the high-speed, off-road king &#8211;   the SVT Raptor, we can give you some serious power &#8211; on or off the road. </p>
<p> <img src="/wp-content/themes/bennettdunlopford/images/SVT-938x300.jpg" alt="" height="300" width="938">  </p>
<p> <strong>Hennessey Performance<br />
</strong>If you&#8217;re looking for even more performance out of your   vehicle, we also offer Hennessey Performance upgrades for Ford   vehicles. Started by world-renowned and respected tuner John Hennessey,   Hennessey Performance offers a complete line of street-legal,   track-tested and dyno-proven high-performance aftermarket upgrades. If   you love serious amounts of horsepower, you&#8217;ve come to the right place!   From simple tuner upgrades to 1000+ HP monsters, set yourself apart. </p>
<p> If you&#8217;d like more information about a Hennessey vehicle or upgrade, email us at <a href="mailto:hennessey@dcag.ca">hennessey@dcag.ca</a>. </p>
<p> To learn more about Hennessey Performance, check out their website &#8211; <a href="http://www.hennesseyperformance.com" target="_blank">http://www.hennesseyperformance.com</a></p>
