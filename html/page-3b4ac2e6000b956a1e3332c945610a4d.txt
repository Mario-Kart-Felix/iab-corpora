<h5>Biodiesel Grades</h5>
<font size="2"><b>B10 Biodiesel</b> is 10% vegetable oil to 90% diesel fuel.
<br />
<b>B100 biodiesel</b> is 100% vegetable with no diesel-petroleum fuel at all.

All grades can be run in a diesel engine but there are some important equipment considerations when using biodiesel in your car, truck or SUV. Biodiesel often has an effect on certain gaskets and fuel lines and may require more careful filtration.
