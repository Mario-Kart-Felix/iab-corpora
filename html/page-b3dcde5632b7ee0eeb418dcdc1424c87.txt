		<div class="page">
			<h1>Hybrid / PHEV Automotive</h1>
		</div>
	</div>
		
		
	
	<div class="page overview">
		
		        			
			<div class="main">
				<p>Hybrids and plug-in hybrids require the seamless packaging and integration of two powertrain systems into a single chassis. The high power density of Mission’s technology makes our components ideal for this application.</p>
<p>Mission’s patented architecture and cooling systems make our components among the lightest, most compact solutions in use today. Our software and electronic control systems are easily integrated to existing vehicle control systems.</p>
<p>For PHEVs, Mission offers a blend of power and energy combined with advanced software that gives vehicle developers the flexibility to balance performance and efficiency in every driving mode.</p>
<p>Our extensive characterization of battery chemistries in the laboratory, combined with our experience in vehicle modeling and on-road validation testing, has given Mission a deep understanding of real world battery performance.  With this expertise, we work with our OEM partners to optimize the design and use of the energy storage system to meet aggressive warranty requirements.</p>
