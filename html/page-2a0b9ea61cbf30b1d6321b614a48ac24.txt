<p>Fister Automotive is a manufacturer of electric cars based in California, U.S, have a plan to give their electric cars to customers. Electric cars to be given are called Fister Karma. <strong>Fister Karma</strong> is an electric car which has plug-in hybrid car technology.</p>
<p>Distribution of electric car is a manufacturer’s way to test these electric cars. Besides of safety internal testing according to a car standard industry, some of electric cars will be given to customers for testing in a real condition on the highway.<span id="more-226"></span> Fisker Automotive have a plan to produce about 100 units of Fister Karma in this year. They have a contract with Valmet Automotive. Valmet Automotive have manufactured <a href="http://www.motorauthority.com/" target="_blank">Porsche</a> Boxsters and Porsche Caymans.</p>
<p>For the first time, this plug-in hybrid car was exhibited at North American International Auto Show on January 14th, 2008. Design of Fister Karma almost seems like <a href="http://www.motorauthority.com/" target="_blank">Aston Martin</a>, a sport car with a luxury and sporty designs. Fisker Automotive claimed Fister Karma is a first electric sport car.</p>
<p>These luxury electric sport cars are powered with rechargeable batteries. With its electric driving mode, Fister Karma can reach up to 50 miles (80 km). For a long distance, you can change to hybrid mode, it makes a Fister Karma capable to reach up to 300 miles (480 km).</p>
<p><a href="http://www.allcarselectric.com/" target="_blank">Fisker</a> Automotive will start a mass production of Fister Karma in 2011 and have an estimated production of 15,000 electric cars per year. They expected to sell a Fister Karma on price range of U.S. $ 87,900.</p>
