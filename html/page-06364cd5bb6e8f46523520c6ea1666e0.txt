<span class="title">Welcome to our website!</span><br>

Performance Automotive is conveniently located in Winston-Salem, NC, near Hanes Mall just off Griffith Road on Spaugh Industrial Drive.  We hope that you will find information on our site that will be helpful to you as you make a decision about where to take your car for service.  We are able to provide maintenance and repairs for both foreign and domestic automobiles and trucks. We can also do fleet service and will provide references upon request.  We look forward to hearing from you!  Do not hesitate to <a href="contact.htm">contact us</a> with questions about a repair or service.<br>
<br>
Our new state of the art facility has the latest equipment and our staff is trained to service your car and take care of you.  We are competitively priced and at the same time offer quality repairs and service. We use only quality OE or better parts including but not limited to ACDelco, Beck Arnley, Bosch, NGK and others. We also carry Interstate Batteries. We think you will be pleased with our facility, our service and our price!<br>
<br>
Please call, email or come by the shop. You may also wish to visit our<a href="http://www.facebook.com/pages/Performance-Automotive/195140141904" target="_blank"> Facebook page</a> for information, pictures, special offers and coupons. We will do our best to make your car care experience as stress free as possible.  Thank you for taking the time to visit us online and we encourage you to stop by and visit us in person!
