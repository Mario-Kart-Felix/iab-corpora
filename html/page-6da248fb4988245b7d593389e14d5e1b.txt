<p>The <a href="/Declasse" title="Declasse">Declasse</a> <b>Dragon Wagon</b> is a delivery van is featured in <a href="/Grand_Theft_Auto:_Chinatown_Wars" title="Grand Theft Auto: Chinatown Wars">Grand Theft Auto: Chinatown Wars</a>. It is arguably the best van in the <a href="/Grand_Theft_Auto" title="Grand Theft Auto">Grand Theft Auto</a> series and perhaps one of the most damage resistant vehicles. When the Dragon Wagon is entered, it automatically starts the <a href="/Noodle_Run" title="Noodle Run">Noodle Run</a> side mission.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Description">Description</span></h2>
<p>The Dragon Wagon is a modified yellow <a href="/Burrito" title="Burrito">Burrito</a> with a large dragon prop on the roof. It is used by a Chinese noodle company to deliver noodles to their customers, rivaling <a href="/Noodle_Exchange" title="Noodle Exchange">Noodle Exchange</a>.
</p><p>The van has superior handling and its top speed is high. The van is also extremely durable; it is able to withstand up to 3 rocket hits without exploding. This makes the van one of the best vehicles in the game.
</p>
