				<h1 class="title" id="page-title">Off-Road Parts for High Performance</h1>				<div class="tabs"></div>																
				  <div class="region region-content">
    <div id="block-system-main" class="block block-system">

    
  <div class="content">
    <div id="node-83" class="node node-page clearfix" about="/performance" typeof="foaf:Document">

  
      
  
  <div class="content">
    <div class="field field-name-field-landingimage field-type-image field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even"><img typeof="foaf:Image" src="http://spicerparts.com/sites/default/files/landingimage/performance_large.jpg" width="625" height="216" alt="" /></div></div></div><div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"><h2><a href="http://spicerparts.com/sites/default/files/performance_packs.jpg" rel="lightbox"><img class="imgright" src="http://spicerparts.com/sites/default/files/resize/performance_packs-200x176.jpg" alt="" width="200" height="176" /></a></h2>
<h2><span>High Performance Automotive Parts. Wherever Limits Are Pushed.</span></h2>
<h3><strong>Off-road Recreation</strong></h3>
<p>As off-roading has grown in popularity, the Spicer brand has been growing right along with it. Boundaries are expanding, and Dana has the performance automotive parts and performance replacement parts that feature the Spicer name and meet the demands of higher engine horsepower and torque.</p>
<p>Spicer parts can be found performing on all types of vehicles at off-road events across the country. Rest assured, we understand the unique requirements of the off-road community, and we’ll be there to meet them, with off-road parts that meet your needs.&nbsp;</p>
<h3 class="p1"><a href="http://spicerparts.com/sites/default/files/jeep_small.jpg" rel="lightbox"><img class="imgright" src="http://spicerparts.com/sites/default/files/resize/jeep_small-200x200.jpg" alt="" width="200" height="200" /></a></h3>
<h3 class="p1">&nbsp;</h3>
<h3 class="p1">Providing Genuine Spicer OEM Parts for Jeep Enthusiasts</h3>
<p>Jeep enthusiasts know what they want, and that’s why we offer quality Spicer aftermarket parts engineered to exact Dana standards. In fact, Spicer replacement parts meet OEM standards, ensuring longer life for your Jeep.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3><a href="http://spicerparts.com/sites/default/files/drag_race_car.jpg" rel="lightbox"><img class="imgright" src="http://spicerparts.com/sites/default/files/resize/drag_race_car-200x200.jpg" alt="" width="200" height="200" /></a>Drag Race / Street Rod</h3>
<p>Drag racing. Seconds of abuse that can destroy an inferior part. From drag racing’s earliest days, the strength and reliability of the Dana 60™ axle and Spicer driveline components have made them the standard. That is because we understand exactly what it takes to survive in that environment. We are proud to carry on that tradition on to this day, and we are never done innovating.&nbsp;When it comes to custom rebuilds and restoration, people in the know demand Spicer® parts for their drivetrain and axle products. As people look for options strong enough to take on today’s increased horsepower, we have met their needs over the years, and we’ll do so into the future.&nbsp;</p>
<h3><br />Circle Track</h3>
<p>It takes tough U-joints and axle components to survive the brutal forces that go hand in hand with the severe stresses of the racing environment. That is why Spicer® driveline and axle components are the standard that to which all others are compared. We deliver technology vital to ensuring reliability and you can count on us to keep innovating as speeds continue to climb.</p></div></div></div>  </div>
