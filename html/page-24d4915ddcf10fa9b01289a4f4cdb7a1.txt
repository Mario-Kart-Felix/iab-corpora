                <p class="bold">Help is just a toll-free call away!</p>
                    <p class="content-wrap">
                      NJM Personal and Commercial Auto Policies include complimentary roadside assistance.</p>
                    <p>For more information, view and/or print our brochures.</p>
                    <ul>
                      <li><a href="../pdf/A-754.pdf" target="_blank">Personal Auto Policyholders</a> <img src='../images/pdficon.gif' alt="PDF" /></li>
                      <li><a href="../pdf/A-754c.pdf" target="_blank">Commercial Auto Policyholders</a> <img src='../images/pdficon.gif' alt="PDF" /></li>
                    </ul>
                    <p>Getting NJM's help in response to a vehicle disablement is as simple as one phone call, anytime. This value-added service, which includes emergency towing, is available anywhere in the United States or Canada, 24 hours a day, 365 days a year.</p>
					
					<p class="content-wrap">With NJM Roadside Assistance, each time your covered vehicle is disabled, we will pay reasonable and necessary charges up to $75. All our customers need to do is call our toll-free number, 1-800-FOR-NJMI (1-800-367-6564), and help will be on the way if any of these events disables your covered auto:</p>
					
                 <ul id ="bullets">
                   <li>Mechanical Breakdown</li>
                   <li>Flat Tire</li>
                   <li>Battery Failure</li>
                   <li>Lockout</li>
                   <li>Insufficient Supply of Fuel, Oil or Water  <br>(up to 2 gallons)                 </li>
                  </ul>
                  <p class="space5"></p>
                 <div class="content-box-big-left">
                   <p class="subheading-padding"> Did You Know?</p>
                   <p class="content-nowrap">If you have paid a service provider directly and would like to receive reimbursement up to $75, you may submit the itemized receipt for review to: </p>
				   NJM Roadside Assistance<br />
                    PO Box 9145<br />
                    Medford, MA 02155<br /><br />
										
					<p class="content-nowrap"> Please include your policy number on all correspondence. Reimbursements take approximately 30 days to process.</p>
