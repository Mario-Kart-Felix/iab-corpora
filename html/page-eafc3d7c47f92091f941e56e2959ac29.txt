			<div class="tab-desc"><p class="black-bold"><b>Program offered is a Diploma.</b></p><p class="default_font"><img src="/uploads/images/Nashville-Auto-Diesel-College/diesel.jpg" alt="Diesel" title="Diesel" width="283" height="153" style="border: 0pt none; float: right; margin-left: 5px; margin-right: 5px;" />Make it big working with the big rigs! Gear up for  diesel training at Lincoln and get a heavy-duty education in repairing  and maintaining these mechanical giants.</p>
<p class="default_font">From  18-wheelers to buses, trains and mining and construction equipment,  you'll get hands-on experience in every aspect of diesel training with  industry-standard vehicles and tools, from certified instructors.</p>
<p class="default_font">You'll receive hands-on training in:</p>
<ul class="default_font">
<li>Diesel Engines and Repair </li>
<li>Truck Brakes &amp; Chassis </li>
<li>Diesel Fuel Systems </li>
<li>Diesel Electronics &amp; Diagnosis </li>
<li>Hydraulic Applications </li>
<li>And so much more</li>
</ul>
<p class="default_font">Whether  you go for the Diploma, Associate* or Advanced Degree* Diesel programs,  skills learned at our Diesel School can put you in big demand around  the country. And after graduation, Lincoln's national placement  assistance can give you the competitive edge you need to start your  career as a real "mover and shaker" in the world of big rigs and diesel engines.</p>
