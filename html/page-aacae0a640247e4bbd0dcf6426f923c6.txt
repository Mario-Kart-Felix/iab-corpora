<div class="grid_4" id="module_content_split6_4">
	<h4>
		Proven to reduce CO2 emissions and fuel cost by a minimum of 15%, Ashwoods Hybrid Drive is an advanced, patented solution designed for rear-wheel drive commercial vehicles.</h4>
	<p>
		Inspired by Formula 1&rsquo;s KERS (Kinetic Energy Recovery Systems), Hybrid Drive recovers the kinetic energy usually wasted through braking and deceleration &ndash; instead using it for battery charge. Formula 1 uses this recovered energy for additional power. Ashwoods uses it to assist the engine &ndash; lowering the loading and the resulting fuel consumption.</p>
