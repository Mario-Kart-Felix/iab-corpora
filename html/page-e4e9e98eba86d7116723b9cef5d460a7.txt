		   <h3><a title="Compare Acura Spoiler Styles">COMPARE Ford SPOILER STYLES:</a></h3>
		   <p><span><a title="Factory Style Car Spoilers">Factory Style</a></span>Replica of Factory issued spoiler. Should not be used to replace OEM installed spoiler as holes will not match.</p>
		   <p class="green"><span><a title="Custom Style Car Spoilers">Custom Style</a></span> Alternate choice to factory style, usually fits more than one vehicle. Placement varies w/each vehicle. Should not be used to replace OEM installed spoiler as holes will not match.</p>
		   <p><span><a title="Mid-High Car Spoilers">Mid High</a></span> Usually 7 to 8 in. high. Fits more than one vehicle. Placement varies w/each vehicle. See <a title="Car Spoiler Tech Tips" href="?a=techtips" class="temp-link">Tech Tips</a> for how to install this spoiler.</p>
		   <p class="green"><span><a title="Thruster Style Car Spoilers">Thruster Style</a></span>Usually 6 to7 in. high with longer legs than mid high style. Fits more than one vehicle. Placement varies w/each vehicle. See <a href="?a=techtips" class="temp-link">Tech Tips</a> for how to install this spoiler.</p>
		   <p><span><a title="Supra Style Car Spoilers">Supra Style</a></span>Usually 9 to 12 in. high. Fits more than one vehicle. Placement varies w/each vehicle. See <a title="Car Spoiler Tech Tips" href="?a=techtips" class="temp-link">Tech Tips</a> for how to install this spoiler.</p>
		   <p class="green"><span><a title="Universal Car Spoilers"> Universal Spoilers</a></span> Wings with legs closer together in the middle will fit virtually all vehicles. Wings with legs further apart will fit most vehicles with relatively flat and/or squared up trunk lid edges.</p>

<h4 class="style4"><span class="style3">TYPES OF <a title="Car Spoilers, Rear Spoilers, Car Wings">CAR SPOILERS</a></span></h4>
     <span class="style1">
     <a title="FORD FOCUS SEDAN Car Spoilers, Rear Spoilers, Car Wings">FORD FOCUS SEDAN Car spoilers</a> and wings are one of the most popular car accessories available on the aftermarket. From mild to wild, a FORD FOCUS SEDAN car spoiler can change the entire look of the rear of your vehicle. You can choose from the standard OEM style factory spoiler, a custom factory style spoiler (if available), or if you like to be different you can choose from one of our custom spoilers that are available in our <a href="/spoilers/universal-wings/universal-fit/" title="Universal Spoilers and Wings">universal spoilers</a> section. <br />
     <br />
     </span>
     <h3 class="style4"><span class="style3"><a title="Factory Car Spoilers">Factory Spoilers</a></span></h3>
     <span class="style1">Factory car spoilers are what make up most of the spoiler aftermarket. You will find some vehicles come standard with a spoiler now, like the <a href="/spoilers/ford/mustang/" title="Ford Mustang Spoilers">Ford Mustang spoiler</a>, the <a href="/spoilers/scion/tc/" title="Scion tC Spoilers">Scion tC spoiler</a> and the <a href="/spoilers/toyota/yaris/" title="Toyota Yaris Spoilers">Toyota Yaris spoilers</a>, and it may even include the FORD FOCUS SEDAN. This style of a car spoiler is usually more of a lower profile, mildly agressive type of a spoiler, a more common type found on many cars, and are usually pleasing to the eye with their sporty appearance. If you like the factory look and like to dress up your car without getting carried away, then a factory spoiler is probably for you.        
     <br />
     <br />
     </span>
     <h3 class="style4"><span class="style3"><a title="Rear Lip Spoilers">Rear Lip Spoilers</a></span></h3>
     <span class="style1">Rear lip spoilers are flush mounted to the rear deck lid. They usually sit on the lip or edge of the trunk just where the lid curves down towards the bumper. It has a very low profile, and gives the sleek appearance of a luxury car. Cars that have a lip spoiler are not trying to make a statement, more likely they would give the car a more high end appearance. Rear lip spoilers are usually found on luxury cars like the <a href="/spoilers/infiniti/m45/wg1756/" title="Infinity M45 Spoilers">Infinity M45 spoiler</a> or the <a href="/spoilers/mercedes/cls/wg1598/" title="Mercedes Benz CLS Spoilers">Mercedes CLS Spoiler</a>.
     <br />
     <br />
     </span>
