      <p align="left"><span class="c2">      
      <h2>EAA Silicon Valley is a chapter of the Electric Auto Association, a nonprofit organization formed 
      in 1967 <br /> to promote the use of electric vehicles as a viable transportation alternative.</span> </h2>
 
      <p align="left">
      We meet monthly on the 3rd Saturday of the month, and host an annual EV Rally. <br />
      See our <a href="http://www.eaasv.org/meetings.html">Meetings page</a> for lists of future and past speakers.
      <br /><br />
      Join our <a href="http://autos.groups.yahoo.com/group/eaasv/">Yahoo! Group</a>
      for meeting announcements and other EV discussions. 
      <br /><br />      
      There are EAA chapters across the US, Canada, and Germany. <br />
      Visit the <a href="http://www.eaaev.org/">national website</a> 
      for information on EVs, chapters, news, and EAA merchandise. 
      </p>
      
      <p align="left">
      Become an EAA <a href="https://electricauto.site-ym.com/general/pick_username.asp">member</a> 
      to show your support of EVs and follow the latest EV news in the monthly <b><i>Current EVents</i></b> publication.
      <br /><br />
      The Silicon Valley chapter is run by volunteers and offers free, fun events to the public, <br />
      please help us cover our costs through a 
      <a href="https://electricauto.site-ym.com/donations/donate.asp?id=5116">donation</a>. Thank you for your support.
      <br /><br />      
      EAA memberships and donations are tax deductible.
      </p>
