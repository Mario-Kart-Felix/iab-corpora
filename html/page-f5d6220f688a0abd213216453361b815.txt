<tr><td colspan="2">
<div class="subtitle">Roadside Assistance</div>
<a href="http://www.aaa.com/scripts/WebObjects.dll/AAAOnline?association=aaa&club=238&page=ERSHomePage"><img src="pix/home/towtruck.gif" align="right" border="0" /></a>
<!--------MAIN CONTENT HERE-------->


Members of AAA Southern Pennsylvania are entitled to AAA's 24-hour Roadside Assistance. (AAA Plus members receive <a href="javascript:openWindow('plustable.html');">additional assistance</a>). Roadside Assistance benefits are activated 72 hours after becoming a AAA member. Click <a href="join.html"><b> here</b></a> to join AAA. <b>Please note:</b> Roadside Assistance guidelines are subject to change without notice. 
<p>
<div class="subtitle">Eligibility</div><br />
<b>Roadside Assistance Providers</b><br />


AAA selects Roadside Assistance facilities for their ability to render Roadside Assistance and perform emergency repairs. AAA cannot guarantee that these facilities will have the parts and equipment to make repairs, nor can it guarantee priority service.<p>
<b>Eligible parties</a></b><br />
Roadside Assistance covers the AAA member, not the vehicle. Service will not be provided to non-members, even if they have possession of a member's car. However, any eligible vehicle -- see below --, if occupied by a AAA member, is entitled to Roadside Assistance service.<br />
<p>
<b>Eligible vehicles</b><br />
Roadside Assistance will be provided for:<ul>
<li>All properly licensed and inspected four-wheeled motor vehicles of passenger, pleasure, or recreational type, if Roadside Assistance services can be safely delivered
<li>Dual wheel campers / motor homes are eligible for all services except towing, extrication / winching and tire service. Dual wheel unloaded pickups are eligible for everything except tire service.
<li>Rented passenger and commercial vehicles are eligible for service - with the <b>exception</b> of taxi cabs and limousines.
<li>Vehicles are <b>not</b> eligible for service if they have been abandoned, illegally parked or impounded due to an ordinance infraction.
<li>Trailers are not eligible for service.</ul>
<b>Allowable Service Calls</b><br />
AAA members requiring more than four (4) Roadside Assistance calls in a Membership Year will be charged for the fifth and each call thereafter by the facility rendering the service or billed by the Club. Also, no reinbursement will be made when the four-call limit is exceeded.<br />
<br />
Your eligible membership benefits are provided as explained. However, if Roadside Assistance benefits are used excessively, AAA Southern Pennsylvania reserves the right to deny renewal of membership.
<p>


<div class="subtitle">Roadside Assistance Services Provided</div><br />
<b>Mechanical Adjustments</b><br />

Minor adjustments will be made in an attempt to enable vehicle to operate under its own power. Costs of parts / materials are at  owner's expense. If vehicle can't be started, towing will be provided.<p>

<b>Mobile Battery Service</b><br />
When our Battery Service specialist arrives, he or she will use a state-of-the-art battery tester, which was specifically developed for mobile use and is <b>FREE</b> to any AAA member. The tester will calculate the battery charging system operating level, which will notify our specialist if the battery simply needs a boost or should be replaced. This diagnostic test eliminates the guesswork and gets you on your way. The test is quick, professional and <b>FREE</b>. <a href="batteryserv.html">More information here.</a><p>

<b>Tire Service</b><br />
If vehicle's spare tire is inflated and serviceable, it will be installed to replace flat tire. If no such spare is available, vehicle will be towed. Tire repairs, and installation or removal of snow tires or chains is <b>not</b> covered.<p>

<b>Extrication and Winching Service</b><br />
Vehicle will be extricated / winched when it can be safely reached from a normally travelled road or established thoroughfare by a service vehicle. Additional help or services are <b>not </b>covered. Service does <b>not</b> include:<ul>
	<li>Snowbound vehicle recovery
	<li>Vehicles driven off regularly travelled roads
<li><b><font color="#E41E1E">AAA Plus coverage includes delivery of services by a second truck and operator for one hour at the scene.</font></b></ul>

<b>Towing Services</b><br />
When vehicle cannot be driven after attempting all services listed above, it will be towed one time per disablement at no charge to one of the following:
<ul><li>The Roadside Assistance facility rendering the service
	<li>A maximum of three miles in any direction from the scene.
<li><b><font color="#E41E1E">AAA Plus provides free towing up to 100 miles from the scene</font></b></ul>

<b>Lockout Service</b><br />
Service will be sent to gain entrance to a vehicle having its keys locked inside. If key is broken or lost  -- or if the service provider cannot gain entrance, locksmith service up to $50.00 (or reimbursement for commercial locksmith work) will be provided.<br />
<b><font color="#E41E1E">AAA Plus provides up to $100.00 for locksmith service, if such service is necessary to unlock the vehicle or make it operable</font></b><p>

<b>Fuel Delivery</b><br />
A supply of fuel (enough to reach the nearest service station) can be provided, at the cost of current pump prices.<br />
<b><font color="#E41E1E">AAA Plus provides the supply of fuel at no cost.</font></b><p>

<b>Emergency Repair Check Acceptance</b><br />
Roadside Assistance facilities will accept a AAA member's personal check of up to $250.00 for any services or repairs. A valid AAA Membership Card and identification must be presented. The name on the check must match AAA member's name.<p>



<div class="subtitle">To Obtain Service</div><ul>
<li> Visit our <a href="http://www.aaa.com/scripts/WebObjects.dll/AAAOnline?association=aaa&club=238&page=ERSHomePage"><b>Roadside Assistance Online</b></a> page to request service over the internet.
<li>In AAA Southern PA area, call <b>(717) 600-8800</b>
<li>Outside club area, call <b>800-222-1469</b>
<li> When calling, please have the following ready:
<ul><li>Your membership number
<li>Your club name (AAA Southern Pennsylvania)
<li>Your club code number (238)
<li>Specific location of vehicle
<li>Description of vehicle
<li>The nature of the problem</ul>
<li>After calling for service, members should stay with their car, or make arrangements so they'll know when service arrives. Members must present their AAA Membership Card and photo ID before service will be rendered. If vehicle becomes driveable before the Roadside Assistance vehicle arrives, please call back and cancel request.
<li>If You Cannot Be with the Vehicle:<ul><li>Except in situations involving a highway emergency, AAA members must initiate the request for service and identify the person who will serve as their designated agent. The agent may be required to sign a release form, and must have possession of AAA member's current card at the scene. Service to unattended vehicles is at the sole discretion of the service provider.</ul></ul>

<p>
<div class="subtitle">Alternate Service</div><br />
If AAA Service is not available, or if AAA contractor access is restricted, AAA will provide reimbursement for covered services at the prevailing commercial rate for the region. NOTE: certain rules and restrictions apply.
<br /><center><b>Call AAA at 800-222-1469</b> for details.
