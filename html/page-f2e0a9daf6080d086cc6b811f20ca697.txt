<p>Conventional wisdom (and <a href="http://admin.cdnew.hfmus.com/news/spied/10q1/2011_chevrolet_aveo-spied" target="_self">spy photos</a>) told us that the next Chevrolet Aveo hatchback would draw heavily upon the styling of the <a href="http://admin.cdnew.hfmus.com/news/car/10q1/chevrolet_aveo_rs_concept-auto_shows" target="_self">Aveo RS concept</a>, and now we know that to be fact. The production-ready Aveo five-door you see here will debut at the <a href="http://admin.cdnew.hfmus.com/news/shows/2010_paris_auto_show" target="_self">Paris auto show</a> later this month in European-market trim.</p>
