<h1 id="page-title">Hybrid auto insurance discount</h1>
<p>A car that's environmentally friendly is good for the earth &ndash; and your wallet. You can get a discount on your Travelers auto insurance if you own or lease a hybrid car.</p>
			
		<h3>Key benefit of the Travelers Hybrid Auto Discount*</h3>		
			<ul>
				<li>You've made a conscious decision to have a positive effect on the environment, and now it's your turn to be rewarded. Travelers is proud to offer an auto insurance discount for purchased or leased hybrid vehicles</li>				
			</ul>
			
		<h3>To be eligible for our Hybrid Auto Discount</h3>
		<ul>
			<li>You must own or lease a hybrid car.</li>						
		</ul>
		<p>Speak to a Travelers representative to see if you can take advantage of our hybrid auto insurance discount, and for help evaluating your auto insurance needs.</p>

		<p class="disclaimer-text disclaimer-text1">*The discount for hybrid vehicles does not apply to the uninsured motorists and personal injury protection portions of your coverage. A good driving record will have a positive effect on the total cost of car insurance for any Travelers customer</p>
