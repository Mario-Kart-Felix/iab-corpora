					<div id="introtext">
						<p>New York Automotive and Diesel Institute is the center for Automotive, Diesel, and Auto-body technology. Train to become a Master Certified Automotive and Truck & Diesel Technician or a Master Certified Collision Repair Technician.  We are more than just another auto mechanic school; at New York Automotive and Diesel Institute, we strive to provide the highest level of instruction available, utilizing the finest, most up-to-date training resources available. <br><br>

We believe that academic excellence and quality vocational/technical training will provide the student with the essential “tools of the trade”. This is achieved through both theoretical instruction and “hands on” practical exercises, on equipment found in the field. Graduates will possess the skills necessary to effectively diagnose and service specialized automotive/heavy duty truck and diesel systems. Students will be prepared to meet the requirements of an ever-changing automotive/heavy duty truck and diesel and collision repair service market, thus enhancing the graduate’s technical skill level, marketability, and employment potential.  Come find out why we're New York's top <a href="/portfolio/auto-mechanic-institute/">auto mechanic school</a>!
