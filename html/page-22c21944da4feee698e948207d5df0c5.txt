        <h2 class="home"><a href="http://autoevaluators.com/2013/08/08/st-louis-car-break-ins-all-time-high/">St.Louis car break-ins all time high!</a> </h2>                <div class="recover"></div>
    </div>

				
    <div class="entry_content">
        <div id="yui_3_7_2_1_1376013683882_2288"><a rel="attachment wp-att-871" href="http://autoevaluators.com/2013/08/08/st-louis-car-break-ins-all-time-high/car-break-ins/"><img class="size-medium wp-image-871 alignright" title="car-break-ins" src="http://autoevaluators.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/car-break-ins-300x168.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="168" /></a></div>
<div>Car break-ins reach epidemic levels in St.Louis!!<br />
<em>Auto Evaluators wants to help!</em></div>
<div><em><br />
</em></div>
<div id="yui_3_7_2_1_1376013683882_2363">Unknown facts to <strong>protect your Vehicle and valuables.</strong></div>
<div><strong><br />
</strong></div>
<div id="yui_3_7_2_1_1376013683882_2362">1) <strong>Locking valuables in the trunk may not be safe! </strong>Thieves know to push the trunk release button</div>
<div id="yui_3_7_2_1_1376013683882_2316">to access the trunk. Most cars have a disable feature to disable this check your owners manual.</div>
<div id="yui_3_7_2_1_1376013683882_2322">2) Even though you may not actually leave valuables in the car any hint of possible valuables can <strong>provoke a break-in</strong></div>
<div>ex. power cords, empty cases, and bags. This can lead to hundreds of dollars of break-in damages!</div>
<div id="yui_3_7_2_1_1376013683882_2324">3) <strong>Does your alarm work??</strong> Test your alarm! Leave a window open and set the alarm, after 10min reach in</div>
<div id="yui_3_7_2_1_1376013683882_2353">and open car door from the inside the alarm should sound immediately! If not repairs are required.</div>
<div id="yui_3_7_2_1_1376013683882_2325">(make sure you know how to reset your alarm before testing)</div>
<div id="yui_3_7_2_1_1376013683882_2326">4) <strong>Only 1 out of 5 vehicle break-ins file</strong> a police report and are reported!! If you hear of  break-ins in the</div>
<div id="yui_3_7_2_1_1376013683882_2329">area it usually is worse than reported!! Always request to file a report this will help provide the officials</div>
<div id="yui_3_7_2_1_1376013683882_2330">with valuable statistic that can add additional security in your area!!</div>
<div id="yui_3_7_2_1_1376013683882_2332"><em>Auto Evaluators is committed to keeping you and your vehicle safe!</em></div>
<div><em> Please contact Mark or Ray if we can be of assistance to you. </em></div>
<div><em> Thanks! </em></div>
