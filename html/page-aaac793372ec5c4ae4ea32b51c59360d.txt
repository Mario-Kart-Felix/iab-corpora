			<div class="KonaBody micro-share">
			    <p>There are 9 different versions of the 2011 Toyota Sienna Minivan. Of all 9 Minivans only one of them has the base 2.7 liter engine while the other 8 have a 3.5 liter V6 engine. Both the 2.7 liter and 3.5 liter engines are good performers as they are able to travel high speeds while maintaining the power of the engines and reducing fuel emissions. The combination of these qualities makes the used Minivan engine of the 2011 Toyota Sienna a great find.</p> <p>2.7 liter Used Engine Features and Performance</p> <p>Gas engine</p> <p>2.7 Liters</p> <p>16 valves</p> <p>4 inline cylinders</p> <p>Double overhead cam</p> <p>187 horsepower at 5800 revolutions per minute</p> <p>A torque of 186 feet per pounds at 4100 revolutions per minute</p> <p>The used Minivan engine of the 2011 Toyota Sienna can perform on the same level as that of many other crossover SUVs. This 2.7 liter engine along with its 6-speed automatic transmission is very fast, light, powerful and fuel-efficient. The 2011 Sienna uses the power provided by its 2.7 liter engine to carry the potential weight of the vehicle and its occupants. When the vehicle is empty, the engine feels lighter and is able to go faster.</p> <p>3.5 liter Used Engine Features and Performance</p><div class="print image_sharing_exclude"><script type="text/javascript"><!--

</script><script type="text/javascript" src="http://pagead2.googlesyndication.com/pagead/show_ads.js"></script></div> <p>Gas engine</p> <p>3.5 Liters</p> <p>24 valves</p> <p>V6 cylinders</p> <p>Double overhead cam</p> <p>266 horsepower at 6200 revolutions per minute</p> <p>A torque of 245 feet per pounds at 4700 revolutions per minute</p> <p>With so many versions of the 2011 Toyota Sienna Minivan, some buyers may not know what version to get. When making a decision, always opt for any version containing the 3.5 liter, V6 engine. Its performance is not only incredible for a used Minivan engine but for Minivans both used and new as well as some crossover mid-sized SUV&#39;s. The 3.5 liter engine comes with a 6-speed automatic transmission that maximizes its power to the fullest allowing it to go from zero to sixty in 7.9 seconds. This is incredible because many vehicles are not able to attain this speed below 10 seconds.</p> <p>There are no losers for buyers of the used 2011 Toyota Sienna Minivan engine. The 2.7 liter and 3.5 liter engines, both with a 6-speed automatic transmission, are surprisingly fuel-efficient, very fast and extremely powerful. Although the 3.5 liter engine has major advantages over the 2.4 liter engine in terms of speed and power, the 2.4 liter engine has a slight advantage over it in terms of fuel economy and is still able to travel at high speeds with comparable power. Whatever the final choice of the buyer, they are sure to walk away with an excellent deal.</p>
