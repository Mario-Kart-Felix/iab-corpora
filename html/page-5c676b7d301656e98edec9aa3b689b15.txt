	<article id="post-3699" class="post-3699 post type-post status-publish format-standard hentry category-perodua">
				
<header class="entry-header">
			
						<h1 class="entry-title">
				<a href="http://automagazined.com/2013/08/perodua-cheapest-model-market-year/" title="Permalink to Perodua to come out with cheapest model in the market next year" rel="bookmark">Perodua to come out with cheapest model in the market next year</a>
			</h1>
										<div class="comments-link">
					<a href="http://automagazined.com/2013/08/perodua-cheapest-model-market-year/#respond" title="Comment on Perodua to come out with cheapest model in the market next year"><span class="dsq-postid" rel="3699 http://automagazined.com/?p=3699"><span class="leave-reply">Leave a reply</span></span></a>				</div><!-- .comments-link -->
					</header><!-- .entry-header -->


				<div class="entry-content">




			<p>Perusahaan Otomobil Kedua Sdn Bhd&#8217;s (Perodua) will come out with a new model next year that will be the cheapest in the market, said Prime Minister Datuk Seri Najib Razak.</p>
<p><img src="http://automagazined.com/wp-content/plugins/jquery-image-lazy-loading/images/grey.gif" data-original="http://automagazined.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/perodua-640x393-600x368.jpg" alt="grey Perodua to come out with cheapest model in the market next year" width="600" height="368" class="lazy aligncenter size-medium wp-image-3700" title="Perodua to come out with cheapest model in the market next year" /><noscript><img src="http://automagazined.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/08/perodua-640x393-600x368.jpg" alt="perodua 640x393 600x368 Perodua to come out with cheapest model in the market next year" width="600" height="368" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-3700" title="Perodua to come out with cheapest model in the market next year" /></noscript></p>
<p>He said the new model will enable the rakyat to own a car which will have sophisticated and strong safety features. Perodua has invested RM1.3 billion to build a new factory which would be ready next October to produce the new model that would be competitive. </p>
<p>&#8220;I hope the factory will help Perodua export more cars, especially to neighbouring countries. Currently, Perodua exported 10,000 units a year and I hope the figure can be increase further,&#8221; he said at Perodua&#8217;s Gala Raya, held in conjunction with its 20th anniversary here today.</p>
<p>Najib said Perodua&#8217;s strategy all along has been to focus on &#8216;niche&#8217; market, producing compact quality cars and to find ways to improve quality and reduce production costs. Currently, Perodua&#8217;s ecosystem comprised vendors and dealers nationwide of 130,000, he said.</p>
<p>&#8220;Considering this big ecosystem, the success of Perodua will not only benefit the 130,000 Malaysians but also the car buyers in the country,&#8221; he said.</p>
<p>Najib also urged car manufacturers to study the cost structure and business model so that they could be more efficient to compete in a market that has become competitive and more liberalised.</p>
<p>He said the Ministry of International Trade and Industry has encouraged the car manufacturers to voluntarily reduce the prices. </p>
<p>&#8220;The move is appropriate as we can offer a car price that is competitive and the lower cost can be passed on to the rakyat,&#8221; he said.</p>
<p>At the same function, which was attended by 20,000 people, Najib also launched a special coffee table book chronicling Perodua&#8217;s 20-year journey and the company&#8217;s hope ahead.</p>

<p>Nasim Sdn Bhd recently launched the new model of <strong>2013 Peugeot 208</strong> officially with the mission to sell around 200 units monthly. It offers 2 variant which are <strong>3-doors</strong> and <strong>5-doors</strong>, both at the price of <strong>RM95,888.00</strong> and <strong>RM85,888.00</strong> respectively.</p>
<p>In term of specification, both <strong>Peugeot 208</strong> model comes with 1.6 VTi engine which capable of producing 122PS and 160Nm torque. The engine is well equipped with an adaptive Tiptronic gear with mod Sport function. We&#8217;ve tested this car back before and could say that this car has enough power whether at high speed crusing and even for town driving. </p>
<p>Also comes are standard 6-airbag, Electronic Stability Program system, ABS, EBD, EBA, DSC, and ASR. Other safety features such as Hill Start Assist, 2-point ISOFIX at back for child seat and all round one-touch anti-pinch power windows with electric child-safety door locks also equipped. Both varian comes with 5 years unlimited millege warranty.</p>
<p>As for interior side, it comes with a 7 inch infotainment touch screen with HD for drivers to access multiple function which includes entertainment and also car configuration. </p>
<p>There are 6 colors available; <strong>Digital White</strong>, <strong>Platinum Black</strong>, <strong>Virtual Blue</strong>, <strong>Rogue Red</strong>, and <strong>Passion Silver</strong>.</p>
