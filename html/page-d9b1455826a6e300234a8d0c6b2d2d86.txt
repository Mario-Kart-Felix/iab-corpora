<div class="custom"  >
	<p style="color: #000000; font-family: Verdana,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif; line-height: normal; font-size: small; text-align: justify;">Tiger Truck has been delivering a series of light utility trucks and vans for offroad use since 1999. They are the only TAA Standards Compliant Off Road Utility Truck available. All Tiger vehicles meet the stringent EPA standards and are available powered by a variety of fuels or electric motor and include standard shift.</p>
<p style="color: #000000; font-family: Verdana,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif; line-height: normal; font-size: small; text-align: justify;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="color: #000000; font-family: Verdana,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif; line-height: normal; font-size: small; text-align: justify;">Tiger Truck has the largest available array of truck body options for niche applications. Primary distribution in the U.S. is through a select dealership network.</p>
<p style="color: #000000; font-family: Verdana,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif; line-height: normal; font-size: small; text-align: justify;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="color: #000000; font-family: Verdana,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif; line-height: normal; font-size: small; text-align: justify;">Tiger Truck is a privately-held international company with distribution outlets in Central America and the Caribbean basin and maintains its headquarters in Poteau, Oklahoma where Tiger has an advanced assembly plant with more than 180,000 sq. ft. in five interconnected buildings and sources components both domestically and globally.</p></div>
