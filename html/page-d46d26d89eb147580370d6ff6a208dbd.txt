			<p><strong><img class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-13419" alt="2010 Ford Fusion" src="http://www.coautofinders.com/assets/2010-Ford-Fusion-300x184.jpg" width="300" height="184" />2010 Ford Fusion Sedans</strong> are one of the most favorite vehicles in America and the American-made ones are on top list. Anywhere you go you can find used car resellers that have great deals on sedans. Under this category fall highly valuated  <a title="Used Cars in Denver" href="http://coautofinders.com" ><strong>used cars In Denver</strong></a>, one of the most trusted used cars providers is Colorado Auto Finders. You can find everything from traditional sedans with a classical look to modern looking sporty sedans and the quality is always the best on the market.</p>
<p>The<strong> 2010 Ford Fusion SE</strong> is available at <strong>Colorado Auto Finders</strong> plus you will find this sedan to be a modern looking, mid-sized car that delivers a first class driving experience. This is one of the best choices in its category, in terms of styling, interior design, mechanics and fuel efficiency.</p>
<p>What makes the Fusion an agile vehicle is a 2.5-liter four-cylinder engine that delivers 175 horsepower and comes with six-speed automatic transmission.</p>
<p>The six-way power adjustable driver&#8217;s seat has good lateral support and lumbar support. The steering wheel features mounted cruise-control buttons on the left side and audio controls on the right. Telescopic steering column, tilt wheel and power steering provide adequate flexibility for the driver to feel comfortable when driving. Power mirrors, power windows, power locks and key-less entry underline the high level of controls optimization.</p>
<p>The Fusion’s dashboard emphases the classic style and practicality  Ford is famous for, therefore gears are easy to spot, buttons are reachable and the driver can control everything without having to take the eyes off the road.</p>
<p>The cabin is spacious and offers a relaxed ride to all occupants. There is plenty headroom and legroom both in front seats and the back seats. Back seats are fold-able offering more cargo space in case of need. The trunk comes with an anti-trap device. There are also available cargo area tie-downs.</p>
<p>Air conditioning is yet another option of this sedan permitting to have an optimal ambience temperature.</p>
<p>The <a title="2010 ford fusion SE" href="http://www.coautofinders.com/used-car/2010-ford-fusion-se/" ><strong>2010 Ford Fusion SE</strong></a> exterior design combines vigorous and aerodynamic elements however it maintains the elegance a sedan is supposed to have.</p>
<p>Handling a sedan is generally pleasant, but enjoying the Fusion’s performances is more of a high-class experience, an exciting sensation that involves both driver and passengers. Ford engineering, by definition, offers quality mechanics and reliable vehicles consequently the 2010 Fusion SE responds perfectly to the pilot inputs. Being a powerful car it has a solid body that provides protection in emergency situations.</p>
<p>Safety features on the <strong>Fusion model</strong> available at Colorado Auto Finders include front and side airbags for the driver and passenger, seatbelt pretensioners, anti-lock brakes, and traction control, tire-pressure monitoring, alarm system and child safety door locks.  The ABS and four-wheel disc brakes offer an effective stopping system. Cruise control and trip computer are also available on this model.</p>
<p>The options and characteristics this vehicle comes with, make it well suited for families, for daily commuting, for long trips, going to work in a tasteful ride or just for making a stylish entrance at a party.</p>
<blockquote><p>The Fusion SE is an ergonomic mid-sized sedan of unquestionable class and forceful performance.</p></blockquote>
