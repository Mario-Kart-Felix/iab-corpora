						<div class="entry-title"><h1 class="epi-fontLg bwalignc">
      <b>Leading Automotive and Motorcycle Expert Joins KLD Energy Technologies</b>
    </h1>
    </div> 
							
								
								<meta itemprop="headline" content="Leading Automotive and Motorcycle Expert Joins KLD Energy Technologies"/>
							
							

						<div id="story_subheadline">
							
						</div>

						

						<!-- start story body -->
						
						<div class="entry-content" itemprop="articleBody">
						
						<p>AUSTIN, Texas--(<span itemprop="provider publisher copyrightHolder" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Organization" itemid="http://www.businesswire.com" class="author source-org vcard"><span itemprop="name" class="org fn"><a itemprop="url" href="http://www.businesswire.com/">BUSINESS WIRE</a></span></span>)--KLD Energy Technologies, Inc., leaders in electric propulsion and 
      generation systems, has brought onboard John Bautista to lead its 
      business development and research group. Bautista has an extensive 
      background in automotive, motorcycle, motorsports, marine, automotive 
      aftermarket and environmental-technology industries, including serving 
      as director of mechanical engineering at Zero Motorcycles, Inc. With 25 
      years of business development and engineering expertise, Bautista will 
      be responsible for identifying and analyzing new market and product 
      opportunities for KLD Energy Technologies.
    </p>
<blockquote><p>&#8220;John’s career in clean energy and automotive and motorcycle development 
      has an instant impact on our current vision. His understanding of key 
      markets will be invaluable as we continue to work with leading global 
      companies across many industries to implement our electric propulsion 
      and generation systems.&#8221;</p></blockquote>

    <p>
      “John brings a critical skill-set and expertise to KLD Energy 
      Technologies,” said Christian Okonsky, founder and CEO of the company. 
      “John’s career in clean energy and automotive and motorcycle development 
      has an instant impact on our current vision. His understanding of key 
      markets will be invaluable as we continue to work with leading global 
      companies across many industries to implement our electric propulsion 
      and generation systems.”
    </p>
    <p>
      The first application of KLD Energy Technologies’ electric generation 
      and propulsion system is in the two-wheel electric vehicle market where 
      Bautista’s professional experience is unparalleled. Bautista has been 
      engaged at all levels of two-wheel vehicle development and production. 
      Serving as director of mechanical engineering at Zero Motorcycles, Inc., 
      he brought high-performance, zero-emissions electric motorcycles to 
      market. Bautista worked to lower emissions by advancing electric vehicle 
      technology while with energy-efficiency focused STWA, Inc. Bautista, a 
      graduate of the University of California at Davis, has also built, raced 
      and served on the technical crews for SCCA, IMSA and NHRA cars, IHBA 
      boats and SCORE trucks.
    </p>
    <p>
      “KLD Energy Technologies’ unique generation and propulsion system is 
      truly without comparison,” said Bautista. “I am excited to collaborate 
      with the team on expanding our presence in a global market; there is 
      enormous opportunity worldwide.”
    </p>
    <p>
      KLD Energy Technologies’ initial application of its technology is in the 
      two-wheel electric vehicle market. The company also continues to expand 
      in diversified industrial channel markets that can benefit from 
      efficient, high-torque motor systems, including wind energy generation 
      equipment, forklifts, elevators, escalators, air conditioners, pumps, 
      and three- and four-wheel electric vehicles.
    </p>
    <p>
      <b>About KLD Energy Technologies, Inc.</b>
    </p>
    <p>
      Austin, Texas-based KLD Energy Technologies’ mission is to develop 
      innovative, sustainable technologies. The company, focused on the 
      research, development and commercialization of advanced technologies, 
      has received acclaim and worldwide interest in its first product, a 
      complete electric propulsion and generation system with applications in 
      dozens of industries. The system is designed for high-frequency 
      operations, offering revolutionary system efficiency. KLD Energy 
      Technologies is dedicated to bringing clean technologies to a global 
      marketplace. For more information, visit <a target="_blank" href="http://cts.businesswire.com/ct/CT?id=smartlink&amp;url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.KLDEnergy.com&amp;esheet=50011225&amp;lan=en-US&amp;anchor=www.KLDEnergy.com&amp;index=1&amp;md5=2c9f814cbe871bc4113e8f03966465c2">www.KLDEnergy.com</a>.
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
