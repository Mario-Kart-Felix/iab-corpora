<p><span class="header">Request Roadside Assistance</span></p>


<p>Any time, day or night, members can rely on AAA for help with emergency roadside assistance no matter where they have a problem.</p>


<p><br />
	
	</p>


<p><span style="font-style: italic; font-weight: bold;">If you require immediate assistance, please contact us at (952) 927-2727 or 1-800-222-4357. <br />
		<br />
		
		You may also <a href="http://www.aaa.com/services/automotive/rso/rsoHome.html?club=057&amp;association=AAA" class="popup950">request roadside assistance online.</a><br />
		
		</span></p>	
	</div>
</div>
</div>

<div class="subcontent">			




<p>Members can count on AAA for emergency road service response 24 hours a day, 365 days a year, anywhere in the United States and Canada. We will either enable the vehicle to operate under its own power or move it to a safer location. Basic AAA membership includes towing five miles in any direction or back to the nearest AAA facility; AAA Plus, AAA Plus RV AAA Premier and AAA Premier RV memberships provide enhanced towing benefits.<br />
	
	
	
	<br />
	
	
	
	Emergency road service response is provided to members at no charge; the nearest contract station will send a truck to the eligible disabled vehicle. The AAA member must have been present when the vehicle became disabled and must show a valid membership card and picture identification to the response unit (unless being present would jeopardize your personal safety).<br />
	
	
	
	





</p>
