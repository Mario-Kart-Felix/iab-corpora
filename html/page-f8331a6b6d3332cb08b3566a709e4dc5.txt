			Motorcycle engine parts mainly consist of piston rings, valves, cam chains, clutches, <a href="/automotive-lubrication/oil-filters.html">oil filters</a>, motor bolt kits, valve shims, conrods, barrels etc. Engine parts can also be divided into two categories: Original Equipment Manufacturer engine parts (OEM) and Aftermarket Performance Engine Parts. OEM parts are those that are originally manufactured and designed by the company. They include camshaft, carburetor, clutch, crankcase, <a href="/engine/crankshafts.html">crankshaft</a>, cylinder etc. Aftermarket parts include cables, <a href="/transmission-system/clutch-kits.html">clutch kits</a>, clutch plates, gasket kits, oil filters etc. These are the parts which are readily available in the market under various brands. The Global Motorcycle sales were recorded 137,932 units in July 2007, 15.52% lower than the 163,280 units sold in July 2006, and 9.64% lower than were sold in June 2007.
