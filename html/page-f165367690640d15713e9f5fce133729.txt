    		<H1>
    		Automotive Cables</H1>
            <H2>
            Crosslinkable polyethylene (XLPE) provides ideal insulation solution for automotive primary cables</H2>
            <span class="brodtext">

            
<P>In striving to continuously improve vehicle performance, automotive OEMs and manufacturers place increasingly higher demands on the materials used for vital components. </P>
<P>Central to virtually all vehicle functions those demands extend to automotive wiring, which is expected to deliver <STRONG>higher temperature resistance</STRONG>, <STRONG>greater flexibility</STRONG> and contribute to <STRONG>vehicle weight reduction</STRONG>, as well as production cost savings.&nbsp; </P>
<H3>Performance supporting automotive progress </H3>
<P>To meet these progressively tougher criteria, Borealis offers a complete range of peroxide and irradiation crosslinkable polyethylene (XLPE) flame retardant compounds specifically developed to provide optimal automotive primary wiring solutions. All grades have a successful track record in the market. </P>
<P>Borealis’ XLPE solution offer a range of benefits to the automotive OEM and manufacturer. These advantages include:&nbsp; </P>
<UL><LI>halogen free flame retardancy </LI>
<LI>temperature performance from -40°C to 125 °C </LI>
<LI>Society of Automotive Engineers (SAE) J-1127 and SAE J-1128 compliance </LI>
<LI>non-tarnishing characteristics </LI>
<LI>excellent heat stability and easy extrusion </LI>
<LI>easy colourability </LI></UL></span>
