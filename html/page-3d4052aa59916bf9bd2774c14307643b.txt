	<p><strong>zMAX Soaks Into Metal</strong></p>
<p>The only product of its kind, zMAX is a "micro-lubricant." zMAX micro-molecules have been reformulated to be smaller than regular oil molecules.This allows zMAX micro-molecules to soak into metal - a product attribute none of our competitors can duplicate. As it soaks into metal, zMAX is able to disperse carbon and other harmful deposits. These deposits are a leading cause of engine damage and inefficient operation. The unique ability of zMAX to soak into metal and reduce harmful engine deposits results in:</p>
<ul>
<li>Increased Gas Mileage</li>
<li>Improved Performance</li>
<li>Reduced Wear on Vital Engine Parts</li>
<li>Help Clean Fuel Injectors and Carburetors</li>
<li>Extended Engine Life</li>
<li>Reduced Emissions</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>How zMax Works</strong></p>
<p>The fluids in your vehicle act as a carrier for zMAX. When zMAX reaches the fuel and engine systems, it soaks into the metal. Once the metal is treated with zMAX, it is cleaned and continuously protected from carbon and harmful deposits. Â For use in gasoline and diesel engines.</p>
<p><strong>For the Engine:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Cleans and conditions cylinder walls, pistons, piston rings, bearings and valves</li>
<li>Provides continuous lubrication of your engine's internal parts</li>
<li>Protection for both new and used engines - helps with engine break in</li>
</ul>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">The result is reduced wear on vital parts, longer engine life and restored power</p>
<p><strong>For Fuel System:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Cleans your fuel system and conditions the combustion chambers of your engine</li>
<li>Removes carbon and other deposits from the intake / exhaust valves, valve guides, cylinder walls and piston rings</li>
</ul>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">The result is increased gas mileage, improved performance, increased horsepower and lower emissions</p>
<p><strong>For Transmission:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Cleans and lubricates the parts of your transmission</li>
<li>Increased lubrication helps prevent premature wear of internal transmission components</li>
</ul>
<p style="padding-left: 30px;">The result is smoother shifting, reduced wear and extended life for your transmission</p>
<p><strong style="font-style: italic;">Some vehicles have "Sealed Transmissions" (no place to fill or check the fluid level) To add zMAX Transmission Formula, please check with your dealer or service provider.</strong>Â <em><strong>Verify with your Owner's Manual if this applies to your vehicle.</strong></em></p>
<p><strong>zMAX Testing:</strong></p>
<p>Over the past 50 years, hundreds of tests have been conducted on zMAX. Many have been consumer tests using the product in everyday, real-life situations. But the important tests are the industry test protocols conducted in our own laboratory and by independent labs around the country. The results of these tests show that zMAX soaks into metal; reduces friction; increases horsepower; dissipates engine heat; helps improve or restore gas mileage and reduce emissions in older cars, by virtue of reducing engine deposits; helps maintain gas mileage and emissions in newer cars, by virtue of reducing engine deposits; helps reduce engine wear on vital engine parts, by virtue of reducing engine deposits; and helps to extend engine life, by virtue of reducing engine deposits. For more information, visit our <a href="http://www.zmax.com/why/technical/" title="Technical" target="_self">technical link</a>.</p></div>
