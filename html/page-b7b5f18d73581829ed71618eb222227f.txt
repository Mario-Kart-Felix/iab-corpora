<p>Oil prices combined with climate changes caused by COx emissions and other exotic pollutants emitted to the air are pushing the need for less dependence on oil and more dependence on alternative fuels.</p>



<p>Alternative fuels like Biodiesel, Electricity, Ethanol, Hydrogen, Methanol, Natural Gas and LPG are being researched and used every day. The usage and restrictions of these fuels requires a joint effort from the government and automotive industry.</p>



<p>Join this powerful 4-day conference that combines the <strong>largest number of OEM companies with Government officials</strong> whose work in solely dedicated to the automotive industry.</p>
