<h1 class='post-title entry-title'>
2013 Mazda 5 The Spacious Minivan
</h1>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content' id='post-body-848036423626500947'>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
In 2013, Mazda 5 came out with pure athletic style with sporty performance and a spacious interior. Mazda 5 opposed category labels while providing flexibility. Mazda 5 to offering 6 different colors, ie Clear Water Blue Mica, Zeal Red Mica, Meteor Gray Mica, Liquid Silver Metallic, Jet Black Mica and Crystal White Pearl.</div>
<br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-teSFkK-5gt4/UUcCSmagDpI/AAAAAAAAAC4/QYjVZfgFEVg/s1600/2013+Mazda+5+Front+View.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img alt="2013 Mazda 5 Front View" border="0" height="286" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-teSFkK-5gt4/UUcCSmagDpI/AAAAAAAAAC4/QYjVZfgFEVg/s640/2013+Mazda+5+Front+View.jpg" title="2013 Mazda 5 Front View" width="640" /></a></div>
<br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-YiSgblOLs2s/UUcCc-SJeAI/AAAAAAAAADQ/hBAnsdWzPkk/s1600/2013+Mazda+5+Rear+View+2.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img alt="2013 Mazda 5 Rear View 2" border="0" height="300" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-YiSgblOLs2s/UUcCc-SJeAI/AAAAAAAAADQ/hBAnsdWzPkk/s640/2013+Mazda+5+Rear+View+2.jpg" title="2013 Mazda 5 Rear View 2" width="640" /></a></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
<b><u>Mazda 5</u></b> has a length of 458 cm (180.5 inches) with a width of approximately 175 cm (68.9 inches) and height 162 cm (63.6 inches) and wheelbase 275 cm (108.3 inches). Equipped with a 2.5-liter DOHC 16-valve 4-cylinder with &nbsp;VVT powered 157 hp at 6000 rpm, Torque by 163 lb-ft at 4000 rpm.<br />
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-VBJWQY28-6Y/UUcCbQmB0WI/AAAAAAAAADA/uc0lgwnNhxM/s1600/2013+Mazda+5+Interior.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img alt="2013 Mazda 5 Interior" border="0" height="282" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-VBJWQY28-6Y/UUcCbQmB0WI/AAAAAAAAADA/uc0lgwnNhxM/s640/2013+Mazda+5+Interior.jpg" title="2013 Mazda 5 Interior" width="640" /></a></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-x5-PL9tPDZo/UUcCbkWUtRI/AAAAAAAAADE/PN41E3joDF4/s1600/2013+Mazda+5+Interior+2.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img alt="2013 Mazda 5 Interior 2" border="0" height="282" src="http://4.bp.blogspot.com/-x5-PL9tPDZo/UUcCbkWUtRI/AAAAAAAAADE/PN41E3joDF4/s640/2013+Mazda+5+Interior+2.jpg" title="2013 Mazda 5 Interior 2" width="640" /></a></div>
<br />
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-gaXAtJRG7vY/UUcCkkBBYlI/AAAAAAAAADY/3ex8NgbEuPg/s1600/2013+Mazda+5+Slidding+Door.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img alt="2013 Mazda 5 Slidding Door" border="0" height="296" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-gaXAtJRG7vY/UUcCkkBBYlI/AAAAAAAAADY/3ex8NgbEuPg/s640/2013+Mazda+5+Slidding+Door.jpg" title="2013 Mazda 5 Slidding Door" width="640" /></a></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
Three rows with seating for six, two sliding rear doors and 44.4 cubic feet of cargo space with the 3rd row folded down. Other interior of the new Mazda 5 is a Bluetooth hands-free phone and audio capability, AM/FM/CD/MP3-compatible stereo with 6 speakers, SiriusXM Satellite Radio-compatible audio system, USB audio input, Steering-wheel-mounted audio controls, and auxiliary-audio input jack.<br />
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-UKROxxQ1yXg/UUcCwEsFelI/AAAAAAAAADg/EkIziPLupq0/s1600/Zeal+Red+Mica+2013+Mazda+5.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img alt="Zeal Red Mica 2013 Mazda 5" border="0" height="290" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-UKROxxQ1yXg/UUcCwEsFelI/AAAAAAAAADg/EkIziPLupq0/s640/Zeal+Red+Mica+2013+Mazda+5.jpg" title="Zeal Red Mica 2013 Mazda 5" width="640" /></a></div>
<br />
<div style="text-align: justify;">
For safety features, <a href="http://automotives-zone.blogspot.com/2013/03/2013-mazda-5-spacious-minivan.html" target="_blank">Mazda 5</a> has advanced front air bags, front side-impact air bags and side-impact air curtains for all three rows. New rear backup sensors on Touring and Grand Touring models also help to avoid hidden obstacles when reversing.</div>
<br />
<div style="text-align: justify;">
Because of its small size, the Mazda 5 really do not have a direct rival. The other "minivan" in the market is a much larger vehicle like the Honda Odyssey and Dodge Grand Caravan. Buyers cross shop imaginable such as the Ford Escape compact crossover and Toyota RAV4, which offers more butch styling but less interior space.<br />
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-Ba60yySiHss/UUcCzH8MWNI/AAAAAAAAADo/rhlU4cUxukM/s1600/2013+Mazda+5+Rear+View.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img alt="2013 Mazda 5 Rear View" border="0" height="394" src="http://1.bp.blogspot.com/-Ba60yySiHss/UUcCzH8MWNI/AAAAAAAAADo/rhlU4cUxukM/s640/2013+Mazda+5+Rear+View.jpg" title="2013 Mazda 5 Rear View" width="640" /></a></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<br /></div>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;">
<a href="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-90bnsU5o9Fc/UUcCKPOZXNI/AAAAAAAAACw/4khWawBJo7E/s1600/2013+Mazda+5+Front+View+2.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img alt="2013 Mazda 5 Front View 2" border="0" height="290" src="http://2.bp.blogspot.com/-90bnsU5o9Fc/UUcCKPOZXNI/AAAAAAAAACw/4khWawBJo7E/s640/2013+Mazda+5+Front+View+2.jpg" title="2013 Mazda 5 Front View 2" width="640" /></a></div>
<br />
<div style="text-align: justify;">
The <b><i>2013 Mazda 5 price</i></b> is start from $19,940 for Sport category, $22,070 for Touring category and $24,470 for Grand Touring.<br />
