                <h1>Its Hump Day Hopped Up Hot Horny Hatchback</h1>
<h1>2013 Sports Cars</h1>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>This is a look at Hatchback <b>2013 Sports cars</b> that have a little bit of moxie. Americans love speed and southern Missourians love meth. But as Americans we are still noobs when it comes to the pocket rockets. We have had the Civic Si and Mazda 3 for a bit as well as the GTI’s but these have still not become the rage as they have across the pond. Probably because of our fuel prices we can still get away with super sweet sexy V8’s. But since gas prices are steadily becoming a bitch and we still have cigarettes, old milwaukee, and lotto tickets to buy, we need to start downsizing in length and boosting up in PSI’s. The list is indeed getting longer and as more manufacturers turn to forced induction we shall hopefully see some of these pocket rockets getting some stellar Dyno results. So lets take a look at cars that will soon be seen on YouTube crashing as wannabe racers realize that body kits are less important then quality tires.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span id="more-893"></span></p>
<h2><a href="http://www.newcarsfor2013.org/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/2013-volkswagen-golf-r.jpg"><img class="alignleft size-large wp-image-895" title="2013-volkswagen-golf-r" src="http://www.newcarsfor2013.org/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/2013-volkswagen-golf-r-1024x680.jpg" alt="2013 Sports Cars"width="645" height="428" /></a>Hatchback 2014 Sports Cars – The List (Probably Missing a Few)</h2>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>2013 Audi A3 Quattro</strong> - Awesome Factors – Turbo 200Hp &#8211; AWD</p>
<p><strong>2013 Chevrolet Sonic</strong> – Awesome Factors – Turbo 138Hp -40 MPG’s</p>
<p><strong>2013 Fiat 500 Abarth</strong> – Awesome Factors – Turbo 160Hp &#8211; 2533 Lbs – Nimbly Bimbly</p>
<p><strong>2013 Fiat 500 Abarth Venom</strong> – Awesome Factors – Turbo 200Hp &#8211; No Fiat Badges</p>
<p><strong>2013 Ford Focus ST</strong>– Awesome Factors – Turbo 247Hp – Updated MyTouch</p>
<p><strong>2013 Ford Fiesta ST</strong>– Awesome Factors – Turbo 178Hp – 3-door</p>
<p><strong>2013 Honda CR-Z</strong>– Awesome Factors – Only Honda Hatchback that won’t get shitty body kits put on it (Si?)</p>
<p><strong>2013 Hyundai Veloster Turbo</strong> – Awesome Factors – Turbo 201Hp – Looks Like Its Screaming</p>
<p><strong>2013 Lexus CT 200h</strong> – Awesome Factors – MPG’s (43 MPG) – That’s about it</p>
<p><strong>2013 Mazda MAZDASPEED3</strong> – Awesome Factors – Turbo 263Hp 280 ft lbs! – Looks ready for business</p>
<p><strong>2013 Mini John Cooper Works</strong> – Awesome Factors – Turbo 208Hp – Twin Scroll design means less lag</p>
<p><strong>2013 Subaru WRX</strong> – Awesome Factors – Turbo 265Hp – AWD – Solid Powertrain</p>
