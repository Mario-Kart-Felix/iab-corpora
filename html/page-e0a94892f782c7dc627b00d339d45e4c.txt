             <div class="contentBoxArea"><p><img src="/wp-content/uploads/2010/11/42-23069517-300x199.jpg" alt="" title="Rear view of woman holding umbrella in field" width="300" height="199" class="alignright size-medium wp-image-304" />We say “Drive On” because there’s never any time to lose.  We get your customers back behind the wheel as quickly and efficiently as possible.</p>
<p><strong>Emergency Roadside Assistance</strong></p>
<p>This has been the core of our business, and we do it better than anyone. Our staff of courteous and professional Assistance Coordinators is trained to meet our customers&#8217; roadside emergency needs 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, every day of the year.  Our emergency roadside assistance services include:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>24-hour dispatch of emergency towing assistance:</strong><br />
Tow service to the nearest qualified service facility, or to the issuing dealership,      or other destination designated by the client.</li>
<li><strong>24-hour dispatch of emergency supplies:</strong><br />
Emergency supply of gasoline, oil, fluid and water.</li>
<li><strong>24-hour dispatch of flat tire assistance:</strong><br />
Assistance to inflate, repair or replace the flat tire with the vehicle’s      spare tire</li>
<li><strong>24-hour emergency battery assistance:</strong><br />
Assistance to jump-start the vehicle</li>
<li><strong>24-hour emergency lock-out assistance:</strong><br />
Assistance of a locksmith to allow entry into the vehicle. Duplicate keys      can be made at the customers&#8217; request and expense</li>
<li><strong>24-hour emergency dispatch for collision towing:</strong><br />
Tow service to the nearest qualified repair facility, or to the issuing      dealership, or other destination designated by the client.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Customization</strong></p>
<p>Every Road America program can be customized to fit the specialized needs of every client.</p>
<ul>
<li>Client can customize roadside benefit to include tie-back to the issuing dealer or any other location</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Program is designed as a stand-alone      profit center or as an enhancement or promotional item</li>
<li>Field Issued Enrollment <em>(membership card fulfillment available)</em></li>
<li>Terms for stand-alone memberships range      from 1-7 years</li>
<li>Renewable Membership and Commission</li>
<li><em>“Sign      and Drive”</em> roadside      assistance with customized benefit levels</li>
</ul>                          </div>
