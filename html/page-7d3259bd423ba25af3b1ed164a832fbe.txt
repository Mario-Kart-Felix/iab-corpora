 <div class="prod-desc">
 <h3>
 Product Overview
 </h3>
 <div class="richtext"><p><strong>WHY TAKE THIS COURSE:</strong> </p><p>There are over two million hybrid cars on the road today. Every major car company is adapting their fleet to meet increasing hybrid demand. Electric vehicle technology is continually improving and changing, it is important that every after-market auto shop be confident in their ability to service these fundamentally different vehicles. Research has shown that every time a technician purchases a new component of hybrid vehicles instead of repairing it, <strong>they are losing $1000</strong>. This course will prepare mechanics to accurately diagnose and troubleshoot maintenance issues with hybrid and electric vehicles. <p></p></p><p><strong>WHAT YOU'LL LEARN:</strong> </p><p>CleanEdison's 5-day intensive hands-on course provides participants with a working knowledge of advanced vehicle technologies found in hybrid and electric vehicles. Our skilled and qualified instructors address “real world” problems that technicians face every day. Because of the potentially dangerous nature of high-voltage batteries, the entire first day is spent on safety equipment and procedures. The rest of the course delves into every aspect of how to properly maintain and repair hybrid and electrical vehicles of all makes and models. Topics include energy management, power converters, dc-dc converters, electric machines, battery packs and much more. </p><p>This course is ideal for vehicle maintenance and repair shops and other individuals interested in advanced vehicle technology. <p></p></p><p><strong>WHAT'S INCLUDED</strong> </p><ul><li>Hybrid Electric Vehicle Technology Student Textbook </li><li>Worksheets and Course Handouts (on CD) </li><li>Course Completion Certificate </li><li>CleanEdison Course Policy</li></ul></div>
