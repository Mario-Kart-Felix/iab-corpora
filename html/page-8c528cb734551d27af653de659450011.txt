<h2>Insurance For Your Collector Car</h2>
<h3>Protection through our Antique/Classic Program</h3>
<p>Whether you lovingly rebuilt your antique or classic car from the ground up or you bought a perfectly restored showpiece, it&#146;s much more than a car to you. It&#146;s your passion, your pride and joy. State Farm&#174; can help protect it. We&#146;ve offered affordable, quality auto coverage since 1922. When your collector car was new, it may have been covered by State Farm. Who better to insure it again?"</p>
<br />
<h3>Is your car eligible for coverage?</h3>
<p>If you&#146;re not sure, these definitions may help:</p>
<ul>
	<li><strong>Classic Automobile</strong>: A motor vehicle ten or more years old, which is rare or of special historical interest because of exceptionally fine workmanship or limited production. A classic motor vehicle 25 years old or older is covered as an antique.</li>
	<li><strong>Antique Automobile</strong>: A motor vehicle 25 or more years old.</li>
</ul>
<p>And here are some important conditions for eligibility:</p>
<ul>
	<li>Your antique or classic car must be used on a very limited basis, such as exhibitions, club activities, and parades or similar events.</li>
	<li>You need to have restored, maintained, or preserved your antique or classic car.</li>
</ul>
<h3>Available Coverages</h3>
<ul>
	<li>Liability</li>
	<li>Medical Payments/No Fault</li>
	<li>Comprehensive</li>
	<li>Collision</li>
	<li>Uninsured Motor Vehicle/Underinsured Motor Vehicle</li>
	<li>Emergency Road Service</li>
	<li>Death, Dismemberment and Loss of Sight</li>
	<li>Loss of Earnings</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Note</strong>: This is only a general description of available coverages and is not a statement of contract. All coverages are subject to policy provisions and applicable endorsements. For further information on antique and classic insurance see a State Farm Agent.</p>
<h3>More Reasons to Choose State Farm</h3>
<ul>
	<li>Lower premiums since your antique or classic car is used on a very limited basis such as exhibitions, club activities or parades.</li>
	<li>When you insure your antique or classic car with us, you and State Farm will agree on the value of the car. If the car experiences a total covered loss, we will pay this agreed upon value, rather than the actual cash value of the car.*</li>
	<li>Up to $500 coverage for your car&#146;s spare parts.*</li>
	<li>24 Hour Good Neighbor Service &#150; During the day or in the middle of the night, you can call your agent&#146;s number and speak with a State Farm representative to handle your auto insurance needs.</li>
	</ul>
    
<p>*Not applicable in Massachusetts, North  Carolina, and Texas &ndash; Contact your State Farm Agent for details.</p>
    
<h3><strong>State Farm Collector Car Expertise</strong></h3>
<p>  State  Farm employees love their cars and that's why our&nbsp;State Farm  Vehicle Research Facility&nbsp;built this 1968  Chevrolet Camaro RS convertible. Built as a half-and-half &quot;Split Personality&quot; car for training purposes and PR display, the driver&rsquo;s side of the car is a  high-quality restoration to correct factory-stock specs while the passenger  side is modified with lots of custom parts and custom bodywork, but  deliberately less-exacting craftsmanship.</p>
<p>  In addition to  serving as an eye-catching car-show display piece, this car was built by State  Farm employees who love their cars, enjoy restoring cars and have fun working  on cars both personally and professionally. This 1968 Camaro is being used to  educate State Farm associates on the special challenges of assigning proper  values to collector cars. This knowledge enables our associates to accurately  assess the vehicle for its proper value. </p>
<p>  See more of  the State Farm Split Camaro on <a class="video-thumb" data-width="560" data-height="315" href="http://www.youtube.com/embed/fgSQJnf0Rl8" >Jay Leno's Garage</a> <img src="http://www.statefarm.com/_images/video_icon.gif" alt="video icon" /></p>
<p>You  can also see some images of the Split Camaro on <a href="http://www.flickr.com/photos/statefarm/sets/72157629103097163" target="_blank">Flickr</a></p>
