		<h1 class="entry-header">New VW Minivan On the Way</h1>
	
	<div class="entry-content">
		<div class="entry-body">
			<p><img border="0" alt="Microbusconcept" title="Microbusconcept" src="http://blogs.cars.com/photos/uncategorized/2008/01/17/microbusconcept.jpg" />
</p>

<p>The Detroit News is reporting that Volkswagen will debut an all-new minivan at the Chicago Auto Show next month. We know, we’re not even done writing about the <a href="http://blogs.cars.com/kickingtires/2008_detroit_auto_show/">Detroit auto show</a> and here’s something about Chicago. Sorry — news happens.</p>

<p>The VW van will be called Routan, a reference to the word &quot;route.&quot; The big surprise is that it will be based on the Dodge Caravan and Chrysler Town &amp; Country minivans. The Routan will be built in Canada.</p>

<p>We’d like to think the van will look like the classic Microbuses of old or the cool concept shown here, but that’s doubtful. Still, a VW interior in a minivan might be quite welcome.<br /><a href="http://info.detnews.com/redesign/blogs/autoshowblog/index.cfm?blogid=280"><br />VW Routan: Missing the Minivan Crest?</a> <span style="color: #999999;">(Detroit News) </span>

</p>
		</div>
