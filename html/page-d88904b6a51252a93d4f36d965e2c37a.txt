        <td width="279" class="texta1" colspan="1" itemprop="articleBody"><!-- google_ad_section_start -->Axon Automotive announced that they entered their Axon hatchback in the Automotive X Prize competition. The Axon hatchback is built on a carbon fiber chassis and thanks to its light weight will be able to offer a 100 mpg fuel consumption. This will make the Axon hatchback one of the most fuel efficient car in its class when it will go on sale in 2010.
<br><br>
Axon Automotive is a new UK company that uses affordable carbon fiber manufacturing techniques to build fuel efficient cars. Their new hatchback has been launched at the <i>Sexy Green Car Show</i> held between 23 and 31 of May, 2008.
<br><br>
The Automotive X Prize is a 10 million USD competition to build the most fuel efficient car.<!-- google_ad_section_end --></td>
