							<h2 class="title"><a href="http://www.autojobsvi.com/valves-are-crucial-to-how-nuclear-submarines-work/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link to Valves Are Crucial To How Nuclear Submarines Work">Valves Are Crucial To How Nuclear Submarines Work</a></h2>
							<div class="postdate"><img src="http://www.autojobsvi.com/blog/wp-content/themes/CarOne/images/date.png" /> March 1st, 2013 <img src="http://www.autojobsvi.com/blog/wp-content/themes/CarOne/images/user.png" /> Irfan </div>
			
							<div class="entry">
								<p style="text-align: justify;">Any kind of nuclear powered installation or vehicle needs to keep safety absolutely paramount in all of its activities. This is especially true of the nuclear submarines used by so many naval powers in this day and age. One component which is vital to keeping a submarine safe is the valves, high quality valves such as <!-- Irfan --><a href="http://www.truflomarine.com/nuclear.php" target="_blank">nuclear valves by Truflo</a> are essential.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">A nuclear submarine differs to a more conventionally powered submarine in a number of ways, with the power generation system being the most obvious difference. Nuclear submarines use a nuclear reactor. This either powers an electric motor which is connected to the propeller shaft, or use the heat generated by the reactor to power steam turbines.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">One drawback of using this means of power is that it reduces the stealth capabilities of the vessel. Nuclear powered submarines cannot move silently and largely undetectably through the ocean as the noise that their reactors make can be picked up on sonar. As much of the cooling process is left to the waters of the ocean through which they are travelling, they also leave a thermal wake which can be spotted by FLIR imaging.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">The need for absolute safety while using such a volatile means of energy is obvious. It is especially so when you consider that a submarine of this kind will often use highly enriched fuel, sometimes greater than 20 per cent, to power its engines. Valves therefore play a massive role in keeping the submarine operational and safe.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">A number of valves are used in the engines of nuclear submarines. An example is the so-called flood valves. These are used to make the submarine more buoyant when required. The Kingston valve is located close to the bottom of the ballast tank of the submarine. This is used to help the vessel submerge and re-emerge from under the water. When a submarine is at the surface, the valve allows the tank to fill with air. When it is time to submerge, the valve opens to allow water into the ballast tank.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">Using such a volatile source of energy as nuclear power makes every component in the vessel crucial. Missing or faulty valves can cause real disaster in a vessel like this.</p>

Bunded steel storage tanks are ideal for this purpose.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;"><strong>How Bunded Steel Storage Tanks Work</strong><br />
It is now necessary that each business get bunded storage systems to store petroleum liquids. A bunding refers to a storage tank that prevents spillage or leakage of liquids. In this context, a bunded steel storage tank refers to a tank constructed within another tank.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">Bunded steel storage tanks act as a durable double wall that is made from steel to prevent any leakage of oil or fuel stored within it. Petroleum liquids are environmental hazards that need to be contained so that pollution and accidents are minimized.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">Steel is used for bunding because it is impervious to and compatible to a wide range of liquids. Diesel storage tanks are manufactured to store diesel for your machinery and generators.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">Bunded steel storage tanks are designed in such a way that any rainwater or snow does not enter and mix with the liquid stored within. This can be via a roof that has drains or draining facilities. External elements such as rainwater can contaminate the liquid stored within, making it less viable.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;"><!-- Irfan --><a href="http://www.blakegroup.co.uk/storage-tanks/diesel-tanks" target="_blank">Diesel tanks by Blake Group</a> are effective when used for storage of your diesel. Apart from this, they can be used for storage of waste oil. The tank is designed for safety as well as durability. The tanks are constructed in using double walls that makes them very strong and much more effective against leakage and spillage.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">Bunded steel storage tanks are easy to install and relocate to remote areas. You can select additional accessories and features to be fitted to meet the storage requirements of your business as well as normal standards. The tanks have a holding capacity of between 1,000 &#8212; 150,000 litres that makes this a cost-effective as well as a versatile system for your business.</p>
