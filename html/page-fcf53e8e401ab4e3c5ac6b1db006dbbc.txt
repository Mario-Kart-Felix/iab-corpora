            <h1>Roadside Assistance</h1>

<p>Drive your car with complete peace of mind thanks to the free roadside assistance service Assistance Plus offered by your AUTO-SELECT<sup>®</sup> repair shops!</p>

<p>Whenever you get auto repairs or car maintenance done in an AUTO-SELECT garage, ask for the Assistance Plus coverage for breakdown services for up to $75, valid for a 90-day period.</p>

<p>With this roadside assistance service, you will benefit from one of the following emergency services for free:</p>

<ul>
	<li>Towing Service (within a 40 kilometers radius or to the closest garage)</li>
	<li>Lock-Out Service</li>
	<li>Battery Boost</li>
	<li>Fuel Delivery</li>
	<li>Flat Tire Change</li>
</ul>

<p>Ask your AUTO-SELECT auto mechanic to provide you with a free roadside assistance card including the toll-free number to call in case of an emergency (24/7).</p>
