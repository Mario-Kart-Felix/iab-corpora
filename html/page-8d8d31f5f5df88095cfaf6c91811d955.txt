<div class="post"><div class="posttop"><div class="username">del</div><div class="date">12-21-2002, 02:08 AM</div></div><div class="posttext">you guys are aware that when mentioning 'off-road' that leads to other things.  like what types?  goin over large  rocks, a shorter wheelbase would be better.  where a jeep wrangler would fare better than the longer and wider hummer.  but traversing a desert sand dune, a dune buggie would be the choice.  forging through the forest in the hummer isn't such an easy task when you have to figure that you'll face squeezing through  tight areas that aren't ideal for the hummer.  the hummer has limitations where a jeep wrangler would find with ease just due to the smaller size and shorter wheelbase.    in a wide open area, the hummer can dominate, but when it comes to smaller and tighter places, i'd pick a jeep wrangler.   but forging up a steep incline or hill, i'd pick a hummer for more stability.  picking the best off-road vehicle isn't as easy as you'd think.  best at what type of off-road?  8&quot; deep mud, snow?  climbing rocks, digging through sand?  some are just better at certain areas than others.</div></div><hr />
