                                        <h1>Emergency  Roadside Assistance</h1><p>Since not every trip goes as planned, adding a roadside assistance program to your insurance policy may be a good idea. Emergency roadside assistance can get you back on your way by providing towing, fuel delivery and other services.</p><h2>Stay covered with emergency roadside assistance</h2><p>Roadside assistance coverage typically includes*:</p><p><strong>Towing.</strong> If your vehicle is mechanically disabled or can't be driven after an accident, you can have it towed to a repair facility.</p><p><strong>Tire Change.</strong> Flat tires caused by road hazard, collision or natural wear may be covered.</p><p><strong>Battery Jump Start.</strong> Dead battery? Get help jump-starting your vehicle.</p><p><strong>Fuel Delivery.</strong> Out of gas? Roadside assistance may deliver you fuel. </p><p><strong>Lock-out Service.</strong> Lock your keys in your car? Lock-out service to get you back in.</p><p><strong>Vehicle Extrication.</strong> If you've run off a public roadway, you may have your vehicle winched back onto the pavement.</p><p>Stay protected on the road. Ask your Titan Insurance agent about emergency roadside assistance today.</p><p>*Subject to eligibility. Terms and conditions apply.</p>
