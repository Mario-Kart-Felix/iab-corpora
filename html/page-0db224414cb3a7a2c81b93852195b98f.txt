      <div id="creditsHolder" style="margin:10px 0 0 0;">

        <div id="fbLikeHolder1" style="display:inline-block;"></div>

        <small class="post-credits">

        <p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-98272" title="2014 Kia Forte Cerato" src="http://blog.caranddriver.com/wp-content/plugins/lazy-load/images/1x1.trans.gif" data-lazy-src="http://blog.caranddriver.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/2014-Kia-Forte-Cerato.jpg" alt="2014 Kia Forte Cerato" width="626" height="382" /><noscript><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-98272" title="2014 Kia Forte Cerato" src="http://blog.caranddriver.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/11/2014-Kia-Forte-Cerato.jpg" alt="2014 Kia Forte Cerato" width="626" height="382" /></noscript></p>
<p>Kia has announced it is bringing the 2014 Forte sedan to the <a href="http://www.caranddriver.com/2012-la-auto-show">2012 L.A. auto show</a>, or at least the car that previews the next-generation Forte. The company posted the image above, which includes the Cerato—as the next-gen Forte is known in global markets where it’s already on sale—and the dates of the L.A. auto show to its Facebook page.<span id="more-98267"></span></p>
<hr noshade="noshade" />
<ul>
<li><a href="http://www.caranddriver.com/comparisons/2010-kia-forte-sx-vs-2010-mazda-3-s-sport-2010-volkswagen-golf-comparison-tests">Comparison Test: 2010 Kia Forte SX vs. 2010 Mazda 3 s Sport, 2010 Volkswagen Golf</a></li>
<li><a href="http://www.caranddriver.com/comparisons/2013-ford-escape-vs-2012-honda-cr-v-2012-hyundai-tucson-2012-kia-sportage-2013-mazda-cx-5-2012-toyota-rav4-comparison-test">Comparison Test: 2013 Ford Escape SEL 4WD vs. 2012 Honda CR-V EX-L AWD, 2012 Hyundai Tucson Limited AWD, 2012 Kia Sportage EX AWD, 2013 Mazda CX-5 Grand Touring AWD, 2012 Toyota RAV4 Limited 4&#215;4</a></li>
<li><a href="http://www.caranddriver.com/reviews/2013-kia-rio5-sx-manual-instrumented-test-review">Instrumented Test: 2013 Kia Rio5 SX Manual</a></li>
</ul>
<hr noshade="noshade" />The Cerato/Forte’s appearance in L.A. isn’t a huge surprise, given we already knew the next Forte would be closely related to the global Cerato and that it would probably debut in the City of Angels. <a href="http://blog.caranddriver.com/kia-reveals-next-global-cerato-sedan-previews-the-new-forte-coming-here/">Kia unveiled the Cerato</a> this past summer, and and we dug the sedan’s handsome styling, which rather attractively mixes styling cues from the Optima and Rio. Expect more information on the next-gen Forte to surface later this week, when the car debuts in Los Angeles.</p>
