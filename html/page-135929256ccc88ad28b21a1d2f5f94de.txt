Front row air bags that deploy from the steering wheel and passenger side dashboard airbags are not affected.</p><p xmlns="http://www.coremedia.com/2003/richtext-1.0">The recall covers the following 2013 models: Dodge Grand Caravan, Chrysler Town &amp; Country and Ram Cargo Van.</p><aside>
