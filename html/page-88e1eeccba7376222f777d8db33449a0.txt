A brief feature on the upcoming Toyota Sienna Minivan includes an available Dynamic Radar Cruise Control, 18-inch 10-spoke machine-finished alloy wheels, LED Tail and stop lamps, front/rear parking sonar, High Density Discharge headlamps with manual level control, auto on/off feature, Daytime Running Lights with manual on/off feature, and Automatic High Beam feature. Sienna&#8217;s inside boasts a Light Grey leather with removable second-row lounge seating with long slide feature, dual moonroof with front power tilt/slide and rear slide, and a touchscreen DVD navigation system. As this is intended for family use, it is sure to equip the best safety and dynamic features.<br/>
