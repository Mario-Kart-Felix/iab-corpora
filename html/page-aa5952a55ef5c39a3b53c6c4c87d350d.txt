            <div class="content_box_title_wide">QUICK GUIDE - DOING BUSINESS IN SF</div>
            <div class="content_text">
            <p><strong>Start in SF</strong><br />
            Mayor Edwin Lee has often referred to San Francisco as a city of doers and dreamers, and a city of innovators and entrepreneurs. Indeed, the entrepreneurial spirit runs deep in the city. And whether it&rsquo;s our world class workforce, longstanding international reach and reputation, or a citizenry of early adopters and trend setters that&rsquo;s always on the cutting edge, businesses who choose to locate in San Francisco enjoy a critical business edge. For these reasons, the City of San Francisco actively welcomes and encourages all budding entrepreneurs to take a serious look at San Francisco as an ideal headquarter city.</p>
            <p>The first steps to starting a business are deciding upon a <a href="http://sfgsa.org/index.aspx?page=4217">business structure</a>, developing a <a href="http://sfgsa.org/index.aspx?page=4215">business plan</a>, finding the right <a href="http://sfgsa.org/index.aspx?page=4221">location</a> (you can start by using the <a target="_self" href="http://www.sfgov.org/gis">City's GIS, or Geographic Information Systems, applications</a>), <a href="http://sftreasurer.org/index.aspx?page=22">business registration</a> as well as deciding upon (and protecting!) your <a href="http://www.sfgov2.org/index.aspx?page=31">business&rsquo; name</a>.             <p><strong>Grow in SF</strong><br />
            You&rsquo;ve started your business in San Francisco (great choice!), and now you are looking to take it to the next level. Read on to find out more about <a href="http://sfgsa.org/index.aspx?page=4220">financing your business</a>, any <a href="http://sf-moh.org/index.aspx?page=336">available City loans </a>, the array of <a href="http://sfgsa.org/index.aspx?page=4588">tax incentives</a> available and the available assistance and incentives for <a href="http://sfgsa.org/index.aspx?page=4590">greening your business</a>. Assistance is also available in <a href="http://www.sfassessor.org/index.aspx?page=104">filing</a> for <a href="http://www.sfassessor.org/index.aspx?page=55">business property information.</a></p>
            <p><strong>Move to SF<br />
            </strong>Come find out what so many businesses already know&mdash;a location in San Francisco offers businesses a considerable advantage.             <p><strong>Hire in SF<br />
            </strong>One of San Francisco&rsquo;s most valuable assets and attractive qualities is its residents. Using the newly launched <a href="http://www.hiresf.org/">HireSF</a>, begin to explore the <a href="http://www.hiresf.org/resumesrch/default.asp?session=resumesrch&amp;geo=0601000000">immense pool of highly qualified job seekers</a> San Francisco has to offer.</p>
