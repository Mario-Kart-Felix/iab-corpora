    <h2>Silicon Valley International Auto Show</h2>
    <p>Always a favorite the Silicon Valley International Auto Show will be held once again in January. The Silicon Valley International Auto Show is a trade event where manufacturers show off their preproduction and models for the upcaoming model year. This is one of the must sees if you are wanting to get information on the latest hybrid, crossover and luxury cars.</p>  
    <h2>Pacific Coast Dream Machines Show</h2>
    <p>The Pacific Coast Dream Machines Show in Half Moon Bay is being held in April this year. It will also be a 2 day show. This event has everything!     <p>Reddings Kool April Nites has always been one of my favorites. Classic and vintage cars are the main subject of this car show, but there is also a nice mix of modifieds, and hot rods. One of the highlights is the cruise held the Friday night of the show through Redding Ca. </p>
    <h2>Sacramento Autorama</h2>
