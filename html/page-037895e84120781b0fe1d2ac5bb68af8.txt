				<h1>Mechanic Schools and Careers</h1><div class="line"></div><h2>
	Are you on the hunt for a training program on automobiles, trucks, boats, motorcycles or airplanes? Let us help you discover schools and programs.</h2>
<p>
	Let <a href="/">Mechanic Schools</a> assist you in searching for a mechanic school, college, or career training program which may be useful for your career in automotive technology, diesel technology, motorcycle mechanics, marine mechanics, or aviation technology.</p>
<p>
	Research accredited schools by state or specialty, or even manufacturer-partnered training program.</p>
<div class="line"></div><h2>Featured Categories</h2><div class="box">
				<div class="ima"><img alt="Auto Mechanic Schools" src="/sites/default/themes/mechanic/images/boxAuto.png"></div>
				<h4><a title="Automechanic Schools" href="/automotive/">Auto Mechanic Schools</a></h4>
				<p>The demand for professional auto mechanics continues to rise. Auto mechanics' pay is determined in different ways. Some businesses pay their mechanics a flat rate per job, and the flat rate will vary from job to job. </p>
				<div class="clear"></div></div><div class="box">
				<div class="ima"><img alt="Motorcycle Schools" src="/sites/default/themes/mechanic/images/boxMoto.png"></div>
				<h4><a title="Motorcycle Schools" href="/motorcyclerepair/">Motorcycle Schools</a></h4>
				<p>Motorcycle mechanics repair and service small motorized vehicles, including scooters, mopeds, motorcycles, and dirt bikes. Most of the people who go into this profession do so because they love the work.</p>
				<div class="clear"></div></div><div class="box">
				<div class="ima"><img alt="Diesel Training  Schools" src="/sites/default/themes/mechanic/images/boxDiesel.png"></div>
				<h4><a title="Diesel Training Schools" href="/diesel/">Diesel Training  Schools</a></h4>
				<p>Diesel-powered vehicles are standard in heavy use vehicles such as 18-wheelers, trains, buses, and farm equipment, but are also becoming more common in lighter trucks. Because today's diesel is much cleaner burning than the dirty fuel of the past, it is expected that more passenger vehicles will adopt diesel fuel in the near future.</p>
				<div class="clear"></div></div><div class="box">
				<div class="ima"><img alt="Airframe &amp; Powerplant Schools" src="/sites/default/themes/mechanic/images/boxAir.png"></div>
				<h4><a title="Airframe &amp; Powerplant Schools" href="/aircrafttechnician/airframepowerplant/">Airframe and Powerplant Schools</a></h4>
				<p>Avionics technicians have many job opportunities across the United States and even around the world. Some positions are with national and international airlines, government services, research and development companies, and the military.</p>
				<div class="clear"></div></div><div class="box">
				<div class="ima"><img alt="Maritime Schools" src="/sites/default/themes/mechanic/images/boxMaritime.png"></div>
				<h4><a title="Maritime Schools" href="/marine/">Maritime Schools</a></h4>
				<p>Marine mechanics will experience growth in the demand of their chosen profession over the next several years due to varying factors and industry trends, one of which is varying technologies. </p>
				<div class="clear"></div></div><div class="box">
				<div class="ima"><img alt="Aircraft Technician Schools" src="/sites/default/themes/mechanic/images/boxAir.png"></div>
				<h4><a title="Aircraft Technician Schools" href="/aircrafttechnician/">Aircraft Technician Schools</a></h4>
				<p>Aircraft are undeniably powerful machines and they need knowledgeable and trained technicians to keep them in the skies and running smoothly.</p>
				<div class="clear"></div></div><div class="box">
				<div class="ima"><img alt="Truck Driving Schools" src="/sites/default/themes/mechanic/images/boxDiesel.png"></div>
				<h4><a title="Truck Driving Schools" href="/truckdriving/">Truck Driving Schools</a></h4>
				<p>The backbone of U.S. industry, commercial trucks are the main source of freight transportation in the United States. Yet there is an acute shortage of truck drivers in the U.S., making truck driving a high-demand career.</p>
