			<h1>Best Luxury Station Wagons</h1>
			<small>Sunday, June 20th, 2010</small>

			<div class="entry">
				
<!-- Quick Adsense WordPress Plugin: http://quicksense.net/ -->
<div style="float:left;margin:10px 10px 10px 0;">

<p>Luxury and station wagons doesn’t  have to be an oxymoron when you consider the range and variety of best  luxury station wagons offered by today’s automakers. Generally priced  over $30,000, luxury station wagons aim to offer consumers a luxurious  and sporty alternative to SUVs. To that end, they largely succeed, some  more so than others.</p>
<p>Here are three top picks for Best  Luxury  Station Wagons:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="http://www.iseecars.com/Used_Cars/used-Cadillac_CTS%20Wagon"  target="_blank"><strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Cadillac     CTS Sport Wagon</span></strong></a><strong> – </strong>Cadillac markets the CTS Sport Wagon as a vehicle that offers    the sportiness of <a href="http://blog.iseecars.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/06/cadillac_cts_sport_wagon_2010.jpg" ><img class="alignright size-medium wp-image-1422" title="cadillac_cts_sport_wagon_2010" src="http://blog.iseecars.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/06/cadillac_cts_sport_wagon_2010-300x187.jpg" alt="cadillac cts sport wagon 2010 300x187 picture" width="300" height="187" /></a> the CTS sedan and the utility of a capable SUV –    and rightfully so. The CTS Sport Wagon for 2010 is nothing if not a    terrific example of exemplary driving dynamics with two powerful  engines    and available all-wheel drive coupled with numerous luxury features.    In fact, Consumer Guide named CTS (including Sport Wagon) a 2010 Best    Buy, and Car and Driver gives it a 10Best accolade for 2010. Editors    at <a href="http://www.caranddriver.com/features/09q4/2010_10best_cars-10best_cars/2010_cadillac_cts_2f_cts-v_page_4" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.caranddriver.com']);" target="_blank"><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Car    and Driver</span></a> comment: “The    CTS manages to blend refinement, driving dynamics, comfort, and  performance    in a refreshing, even exhilarating way that satisfies enthusiast  drivers    and luxury seekers simultaneously…For 2010, the sedan is joined by    a new Sport Wagon that maintains the connected and rewarding feel of    the sedan but adds practicality and arguably an even more stylish  appearance.”    Look for starting manufacturers suggested retail prices (MSRPs) from    $38,265 to $53,265.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><a href="http://www.iseecars.com/Used_Cars/used-Audi_A4"  target="_blank"><strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Audi     A4 Avant</span></strong></a><strong> – </strong>Featuring standard Audi Quattro all-wheel drive, the 2010 Audi    A4 Avant is one luxury <a href="http://blog.iseecars.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/06/audi_a4_avant_2010.jpg" ><img class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-1423" title="audi_a4_avant_2010" src="http://blog.iseecars.com/wp-content/uploads/2010/06/audi_a4_avant_2010-300x199.jpg" alt="audi a4 avant 2010 300x199 picture" width="300" height="199" /></a>station wagon that commands attention on all    fronts. It’s supremely quiet, features a luxurious cabin, attractive    exterior design, and provides sporty and comfortably handling on the    road. Top-ranked safety ratings (including being named a Top Safety    Pick by the Insurance Institute for Highway Safety) make the A4 Avant    a natural choice for families seeking a solid but luxurious station    wagon. Starting MSRP for the 2010 A4 Avant is $35,350. <a href="http://www.kbb.com/new-cars/audi/a4/2010/review" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.kbb.com']);" target="_blank"><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Kelley Blue Book</span></a> remarks: “While Audi’s lineup has increased    over the years, the A4 sedan and wagon (Avant, in Audi-speak) has  remained    the German automaker’s volume leader, thanks to its ability to blend    spirited driving performance with supreme passenger comfort.”</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li><a href="http://www.iseecars.com/Used_Cars/used-BMW_3%20Series"  target="_blank"><strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;">BMW    3-Series Sports Wagon</span></strong></a><strong> – </strong>The driving dynamics of all BMW 3-Series models – including    the 3-Series Sports Wagon – make it the benchmark in this class.  Available    for 2010 in 328i and 328i xDrive models, MSRPs start at $35,700 and    $37,700, respectively. Both are powered by a 230-hp 3.0-liter inline    <span class='wp_keywordlink'><a href="http://www.iseecars.com/cars/6-cylinder-cars " title="6 cylinder cars list
" target="_blank">6-cylinder</a></span> engine mated to a standard 6-speed <span class='wp_keywordlink'><a href="http://www.iseecars.com/cars/manual-transmission-cars " title="manual cars list
" target="_blank">manual transmission</a></span>  (automatic    is optional). Consumers can expect comfortable seats, smooth  performance,    first-rate safety scores, and the prestige of the BMW brand. <a href="http://www.edmunds.com/bmw/3series/2010/review.html" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.edmunds.com']);" target="_blank"><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Edmunds</span></a>, which comments that 3-Series is nearing the    end of this model’s production run, says, “The 3-Series’ long-running    double act is truly extraordinary. It speaks the language of driving    enthusiasts, yet its upscale image and comfortable interior give it    unrivaled mass appeal.”</li>
</ul>
<p>Still more makes to consider for Best  Luxury Station Wagons include <a href="http://www.iseecars.com/Used_Cars/used-Volvo_XC70"  target="_blank"><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Volvo  XC70</span></a> and <a href="http://www.iseecars.com/Used_Cars/used-Volvo_V70"  target="_blank"><span style="text-decoration: underline;">V70</span></a>, <a href="http://www.iseecars.com/Used_Cars/used-Audi_A6"  target="_blank"><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><span class='wp_keywordlink'><a href="http://www.iseecars.com/cars/audi-a6" title="Audi A6" target="_blank">Audi A6</a></span> Wagon</span></a>, and <a href="http://www.iseecars.com/Used_Cars/used-BMW_5%20Series"  target="_blank"><span style="text-decoration: underline;">BMW  5-Series Wagon</span></a>.</p>
