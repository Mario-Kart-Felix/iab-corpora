          <br clear="all" />
                        <div id="tab1">
                <p>
	<span><span>Find peace of mind with the right protection for you and your vehicle with Aviva&rsquo;s auto insurance solutions.</span></span></p>
<p>	Designed to provide you with the coverages you need at a reasonable price, you can also select additional features that enhance the quality of your protection.</p>
<p>	<strong>Protecting you and your family</strong><br />
	The physical and financial impact of an automobile accident can be devastating - that&rsquo;s why all Canadian provinces and territories require drivers to have mandatory coverage like third party liability, uninsured auto coverage and accident benefits.</p>
<p>	Choose optional coverage to enhance your protection - such as collision, comprehensive and increased accident benefits. </p>
<p>
	<span><span>Speak to an insurance broker today to find out more.</span></span></p>
<p>
	<a href="https://www.avivacanada.com/webfm_send/477" target="_blank">Click here</a> to print your Aviva <em>Customer Care Card</em>, a great way to keep your auto policy information handy and record accident details.&nbsp; Keep it with your pink slip so it&#39;s there when you need it!</p>
              </div>
                          <div id="tab2">
                <p>
	<strong>Setting the cost of auto insurance</strong></p>
<p>	The cost of your auto insurance is based on a number of factors including:</p>
<ul>
<li>
		The coverage and deductible you choose</li>
<li>
		Type and model year of your vehicle(s)</li>
<li>
		Why you drive your vehicle - for personal, business or both</li>
<li>
		The amount you drive each year</li>
<li>
		The number of drivers in your household and their ages</li>
<li>
		The driving record of drivers in your household</li>
</ul>
<p>
	Did you know? You can reduce the cost of your auto insurance by increasing your deductible.</p>
<p>
	&nbsp;<strong>Saving money</strong></p>
<p>	Ask your insurance broker how you can save money with discounts for:</p>
<ul>
<li>
		Good driving, such as being &quot;Claims Free&quot;</li>
<li>
		Your road profile, such as age, a hybrid vehicle or anti-theft devices</li>
<li>
		Loyalty and bundling, such as multi-vehicle, multi-line or renewal loyalty&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
<p>
	<span>* The Manitoba, Saskatchewan and British Columbia provincial governments require residents to purchase automobile insurance directly from them.&nbsp; However, optional coverage and add-ons may be available through Aviva.</span></p>
              </div>
                          <div id="tab3">
                <p>
	Quick and convenient service is just a call away with Aviva&rsquo;s Satellite Roadside Assistance Program. You can access this service 24 hours a day, 7 days a week anywhere in Canada or the continental United States.<br />
	<br />
	Our helpful representatives are ready to take your call directly and will get you back on the road as soon as possible.<br />
	<br />
	Be it a tow, battery boost, or if you&#39;ve accidently run out of gas - you&rsquo;re covered for these emergencies and more for just pennies a day.<br />
	<br />
	Additional services include:</p>
<ul>
	<li>
		advice on vehicle maintenance and repairs</li>
	<li>
		directions if you&rsquo;re lost</li>
	<li>
		access to travel assistance - such as lodging and car rental discounts to trip interruption coverage</li>
</ul>
