we opened our first retail store in Rockville, MD. Soon we had many enthusiastic 
customers who stayed with us through the van conversion craze, the 4 wheel drive 
craze and mini truck fad. Now, more than thirty years later, we are still on the 
top of the market and have customers from the early seventies continue to visit 
and support our stores. <br /> <br /> The 1990's introduced the compact and import 
performance era. We began customizing cars and in 1998 changed our name to Trick 
Trucks and Cars. As word spread about our exceptional customer service, great 
product lines and discount prices, we readily opened more locations. Now, with 
stores in MD, VA, WV and DE, we have the buying power of a multi-location business 
and can offer a full line of accessories with continued exceptional service, superior 
installations and discount prices. <br /> <br />
