                </div><p class="releaseDateline">Temecula, CA (PRWEB) January 11, 2012 </p><p> Best Buy Automotive Equipment recently launched a new motorcycle trailer unlike any other on the market: the Easy Load EL-2000MT Motorcycle Trailer. </p>
<p>The new motorcycle trailer most notably features an easy, safe loading and unloading mechanism that lowers the trailer to the ground. Durable construction includes a heavy-duty hand winch that easily lowers the trailer. The motorcycle is securely pinned and locked in the upright position, ensuring user safety and preventing damage. </p>
<p>“We’re very proud of this product,” commented Jeff Tanner, owner of Best Buy Automotive Equipment. “The EL-2000MT is safe, easy to use, and efficient for people with either one bike or two. Our customers will be impressed with its solid construction that will ensure its durability,” he added.</p>
<p>The EL-2000MT features a 2,000 lb. capacity, which means two full-dress Harley Davidsons would be fully supported. The Easy Load EL-2000MT Motorcycle Trailer also features a standard trailer hitch, running lights, brake lights, and directionals. </p>
<p>Specifications to keep in mind for storage: the trailer weighs 950 pounds, is 99&#189; inches wide, 149⅝ long, and 24&#189; inches high.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.bestbuyautoequipment.com/motorcycle-trailer-EL-2000mt-by-easy-load-p/el-2000mt.htm" onclick="linkClick(this.href)" rel="nofollow">View pictures and find other information</a> about the trailer, check out complementary products like a <a href="http://www.bestbuyautoequipment.com/Motorcycle-Lifts-s/292.htm" onclick="linkClick(this.href)" rel="nofollow">motorcycle lift</a>, or contact Best Buy Automotive Equipment to learn more.</p>
<p>About Best Buy Automotive Equipment</p>
<p>Open since 1987 and with over 80 years of combined staff experience, Best Buy Automotive Equipment provides automotive repair equipment such as <a href="http://www.bestbuyautoequipment.com/Car-Lifts-s/410.htm" onclick="linkClick(this.href)" rel="nofollow">auto lifts</a> and parts for a variety of vehicles, including trucks, cars, motorcycles, ATVs, and more. Thirteen fully-stocked warehouses across the nation feature showrooms for customers to view over 25,000 high quality items. Items include <a href="http://www.bestbuyautoequipment.com/Car-Lifts-s/410.htm" onclick="linkClick(this.href)" rel="nofollow">car lifts</a>, motorcycle lifts, tire changers, wheel balancers, brake lathes, jacks, shop presses, trailers, and more, with dependable names like BendPak, Direct-Lift, Rotary, Robinair, and many more. Contact Best Buy Automotive Equipment via info@bestbuyautoequipment.com, (800) 296-3860, or view products at <a href="http://www.bestbuyautoequipment.com." onclick="linkClick(this.href)" rel="nofollow">http://www.bestbuyautoequipment.com.</a></p>
