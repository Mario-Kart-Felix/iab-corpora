      <h1>Roadside Coverage When You Need It</h1>
      <p>None of us  likes to be stranded by a broken-down car. At best, it's inconvenient. At worst  — depending on the time of day or night, the weather conditions and the  location of the breakdown — it can be downright dangerous. </p>
      <p>We don't  want you to end up in that scenario, but if you do, we want you to have help  ready. Erie Insurance makes road service coverage available through your auto  insurance policy. </p>
      <p>If your auto  is disabled, this coverage pays for reasonable auto towing and required labor costs  at the site of the vehicle breakdown.<sup>1</sup> An additional premium or cost  is required for this coverage. <a href="/Applications/SiteAdvantage/Default.aspx?searchType=agent">Contact your local Erie Insurance  Agent</a> to find out  how to add this coverage to your policy or <a href="/Applications/ExpandedQuote/GeneralInfo.aspx">get an auto insurance quote now</a>. </p>
      <p><em><sup>1</sup></em><em> In North Carolina, a limit on the  amount of coverage applies ($25, $50 or $100 per car) for reasonable auto  towing and required labor costs at the site of the vehicle</em> <em>breakdown.</em></p>
