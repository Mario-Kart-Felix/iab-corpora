			<h2 class="head">Why Use Roadside Assistance?</h2>
            <p class="underheader">
                <div style="width:619px; float:left; padding:5px;">


<p>Cars are arguably one of the greatest inventions of the 20th century, and they have become essential to the everyday lives of billions. And with the limitless freedom that cars provide, making it easy to travel as far as your pocketbook can take you, it's no wonder people put a lot of time and money into caring for their automobiles. When a road incident occurs, whether it's needing a tow or requiring lock out assistance, car owners are turning to<strong> roadside assistance</strong> plans to keep their cars, as well as their families, protected.</p>
<p>Roadside assistance plans provide insurance for common roadside emergencies, including car towing services, tire change services, gasoline delivery, lock out assistance, minor auto adjustments, extrication (winching) and dead battery services. Many motor clubs guarantee roadside help no matter where you are nationwide, and they will even pay for any travel interruption expenses, including hotel and transportation costs, in case your car breaks down over a hundred miles from home. The best car breakdown services also offer complimentary extras, such as retail discounts and travel planning services.</p>
<p>The roadside assistance plans we reviewed provide all the essential services that you need when traveling the open road. Be sure to check out our <a href="http://roadside-assistance-services-review.toptenreviews.com/learning-center.html">articles on roadside assistance services</a>, as well as the best roadside assistance service comparisons and reviews, including our top rated plans: <a href="http://roadside-assistance-services-review.toptenreviews.com/allstate-motor-club-review.html">Allstate Motor Club</a>, <a href="http://roadside-assistance-services-review.toptenreviews.com/bp-motorclub-review.html">BP Motor Club</a> and <a href="http://roadside-assistance-services-review.toptenreviews.com/aaa-review.html">AAA</a>.</p>
<div id='ad_as_2'></div><h3>Roadside Assistance: What to Look For</h3>
<p>The roadside assistance plans in our review are the cream of the crop in providing 24/7 emergency roadside services. They will assist with any roadside emergency, from setting up a tow to jumping a dead battery. And the plans include loads of extras, including retail discounts and concierge service, as well as a variety of travel planning tools. Since roadside assistance is tied to your safety, the plans we reviewed also provide top-rated customer support.</p>
<p>A quality roadside assistance plan should provide every possible roadside service, appreciated extras and quality customer support. We used the following criteria to evaluate the top roadside assistance plans.</p>
<p style="margin-left: 40px;"><strong>Services</strong><br />
There are several important features to look for when choosing a roadside assistance plan. We looked at the most important services a roadside assistance plan should provide: 24/7 roadside assistance, lock out assistance, minor roadside adjustments, towing, tire change service, extrication, gasoline delivery and dead battery services. Other key services we looked at include an on-time arrival guarantee, coverage for travel interruption expenses and whether an arrest bond certificate was provided.</p>
<p style="margin-left: 40px;"><strong>Extras</strong><br />
A roadside assistance plan cannot only get you out of a sticky situation but they can also shower you with several cool extras. Top plans offer online trip planning tools (including online maps), travel materials (including paper maps), travel planning assistance, concierge services, retail discounts and emergency travel assistance. We also looked at whether the plan sells car insurance and offers a mobile application for getting even quicker assistance.</p>
<p style="margin-left: 40px;"><strong>Customer Support</strong><br />
A roadside assistance plan should offer toll-free phone support to answer any questions you may have before signing up, or to address any concerns once you're a member. They should also offer email support as an additional option. We also looked for FAQs on each plan's website site, since a good FAQs section can help you find answers to your questions quickly.</p>
<p>Roadside assistance plans are popular with millions of individuals and families, and for good reason: They provide peace of mind in knowing help is only a phone call away, no matter the situation that may occur on the road. Whether you&rsquo;re in need of a tow or lock out assistance in the middle of the desert, these plans deliver. Add in their exciting extras, from travel planning assistance to retail discounts, and the <strong>roadside assistance</strong> plans in our review are sure to provide every feature you&rsquo;re looking for when choosing a great motor club.</p>

function show_one_rate_graph(selected_rating_position)
{
	var num_products = 10;
	var num_ratings = 3;
	currently_selected_graphr = selected_rating_position;
	//reset unselected for overall ratings
	document.getElementById("graphr_rate_item_0").style.background = "#ffffff";

	document.getElementById("graphr_rate_item_0").style.color = "#2a80ea";

	{			
		for (var j=1;j<=num_ratings;j++)

				document.getElementById(el_id).style.display = "none";

					//document.getElementById(bigel_id).style.display = "block";

				}

				{
					document.getElementById(bigel_id).style.display = "none";	
				}
			}
			else
			{
				//document.getElementById(el_id).style.display = "block";
				$('#'+el_id).slideDown("slow");

			document.getElementById(clickable_id).style.borderColor = "#ffffff";
			document.getElementById(clickable_id).style.color = "#2a80ea";
		}		
	}
	//set selected row
	var clicked_on_div_id = "graphr_rate_item_"+selected_rating_position;

	document.getElementById(clicked_on_div_id).style.borderColor = "#cdcdcd";
	document.getElementById(clicked_on_div_id).style.color = "#000000";
	
	
}
