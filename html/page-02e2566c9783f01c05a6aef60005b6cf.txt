<h1>From the organizer of the World&acute;s outstanding automotive transmission meeting place </h1>
<p><em> </em>The  International CTI 


 Symposium

is held by the Car Training Institute (CTI), as a spin-off of the world&acute;s largest annually held automotive transmission meeting in Berlin (Germany). International experts discuss the latest developments and issues dealing with automotive transmissions and drives. The CTI Symposium China offer the perfect opportunity for international transmission and power train experts to exchange experience and opinions. </p>
<p>Visit the 2nd  CTI Symposium in China and get late-breaking updates in transmission for conventional, HEV and EV drives by technology-leaders coming from Europe, America and Asia. </p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h1><strong>The CTI Symposium China</strong></h1>
<p>Benefit from 10 years of experience &amp; gear up for transmission and drive train business! </p>
<p>The CTI Symposium &amp; Exhibition is THE international meeting place for the transmission community. Thanks to its unique combination of conference, exhibition and networking opportunities, the CTI Symposium represents an unrivaled forum for doing business and sealing deals. </p>
<p>The CTI Symposium China is CTI&acute;s new international platform to exchange experiences and a unique possibility for an update in this important field of automotive engineering. Here you find your future partners for development, sales and purchase of whole systems and components. </p>
<p>The 2nd CTI Symposium China will be held on  25-27 of  September 2013  in Shanghai. </p>

<h2> Key Facts</h2>
<br />
<p><strong>The CTI Symposium China consists of</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li>an introductory day into the basics and practice of automotive transmissions for newcomers and those who want to refresh their knowledge</li>
  <li>      2 main days with 


 more than 50

 technical presentations from international experts discussing latest technologies and strategies on automotive transmissions and HEV &amp; EV drive for passenger cars and commercial vehicles</li>
  <li>Presentations  held by automobile manufacturers, system and component suppliers and engineering consultants</li>
  <li>Exhibition area with nearly 40 companies presenting latest products and services </li>
  <li>Evening event for extended networking opportunities</li>
  <li>Conference languages 


 and documents: English and Chinese</li>
  <li>Simultaneous translation </li>
</ul>

<h1>Get impressive insights about CTIs mega event</h1>
<p>The International CTI Symposium in Berlin (Germany) with more than 1,000 delegates is the world&lsquo;s most visited annual conference on Automotive Transmission and Hybrid &amp; Electric Drive Technology. In 2012 over 20 key experts took part in the plenum and 


 the delegates could choose between

more than 70 technical presentations in 10 parallel sessions.</p>
