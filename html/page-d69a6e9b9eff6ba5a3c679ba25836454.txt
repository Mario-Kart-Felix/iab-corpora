            <br>
            To provide the highest quality customer service by
            efficiently and accurately issuing vehicle titles and
            registrations; licensing Idaho vehicle dealers and salesmen;
            maintaining and providing consistent and accurate motor
            vehicle and dealer records; administering titling,
            registration and dealer laws; and providing guidelines and
            solutions to titling, registration, and dealer compliance
            problems within the scope of the law. </td>
