			<div class="widget-content"><h1>Hybrids</h1>
<p>First I would like to introduce myself as I am our Hybrid Tech. My Name is Charlie Spirka I go by Chas most of the time. As most have noticed hybrid vehicles have become main stream and here at Automotive Specialties we have embraced them. We offer full service and repair of hybrid vehicles. From simple oil change to diagnosing the dreaded red triangle of death a Prius owner fears the most. Also I would like to extend the olive branch to anyone who is considering buying a hybrid I welcome questions. Also I can offer as chance to drive a hybrid as I am a Prius owner.<br /><br />For many years now the aftermarket repair industry has not keep up with service and repair of these vehicles. We know from personal experience but that has changed now. I am ACDC Master Hybrid Auto Tech. What does that mean? Well means I traveled to a hand on weeklong training class. That was over 50 hours of training. There I spent a week learning theory of operation, safety steps needed, common problems, service requirements that differ from none hybrids, and got to pre form lots of hands task such as battery removal, transmission teardown and inspection. The list goes on and on. The training<br />focused on all makes. When we talk hybrid most think of the Prius, but there are many more makes.</p>
<p>We can offer repairs and service that the dealers cannot. Such as batter replacement with a rebuilt unit and replacement of other hybrid/high voltage controllers and/or modules from used sources. We can inspect and test the condition of the big battery with our Midtronics HYB hybrid car battery tester. We also understand that certain areas of these vehicles have different service needs than a conventional vehicle. We provide the extra attention needed. We also employ many factory tools so we are at thesame level as the dealer. Also if we do not have a tool we have access to the tool and last we have access to a large network of technicians with years of hybrid experience.</p>
