		<h2 class="txt16b">Car Electronics/Mechatronics</h2>
		<div class="lede">
			<p class="pg">Mitsubishi Electric manufactures a variety of intelligent power control devices and systems for tomorrow's vehicles, including ECUs, electric power steering components, millimeter-wave radar and other car electronic/mechatronic components. These products are designed with concern for both safety and ecology.</p>
			<p class="txt10b">PLEASE NOTE: Automotive equipment from Mitsubishi Electric is sold as original equipment mainly to automobile manufacturers. If you have an inquiry regarding non-aftermarket parts, please contact your car manufacturer or local service dealer.</p>
		</div>
		<div class="section">
			<div class="thumb"><img src="images/thumb_airbag.jpg" alt="Airbag Control Systems" width="130" height="95" /></div>
			<div class="wrap">
				<h3 class="txt14b">Airbag Control Systems</h3>
				<p class="text">Airbag control systems designed to protect vehicle occupants from the impact of collisions by maximizing airbag effectiveness. The systems incorporate sensors that detect front/side impact and other factors, and use the data to optimize airbag deployment and the tension applied to the seatbelt pretensioners. Mitsubishi Electric aims to achieve the highest level of safety for passengers.</p>
				<div class="more"><a href="/bu/automotive/products/safety_and_security2/safe06.html">More information</a></div>
			</div>
		</div>
		<div class="section">
			<div class="thumb"><img src="images/thumb_egr.jpg" alt="EGR Valves" width="130" height="95" /></div>
			<div class="wrap">
				<h3 class="txt14b">EGR Valves</h3>
				<p class="text">EGR valves contribute to preventing air pollution and global warming through cleaner exhaust gases and reducing CO<sub>2</sub> emissions. In diesel vehicles, Mitsubishi Electric's stepper motor-based EGR valves reduce the engine temperature, thereby reducing the generation of nitrogen oxide, an air pollutant and cause of acid rain. In gasoline-powered vehicles DC motor-based EGR valves reduce energy loss, which improves fuel efficiency.</p>
				<div class="more"><a href="/bu/automotive/products/enviroment2/env08.html">More information</a></div>
			</div>
		</div>
		<div class="section">
			<div class="thumb"><img src="images/thumb_electronic_keys.jpg" alt="Electronic Keys" width="130" height="95" /></div>
			<div class="wrap">
				<h3 class="txt14b">Electronic Keys</h3>
				<p class="text noMore">Laser-encrypted keys incorporate Mitsubishi Electric's world-class encryption and wireless communication technologies. These keys offer added convenience through remote electronic entry, and simultaneously contribute to preventing theft, enhancing security and peace of mind.</p>
				<!--<div class="more"><a href="">More information</a></div>-->
			</div>
		</div>
		<div class="section">
			<div class="thumb"><img src="images/thumb_ems.jpg" alt="Engine Management Systems" width="130" height="95" /></div>
			<div class="wrap">
				<h3 class="txt14b">Engine Management Systems</h3>
				<p class="text noMore">Mitsubishi Electric engine management systems enable a vehicle's engine to be controlled efficiently and economically. Central to the system is the Engine Control Unit (ECU), which monitors various engine components and controls them to maximize performance. The ECU controls fuel injection, fuel combustion timing, idling speed, other load functions and fault diagnosis.</p>
				<!--<div class="more"><a href="">More information</a></div>-->
			</div>
		</div>
		<hr class="breakPoint" />
		<div class="section">
			<div class="thumb"><img src="images/thumb_hid.jpg" alt="HID Ballast Control Unit" width="130" height="95" /></div>
			<div class="wrap">
				<h3 class="txt14b">HID Ballast Control Unit</h3>
				<p class="text">High-intensity discharge (HID) headlamps with ballast control offer numerous benefits over conventional halogen lamps, including double the brightness, a service life four times longer and 40% less power consumption. This control unit also contributes to improved visibility at night and during poor weather conditions, making it easier to see distant objects more clearly.</p>
				<div class="more"><a href="/bu/automotive/products/safety_and_security2/safe05.html">More information</a></div>
			</div>
		</div>
		<div class="section">
			<div class="thumb"><img src="images/thumb_ipu.jpg" alt="Intelligent Power Units" width="130" height="95" /></div>
			<div class="wrap">
				<h3 class="txt14b">Intelligent Power Units</h3>
				<p class="text">Since 1997 and the introduction of the first hybrid electric vehicles (HEVs), Mitsubishi Electric has been a leading supplier of intelligent power units (IPUs). The IPU is a DC/AC inverter located between the battery and electric motor, and operates to convert DC battery power to alternating current for use by the electric motor. As the popularity of HEVs rises, so will the need for increasingly more efficient IPUs to the environment-conscious automobile industry.</p>
				<div class="more"><a href="/bu/automotive/products/enviroment/index.html">More information</a></div>
			</div>
		</div>
		<div class="section">
			<div class="thumb"><img src="images/thumb_mwr.jpg" alt="Millimeter-wave Radar" width="130" height="95" /></div>
			<div class="wrap">
				<h3 class="txt14b">Millimeter-wave Radar</h3>
				<p class="text">Mitsubishi Electric has developed millimeter-wave radar that enables the collection of data such as vehicle speed, yaw rate and distance to the vehicle ahead. This data is utilized in driving support systems including collision avoidance systems, adaptive cruise control and automated driving. Compared to other types of sensors, millimeter-wave radar provides the benefit of stable detection of targets even in inclement conditions such as rain or snow.</p>
				<div class="more"><a href="/bu/automotive/products/safety_and_security/index.html">More information</a></div>
			</div>
		</div>
		<div class="section">
			<div class="thumb"><img src="images/thumb_power_steering.jpg" alt="Power Steering Actuators &amp; Motors" width="130" height="95" /></div>
			<div class="wrap">
				<h3 class="txt14b">Power Steering Actuators &amp; Motors</h3>
				<p class="text">Electric power steering (EPS) actuators and motors assist the driver in turning the steering wheel, and contribute to improved fuel efficiency. Mitsubishi Electric is a leader in the mass production of compact and lightweight EPS systems. An innovative control algorithm developed for power steering actuators offers improved handling as well.</p>
				<div class="more"><a href="/bu/automotive/products/enviroment/index.html">More information</a></div>
			</div>
		</div>
		<div class="section">
			<div class="thumb"><img src="images/thumb_actuators.jpg" alt="Turbocharger Actuators" width="130" height="95" /></div>
			<div class="wrap">
				<h3 class="txt14b">Turbocharger Actuators</h3>
				<p class="text">Pressure- and vacuum-driven actuators specially designed for turbocharged engines. Pressure-driven actuators come in multiple sizes, and vacuum-driven actuators are available with or without a position sensor. </p>
				<div class="more"><a href="/bu/automotive/products/enviroment2/env18.html">More information</a></div>
			</div>
		</div>
