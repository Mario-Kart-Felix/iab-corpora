	<h2 class="title">Ford Luxury Car</h2>
	
	<div id="mini_cooper_post_top">

	<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;"><span>There is hundreds of <a title="Ford" href="http://www.malaysiaminilover.com/cars/ford" target="_blank">Ford</a> <a title="cars" href="http://www.malaysiaminilover.com" target="_blank">cars</a> were built over decades and among these hundreds, we find the best top five Ford luxury cars. Many Ford luxury cars have been built in the recent years although <a title="Ford Cars" href="http://www.malaysiaminilover.com/ford-cars" target="_blank">Ford cars</a> are always well-known for affordable for the average consumer since 1910s when the Ford Model T went into mass production. Check out what these cars are.<span id="more-4004"></span></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;"><a title="Lincoln" href="http://www.malaysiaminilover.com/cars/lincoln" target="_blank">Lincoln</a> is the luxury brand of Ford just like Acura for Honda and Lexus for Toyota. However, here we list the top five luxury car originally from Ford.</p>
<h3><strong><span>Ford Thunderbird</span></strong></h3>
<p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-8261" title="ford-thunderbird" src="http://www.malaysiaminilover.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/04/ford-thunderbird.jpg" alt="Ford Thunderbird" width="500" height="317" /></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;"><span>Ford Thunderbird of T-Bird was first introduced in 1955. It was eventually known as the personal luxury car when the model was introduced. The production went on from 1955 through 2005 over eleven model generations. It is a 2-door model that has everything you need to define luxury from inside out.</span></p>
<h3><strong><span>Ford Elite</span></strong></h3>
<p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-8262" title="ford-elite" src="http://www.malaysiaminilover.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/04/ford-elite.jpg" alt="1975 Ford Elite" width="400" height="244" /></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;"><span>The Elite is definitely one of the most luxury <a title="Muscle cars" href="http://www.malaysiaminilover.com/cars/muscle-cars" target="_blank">muscle cars</a> in the ‘70s. It was a more affordable luxury car than the Thunderbird in the ‘70s.</span></p>
<h3><strong><span>Ford Taurus</span></strong></h3>
<p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-8263" title="2012-ford-taurus" src="http://www.malaysiaminilover.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/04/2012-ford-taurus.jpg" alt="Ford Taurus 2012" width="500" height="284" /></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;"><span>Although you may not agree the first few Taurus generations is the luxury car, but you have to admit that Ford Taurus from 2010 onwards are truly a car with 6-packs luxury abs. Ford loaded the Taurus with many luxury features so that owners can get a smoother and more quiet ride in a safety way. When you see many people trade in their <a title="Mercedes-Benz" href="http://www.malaysiaminilover.com/cars/mercedes-benz" target="_blank">Mercedes-Benz</a>, <a title="BMW" href="http://www.malaysiaminilover.com/cars/bmw" target="_blank">BMW</a>, or an <a title="Audi" href="http://www.malaysiaminilover.com/cars/audi" target="_blank">Audi</a> for a Taurus, you know it has entered the luxury zone.</span></p>
<h3><strong><span>Ford GT40</span></strong></h3>
<p><img src="http://www.malaysiaminilover.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/ford-gt.jpg" alt="Ford GT40" /></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;"><span>No one will disagree if we say the Ford GT40 is one of the best Ford luxury <a title="Sports cars" href="http://www.malaysiaminilover.com/cars/sports-cars" target="_blank">sports cars</a> at all time. Production from 1964 to 1969 and it is still highly sought-after by many car enthusiasts today.</span></p>
<h3><strong><span>Ford Crown Victoria</span></strong></h3>
<p><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-8265" title="ford-crown-victoria-2011" src="http://www.malaysiaminilover.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/04/ford-crown-victoria-2011.jpg" alt="2011 Ford Crown Victoria" width="500" height="258" /></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;"><span>The brand new Ford Crown Victoria remains one of the last body-on-frame, real-wheel drive, V-8 powered sedans available. It is definitely a Ford luxury car due to its extreme durability and able to fit in six passengers.</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: justify;"><span>As one of the earliest <a title="Car brands" href="http://www.malaysiaminilover.com/cars/car-brands" target="_blank">car brands</a> in the world, Ford is constantly changing their design, quality, and price base on consumer demands (See <a title="Ford logo" href="http://www.malaysiaminilover.com/ford-logo" target="_blank">Ford Logo</a>). Ford offers vehicles and cars with a variety of sizes, performance, seating configurations, interior designs, as well as electric engine. It is no doubt that Ford has more than five luxury car including the Ford GTX1, Ford Mustang, Ford Edge as well as the classic Ford Model A. They are all Ford luxury cars.</span></p>
