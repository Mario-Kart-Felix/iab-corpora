<p>Mazda seems to be following a strange release schedule for information on the 2014 Mazda 3. Things seem to be coming out in batches &#8211; first we had a look at the hatchback but with a limited photo gallery. Then the sedan was announced but not unveiled. Then the sedan was leaked.</p>
<p>And now we finally have a new mega gallery of the hatchback, wearing a Californian plate on the rear. There&#8217;s no plate on the front to block that big grille, though Californian law actually calls for front license plates, although many US states don&#8217;t require front plates.</p>
