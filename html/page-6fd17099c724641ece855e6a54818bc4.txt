          <p class="calloutText"><a href="/request-information/index.php"><strong>Request More Information</strong></a> about the UNOH College of Applied Technologies</p>


          <p>The College of Applied Technologies offers associate degrees, diplomas and certificate programs.  These programs take from 36 weeks to 2 years to complete.  <!--Classes are Monday through Thursday in the morning, afternoon, and evening while early morning classes are Tuesday through Thursday.--></p>
            <p>Enrollment per class in the technical courses of the College of Applied Technologies is limited to 20 students.  Graduates of the College of Applied Technologies programs are trained to perform entry-level skills in their field(s) of endeavor. </p>
