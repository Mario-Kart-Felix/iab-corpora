				<h1 class="title">RTI</h1>
				<div class="entry">
					<p><a href="http://taeradio.com/episodes/archive/2794/who-needs-college.-why-trade-schools-are-driving-the-new-economy-denni/"><img class="alignright size-full wp-image-580" title="trade" src="http://www.rosedaletech.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/05/trade1.png" border="0" alt="" width="222" height="151" /></a><br />
Our high energy, hands on atmosphere includes:</p>
<ul>
<li>A spacious shop area with over 20 automotive lifts, and multiple brake lathe and alignment machines.</li>
<li>A two story house frame containing sections for both commercial and residential electrical work, as well as training labs for everything from motor repair to PLC’s.</li>
<li>Multiple diesel trainer engines, heavy truck lifts, as well as diagnostic tools.</li>
</ul>
<p>Rosedale provides the opportunity for you to earn the licenses and certifications necessary for your field:</p>
<ul>
<li><a href="/automotive-technician/" title="Automotive Pittsburgh">Auto Mechanic School Pittsburgh</a> – PA state inspection and enhanced emissions inspection certification; ASE certification; refrigerant recovery certification</li>
<li><a href="/diesel-technicians/" title="Diesel Certification Pittsburgh">Diesel School Pittsburgh</a> – Class A CDL, PA safety inspection certification;  ASE certification</li>
<li><a href="/electrician/" title="Electrical Technician School">Electrical Technician School</a> &#8211; scissor lift and aerial lift training, as well as OSHA certifications</li>
<li><a href="/hvac/" title="HVA Training School">HVAC Training School</a> &#8211; hands-on training that will allow graduates to work in residential and commercial applications.</li>
</ul>
<p>Training at Rosedale qualifies for Government backed programs to help students who qualify, finance their tuition.  We make the process quick, easy and we’ll help every step of the way. Rosedale offers continuous job placement assistance.  Whether you are looking for a part time job while attending school, or that “perfect fit” after graduation, our placement department prepares you for the interview process.</p>
