		<h1 class="entry-title">Minivans are Back in Style with the New Chrysler Town &amp;&nbsp;Country</h1>

				<div class="entry-meta">
						<span class="sep">Posted on </span><a href="http://allstarautomotivenews.com/2013/06/05/minivans-are-back-in-style-with-the-new-chrysler-town-country/" title="2:19 pm" rel="bookmark"><time class="entry-date" datetime="2013-06-05T14:19:53+00:00">June 5, 2013</time></a><span class="by-author"> <span class="sep"> by </span> <span class="author vcard"><a class="url fn n" href="http://allstarautomotivenews.com/author/allstarautomotivela/" title="View all posts by All Star" rel="author">All Star</a></span></span>		</div><!-- .entry-meta -->

	<div class="entry-content">
		<p><a href="http://www.allstarautomotive.com/inventory/view/Make/Chrysler/Model/Town%20and%20Country/New/SortBy0/"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-878" alt="Town &amp; Country Exterior" src="http://allstarautomotivela.files.wordpress.com/2013/05/town-country-exterior.jpg?w=584"   /></a>A minivan wears several different hats for the families that own them. It’s a school bus, playroom, weekend road warrior, living room on wheels, and- wait for it- a stylish vehicle? Since when did stylish and minivan go in the same sentence? When you consider the new <a href="http://www.allstarautomotive.com/inventory/view/Make/Chrysler/Model/Town%20and%20Country/New/SortBy0/">2013 Chrysler Town &amp; Country S</a>, that doesn’t seem so bizarre.<span id="more-877"></span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.allstarautomotive.com/inventory/view/New/Make/Chrysler/">Chrysler</a> has been working on creating a new hip image with its &#8220;Imported from Detroit&#8221; slogan; its new star-studded advertising is also trying to inject some mojo into its minivan. <a href="http://www.allstarautomotive.com/inventory/view/New/Make/Chrysler/">Chrysler</a> has given the <a href="http://www.allstarautomotive.com/inventory/view/Make/Chrysler/Model/Town%20and%20Country/New/SortBy0/">Town &amp; Country S</a> a dose of attitude inside and out with a sport suspension, black chrome grille, a black step pad, 17-inch aluminum wheels with black pockets and blacked-out headlights. Inside, it&#8217;s more black-on-black: leather seats with gray contrast stitching, glossy black plastic trim on the instrument panel and steering wheel, a black headliner and a black center console.</p>
<p>So what type of moms and dads would go for a style-cramping, blinged-out, blacked-out minivan? &#8220;The <a href="http://www.allstarautomotive.com/inventory/view/Make/Chrysler/Model/Town%20and%20Country/New/SortBy0/">Town &amp; Country S</a> will appeal to a younger demographic that needs the functionality of a minivan, but who wants some modern style in their vehicle,&#8221; <a href="http://www.allstarautomotive.com/inventory/view/New/Make/Chrysler/">Chrysler</a> spokesman Patrick Hespen said.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.allstarautomotive.com/inventory/view/Make/Chrysler/Model/Town%20and%20Country/New/SortBy0/"><img class="aligncenter size-large wp-image-879" alt="Town &amp; Country interior" src="http://allstarautomotivela.files.wordpress.com/2013/05/town-country-interior.jpg?w=584&#038;h=365" width="584" height="365" /></a>The minivan segment could use a jolt, since overall sales are down. Through April, shoppers bought 164,583 new minivans, according to Automotive News, which is down 7.4% versus 2012; and it represents a pretty significant decline in minivan popularity. A year ago, one in 26 new cars sold was a minivan; today it&#8217;s one in 30. Shoppers clearly prefer large, three-row crossovers, which outsold minivans by more than 3 to 2 through April. Maybe with this new stylish <a href="http://www.allstarautomotive.com/inventory/view/Make/Chrysler/Model/Town%20and%20Country/New/SortBy0/">Town &amp; Country S</a>, shoppers will see the minivan in new and modern light!</p>
<p>Get more information on the <a href="http://www.allstarautomotive.com/inventory/view/Make/Chrysler/Model/Town%20and%20Country/New/SortBy0/">2013 Chrysler Town &amp; Country S</a> through the <a href="http://www.allstarautomotive.com/inventory/view/New/Make/Chrysler/">All Star Automotive website</a>, where you can also <a href="http://www.allstarautomotive.com/forms/test-drive?masterpage=car-dealer">schedule a test drive</a>!</p>
