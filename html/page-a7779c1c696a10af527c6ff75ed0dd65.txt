<img class="buffered" alt="Electric Vehicle Charging Solutions" align="left" src="images/electricvehiclechargingsolutions.jpg" />

Eaton offers a full family of reliable, responsible electric vehicle (EV) chargers for residential applications. Our established excellence in the automotive and electrical distribution and control industries allows us to provide a wide range of innovative EV charging solutions to suit your individual needs. In addition, the Eaton Certified Contractor Network (ECCN) can provide turnkey services, from design to installation.<br><br><br>

<b>Features</b><br><br>

<strong>Level 1 Charger</strong></p>
<ul style="display: block;"><li>This 120 Vac charging station provides an economical and versatile EV charging solution</li></ul>

<p><strong><br>Level 2 Charger</strong></p>
<ul style="display: block;"><li>Providing 240 Vac, this charging station replenishes an EV's battery five times faster than with the vehicle's cord set</li></ul>

<p><strong><br>Universal Unit</strong></p>
<ul style="display: block;"><li>This 120 Vac allows up to four EVs to be charged at the same time</li></ul>


<p><strong><br>Simulator</strong></p>
<ul style="display: block;"><li>Eaton's EV Simulator enables installers to immediately test the functionality of the EVSE on site during installation</li>
