			}

		function readCookie(name) {
			var nameEQ = name + "=";
			var ca = document.cookie.split(';');
			for(var i=0;i < ca.length;i++) {

				<h1>Hybrid Car</h1>
			</div>
				<p class="module-description">
					A hybrid car combines a conventional internal combustion engine propulsion system with an electric propulsion system. With this class of car, the electric power train component is intended to achieve either better fuel economy than a conventional vehicle, or better performance. A variety of hybrid cars exist, and the degree to which they function as electric vehicles can vary greatly. The most common form is the hybrid electric car, although hybrid electric trucks (pickups and tractors) and buses also exist.				</p>
