				<h1 style="text-align: left;">One of the world&#8217;s greatest collections of racing sports cars</h1>
<h2><a href="http://simeonemuseum.org/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/CarsWeb.jpg"><img class="aligncenter" title="CarsWeb" alt="" src="http://simeonemuseum.org/wp-content/uploads/2011/06/CarsWeb.jpg" width="650" height="96" /></a><br />
<blockquote class="alignleft">The first race was conceived when the second car was built.</p></blockquote>
</h2>
<h4 style="text-align: left;">From the dawn of the automotive era, racing has been used to demonstrate the superiority of one manufacturer over another. It is this “Spirit of Competition” that we celebrate at the Simeone Foundation Automotive Museum.</h4>
<h4 style="text-align: left;">Assembled by renowned neurosurgeon Dr. Frederick Simeone over a span of 50 years, the Museum collection contains over 60 of the rarest and most significant racing sports cars ever built. The Simeone Automotive Museum was chosen &#8220;Museum of the Year&#8221; by the International Historic Motoring Awards in London.</h4>
<h4 class="aligncenter" title="Linebreak">This Web site details the various cars in the exhibit, as well as information on events at the Museum. We extend our invitation for you to visit us soon.</h4>
<p><a href="http://simeonemuseum.org/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/StationaryLogo3.jpg"><img class="aligncenter  wp-image-1238" title="Thin MofY Banner" alt="" src="http://simeonemuseum.org/wp-content/uploads/2012/01/StationaryLogo3-1024x102.jpg" width="601" height="58" /></a></p>
