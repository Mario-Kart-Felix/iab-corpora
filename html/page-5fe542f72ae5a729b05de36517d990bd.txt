<p>The <a href="/Vapid" title="Vapid">Vapid</a> <b>Minivan</b> is a four-door minivan in <a href="/Grand_Theft_Auto_IV" title="Grand Theft Auto IV">Grand Theft Auto IV</a> and <a href="/Grand_Theft_Auto_V" title="Grand Theft Auto V">Grand Theft Auto V</a>. It replaces its previous counterpart, the <a href="/Blista" title="Blista">Blista</a>, featured in <a href="/Grand_Theft_Auto_III" title="Grand Theft Auto III">Grand Theft Auto III</a> and <a href="/Grand_Theft_Auto:_Liberty_City_Stories" title="Grand Theft Auto: Liberty City Stories">Grand Theft Auto: Liberty City Stories</a>. After collecting all thirty cars for <a href="/Stevie" title="Stevie">Stevie</a> he agrees to purchase cars from <a href="/Niko_Bellic" title="Niko Bellic">Niko Bellic</a>, with the Minivan fetching <a href="/Money" title="Money">$</a>2,000 in perfect condition.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Design">Design</span></h2>
The Minivan is modeled after the Fourth Generation Dodge Caravan, although its lower ground clearance and sleeker design make it look more regal. The rear of the Minivan resembles that of the <a href=" http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mazda_MPV" class="extiw" title="wp:Mazda MPV">Second Generation Mazda MPV</a>. It can be <figure class="thumb tright thumbinner" style="width:182px;"><a href="http://images3.wikia.nocookie.net/__cb20110404003242/gtawiki/images/c/ce/Minivan_badges.png" class="image"><img alt="Minivan badges" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" width="180" height="90" class="thumbimage lzy lzyPlcHld" data-image-name="Minivan badges.png" data-image-key="Minivan_badges.png" data-src="http://images4.wikia.nocookie.net/__cb20110404003242/gtawiki/images/thumb/c/ce/Minivan_badges.png/180px-Minivan_badges.png" onload="if(typeof ImgLzy==&quot;object&quot;){ImgLzy.load(this)}" /><noscript><img alt="Minivan badges" src="http://images4.wikia.nocookie.net/__cb20110404003242/gtawiki/images/thumb/c/ce/Minivan_badges.png/180px-Minivan_badges.png" width="180" height="90" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Minivan badges.png" data-image-key="Minivan_badges.png" /></noscript></a><a href="/File:Minivan_badges.png" class="internal sprite details magnify" title="View photo details"></a><figcaption class="thumbcaption">Badging on the Vapid Minivan.<div class="picture-attribution"><img src="http://images4.wikia.nocookie.net/__cb2/common/avatars/thumb/c/c1/3324597.png/20px-3324597.png" width="16" height="16" class="avatar" alt="Ultorultor" />Added by <a href="/User:Ultorultor">Ultorultor</a></div></figcaption></figure>found with a roof spoiler or luggage rails (although both cases feature a "Minivan Sport" badge on the rear liftgate). The Minivan is the basis for the GTA IV <a href="/Cabby" title="Cabby">Cabby</a>, which has its own "Minivan Cabby" lift-gate badge. The van may come with a roof-mounted luggage rack or a rear spoiler.
The Minivan is badged as both a <a href="/Vapid" title="Vapid">Vapid</a> vehicle and, in the case of the Cabby, a <a href="/Schyster" title="Schyster">Schyster</a> one, it may also suggest a partnership between Vapid and Schyster, although <a href="/Schyster" title="Schyster">Schyster</a> is most likely partnered with <a href="/Bravado" title="Bravado">Bravado</a> like Chrysler and Dodge in reality. It may suggest a larger mother company like <a href="http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/General_Motors" class="extiw exitstitial" title="wikipedia:General Motors">General Motors</a> or <a href=" http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fiat" class="extiw" title="wp:Fiat">Fiat</a> (which also owns the Chrysler Group, including the Dodge brand) which produces the same vehicles under different brands. Alternatively, the Cabby's "Limited Vapid Design" badging suggests that the Cabby could be manufactured under licence by Schyster, or could be a rebadge of the Vapid Minivan.<figure class="thumb tright thumbinner" style="width:182px;"><a href="http://images4.wikia.nocookie.net/__cb20121114185755/gtawiki/images/5/53/Minivan-GTAV-trailer-damaged-back.png" class="image"><img alt="Minivan-GTAV-trailer-damaged-back" src="data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIABAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAEALAAAAAABAAEAQAICTAEAOw%3D%3D" width="180" height="162" class="thumbimage lzy lzyPlcHld" data-image-name="Minivan-GTAV-trailer-damaged-back.png" data-image-key="Minivan-GTAV-trailer-damaged-back.png" data-src="http://images3.wikia.nocookie.net/__cb20121114185755/gtawiki/images/thumb/5/53/Minivan-GTAV-trailer-damaged-back.png/180px-Minivan-GTAV-trailer-damaged-back.png" onload="if(typeof ImgLzy==&quot;object&quot;){ImgLzy.load(this)}" /><noscript><img alt="Minivan-GTAV-trailer-damaged-back" src="http://images3.wikia.nocookie.net/__cb20121114185755/gtawiki/images/thumb/5/53/Minivan-GTAV-trailer-damaged-back.png/180px-Minivan-GTAV-trailer-damaged-back.png" width="180" height="162" class="thumbimage" data-image-name="Minivan-GTAV-trailer-damaged-back.png" data-image-key="Minivan-GTAV-trailer-damaged-back.png" /></noscript></a><a href="/File:Minivan-GTAV-trailer-damaged-back.png" class="internal sprite details magnify" title="View photo details"></a><figcaption class="thumbcaption">A Minivan in GTA V's second trailer after being hit by a <a href="/Cheetah" title="Cheetah">Cheetah</a>.<div class="picture-attribution"><img src="http://images3.wikia.nocookie.net/common/avatars/thumb/a/af/4210063.png/20px-4210063.png" width="16" height="16" class="avatar" alt="WildBrick142" />Added by <a href="/User:WildBrick142">WildBrick142</a></div></figcaption></figure>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Performance">Performance</span></h2>
<p>Under the bonnet of the Minivan lies a reasonably torqueful 24-valve V6 engine. Though the output of this smooth-yet-potent engine is far from good, the sheer weight and aerodynamic properties of the Minivan results in a double-figures 0-60mph time and a lowly 125mph (201km/h) maximum. The firmness of the suspension and the rather low ride height give the Minivan ample support and stability when cornering, though under-steer is often noticeable. Braking is good, and the ABS works well on all models. In the event of a crash, the bodywork tends to stay very well in tact, although driver safety isn't exactly class-leading in high-speed accidents. Pedestrian safety is not great; the shape of the car suggests that if involved in a crash with a pedestrian, there is a high risk of being pulled under the wheels and being crushed to death.
</p>
