<p><ul class='smallbullet'><li>Electric car, bus and truck systems</li> <li>Serial/parallel/light hybrid electrical systems for cars/buses/trucks</li> <li>Hydrogen fuel cells</li> <li>Alternative source systems of in-vehicle electricity production</li> <li>High power/energy batteries, battery/energy management and charging systems</li> <li>Electronics for electrical machine/hybrid engine control systems (hard and soft)</li> <li>Electronic architectures and dedicated automotive components</li> <li>Electrical machines for automotive propulsion and traction</li> <li>Braking systems for electrical propulsion and traction systems</li> <li>Specific electrical air conditioning and heating systems for electric vehicles</li> <li>Environmental/cost efficiency, energy savings algorithms</li> <li> "Zero emission" range extension solutions</li> <li>Dedicated security and safety systems</li> <li>Electromagnetic compatibility issues for EHV</li> <li>Social, economic and environmental issues associated with EHV</li></ul></ul><a name='moredesc' id='moredesc'></a><br /><a href='#moredesc' class='show_hide_2'>More on this journal...</a><br /><div class='slidingDiv_2'><strong>Objectives</strong><p>The objectives of IJEHV are to</p>

<li>help to understand the strategic evolutions of industrial policies and international technological tendencies to allow a global coordination of suppliers and manufacturers</li></ul>

<p class="indented"><b>A few essentials for publishing in this journal</b></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>		
	
<ul style="margin-left:14px;" class="orangetriangle"><li>Submitted articles should not have been previously published or be currently under consideration for publication elsewhere. 
</li><li>Conference papers may only be submitted if the paper has been completely re-written (taken to mean more than 50%) and the author has cleared any necessary permissions with the copyright owner if it has been previously copyrighted.</li><li>All our articles are refereed through a double-blind process.
</li><li>All authors must declare they have read and agreed to the content of the submitted article. A full statement of our <a href='http://www.inderscience.com/www/authorethics.pdf' target='_blank'>Ethical Guidelines for Authors</a> is available.
