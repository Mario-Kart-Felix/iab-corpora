<div class="etw_secetion2">
<img src="product/4-1-1-1.jpg" alt="" class="tu2" />
<p>This off road vehicle tire is suitable for working in the field. As the cross country vehicle is driven by both front and rear wheels, it mainly runs on poor road, like in the desert, mud, weak soil and on other unpaved road so that it requires the tire with high trafficable performance. Therefore, our product adopts widened rim, cross section and takes actions to reduce the diameter of the rim so as to enlarge the contact surface, reducing the stress to contact with the ground. The product usually has low barometric pressure or can be adjusted on air pressure according to the road conditions. </p>
<p>Our off road vehicle tire can be supplied with varied types, such as all terrain off road type, sports car off road type and all season SUV off road type, etc. Therein, the all terrain off road type has relatively wide application. It is more compatible on design than common highway product. Due to the large pattern with larger tread void, this product has better durability and traction on unpaved road than the highway product, although its highway performance decreases to some extent. As for the driver who takes the vehicle as common transportation tool and drives occasionally in the field, the all terrain off road tire is a perfect choice.</p>
