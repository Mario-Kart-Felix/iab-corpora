												<p>Here is a Rare Legacy GT wagon in Black. Equipped with an Automatic transmission with steering wheel controls.</p>
<p>This one has a Borla Cat back Exhaust system, Cobb Catted Down pipe and Cobb access port running the stage two Map.</p>
<p>Has had recent brakes including rotors replaced front and rear, as well as new tires and an alignment. The timing belt, water pump and all timing components have been replaced as well. Mileage is 120k.</p>
<p>Other options include heated leather seats, power moon roof, and Jazzy engineering Iphone / Ipod jack.</p>
<p>If you are in the market for one of the best looking Subaru they ever built you really should come have a look at this car!</p>
