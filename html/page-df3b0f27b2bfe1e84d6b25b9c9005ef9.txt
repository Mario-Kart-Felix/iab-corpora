				<div style="margin-bottom:5px;text-align:left;"><strong>Terms to Know About Automobile Electric Services</strong></div> <p> For most people, car repairs and maintenance are best left to a professional mechanic. In a situation where an electric component of your vehicle requires repair, a mechanics’ specialized tools, training and computer diagnostics system will be required. Your car’s heater, radio, automatic windows and locks, tail lights and more are operated by electricity. Here are some terms to know about automobile electric services. </p>

<li> Alternator – The alternator is a very important electromechanical part of your car. This part converts energy generated from your car into electricity to charge the car battery and operate different parts of your vehicle. 

<li> Voltage Regulator – The voltage regulator converts the electric current levels created by the car battery to safe levels for operation. This ensures that fuses, wires, spark plugs and other parts are not damaged by high levels of electricity.

<li> Ignition System – By turning the key in the start switch of your gasoline fueled car you are turning on the ignition system. There are many parts to this system including a distributor, spark plugs, contractors, the battery, capacitor and more. In the simplest terms, the parts work together to create an electric arc from the spark plugs which ignite a fuel and air mixture that turns on your engine.   

<li> Relay Switch – A relay switch converts electricity to a higher current that can be spread and used throughout your automobile. Parts often operated by a relay switch include the radio, lights and more. 

<li> Signaling Equipment – Many electric repairs on your car will concern your signaling equipment which includes your blinkers, turn signals, tail lights and more. These lights are powered by a series of wire connections which will require occasional maintenance to check for corrosion.
