					<h1 class="entry-title">Roadside Assistance: Available to Mercury Auto Clients with Collision!</h1>

					<div class="entry-meta">
						<span class="meta-prep meta-prep-author">Posted on</span> <a href="http://www.alandaleblog.com/2011/02/10/roadside-assistance-available-to-mercury-auto-clients-with-collision/" title="10:50 am" rel="bookmark"><span class="entry-date">February 10, 2011</span></a> <span class="meta-sep">by</span> <span class="author vcard"><a class="url fn n" href="http://www.alandaleblog.com/author/courtney/" title="View all posts by Courtney">Courtney</a></span>					</div><!-- .entry-meta -->

					<div class="entry-content">
						<p><a href="http://www.alandaleblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/02/logo_mercury_main_top.bmp"><img class="size-full wp-image-764 alignleft" title="Mercury Insurance" src="http://www.alandaleblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/02/logo_mercury_main_top.bmp" alt="" /></a>Triple A seems to be popular for their convenient roadside assistance, but we&#8217;ve found that most of our clients don’t realize our carriers offer similar programs. The policies differ from company to company, but <a href="http://www.mercuryinsurance.com/insurance-products/auto-insurance.html">Mercury</a> offers Roadside Assistance automatically to any of their auto policy holders that have collision coverage on their vehicle. The fact that roadside assistance was automatically included with these policies was news to me! Realizing this is a little known fact, we wanted to make certain none of our clients with this automatic coverage were left in the dark. <a href="http://www.alandaleblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/02/MB900386119.jpg"><img class="size-full wp-image-763 alignright" title="Roadside Assistance is incredibly helpful!" src="http://www.alandaleblog.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/02/MB900386119.jpg" alt="" width="192" height="192" /></a>What could be better? Its not even an additional fee! Its already part of your policy if you have collision. The Mercury program includes assistance if you lock you keys in your car, provides towing if your car dies while on the road, offers jump starts if your battery dies, and assistance if you suddenly have a flat tire among other things. Especially helpful about the towing feature is that Mercury pays for up to $70 of the tow, and they pay for it up front while other carriers often require the client to pay for the service initially, and later reimburse the client. Mercury also doesn’t require the client to find a towing service on their own, rather Mercury finds a company and dispatches the tow themselves. Additionally, all the policy holder need do in such an event is call the claims number listed on their insurance card and follow the prompts that will direct them to roadside assistance. This program is so convenient! And if you already have a Mercury policy will collision coverage, it is available to you today! Isn’t it a relief to know you will be taken care of if something doesn’t go according to plan? We certainly think so. <img src='http://www.alandaleblog.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/icon_smile.gif' alt=':-)' class='wp-smiley' />  If you are interested in learning more about this feature or purchasing such coverage <a href="http://www.alandale.com/personal-insurance/auto-insurance/">contact us </a>today! Your safety and peace of mind is our top priority.</p>
