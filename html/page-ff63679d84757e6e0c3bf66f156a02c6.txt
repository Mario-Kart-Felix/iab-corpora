<h2><strong class="font-size-14px">Optional Auto Protection Plan</strong></h2>
<br />
At Direct Auto, we know unexpected events can disrupt lives and financial security. That's why we offer additional Auto Protection plans, including roadside assistance, to cover such emergencies at low, affordable rates. We'll do right by you so you can do right by your family and your budget.
<hr style="margin: 10px 0px 0px; height: 1px;" class="span-13 lighter-gray" />
</div>
<div style="margin-top: 10px; margin-bottom: 2px;" class="span-13 last left justify">
<h2><strong class="font-size-14px">Auto Protection Plans</strong></h2>
<br />
Whether you're driving long or short distances, there's always a chance that an accident or a mishap could occur, leaving your budget in turmoil. In order to protect you from bills suddenly piling up, our Auto Protection plans cover you and related roadside assistance expenses. Read below for more on what these plans offer.<br />
<br />
<ul>
    <li style="padding-bottom: 5px;" class="listd"><strong>Emergency Protection Plan (EPP) -</strong> Although details for each plan vary by state, the EPP generally pays various amounts toward miscellaneous expenses such as ambulance assistance, medical fees, and personal effects. In some cases, these auto protection plans also assist with rental reimbursement and emergency cash. For complete details about what the EPP offers in your state, please call one of our representatives at 1-877-GO DIRECT <strong>(1-877-463-4732)</strong>.</li>
    <li style="padding-bottom: 2px;" class="listd"><strong>Direct Roadside Assistance -</strong> Membership to our roadside assistance plan insures you get immediate dispatches for towing, mechanical first aid, jump starts, fluids delivery, and lost keys or lockout. This program also offers a theft reward and mapping service.</li>
</ul>
<hr style="margin: 10px 0px 0px; height: 1px;" class="span-13 lighter-gray" />
</div>
<div style="margin-top: 10px; margin-bottom: 2px;" class="span-13 last left justify">
<h2><strong class="font-size-14px">Term Life Insurance</strong></h2>
<br />
Insuring your life will help keep your family's financial health safe from the threat of unexpected medical bills, funeral expenses and loss of income. This is no doubt an important policy to have, and our goal is to make it accessible to you for less than 40 cents a day. Some benefits of getting life insurance with Direct include:<br />
<br />
<ul>
    <li style="padding-bottom: 2px;" class="listd">Up to $25,000 of coverage.</li>
    <li style="padding-bottom: 2px;" class="listd">No medical examination required.</li>
    <li style="padding-bottom: 2px;" class="listd">Guaranteed renewable for three years.</li>
    <li style="padding-bottom: 2px;" class="listd">Flexible payment options available.</li>
</ul>
<br />
If you're interested in learning more about our Auto Protection plans, both Emergency Protection Plan and Roadside Assistance, or our term life insurance policies, please contact us for complete information at 1-877-GO-DIRECT <strong>(1-877-463-4732)</strong>.</div>
