<p>Luxury car is everyone&#8217;s dream. Did you know best luxury car today? There are several brands of luxury cars that dominate the list of top <a href="http://writewhat.com/luxury-car-brand/"><strong>10 luxury car</strong></a> in the world. Here is a list of luxury car brands in the world.<br />
<strong><br />
10. Koenigsegg</strong><br />
<div id="attachment_1867" class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 510px"><a href="http://writewhat.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/Luxury-car-Koenigsegg.jpg"><img src="http://writewhat.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/Luxury-car-Koenigsegg.jpg" alt="Luxury car Koenigsegg Top 10 Luxury Car Brands in the World" title="Luxury-car-Koenigsegg" width="500" height="252" class="size-full wp-image-1867" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Koenigsegg</p></div><br />
Koenigsegg ranked tenth as a luxury car brand. The company was founded in 1994 in Sweden by Christian von Koenigsegg, with the aim to create world-class supercar. In 2006, the Koenigsegg CCX production started, the machine is made specifically for the car alone. CCX legal to drive on public roads in many countries, including in United States.</p>
<p><strong>9. Shelby Supercars (SSC)</strong><br />
<div id="attachment_1869" class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 510px"><a href="http://writewhat.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/Luxury-car-Shelby-SuperCars-SSC.jpg"><img src="http://writewhat.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/Luxury-car-Shelby-SuperCars-SSC.jpg" alt="Luxury car Shelby SuperCars SSC Top 10 Luxury Car Brands in the World" title="Luxury-car-Shelby-SuperCars-SSC" width="500" height="333" class="size-full wp-image-1869" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Shelby Supercars</p></div><br />
Shelby Supercars occupies the ninth position as a luxury car brand. The company was founded by Herod Shelby. Shelby cars made with high detail.  One example can be seen on the SSC Ultimate Aero.</p>
<p><strong>8. Maybach</strong><br />
<div id="attachment_1870" class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 510px"><a href="http://writewhat.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/Luxury-car-Maybach.jpg"><img src="http://writewhat.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/Luxury-car-Maybach.jpg" alt="Luxury car Maybach Top 10 Luxury Car Brands in the World" title="oben: 06A4158unten: 06C4225_46" width="500" height="500" class="size-full wp-image-1870" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Maybach</p></div><br />
Occupies the eighth position as the Maybach luxury car brand. Maybach-Motorenbau GmbH is a company of luxury cars from Germany. Founded in 1909 by Wilhelm Maybach with his son, Karl Maybach as director. At first this company is a subsidiary of Luftschiffbau Zeppelin GmbH is also known as &#8220;Luftfahrzeug-Motorenbau GmbH&#8221; (which means it is building Aircraft Engine Company) until 1918. Currently, the Maybach is owned by Daimler AG and based in Stuttgart, Germany.</p>
<p><strong>7. Rolls-Royce</strong><br />
<div id="attachment_1871" class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 510px"><a href="http://writewhat.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/Luxury-car-Rolls-Royce.jpg"><img src="http://writewhat.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/Luxury-car-Rolls-Royce.jpg" alt="Luxury car Rolls Royce Top 10 Luxury Car Brands in the World" title="Luxury-car-Rolls-Royce" width="500" height="375" class="size-full wp-image-1871" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Rolls-Royce</p></div><br />
Rolls-Royce occupies the seventh position as the luxury car brand. Rolls-Royce Motor Cars Limited, is producing luxury cars, owned by BMW, which started delivery of a single model, the Phantom (ghost), in January 2003.<br />
<strong><br />
6. McLaren</strong><br />
<div id="attachment_1872" class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 510px"><a href="http://writewhat.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/Luxury-car-Mclaren.jpg"><img src="http://writewhat.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/Luxury-car-Mclaren.jpg" alt="Luxury car Mclaren Top 10 Luxury Car Brands in the World" title="Luxury-car-Mclaren" width="500" height="342" class="size-full wp-image-1872" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">McLaren</p></div><br />
McLaren is ranked sixth as a luxury car brand. In addition to being one of the best Formula 1 team today. McLaren is a manufacturer of luxury car with high performs.</p>
<p><strong>5. Bentley</strong><br />
<div id="attachment_1873" class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 510px"><a href="http://writewhat.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/Luxury-car-Bentley.jpg"><img src="http://writewhat.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/Luxury-car-Bentley.jpg" alt="Luxury car Bentley Top 10 Luxury Car Brands in the World" title="Luxury-car-Bentley" width="500" height="375" class="size-full wp-image-1873" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Bentley</p></div><br />
Bentley is ranked sixth as a luxury car brand. Bentley Motors Limited is the maker of luxury cars and a big sports car based in Britain. Bentley Motors was founded in England on January 18, 1919 by Walter Owen Bentley, known as WO Bentley or just &#8220;W.O. (1888-1971)&#8221;.</p>
<p><strong>4. Porsche</strong><br />
<div id="attachment_1874" class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 510px"><a href="http://writewhat.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/Luxury-car-Porsche.jpg"><img src="http://writewhat.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/Luxury-car-Porsche.jpg" alt="Luxury car Porsche Top 10 Luxury Car Brands in the World" title="Luxury-car-Porsche" width="500" height="287" class="size-full wp-image-1874" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Porsche</p></div><br />
Porsche was ranked fourth as a luxury car. Porsche (Porsche AG), is a German automobile manufacturer founded in 1931 by Ferdinand Porsche, the technician who created the first Volkswagen. The company is located in Zuffenhausen, a city district of Stuttgart.</p>
<p><strong>3. Buggati</strong><br />
<div id="attachment_1875" class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 510px"><a href="http://writewhat.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/Luxury-car-Bugatti.jpg"><img src="http://writewhat.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/Luxury-car-Bugatti.jpg" alt="Luxury car Bugatti Top 10 Luxury Car Brands in the World" title="Luxury-car-Bugatti" width="500" height="256" class="size-full wp-image-1875" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Buggati</p></div><br />
Buggati ranks third as a luxury car brand. Bugatti is a French car company. The company was founded by Ettore Bugatti was born in Italy, founded in the town of Molsheim in the Alsace region in France. Its main product is the famous car Bugatti Veyron.</p>
<p><strong>2. Lamborghini</strong><br />
<div id="attachment_1876" class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 510px"><a href="http://writewhat.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/Luxury-car-Lamborghini.jpg"><img src="http://writewhat.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/Luxury-car-Lamborghini.jpg" alt="Luxury car Lamborghini Top 10 Luxury Car Brands in the World" title="Luxury-car-Lamborghini" width="500" height="266" class="size-full wp-image-1876" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Lamborghini</p></div><br />
Lamborghini is ranked second as a luxury car brand. Automobili-Lamborghini SpA, commonly called the Lamborghini is a car maker in Italy. The company was founded by Ferruccio Lamborghini in 1963, with the aim to produce a grand tour car that can compete with established offerings from Marques such as Ferrari.</p>
<p><strong>1. Ferrari</strong><br />
<div id="attachment_1877" class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 510px"><a href="http://writewhat.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/Luxury-car-Ferrari.jpg"><img src="http://writewhat.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/11/Luxury-car-Ferrari.jpg" alt="Luxury car Ferrari Top 10 Luxury Car Brands in the World" title="Luxury-car-Ferrari" width="500" height="360" class="size-full wp-image-1877" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Ferrari</p></div><br />
Ferrari is the most popular <a href="http://writewhat.com/luxury-car-brand/"><strong>luxury car</strong></a> in the world. Ferrari is a manufacturer of racing cars and sports cars the Italian high-performance-based in Maranello, Italy. Founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1929, as &#8220;Scuderia Ferrari&#8221;, the company&#8217;s most famous luxury car today. Ferrari is ranked first as a luxury car.</p>
