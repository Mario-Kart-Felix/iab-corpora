<h1>Suzuki launches new SX4 Crossover</h1>
<h2>Updates and rebrands SX4 Sedan to SX4 Aero</h2>
<h6>Text:  / Photos: Suzuki Press</h6>
<h6>posted  May 05, 2010 01:38</h6></hgroup>
<article role="main"><div id="article-content">
<div class="addthis_toolbox addthis_default_style ">

<a class="addthis_counter addthis_pill_style"></a></div><p><img src='http://www.autoindustriya.com/images/posts/post1605.jpg' alt='Suzuki launches new SX4 Crossover image' width='640' /></p>Suzuki Philippines Inc. recently launched its newest car models with the introduction of the new Suzuki SX4 Crossover and the updated Suzuki SX4 Aero (sedan).<br />
<br />
The new SX4 Crossover is equipped with dynamic exterior features such as honeycomb type front grille, fender arch, roof rails, and 16" alloy wheels. Occupant safety is assured by a high rigidity body that utilizes TECT (Total Effective Control Technology). Active safety features include dual side airbags, and anti-lock brakes. While a spacious cargo area and passenger seat under tray complete the SX4 Crossover package.   <br />
<br />
The SX4 Sedan receives not only a restyling but a rebranding as the new Suzuki SX4 Aero featuring a sportier profile and a more aggressive front grille.  A rear spoiler with third brake light, 17" alloy wheels and rear disc brakes make this sedan a standout.<br />
<br />
Inside, the instrument cluster serves as a virtual information center with a fuel consumption gauge, fuel range and digital clock.  The audio system has been upgraded with an 8-speaker system, MP3 CD player and auxiliary input.  Audio controls are mounted on the leather-wrapped steering wheel for easy access. <br />
<br />
Both vehicles share the same M16A engine, a 1600cc 16-valve DOHC engine with variable-valve timing (VVT).  The engine delivers a maximum output of 110hp at 5,600rpm and maximum torque of 150Nm at 3,800rpm. It is mated to a 4-speed gate-type automatic transmission.<br />
<br />
Pricetag for the SX4 Aero is set at Php890,000; while the SX4 Crossover will sell for Php899,000.</div></article>
