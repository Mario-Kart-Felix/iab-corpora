			<p>Becker Automotive Design is the leader in luxury conversion vehicles. We pride ourselves in 
			creating true European luxury interiors that are as productive as they are beautiful. Becker's 
			Cadillac Escalade Mobile office and Mercedes-Benz Sprinter van Mobile Office allow you to work 
			on the road in a stress free environment, wasting no time as you travel to your destination. 
			With Becker's superior background in electronics and the use of Creston touch control systems, 
			Becker vehicles define easy to use, sophisticated technology.</p>
			
			<p>The Becker Mercedes-Benz Sprinter Van Conversion, named the JetVan&trade;, is in a class of its own. 
			Using innovations from the aviation industry, the JetVan&trade; gives you the comfort and feeling of 
			traveling in a private aircraft. With rear seating ranging from 4 to 7 passengers depending 
			upon the Sprinter chassis and floor plan</p>
		</div>
		
		<div class="large-6 columns">
			<p>selected, the Becker JetVan&trade; provides discretion with unmatched interior comfort and 
			leading edge productive capabilities. The Cadillac Escalade ESV Conversion uses the same 
			concepts and approaches as the JetVan&trade;, employing the smaller SUV Chassis. Perfect for principals 
			traveling in rear compartment privacy alone, or with one other VIP and up to two assistants.</p>
			
			<p>Utilizing the best in class from Mercedes-Benz and Cadillac, Becker conversion vehicles 
			present the world's safest and most reliable customized private limousines ever offered.
