				<h1 class="title">2011 Yo-Auto Hybrid Concept</h1> 	
                               <div class="wanci"> Posted at Sep 30th 2011</div>
					<p><a href="http://picsofcars.net/picsofcars/2011-yo-auto-hybrid-concept/2011-yo-auto-hybrid-concept-front-angle/" rel="attachment wp-att-1150"><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-1150" title="2011 Yo-Auto Hybrid Concept-Front Angle" src="http://picsofcars.net/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/2011-Yo-Auto-Hybrid-Concept-Front-Angle-575x307.jpg" alt="2011 Yo Auto Hybrid Concept" width="575" height="307" /></a></p>
<p>The Yo-car brand wants to change the reputation for being innovative. <span style="text-decoration: underline;"><em><strong><a title="2011 Yo-Auto Hybrid Concept" href="http://picsofcars.net/picsofcars/2011-yo-auto-hybrid-concept/">2011 Yo-Auto Hybrid Concept</a></strong></em></span> brand has shown at the IAA 2011 in Frankfurt, with which vehicles they want to modernize the Russian auto industry. An electric motor and possibly a gas-engine drive the study. The construction of a small series is feasible. Visually, the study is quite an eye-catching aerodynamic. The highlight is the new sliding doors that open upward. Appear realistic but the gas-hybrid vehicles, which are subjected to in the fall of first crash tests and will be sold in the second half of 2012. The good four feet Yo-crossover and the 4.20-meter-long truck ё fourgon have plastic bodies and a hybrid of electric motor and gasoline or LPG (61 hp).</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>The consumption will be 3.5 liters, maximum speed 130 km / h. Range: 700 miles. Instead of batteries, super capacitors are on board. They are smaller and lighter, can be charged extremely quickly. The service life is virtually unlimited. Disadvantage: limited memory &#8211; but should be enough for hybrid cars. Meanwhile, both cars can be booked in advance. Price: 8000-10500 Euro.</p>
