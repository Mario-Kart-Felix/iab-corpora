<p>Last week, the new 2014 Mazda3 five-door hatchback was introduced.<br />And now they’ve released the first official photos of the sedan.<br />The car inherits the same lines as its big sister, the fully redesigned and revamped Mazda6. And that’s very good news.<br />Just like the Mazda CX-5 and Mazda6, the 2014 Mazda3 sedan has been infused with the brand’s exclusive Kodo style signature, which includes a flashier grille that the public seems to prefer.</p>
<p><strong>A Skyactiv sedan</strong><br />As with the hatchback, the sedan offered in North America will be available with your choice of two Skyactiv-G four-cylinder engines.<br />These engines – a 2.0-litre and a 2.5-litre, both of which have been taken from the Mazda CX-5 – produce 155 and 184 horsepower, respectively.<br />Both engines can be paired with either a six-speed manual or automatic transmission.</p>
<p>The car also comes with the i-ELOOP system which uses a capacitor to store energy recovered from vehicle deceleration.<br />The new 2014 Mazda3 sedan and hatchback will make their public debuts in September at the Frankfurt International Auto Show.<br />They are expected to hit dealerships at the beginning of fall 2013.</p>					</div>
