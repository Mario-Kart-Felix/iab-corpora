							<h2>Business</h2>
							<p>The City of San Diego understands the important role businesses play in the local economy and quality of life.  The City offers an array of business development programs to assist entrepreneurs in starting and growing businesses.</p>
							<a href="/brc/"><img src="graphics/tilebrc.png" width="200" height="50" border="0" alt="Business Resource Center"></a>
						</div>
						<div class="mainLeftCol">
							<p>The Office of Small Business provides entrepreneurs with <a href="/economic-development/business/starting/">start-up information</a>, development programs, and <a href="/economic-development/business/starting/ambassador.shtml">advocacy and referral services</a> to enhance their success and promote a business friendly climate.   </p>
							<p>The Mayor&rsquo;s Office of Economic Growth Services implements economic development programs for the Mayor in order to create and retain jobs and taxable investment in the City of San Diego.  </p>
							<p>Business incentives and benefits provide companies with an edge in the marketplace to expand and prosper.  These include: <a href="/economic-development/industry/enterprisezones.shtml">Enterprise Zones</a>; <a href="/economic-development/sandiego/trade/tradezone.shtml">Foreign Trade Zones</a>; <a href="/environmental-services/recycling/business/market.shtml">Recycling Market Development Zones</a>; <a href="/redevelopment-agency/projarea.shtml">Redevelopment Project Areas</a>; and <a href="/economic-development/business/starting/improvement.shtml">Storefront Improvement Program</a>. </p>
							
						</div>
						<div class="mainRightCol">
							<p><a href="/economic-development/business/financing/index.shtml">Financing resources</a> include small business loans for small to mid-size business owners with expanding businesses that require capital, but are unable to meet the terms of traditional banks.</p>
							<p>The City also contracts with businesses which can provide quality products and services at competitive prices.  <a href="/purchasing/vendor/index.shtml">Interested companies may register with the City</a> for <a href="/purchasing/bids-contracts/index.shtml">contracting opportunities</a> and to participate in various <a href="/economic-development/business/seeking.shtml">programs designed to increase opportunities and assist businesses in contracting with the City</a>.</p>
							<p>Additionally, the City of San Diego not only assists individual businesses, but also focuses on creating partnerships with other <a href="/economic-development/resources/startup.shtml">service providers</a>, strengthening <a href="/economic-development/resources/commerce.shtml">business organizations</a>, and improving <a href="/economic-development/about/bids.shtml">commercial neighborhoods</a> throughout San Diego.  </p>
						</div>
