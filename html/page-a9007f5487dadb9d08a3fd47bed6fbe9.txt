<a name="Kinds of Motorcycle Wheels include the following:"></a><h4>Kinds of Motorcycle Wheels include the following:</h4>
<table width="100%" cellpadding="0" cellspacing="0"><tr><td class="normal"><strong>Cast motorcycle wheels</strong><br /><br /><ul><li>Cast motorcycle wheels feature a cast construction.</li><li>They are strong and rigid, allowing for excellent handling.</li><li>They are tough enough to bear a great load capacity.</li><li>They are commonly used with tubeless tires.</li><li>They can be used with both straight runs and curve stresses.</li><li>They are maintenance-free.</li><li>They are usually made of aluminum, but the more expensive ones are made of magnesium.</li></ul><strong><br />Spoked motorcycle wheels</strong><br /><br /><ul><li>Spoked motorcycle wheels feature a spoke construction.</li><li>They have smoother surfaces and better detail than cast motorcycle wheels.</li><li>They are extremely light, allowing for excellent control and mobility.</li><li>They usually have a diameter of 17 inches.</li><li>They can be used with both straight runs and curve stresses.</li><li>They are usually made of aluminum, but the more expensive ones are made of magnesium.</li><li>They are high maintenance.</li></ul><br /></td></tr></table><p align="right"><a href="motorcycle_wheels_recommended.html" class="underline" target="_blank"><img src="../images/see_recommended_products.gif" border="0" title="see recommended products" alt="see recommended products"></a></p><p><script type="text/javascript"><!--
