      <h2>Horseless Carriage Carriers, Auto Transport &amp; Storage</h2>
<p>
<a href="http://www.horselesscarriage.com/wp-content/gallery/autos/hcpic-003.jpg" title="auto moving" rel="lightbox[singlepic107]">
	<img class="ngg-singlepic ngg-right" src="http://www.horselesscarriage.com/wp-content/gallery/cache/107__300x220_hcpic-003.jpg" alt="auto moving" title="auto moving" />
</a>
Horseless Carriage Carriers, Inc. has provided the automotive community with immaculate, dependable, enclosed auto transport for antique &amp; classic automobiles and new car stock, for over 25 years.</p>
<p>Catering to the needs of the individual enthusiast for transportation to and from car shows is what put the company on the map. A stellar list of customers, including many of the top museums and private collections of the world, as well as film, TV, sports, and recording stars, have come to regard Horseless Carriage as the only way to move priceless automotive cargo.</p>
<p>Individual and personal attention has made the company grow to thirty units, making our fully insured auto shipping fleet able to handle the largest car shipping jobs offered, but small enough to provide the one-on-one care your auto shipping needs deserve.</p>
<h3>Services Details &amp; Resources: Auto Shipping / Enclosed Auto Transport</h3>
<p><strong>Horseless Carriage Carriers</strong> is an auto shipping company that specializes in the safe door to door transport of cars, trucks, fleets and military vehicles nationwide. Not only are we expert auto movers, but we are contracted by many automotive manufacturers (Lotus &amp; Maserati to name a few) as their auto shipping experts. Our Enclosed auto transports are state of the art, fulling insured &amp; enclosed car shipping vehicles. Our fleet numbers 30 such vehicles, all driven by safe well-trained drivers. Our Car Movers, like the auto transport pictured above, are fully enclosed vehicles. Our auto shipping trailers are designed to keep your car inside and safe, and the weather outside where it belongs. We are a car shipping company that can handle any sized project, from shipping single autos to entire car fleets nationwide.</p>
<h3>Horseless Carriage Carriers &#8212; The Enclosed Auto Shipping Experts</h3>
<p>
<a href="http://www.horselesscarriage.com/wp-content/gallery/autos/hcpic-001.jpg" title="auto shipping" rel="lightbox[singlepic106]">
	<img class="ngg-singlepic ngg-left" src="http://www.horselesscarriage.com/wp-content/gallery/cache/106__300x220_hcpic-001.jpg" alt="auto shipping" title="auto shipping" />
</a>
Horseless Carriage Carriers has been an expert car shipping company for over 25 years. Our dependable enclosed car transports have moved priceless antique and collectible automobiles for some of the nation&#8217;s most revered auto shows and private collectors alike. Our auto transports are some of the most well-design specialized vehicles available, made for the sole purpose of moving your cherished vehicle.</p>

For further information about our <strong>car storage facilities in New Jersey</strong>, visit our <a title="link to auto storage page" href="/enclosed-auto-transport/auto-storage/">Car Storage</a> page.
