This was about to change with the launch of the Civic in 1973. This vehicle offered great space and efficiency and was a small car that had enough space to hold four passengers. There was the Honda Civic Sedan and the Honda Civic Hatchback as well. The two vehicles were practically identical and they had the same look with the only difference being that one has a hatchback and the other a small vertical panel that opened to the trunk. </p><p align="justify">
In 1980 there were some changes to the design of the Honda Civic Hatchback. There was a sleeker body design and there was also a larger wheelbase. There were three transmissions offered. The four speed manual, five speed manual and the two speed automatic. In 1984 the line up was made up of three hatchbacks. There was the bas version the DX and the S versions. Each had there own specific design differences that made them unique. Today there is still the hatchback being produced by Honda and they now have a design that is in keeping with the city car look. The hatchback comes in only the sporty 163 hp Si only.
