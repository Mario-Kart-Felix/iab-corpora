<h2>DOES YOUR CLASSIC CAR REQUIRE SPECIAL HANDLING AND ATTENTION? All Day Auto Transport can provide State of the Art Enclosed Auto Transport for any location in the country.</h2> 

<p>At All Day Auto Transport we provide both open and <a href="/auto-transport/enclosed.htm">enclosed car transport</a> methods to our customers with exotics and Classic Cars, and we will ship your vehicle per your exact preferences. <strong>Luxury Auto Transport</strong> is one of our specialties and we have been shipping Americas antique cars for the past 5 years.</p>

<p>For customers with Classic Cars, exotic cars or new cars, there is often a preference for shipping these vehicles using an <strong>enclosed auto transporter</strong> so that they will be protected from the elements, theft, and remain in showroom condition. We make sure every enclosed car hauler we use is state of the art and the rig has to have had an inspection from the Department of Transportation in the last 12 months. </p>

<p>We do recommend, that if you have a vehicle that warrants extra special handling that you consider an enclosed auto carrier.</p>

<p style="text-align:center;"><img src="/img/enclosed-transport-mercedes-classic-1.jpg" width="250" height="176" alt="Enclosed Classic Car Auto Transport" style="border: 1px solid #000;"><img src="/img/enclosed-transport-mercedes-classic-2.jpg" width="250" height="176" alt="Enclosed Auto Transport" style="margin: 0 0 0 30px; border: 1px solid #000;"></p>

<p><strong>Our Classic Car Transport Services Include:</strong></p>

<ul>
	<li>Vehicle is a classic, rare or high-end model.</li>
	<li>Vehicle is low-clearance.</li>
	<li>Vehicle is extra-wide.</li>
	<li>Vehicle is used in showrooms or advertising.</li>
	<li>If you are concerned about harsh weather conditions.</li>
	<li>Lift-Gate service to make sure there is no scraping of the bumper when loading</li>
	<li>Restoration jobs or part cars with no windows.</li>
	<li>Enclosed car haulers carry $1,000,000 cargo insurance</li>
</ul>

<p>One thing to note about enclosed carriers -- if you are willing to pay the extra cost for enclosed shipping services, rest assured that this does mean that your vehicle will be shipped entirely ENCLOSED. The enclosure in which your classic car or luxury vehicle travels is solid, from floors to walls to the ceiling overhead.</p>

<p>You're also going to get the best service and most reliable drivers available. These <strong>auto transporters</strong> specialize in exotic, high end cars. From Bentley, Ferrari, Lamborghini, or the classic car that's still in showroom condition â our drivers have done it all. They will treat your car with the utmost care as if it was one of their own. Our reputation depends on it and so does theirs.</p>

<p>Most of the transport companies that we use for these jobs only own <strong>Enclosed Car Haulers</strong> and will only employ the best drivers with the most experience. All of the enclosed transporters we use also go through a rigorous pre-screening process. We will get references from 10 different brokers they've used in the past as well as 10 customers and verify all insurance and licensing is up to date with the Department of Transportation.</p>

<p>Another positive factor about using an enclosed hauler as your shipping preference, is that their transit time and delivery time is usually quicker than open car carriers. The reason for this is that enclosed transported only have 3 to 7 cars on their hauler rather than the typical 8 to 11 on an open transporter.</p>

<p>All Day Auto Transport is one of the few 5 star rated brokerages that have maintained a 5 star rating while being in business for over 3 years. If you do choose to ship your car via enclosed auto transporter you will have the finest and most reliable auto transport company possible for your move.</p>
