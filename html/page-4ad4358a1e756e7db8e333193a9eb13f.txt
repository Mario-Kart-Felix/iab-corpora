<p>A hybrid car is a vehicle that uses both gasoline and  electricity to operate. There are 3 different types: </p>


<ul type="disc">
  <li>Mild:       the electric motor and battery assist the internal combustion engine </li>
  <li>Full:       the electric motor and internal combustion engine can work together or       separately</li>
  <li>Plug-in:       the internal combustion engine is the back up to the electric motor and       battery </li>
</ul>




<p>No matter what type of hybrid you&rsquo;re looking at, there are  some incentives to buy one.</p>


<h2>How a hybrid can save you money</h2>


<p>There is no question that a hybrid can help you save money  in fuel purchases. Gasoline prices went down, but they&rsquo;re on the rise again,  and who knows how high they&rsquo;ll go?
