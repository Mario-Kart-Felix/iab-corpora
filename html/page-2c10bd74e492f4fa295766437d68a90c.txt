        <h2 class="bigtitle"><a class="blogtitle" href="/polaris-enter-south-india-with-the-launch-of-off-road_09_2011_postedby_jayashankar-menon" title="">Polaris enter South India with the launch of off-road vehicles in Chennai</a></h2>
        <p><span class="postedtime grytxt">
                Posted by
                <a title="Jayashankar Menon" href="/jayashankar-menon_profile" class="tilemore txtline">Jayashankar Menon</a> -
                  Sep 30 2011 </span></p>
      </div>
      <div class="editorcontent acmecontent"><br><div id='iansnewsitemdisplay'> <div id="iansnewsitemdisplay">
<p><img src="http://carimg.sulekha.com/automotive-albums/default/original/dubey_off_roader.jpg" alt="Dubey_Off_Roader" /><br /><br />Polaris India Private Limited, a wholly owned subsidiary of Polaris Industries Inc, the global leader in off-road and multi-terrain vehicles today entered into South Indian market by launching off-road vehicles in Chennai. The price range of the lifestyle and the luxury product, which starts from Rs 2.84 lakh onwards is available in Chennai through MPL Adventure Sports Vehicles Private Limited.</p>
<p><img src="http://carimg.sulekha.com/automotive-albums/default/original/punkaj_dubey_polaris.jpg" alt="Punkaj_Dubey_Polaris" /><br /><br />While speaking at the launch event, Pankaj Dubey, Managing Director, Polaris India said: "Polaris Industries is the world leader in off-road vehicles and recently decided to venture into the Indian market. Chennai is one of the key markets for us in India. We foresee huge interest and business potential in the market. We have appointed MPL Adventure Sports Vehicles Private Limited as our dealer to offer our innovative products in Chennai market. In the initial phase, we are launching our flagship products including ATVs, Polaris Ranger RZR, Side-by-Side vehicles and Snowmobiles in India. What is more, we are hopeful of giving lot of adventure and utility experience on off-roads in Chennai".</p>
<p><img src="http://carimg.sulekha.com/automotive-albums/default/original/dubey_ravi.jpg" alt="Dubey_Ravi" /><br /><br />While elaborating on the market scenario, Dubey said: "The specialised automobile market is at a nascent stage in India. Our principal aim is to sensitise the consumers and create a new market for the possible options available in recrational vehicles such as ATVs, Ranger, RZR, Side-by-Side vehicles and Snowmobiles and to generate more interest in these activities. Furthermore, we will focus on the utility application of our vehicles in areas of construction, mining, farming, forest, military, police and para military forces".</p>
<p><img src="http://carimg.sulekha.com/automotive-albums/default/original/ravindranathan_mpl.jpg" alt="Ravindranathan_MPL" /></p>
<p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;<strong><em> Photography: M Sathyan.</em></strong></p>
<p><br />Speaking onthe occasion, S Ravindranathan, Dealer Principal and Director of MPL Adventure Sports, the Polaris dealer in Chennai said: "While the Indian market is no stranger to ATVs, the look and feel of ATV's and adventure has been very limited so far. Polaris is a great brand known worldwide for its innovative off-road vehicles. We are proud to associate with them right from the begining and jointly develop the off-road vehicle market in the country something that people desire for, but have been missing".<br /><br /><strong>Portfolio</strong><br /><br />Sportsman ATVS, Polaris Sportsman range of ATVs is the best way to enjoy the outdoors. These are affectionately termed 'quad-bikes'. Through rain or snow, flat or hilly terrain, the ATVs perform at optimum levels no matter what the region or the season. Available in Premium (high quality performance), 2-Up (double seater) and Value (more affordable) ranges.<br /><br /><strong>Polaris Ranger RZR Side-by-Side</strong><br /><br />This is the only sport utility off-roader which is exclusively built for off-road activities. Polaris off-roaders deliver a razor sharp performance every time. There are four variants available: Only Trail (exclusively for hilly and mountain terrain), Only Sport (for dune-surfing and riding in open spaces), Only 4 Seat (world's first four seater Side-by-Side) and Only Xtreme (premium performance under all conditions).</p>
