	<h1>
		MINI Cooper 4-year Plan: Three-door Hatchback Paceman to Launch</h1>
	<p>
		2012-02-07 14:17:52.0 By: GlobalMarket Team</p>
	<dl class="tag">
		<dt>
			Tag:
		</dt>
		<dd>
				<a href="#">MINI Cooper Three-door Hatchback </a>
				</dd>
		</dl>
	<div class="float_r">
		<div class="Section1" style="layout-grid:15.6pt">
	<p style="text-align: justify; ">
		Recently, MINI 4-year new car plan is exposed. Besides upgrade of Hatchback and Clubman tourism car, MINI will releases several multi-function minivan and passenger cars. However, Rocketman released last year in Geneva is not on the list.</p>
	<p style="text-align: justify; ">
		<img alt="MINI Cooper Three-door Hatchback Paceman" src="http://www.globalmarket.com/filestorage/cms/photo/6_1328594474889.jpg" style="width: 554px; height: 396px; " /></p>
	<p style="text-align: justify; ">
		It is said that the first launch model will be Paceman Concept. Paceman is a actually a three-door derivative of the five-door Countryman. It emphasizes more on its exercise performance. It will be released in the beginning of next year, acting as the finale of this MINI model. The next released car is a three-door Hatchback, launched in the end of next year. The five-door derivative will be launched in 2014. It is the one that BMW fights against Mercedes-Benz A-class and Audi A1. In 2015, MINI is going to release a new generation Clubman with its length increased 10 inch.</p>
	<p style="text-align: justify; ">
		In 2015, MINI will also launch SportVan, F58. It will be a multi-function minivan adopting a longer wheelbase. It is also said that this minivan is especially designed for the Chinese market while it will also enter the global market in the future.</p>
