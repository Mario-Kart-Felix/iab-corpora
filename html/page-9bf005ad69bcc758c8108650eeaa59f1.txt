<h1>About Us</h1>

<!-- This content is included in both the About page and the front page - Ross -->

<p><b>The Oregon Electric Vehicle Association (OEVA) is</b> a non-profit association of electric vehicle enthusiasts. We promote electric vehicle education and encourage their safe construction and use.  
We part of the <a href="http://www.mhrc.org/">Multnomah Hot Rod Council</a> and a chapter of the <a href="http://www.electricauto.org/">Electric Auto Association</a>.</p>

<h2>Our Meetings</h2>

<p align="left">Our meetings are held on the <b>second Thursday</b> of every month (<i>except Dec.</i>) in downtown Portland at <a href="http://www.wtcpd.com/" target="_blank">The World Trade Center</a>, on the corner of SW Salmon and 1st Street. See the <a href="http://goo.gl/maps/JnhQd">Google Map</a>. (<a href="/about/map-wtc-location.gif">A backup map</a> is also available).  For further questions, call Chris at <img src="/images/ChrisNumber.png">.</p>

<ul>
	<li>7pm - 7:30, Socializing, Car show and tell</li>
	<li>7:30 - 9pm, Meeting</li>
</ul>

<p>PGE provides outlets for recharging your vehicle, so bring your EV if you have one.</p>



<h2>Membership</h2>
<p>OEVA membership is $20 per year. Additional donations are also accepted. To subscribe or renew, send a check payable to OEVA along with the <a href="/membership/">Membership form</a>.</p>


<h2>E-Mail Announcements List</h2>

<p align="left"><em>Sign up!</em> Our meeting reminders and newsletter announcements are
delivered on the OEVA e-mail list. Subscription is free. 
<a href="http://www.rdrop.com/mailman/listinfo/oeva-list">Sign up for the OEVA list</a>.
A <a href="http://www.rdrop.com/pipermail/oeva-list/">online archive</a>
of this e-mail list also exists.</p>

<p>You may want to check out the fully searchable international 
<a href="http://www.evdl.org/">EV Discussion List (EVDL)</a> too.</p>
