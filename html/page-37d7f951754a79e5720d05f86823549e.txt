<h1>Patent Applied: new subcompact sedan for Guangzhou Auto</h1>
<p class="date">Published on July 31, 2013 by W.E. Ning</p>
<p><a href="http://www.carnewschina.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/guangzhou-auto-e-jet-pro-1.jpg" class="myfancybox"><img class="alignnone size-large wp-image-46928" title="Guangzhou Auto subcompact" alt="Patent Applied: new subcompact sedan for Guangzhou Auto " src="http://www.carnewschina.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/guangzhou-auto-e-jet-pro-1-458x305.jpg" width="458" height="305" /></a></p>
<p>Guangzhou Auto has <a href="http://auto.sohu.com/20130731/n383028721.shtml" target="_blank">applied</a> for patent on a new subcompact sedan, design-wise based on the Guangzhou Auto E-jet concept car that debuted on the Guangzhou Auto Show in December last year. Chinese media reports that the new subcompact will be based on a new &#8216;JC22&#8242; platform that is being developed with Chery Auto.</p>
<p><span id="more-46926"></span></p>
<p><a href="http://www.carnewschina.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/guangzhou-auto-e-jet-pro-1a.jpg" class="myfancybox"><img class="alignnone size-large wp-image-46929" alt="guangzhou-auto-e-jet-pro-1a" src="http://www.carnewschina.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/guangzhou-auto-e-jet-pro-1a-458x298.jpg" width="458" height="298" /></a></p>
<p>The E-jet concept.</p>
<p>Guangzhou Auto and Chery Auto signed a <a href="http://www.carnewschina.com/2012/11/06/guangzhou-auto-and-chery-sign-rd-agreement/" target="_blank">cooperation agreement</a> in November 2011. The new platform will also underpin the eventual successor of the <a href="http://www.sinaimg.cn/qc/photo_auto/photo/20/27/1062027/1062027_950.jpg" target="_blank">current Chery A3</a>. What that means for the line-up of Guangzhou Auto is not entirely clear. The soon-to-be-launched <a href="http://www.carnewschina.com/2013/07/25/production-of-the-guangzhou-auto-trumpchi-ga3-has-started-in-china/" target="_blank">Guangzhou Auto Trimpchi GA3</a> is a subcompact sedan as well and is about the same size as the Chery A3.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.carnewschina.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/guangzhou-auto-e-jet-pro-2.jpg" class="myfancybox"><img class="alignnone size-large wp-image-46930" alt="guangzhou-auto-e-jet-pro-2" src="http://www.carnewschina.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/guangzhou-auto-e-jet-pro-2-458x305.jpg" width="458" height="305" /></a></p>
<p>It might be a sporty variant of the GA3, for positioning between the Trumpchi GA3 and <a href="http://www.carnewschina.com/2012/12/12/guangzhou-auto-trumpchi-ga5-launched-on-the-china-car-market/" target="_blank">Trumpchi GA5</a>. GA4 CC maybe? We will keep you posted&#8230;</p>
<p><a href="http://www.carnewschina.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/guangzhou-auto-e-jet-pro-3.jpg" class="myfancybox"><img class="alignnone size-large wp-image-46927" alt="guangzhou-auto-e-jet-pro-3" src="http://www.carnewschina.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/guangzhou-auto-e-jet-pro-3-458x305.jpg" width="458" height="305" /></a></p>
