						<div class="entry-meta">
				<span class="sep">Posted on </span><a href="http://heaa.info/?p=1" title="2:50 pm" rel="bookmark"><time class="entry-date" datetime="2013-07-09T14:50:16+00:00">July 9, 2013</time></a><span class="by-author"> <span class="sep"> by </span> <span class="author vcard"><a class="url fn n" href="http://heaa.info/?author=1" title="View all posts by admin" rel="author">admin</a></span></span>			</div><!-- .entry-meta -->

				<div class="entry-content">
			<p>On July 11th, we&#8217;ll visit with Tom Gage, CEO of EV Grid.</p>
<p>Here&#8217;s how they describe themselves:<br />
Innovation that enables synergy -<br />
EV: Making vehicle ownership easier and cheaper<br />
GRID: Making the grid responsive and self-balancing</p>
<p>EV Grid was founded in 2012 to promote and develop the integration of the electric vehicle fleet and the power grid. The company creates technologies, systems, and business models that help fleet operators utilize EV technology. In time, these innovations will reach consumers, as well.</p>
<p>We&#8217;ll also have a discussion of this article by a serious critic of EVs:</p>
<p>http://spectrum.ieee.org/energy/renewables/unclean-at-any-speed</p>
<p>And last, but not least, we&#8217;ll discuss National Plug-In Day &#8212; a big event on September 28, 2013.</p>
<p>It should be an interesting session. Come join us!</p>
<p>Thursday July 11th  6:30 PM<br />
Location: Bayland Community Center,  6000 Chimney Rock Rd, Houston, TX 77081</p>
<p>http://www.pct3.hctx.net/CC_Bayland/</p>
