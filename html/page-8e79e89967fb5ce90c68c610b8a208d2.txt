  <h2>California Business Connect Project</h2>  
  <p>The <a href="cbc/">California Business Connect Project</a> will increase efficiencies and allow readily available 
  access to Secretary of State Records.</p>
  
  <hr /><br />
  
  <h2>News &amp; Notices, Customer Alerts and Processing Times</h2>  
  <ul>
     <li class="common"><a href="news-and-notices.htm">News &amp; Notices</a>:  Get the latest information about changes affecting business conducted with the 
     Business Programs Division.</li>  
     <li class="common"><a href="customer-alerts.htm">Customer Alerts</a>:  Get the latest information about confirmed scams against Californians 
     and businesses in the State of California, and what you can do if you have been a victim of a scam.</li>
     <li class="common">Processing Times:  Get the current processing times for <a href="be/processing-times.htm"> Business Entities</a> or 
     <a href="notary/processing-times.htm">
     Notary Public Applications and Authentications</a> and determine which method of submission meets your needs.</li> 
  </ul>    
  
  <hr /><br />
  
  <p><a href="/business/be/">Business Entities</a> &ndash; Forms, samples and fees, name availability information, information requests (certificates, copies 
  and status reports), annual/biennial Statements of Information</p>
  <p><a href="/business/notary/">Notary Public</a> &ndash; Notary public commissions, education, handbook, disciplinary guidelines, seal manufacturers</p>
  <p><a href="/business/ucc/">Uniform Commercial Code</a> &ndash; Forms and fees, information requests, liens, Revised Article 9</p>
  <p><a href="/business/notary/authentication.htm">Authentications: Apostille or Certification</a> &ndash; Authentication of public official signatures on documents to 
  be used outside the United States</p>
  <p><a href="/business/sf/">Special Filings</a> &ndash; Immigration consultant and various other bonds, joint powers, successor&ndash;in&ndash;interest, statement 
  of facts &ndash; roster of public agencies, athlete agents</p>
  <p><a href="/business/ts/">Trademarks and Service Marks</a> &ndash; Registration of trademarks and service marks, container brands, laundry marks and the names of 
  farms, ranches, estates or villas</p>
  <p><a href="/vcfcf/">Victims of Corporate Fraud Compensation Fund</a> &ndash; Restitution to victims of corporate fraud</p>
