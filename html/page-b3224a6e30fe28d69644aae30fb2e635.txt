<h3 class='post-title entry-title'>
<a href='http://automotifcars-news.blogspot.com/2010/06/toyota-minivan-2015.html'>Toyota Minivan 2015</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content'>
<blockquote></blockquote>Minivans are not always that fascinating, but to rid the minivans of that stereotype boring boxy image, here comes the Toyota Minivan concept designed for 2015. Part of an internship project held at Toyota design studio, this <a href="http://automotifcars-news.blogspot.com/">Hybrid Toyota Minivan</a>..<br /><img style="display: block; margin: 0px auto 10px; text-align: center; cursor: pointer; width: 528px; height: 278px;" src="http://www.instablogsimages.com/images/2010/06/10/toyota-minivan-2015_hv1s2_1292.jpg" alt="" border="0" />Minivans are not always that fascinating, but to rid the minivans of that stereotype boring boxy image, here comes the Toyota Minivan concept designed for 2015. Part of an internship project held at Toyota design studio, this Hybrid Toyota Minivan is based on the current 7 seater Toyota Estima. Inspired by the Japanese bullet train and futuristic looking Tokyo River boat, concept&#8217;s futuristic and eye catching exterior brings the emotion impact that have considerable influence on customer&#8217;s mind while selecting a vehicle.<br /><br />The concept features vast transparent door surfaces along with one motion windscreen integrated in glass roof that gives the excellent scenic view to the occupants. On the other, the minivan has three row of seats wherein there are five seats for the adults in the front two rows and third one which is especially designed for the children, is placed above the ground, can rotate.  It not only offers the splendid observation for curious kids, but also provides an option to place electric batteries under the seat. <br /><br /><img style="display: block; margin: 0px auto 10px; text-align: center; cursor: pointer; width: 491px; height: 162px;" src="http://www.instablogsimages.com/images/2010/06/10/toyota-minivan-2015_92wE8_1292.jpg" alt="" border="0" /><br /><img style="display: block; margin: 0px auto 10px; text-align: center; cursor: pointer; width: 496px; height: 191px;" src="http://www.instablogsimages.com/images/2010/06/10/clipboard01_8pc2n_1292.jpg" alt="" border="0" /><br /><img style="display: block; margin: 0px auto 10px; text-align: center; cursor: pointer; width: 500px; height: 290px;" src="http://www.instablogsimages.com/images/2010/06/10/toyota-minivan-2015_ggl93_1292.jpg" alt="" border="0" />
