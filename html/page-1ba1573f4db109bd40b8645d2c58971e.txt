<h1>SAE 2012 Hybrid Vehicle Technologies Symposium</h1>
<p><strong>February 21-22, 2012</strong><br>
<em>Town and Country Resort & Conference Center</em><br>
<em>San Diego, CA</em></p>
<a class="important">Pre-registration deadline extended to February 10!</a><br>


<p>This symposium will provide first-hand knowledge of recent innovations from leaders in the industry through specialized panel discussions and presentations.   Take advantage of networking opportunities with peers as well as leading companies involved in hybrid technology.</p>
<p>Hybrid Hot Topics:
<ul>
<li>Long term technology
<li>Market Projections
<li>Energy storage system innovations
<li>Challenges facing the market
<li>Challenges facing energy storage systems
<li>OEM innovations and technology perspectives
<li>Supplier advances and innovations
<li>Heavy-duty hybrid developments for commercial vehicle and military
<li>Fuel economy and environmental aspects
</ul>
<p><a href="http://www.sae.org/events/training/symposia/evt/"> SAE 2012 Electric Vehicle Technology Symposium</a></p>
