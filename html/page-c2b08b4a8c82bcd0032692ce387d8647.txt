				  <h1>College of Business</h1>
				  <div class="entry">
				    <p>Student success&ndash;that is what we strive for in everything we  do at Eastern New   Mexico University's College of Business. Our diverse faculty hail from  leading business careers and academic institutions. They are here for students  and have a strong desire to help the next business leaders prepare themselves  for successful careers. </p>
				    <p> We hope you will take time to browse the related links and learn about our  interesting and dynamic college. We are certain that we have a program that  will interest you for your future business career. Whether your interest is in  <a href="programs/accounting.shtml" target="_blank"><strong>accounting</strong></a>, <a href="programs/ag-business.shtml" target="_blank"><strong>agricultural business</strong></a>, <a href="programs/finance.shtml" target="_blank"><strong>finance and economics</strong></a><a href="programs/management.shtml">, </a><a href="programs/hospitality.shtml" target="_blank"><strong>hospitality management</strong></a><a href="programs/is.shtml" target="_blank">,<strong> information systems</strong></a>, <a href="programs/management.shtml"><strong>management</strong></a> or <a href="programs/marketing.shtml" target="_blank"><strong>marketing</strong></a>, we have a career field that is sure to propel you to great things. </p>
                    <p>The College of Business enjoys accreditation from the ACBSP  (Accreditation Council for Business   Schools and Programs) for  our BBA and MBA programs. It should be noted, however, that our BS in  information systems, while offered by the College of Business,  is not an accredited program with ACBSP. </p>
                    <p>Our <a href="http://business.enmu.edu/mba/online/index.shtml" target="_blank"><strong>Master of Business  Administration</strong></a> is available completely online, or as a resident student on the  Portales campus. The program consists of a 10-course program taught by resident  faculty with doctorates in their field. Eastern's MBA program is accredited by  ACBSP and our tuition is one of the most affordable in the state and one of the  most affordable in the country. </p>
                    <p>Welcome to the College of Business,  we hope to have you in class soon!                    </p>
