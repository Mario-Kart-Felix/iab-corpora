<h1>Welcome to TheCrosswordGuy.com, the home of thematic crossword puzzles.</h1>
<p>My crosswords are unique because they are completely thematic. They contain none of the off-topic filler material found in other “themed” crosswords.</p>
<p>Thematic crosswords will engage, entertain and educate your readers. If desired, you can enhance ad revenue via advertiser sponsorship of your crosswords.</p>
