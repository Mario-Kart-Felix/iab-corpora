												<p>Let&#8217;s face it; most Americans don&#8217;t really like wagons. Whether it&#8217;s due to their looks or just general association to the Baby Boomer generation, people just generally don&#8217;t like wagons. Yet those people who seem to sample nearly every car, truck, van, suv or wagon on the market can&#8217;t seem to help but love them.</p>
<p>A well-designed sport wagon can pair some of the best attributes of both worlds. Yes, wagons have some really great attributes! The really good ones combine rational, sensible utility-mindedness and versatility with the responsive driving manners of a sport sedan. While the number of good, affordable wagons seems to remain limited for new-car shoppers, there are plenty of good options with luxury nameplates. So if you really want a wagon and you&#8217;re on a more limited budget, going used and buying your next car from ZAG Auto Group is especially smart.</p>
<p>Some of the best used wagons you can find include:</p>
<p>Acura TSX Wagon<br />
Cadillac CTS Sport Wagon<br />
Audi A4 Avant<br />
Audi A6 Avant<br />
Subaru Outback<br />
Volvo XC70</p>
<p>So if you&#8217;re looking for a great used wagon, check out the <a href="http://www.zagautogroup.com/search/search_filter/sort/make/order/asc/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.zagautogroup.com']);">inventory</a> at ZAG Auto Group today!</p>
