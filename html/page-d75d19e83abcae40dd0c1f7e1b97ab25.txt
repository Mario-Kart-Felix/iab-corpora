<p><strong>2013 Honda Civic Hatchback</strong> to be released in the automotive market that will be sold starting from Australia, Honda plans to sell <em>2013 Honda Civic Hatchback</em> in mid June 2012 by presenting two types of VTi and VTiL. Customers will be excited by the arrival of the Honda production car.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.automotivelook.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/honda-civic-hatchbacks-2013-wallpaper1.jpg"><img src="http://www.automotivelook.com/images/1055/1/2013-honda-civic-hatchback.jpg" alt="2013 Honda Civic Hatchback Front Angle" title="2013 Honda Civic Hatchback Pictures 1024 × 683" width="1024" height="683" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1060" /></a></p>
<p>One of the people who we believe to say that the engineers who create design that <u>2013 Honda Civic Hatchback</u> will feature five-door form of a small three-door that is updated from its predecessor.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.automotivelook.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/honda-civic-hatchbacks-2013-side1.jpg"><img src="http://www.automotivelook.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/honda-civic-hatchbacks-2013-side1.jpg" alt="2013 Honda Civic Hatchback Left Side" title="2013 Honda Civic Hatchback Pictures 1024 × 768" width="1024" height="768" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1059" /></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.automotivelook.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/honda-civic-hatchbacks-2013-rear1.jpg"><img src="http://www.automotivelook.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/honda-civic-hatchbacks-2013-rear1.jpg" alt="2013 Honda Civic Hatchback Rear Angle" title="2013 Honda Civic Hatchback Pictures 1024 × 683" width="1024" height="683" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1058" /></a></p>
<p>In terms of machine <a href="http://www.automotivelook.com">2013 Honda Civic Hatchback</a> will use the 1.8-liter four-cylinder with i-VTEC engine with a power of 104 kW at 6500 rpm and 174 Nm of torque at 4300 rpm. Fuel consumption of a manual transmission version of the 16.4 km / liter and an automatic version 15.4 km / liter.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.automotivelook.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/honda-civic-hatchbacks-2013-interior1.jpg"><img src="http://www.automotivelook.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/honda-civic-hatchbacks-2013-interior1.jpg" alt="2013 Honda Civic Hatchback Interior Steering Wheel" title="2013 Honda Civic Hatchback Pictures 1023 × 768" width="1023" height="768" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1057" /></a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.automotivelook.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/honda-civic-hatchbacks-2013-engine1.jpg"><img src="http://www.automotivelook.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/08/honda-civic-hatchbacks-2013-engine1.jpg" alt="2013 Honda Civic Hatchback Engine" title="2013 Honda Civic Hatchback Pictures 1024 × 640" width="1024" height="640" class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-1056" /></a></p>
<p>2013 Honda Civic Hatchback in terms of color will be present with a variety of colors that make the customers look very excited to see it, of which the colors are Yellow Topaz, Alabaster Silver, Crystal Black, Deep Blue Sapphire, Milano Red, Polished Metal, and White Orchid.</p>
