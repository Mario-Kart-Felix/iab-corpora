                        <p><font face="Verdana,Arial,Helvetica,sans-serif" size="3" color="#000000"><b><i>&nbsp;<br />                                                                                    <h1> <p>The 2013 Chicago Auto
                           Show</p> </h1>                       &nbsp;                                                <p><strong>Background</strong>
                           - First staged  in 1901, the Chicago Auto Show is the largest auto show in North America  and has been held more times than
                           any other auto exposition on the  continent. This year marks the 105th edition of the Chicago Auto Show.</p> <p><strong>Producer
                           </strong>- The Chicago Automobile Trade Association  (CATA) is the nation's oldest and largest metropolitan dealer  organization.
                           The CATA has produced the Chicago Auto Show since 1935.</p>  <p><strong>Dates<br /> </strong></p> <ul><li><strong>Public Show:&nbsp;
                           </strong>Saturday, February 9 through Monday, February 18<br />     &nbsp;</li><li><strong>First Look for Charity:</strong>&nbsp;
                           Friday, February 8, 7:00 to 11:00 p.m. Black tie. <a href="http://www.chicagoautoshow.com/first_look/default.aspx"><font  color="#000000">More info</font></a>.<br />     &nbsp;</li><li><strong>Media Preview:&nbsp; </strong>Thursday, February 7 through Friday, February 8. <a href="http://www.chicagoautoshow.com/media/"><font  color="#000000">More info</font></a>.</li></ul> <p><strong>Location </strong>- McCormick Place, 2301 S. Lake Shore Drive, Chicago, Illinois, 60616.</p> <p>The
                           Chicago Auto Show utilizes more than 1 million square feet of the  McCormick Place complex. McCormick Place offers a total
                           of 2.7 million  square feet of exhibit halls with an additional 700,000 square feet of  meeting room availability.</p> <p><strong>Public
                           Show Hours </strong>(<em>Box office closes one hour prior to the end of the show each day.</em>)</p> <ul><li><strong>February
                           9-17:</strong>&nbsp; 10 a.m. to 10 p.m.<br />     &nbsp;</li><li><strong>February 18:</strong>&nbsp; 10 a.m. to 8 p.m.</li></ul>
                           <p><strong>Admission </strong>- Tickets are available at our McCormick Place ticket booths or online at <a href="https://tickets.drivechicago.com/" target="_blank"><font  color="#000000">https://tickets.drivechicago.com</font></a>. Box office closes one hour before the show closes.</p> <ul><li>$12 for adults<br />     &nbsp;</li><li>$6 for seniors aged
                           62 and older<br />     &nbsp;</li><li>$6 for children 7-12<br />     &nbsp;</li><li>Free admission for children 6 and younger
                           when they accompany a paying adult. Family members only.<br />     &nbsp;</li><li>Weekday discount coupons ($6 off the regular
                           adult admission)  available at various area new-car dealers. Participating banks also mail  the discount tickets to their
                           account holders. <a href="http://www.chicagoautoshow.com/about_the_show/weekday_discounts.aspx"><font  color="#000000">More info</font></a>.<br />     &nbsp;</li><li>School &amp; group discounts available. <a href="http://www.chicagoautoshow.com/about_the_show/school__group_ticket_sales.aspx"><font  color="#000000">More info</font></a>.</li></ul> <p><strong>Exhibits </strong>-&nbsp;All Chicago Auto Show exhibits are held  in the McCormick Place complex. &nbsp;Exhibitions
                           include: multiple world and  North American introductions; a complete range of domestic and imported  passenger cars and trucks;
                           sport utility vehicles; and experimental or  concept cars. &nbsp;In total, nearly 1,000 different vehicles will be on  display.
                           Additionally, attendees will also have the opportunity to see  numerous accessories and auto-related exhibits, competition
                           vehicles and  project, antique and collector cars.</p> <p><strong>Special Days</strong><strong><br /> </strong></p> <ul><li><strong>Tuesday,
                           February 12:</strong>&nbsp; Women's Day. Women are  admitted for $6. Manufacturers will present special women-oriented  programs
                           on the purchase and lease of cars and maintenance.<br />     &nbsp;</li><li><strong>Wednesday, February 13 through Friday,
                           February 15:</strong>&nbsp; <a href="http://www.chicagoautoshow.com/about_the_show/food_drive.aspx" target="_blank"><font  color="#000000">Chicago Auto Show Food Drive.</font></a>  Show patrons who bring three cans of food&nbsp;will receive a coupon for $6  admission. All food will be&nbsp;donated to
                           A Safe Haven Foundation.<br />     &nbsp;</li><li><strong>Friday, February 15:</strong>&nbsp; Telemundo Hispanic Day. For
                           more information, visit <a href="http://www.telemundochicago.com/" target="_blank"><font  color="#000000">http://www.telemundochicago.com</font></a>.</li></ul> <p><strong>Appearances</strong> - Special appearances and events take place every day. Please check our <a href="http://www.chicagoautoshow.com/news/events__appearances.aspx"><font  color="#000000">Event Schedule</font></a> for up-to-date listings.</p> <p><strong>Accessibility</strong> - McCormick Place is easily reached by  car or public transportation.
                           Extensive shuttle bus service from all  area parking lots to and from McCormick Place. Weekend-only shuttle  service from
                           the Millennium Park and East Monroe underground garages.  Shuttles stop in 10-15 minute intervals near Columbus/Upper and
                           Lower  Randolph and at Gate 3 at McCormick Place.</p> <p>Scooter and Wheelchair rentals are available by contacting Scootaround
                           toll-free at 1-888-441-7575. You can also book online at <a href="http://www.scootaround.com/rentals/c/chicagoautoshow" target="_blank"><font  color="#000000">www.scootaround.com/rentals/c/chicagoautoshow</font></a>.</p> <p>Scooter rentals are $50.00 a day and wheelchair rentals are $20.00 a  day. Scootaround accepts all major credit cards.
                           Scootaround is located  at Gate 4 of McCormick Place (west end of the Grand Concourse). While  Scootaround will make every
                           effort to accommodate all needs, you are  encouraged to reserve in advance.</p>
