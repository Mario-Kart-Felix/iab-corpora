<p>Allstate Motor Club has over 35,000 providers that are part of their nationwide network, and they offer a 30-minute on-time arrival guarantee, ensuring you&rsquo;ll receive help with 30 minutes of dispatch time. No sign-up fee is required, and they offer four different annual membership plans &ndash; their Road Security Plan, Road Advantage Plan, Deluxe Plan and Platinum Elite Plan. The prices for each plan vary slightly by state.</p>
<p>They also have a separate RV plan that includes personal concierge assistance available 24 hours a day and a $1500 travel interruption benefit. Coverage of one additional adult family member is included with their top three plans, and you can add more members to any of their plans for a small annual fee.</p>
<p>When you become a member of any of Allstate Motor Club's plans, from their basic Road Security Plan to their Platinum Elite plan, you gain access to nearly every roadside service imaginable. Each plan includes 24-hour service as well as a whole range of important roadside services. While the benefit allowances vary per plan, the quality of the services does not. Tire change service, in case you get a flat tire or worse, is included with every plan, as are road and towing services, fuel delivery, dead battery and lock out services.</p>
<p>For roadside assistance no matter your location, you can call Allstate Motor Club&rsquo;s toll-free number to reach their emergency roadside assistance line and 24-hour dispatch center. If your vehicle breaks down when you&rsquo;re far from home, Allstate Motor Club covers travel interruption expenses. This particular service reimburses you a benefit of a certain amount (depending on your plan) that pays for hotel, transportation and food expenses if your car breaks down 100 miles or more from home.</p>
<p>Additionally, an arrest bond certificate, which is a reimbursement in case you&rsquo;re wrongly arrested for certain moving traffic violations, is offered with each plan. The pre-determined benefits vary depending on the plan.
