<h1 class="heading1">

Automotive Aftermarket
</h1>
<div class="resizeme">
<p>Eaton Performance offers products that will maximize engine power and wheel traction.  Whether you&rsquo;re at the track, on the rocks, towing a rig or simply upgrading your daily driver - we have a product that will suit your needs.</p><br /> <p>With over 100 years in the vehicle business, we provide quality that is unmatched.  Each product is tested and validated in our fully equipped labs and 650-plus acre Proving Grounds. Our legendary brands include the Detroit Locker&reg; and TVS&reg; Supercharger which have been chosen by professional racers around the globe.  When you&rsquo;re looking to get the most out of your vehicle &ndash; look no further than Eaton Performance.</p> <p>&nbsp;</p>
