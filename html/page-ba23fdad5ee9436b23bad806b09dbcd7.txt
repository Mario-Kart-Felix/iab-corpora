	    <h2>Custom Glass</h2>
	    <p>Pete's Auto Glass provides  custom auto glass fabrication services for your completely original custom car project or for that hard to find classic car glass replacement. Whether you provide specifications or we measure ourselves, your custom auto glass is guaranteed to fit perfectly as well as  meet or exceed all auto glass safety regulations. For a precision match to classic forms and styles, our exhaustive auto glass database allows us to locate the exact specifications for every piece of glass in every vehicle ever made commercially.</p>
	    <p>When  you need a piece of custom auto glass and you want it done right the first time, call Pete's Auto Glass at <strong>(800) 607-3837</strong>.</p>
	    <p>In some cases, extensive body modifications have changed the shape of opening to receive the glass. The custom glass team at Pete's Auto Glass will have no problem fabricating the shape and size of glass you need, but you may need a conversion kit for a clean finish and tight reveal around the opening. For this, we refer you to our friend in the business: <a href="http://www.onepieceproducts.com/" target="_blank">One Piece Products</a>.</p>
