				<h1 class="entry-title">Electric Vehicle Makers Could Soon Be Seen As Auto Industry Leaders</h1>
				<div class="meta">
					<time datetime="2013-08-27T23:16:24+00:00" pubdate class="updated">July 25th, 2013</time>

				<div class="figure"><img width="320" height="200" src="http://protekparksolar.com/solar-structure-news/wp-content/uploads/evs-could-take-over-as-auto-industry-leaders-_16001146_41351_0_14039350_500-320x200.jpg" class="attachment-news-category-thumb wp-post-image" alt="EVs could take over as auto industry leaders." />					<p class="caption">EVs could take over as auto industry leaders.</p>	
				</div>
					
				<p>Although economic uncertainty in Detroit has been a cause for concern in the auto industry, some aspects of the market are going strong and, should current trends continue, some of the lesser components of the sector may soon be looked&nbsp;to as leaders. The electric vehicle market, for example, is growing rapidly as demand continues to expand.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Several factors have impacted consumer behavior when it comes to buying traditional automobiles. Gas prices are chief among them. This is one of many reasons why Detroit&#039;s economy is in a state of flux, and is why industry stakeholders are looking to EV manufacturers to lead the auto market to an economically viable future.</p>
<p>According to The Detroit News, not only are EVs looked on as the potential savior of the auto industry, their increased popularity has <a href="http://www.detroitnews.com/article/20130725/AUTO03/307250027/Electric-cars-finally-come-into-their-own?odyssey=mod%7Cnewswell%7Ctext%7CFRONTPAGE%7Cs" target="_blank">allowed manufacturers to diversify their offerings</a>.</p>
<p>&quot;With high gas prices, government fuel economy regulations and environmental and other concerns, electric vehicles are making their way to dealer showrooms,&quot; writes contributor Doug Guthrie. &quot;There have never been so many to choose from. Almost every major automaker sells a battery-electric car.&quot;</p>
<p>There are numerous groups of people with an opportunity to capitalize on this trend. Parking lot owners, for example, should accommodate the growing number of EVs by installing charging stations, among other <a href="http://protekparksolar.com/media.htm" target="_self" class="dnautolink">solar parking lot</a> solutions. This will give drivers a place to charge their cars while they are at work, the mall or any other location that requires them to park for a long time.</p>
<p>EV manufacturers are not going to back down from this opportunity to lead the auto market. Therefore, it is up to others to capitalize on this fact.</p>
