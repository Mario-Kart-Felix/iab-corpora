<P><B>Ruling</B>
<P><A NAME="P1"></A><A NAME="#P1"></A><A NAME="P1"></A><A NAME="atP1"></A><A NAME="#atP1"></A><A NAME="atP1"></A>1. The luxury car tax (LCT) threshold and the fuel-efficient tax limit are determined in accordance with Division 25 of the  <I>A New Tax System (Luxury Car Tax) Act 1999</I>  (LCT Act).

<P><B>Luxury car tax threshold</B>
<P><A NAME="P2"></A><A NAME="#P2"></A><A NAME="P2"></A><A NAME="atP2"></A><A NAME="#atP2"></A><A NAME="atP2"></A>2. From 1 July 2012, the LCT threshold will be indexed annually according to a factor to be determined by Parliament or, if such a factor is not determined by Parliament, indexed annually in accordance with movements in the All Groups Consumer Price Index (All Groups CPI).<A NAME="ft1"></A><A NAME="ft1"></A><A NAME="#ft1"></A><A NAME="#ft1"></A><A HREF="/atolaw/view.htm?locid='LCD/LCTD20121/NAT/ATO/fp1'&PiT=99991231235958#fp1" target="_parent"><SUP>1</SUP></A>
<P><A NAME="P3"></A><A NAME="#P3"></A><A NAME="P3"></A><A NAME="atP3"></A><A NAME="#atP3"></A><A NAME="atP3"></A>3. Prior to 1 July 2012 the LCT threshold was indexed in accordance with the motor vehicle purchase sub-group of the Consumer Price Index (CPI).
<P><A NAME="P4"></A><A NAME="#P4"></A><A NAME="P4"></A><A NAME="atP4"></A><A NAME="#atP4"></A><A NAME="atP4"></A>4. Parliament has not determined an indexation factor, so for the 2012-13 financial year the LCT threshold will be indexed in accordance with movements in the All Groups CPI unless the indexation factor is 1 or less<A NAME="ft2"></A><A NAME="ft2"></A><A NAME="#ft2"></A><A NAME="#ft2"></A><A HREF="/atolaw/view.htm?locid='LCD/LCTD20121/NAT/ATO/fp2'&PiT=99991231235958#fp2" target="_parent"><SUP>2</SUP></A>.
<P><A NAME="P5"></A><A NAME="#P5"></A><A NAME="P5"></A><A NAME="atP5"></A><A NAME="#atP5"></A><A NAME="atP5"></A>5. For the 2010-11 financial year this index was 696.1 and for the 2011-12 financial year the index was 716.6<A NAME="ft3"></A><A NAME="ft3"></A><A NAME="#ft3"></A><A NAME="#ft3"></A><A HREF="/atolaw/view.htm?locid='LCD/LCTD20121/NAT/ATO/fp3'&PiT=99991231235958#fp3" target="_parent"><SUP>3</SUP></A> resulting in an indexation factor of 1.029. For the 2010-11 financial year, the LCT threshold was $57,466<A NAME="ft4"></A><A NAME="ft4"></A><A NAME="#ft4"></A><A NAME="#ft4"></A><A HREF="/atolaw/view.htm?locid='LCD/LCTD20121/NAT/ATO/fp4'&PiT=99991231235958#fp4" target="_parent"><SUP>4</SUP></A>. The LCT threshold for the 2012-13 financial year is $59,133 ($57,466 x 1.029).

<P><B>Fuel efficient car limit</B>
<P><A NAME="P6"></A><A NAME="#P6"></A><A NAME="P6"></A><A NAME="atP6"></A><A NAME="#atP6"></A><A NAME="atP6"></A>6. The fuel efficient car limit is indexed annually in line with movements in the motor vehicle purchase sub-group of the CPI,<A NAME="ft5"></A><A NAME="ft5"></A><A NAME="#ft5"></A><A NAME="#ft5"></A><A HREF="/atolaw/view.htm?locid='LCD/LCTD20121/NAT/ATO/fp5'&PiT=99991231235958#fp5" target="_parent"><SUP>5</SUP></A> unless the indexation factor is 1 or less.<A NAME="ft6"></A><A NAME="ft6"></A><A NAME="#ft6"></A><A NAME="#ft6"></A><A HREF="/atolaw/view.htm?locid='LCD/LCTD20121/NAT/ATO/fp6'&PiT=99991231235958#fp6" target="_parent"><SUP>6</SUP></A>
<P><A NAME="P7"></A><A NAME="#P7"></A><A NAME="P7"></A><A NAME="atP7"></A><A NAME="#atP7"></A><A NAME="atP7"></A>7.  For the 2010-11 financial year this index was 385.6 and for the 2011-12 year the index was 376.0<A NAME="ft7"></A><A NAME="ft7"></A><A NAME="#ft7"></A><A NAME="#ft7"></A><A HREF="/atolaw/view.htm?locid='LCD/LCTD20121/NAT/ATO/fp7'&PiT=99991231235958#fp7" target="_parent"><SUP>7</SUP></A> resulting in an indexation factor of 0.975. As the indexation factor is less than 1, the fuel-efficient car limit will not be indexed. For the 2010-11 financial year, the fuel-efficient car limit was $75,375.<A NAME="ft8"></A><A NAME="ft8"></A><A NAME="#ft8"></A><A NAME="#ft8"></A><A HREF="/atolaw/view.htm?locid='LCD/LCTD20121/NAT/ATO/fp8'&PiT=99991231235958#fp8" target="_parent"><SUP>8</SUP></A> The fuel-efficient car limit for the 2012-13 financial year remains at $75,375.

<P><B>Date of effect</B>
<P><A NAME="P8"></A><A NAME="#P8"></A><A NAME="P8"></A><A NAME="atP8"></A><A NAME="#atP8"></A><A NAME="atP8"></A>8. This Determination applies to the financial year commencing 1 July 2012.
