                    <td valign="top" class="body"><span class="body"><br>
                      Maplesoft's suite of engineering tools supports the modeling and analysis of electric vehicle systems, such as powertrains, electric motors, generators, and batteries. These tools allow engineers to easily combine the multi-domain systems required in electric and hybrid-electric vehicles into a single model. Unlike the time and effort typically required to develop high-fidelity system models using traditional simulation tools, Maplesoft's next-generation graphical tools for model development and analysis dramatically reduce the time and cost of up-front analysis, virtual prototyping, and parameter optimization of system designs.</span><br><br>
                      Maple and MapleSim are used extensively for modeling  complex multi-domain systems. The product suite also includes tools for easily  transferring work into an existing design toolchain, such as MATLAB&reg; and Simulink&reg;,  and is especially useful for hardware-in-the-loop simulations.
