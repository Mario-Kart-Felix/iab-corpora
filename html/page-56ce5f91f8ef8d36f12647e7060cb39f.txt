            <div class="breadcrumb"><a href="/">Home</a> » <a href="/tips">Tips</a> » Where to Rent a Luxury SUV</div>                          <h1 class="title">Where to Rent a Luxury SUV</h1>
                                                          </div> <!-- /#content-header -->
        
        <div id="content-area">
				
          <div id="node-1361" class="node node-type-tips"><div class="node-inner">

  
  
  

  <div class="content">
    <p>Several companies offer luxury SUV rentals for customers. Luxury SUVs may be required for <a href="http://www.carrentalexpress.com/travel-resources/long-term-rentals">long trips</a>, special occasions or upper class clientele. These vehicles are spacious and offer several features that make driving an SUV more comfortable. Some rental companies offer luxury SUVs with hybrid capabilities to reduce fuel costs for customers. Luxury SUVs are beneficial for customers with numerous passengers or baggage to transport.</p>
<h3>Where to Find Luxury SUV Rentals</h3>
<p>Customers seeking <a href="http://www.carrentalexpress.com/tips/where-can-i-rent-luxury-suv">luxury SUV rentals</a> should perform a search online. Most independent rental agencies will sort their services by location, rental type and dates of rental. Once this information is entered, the types of luxury SUVs available will appear. Customers will usually select the class, and a vehicle will be reserved for the customer. Online comparison websites of independent rental agencies are helpful for this particular search. This will allow consumers to view available rental agencies offering luxury SUVs. These types of websites save consumers time and money.</p>
<h3>Where are Luxury Rental Agencies Located</h3>
<p>Luxury rental agencies are typically located in the general vicinity of standard rental agencies. In some instances, the services are combined. Luxury rental agencies may be combined with a standard rental agency at the airport or the rental agency may be located off-site. Since the services are specialized, most standard rental agencies must be notified in advance of a luxury rental request. Advanced notification will ensure the vehicle requested will be on premises at the time of rental. Rental agencies specializing in luxury vehicle rentals will always have vehicles on premises to offer customers, unless they are sold out.</p>
<h3>Cost of Luxury SUV Rentals</h3>
<p>Luxury SUV rentals are typically more expensive than standard SUV rental. Depending upon the model, some rental companies may charge as much as $500 per day for a luxury rental. Some models are more affordable and other models are less affordable. These vehicles are more expensive to replace and repair. Therefore, luxury rental companies charge more to ensure that their vehicle is protected and in good hands when it leaves the lot.</p>
<p>Companies cannot afford to lose significant portions of money on high risk renters. Credit cards are typically required for these types of rentals, as well as, insurance. However, some companies may accept cash payments with a deposit.</p>
<h3>Why Rent a High End SUV?</h3>
<p>Clients who travel often cannot take their vehicle with them. Luxury vehicle rental allows clients the same level of comfort that clients experience with their own luxury vehicle. Other clients may be attending a special event such as a class reunion, family reunion, conference, prom, ballet, concert or other event that may require a luxury vehicle. Customers of all types enjoy the features offered in luxury vehicles. Some clients rent luxury SUVs for pleasure or to test drive before purchase. The reasons are plentiful and vary from client to client.</p>
<p>Keep in mind that luxury SUVs are more expensive vehicles to purchase and maintain. An accident in a luxury SUV will cost consumers more on average than a standard vehicle. Many consumers will cover the vehicle under their own personal insurance. Experts recommend securing insurance that will cover the cost of the damages to the vehicle, as well as, any costs that may accrue, while the vehicle is absent from the company’s fleet. This will ensure there are no unpleasant surprises that the consumer cannot afford. Most rental companies offer insurance with these protection clauses.</p>
<p>Car Rental Express (CRX) is a comparison website that allows consumers to compare participating independent rental agencies including <a href="http://www.carrentalexpress.com/specialty/luxury">luxury rental agencies</a>. This website helps consumers quickly identify and compare numerous rental agencies offering luxury SUV rentals.</p>
