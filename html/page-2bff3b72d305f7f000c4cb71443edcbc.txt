<h1>Roadside Assistance</h1>

    </header>
    

<p>Roadside Assistance coverage provides towing to the nearest qualified repair facility and covers a specified amount of the necessary labor at the place of breakdown when your automobile is disabled due to any of the following: 
	<ul class="bulletList">
		<li>Mechanical or electrical breakdown</li>	
		<li>Dead battery</li> 
		<li>Flat tire</li> 
		<li>Lockout, or lost or stolen keys. (We dispatch a qualified service provider; you only pay for the cost of the keys.)</li>
		<li>Insufficient supply of fuel, oil, water or other fluids</li> 
		<li>Entrapment in snow, mud, water or sand within 100 feet of the roadway</li> 
	</ul>	
	</p>	
	<p>Progressive's Roadside Assistance coverage provides immediate sign and drive assistance, which means you have no out-of-pocket expenses &#8212; just sign the receipt and you're back on your way. Roadside Assistance is available 24/7.</p>
	
	<p>Some states require you to have Comprehensive and Collision coverage in order to add Roadside Assistance to your policy.</p>
