<h2>Current laws and rules</h2>
<h3>Vehicles</h3>
<ul><li><a href="http://apps.leg.wa.gov/RCW/default.aspx?cite=46.70">RCW 46.70: Dealers and manufacturers</a></li>
    <li><a href="http://apps.leg.wa.gov/RCW/default.aspx?cite=46.16A.060">RCW 46.16A.060: Registration &mdash; Emission control inspections required &mdash; Exemptions &mdash; Educational information &mdash; Rules</a></li>
    <li><a href="http://apps.leg.wa.gov/rcw/default.aspx?cite=46.16A.300">RCW 46.16A.300: Temporary permits &mdash; Authority &mdash; Dealer fees &mdash; Secure system</a></li>
    <li><a href="http://apps.leg.wa.gov/WAC/default.aspx?cite=173-423-050">WAC 173-423-050: Requirement to meet California vehicle emission standards</a></li>
    <li><a href="http://apps.leg.wa.gov/WAC/default.aspx?cite=308-56A">WAC 308-56A: Certificates of title &mdash; motor vehicles, etc.</a></li>
    <li><a href="http://apps.leg.wa.gov/WAC/default.aspx?cite=308-66">WAC 308-66:  Motor vehicle dealers and manufacturers</a></li></ul>

<h3>Vessels</h3>
<ul><li><a href="http://apps.leg.wa.gov/RCW/default.aspx?cite=88.02">RCW 88.02: Vessel registration</a></li>
    <li><a href="http://apps.leg.wa.gov/WAC/default.aspx?cite=308-90">WAC 308-90: Vessel dealer registration</a></li>
    <li><a href="http://apps.leg.wa.gov/WAC/default.aspx?cite=308-93">WAC 308-93: Vessel registration and certificates of title</a></li></ul>

<h3>Snowmobiles</h3>
<ul><li><a href="http://apps.leg.wa.gov/RCW/default.aspx?cite=46.10">RCW 46.10: Snowmobiles</a></li>
    <li><a href="http://apps.leg.wa.gov/WAC/default.aspx?cite=308-94">WAC 308-94: Snowmobiles</a></li></ul>

<h3>Off-road and nonhighway vehicles</h3>
<ul><li><a href="http://apps.leg.wa.gov/RCW/default.aspx?cite=46.09">RCW 46.09: Off-road and nonhighway vehicles</a></li>
    <li><a href="http://apps.leg.wa.gov/WAC/default.aspx?cite=308-94A">WAC 308-94A: Off-road and nonhighway vehicles</a></li></ul>

<h2>Explanation of abbreviations</h2>
<ul><li>RCW&mdash;Revised Code of Washington (Washington State laws)</li>
    <li>WAC&mdash;Washington Administrative Code (Washington State agency regulations)</li></ul>
