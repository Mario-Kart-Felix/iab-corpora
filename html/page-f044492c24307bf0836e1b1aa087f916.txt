	<h2>Rental</h2>
	<div class="entry">
<p>Thank you for choosing Hybrid Auto for your car rental service.   The rental fee for the car is $59 per day &#8211; weekly rental rates are available upon request.  For your convenience please see forms below to begin the process:</p>
<p><a href="http://www.hybrid-autorentals.com/contact-2/">Make a Reservation</a></p>
<p><a href="http://www.hybrid-autorentals.com/agreement.pdf">Rental Agreement</a></p>
<p><a href="http://hybrid-autorentals.com/CCA.doc">Credit Card Authorization</a></p>
<p>Please fax when completed to 305-981-7673.   Once received and processed you will be contacted to arrange pick up of the hybrid.   If you have any questions regarding this process please contact Darren at 305-785-4307.</p>
