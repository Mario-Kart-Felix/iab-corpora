<p>If talking about a <a title="2012 Ford B-Max MPV with Sliding Door" href="http://autocarinterior.com/2012-ford-b-max-mpv-with-sliding-door/" target="_blank">MPV</a>, <strong>Buick GL8 Minivan</strong>, the latest generation <em>Buick GL8</em> which came out in 2011 should not be forgotten. This new model brings 7 and 27 of breakthrough technology innovations with improvements in design, cabin space and safety, technology, power, features and quality.</p>
<h3>Exterior view of Buick GL8</h3>
<p><a href="http://autocarinterior.com/luxury-design-in-buick-gl8-minivan/2011-buick-gl8-mpv-rear-side-up-view-with-door-open/" rel="attachment wp-att-3579"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-3579" title="2011 buick gl8 mpv rear side up view with door open" src="http://autocarinterior.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/05/2011-buick-gl8-mpv-rear-side-up-view-with-door-open-510x376.jpg" alt="" width="510" height="376" /></a></p><script id="mNCC" language="javascript">  medianet_width='336';  medianet_height= '280';  medianet_crid='312493893';  </script>  <script id="mNSC" src="http://contextual.media.net/nmedianet.js?cid=8CUOV1S6D" language="javascript"></script>  
       

<p>Model of the <span style="text-decoration: underline;">Buick GL8 Minivan</span> was developed by Shanghai <a title="Exclusive Corvette Centennial Edition 2012" href="http://autocarinterior.com/exclusive-corvette-centennial-edition-2012/" target="_blank">General Motors</a> (GM) in collaboration with PATAC (Pan Asia Technical Automotive Center) in Shanghai that are tailored to the needs of its customers in China. And <em>Buick GL8 Minivan</em> offers everything needed by the owner, a car with comfort taste, dynamism, refinement and tranquility.</p>
<p>2011 <strong>Buick GL8 Minivan</strong> comes with a modern design, which can be enjoyed from the lines like the sweep spear from front to rear and grille shaped waterfall.</p>
<h4>Interior viw of Buick GL8</h4>
<p><a href="http://autocarinterior.com/luxury-design-in-buick-gl8-minivan/2011-buick-gl8-mpv-baggage-view/" rel="attachment wp-att-3582"><img class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-3582" title="2011 buick gl8 mpv baggage view" src="http://autocarinterior.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/05/2011-buick-gl8-mpv-baggage-view-510x382.jpg" alt="" width="510" height="382" /></a></p>
<p><em>Buick GL8 Minivan</em> has a length of 5256 mm, width 1878 mm, height 1800, and 3088 mm wheelbase. Dimensions were assessed <a title="2012 Buick Verano is a Leisure" href="http://autocarinterior.com/2012-buick-verano-is-a-leisure/" target="_blank">Buick</a> offers an amazing relief cabin.</p>
<p>MPV which has 6 airbags point, armed with V6 engine, 3,000 cc, Sidi, direct injection or a 2.4 liter Ecotec. All models 6-speed automatic transmission. The suspension has improved so secured more comfortable. <span style="text-decoration: underline;">Buick GL8 Minivan</span> Price starts U.S. $ 43,250.</p>
