<h1>Report: MMPC to launch Mirage... as a sedan</h1>
<h2>4-door Mirage in the works for global market, launch this year</h2>
<h6>Text:  / Photos: MMPC Press</h6>
<h6>posted  January 02, 2013 16:34</h6></hgroup>
<article role="main"><div id="article-content">

<a class="addthis_counter addthis_pill_style"></a></div><p><img src='http://www.autoindustriya.com/images/posts/post3091.jpg' alt='Report: MMPC to launch Mirage... as a sedan image' width='640' /></p><p>It's been barely two months since <strong>Mitsubishi Motors Philippines Corporation <a href="../auto-industry-news/mitsubishi-motors-philippines-holds-grand-launch-for-2013-mirage.html">launched the all-new Mitsubishi Mirage 5-door</a></strong><a href="../auto-industry-news/mitsubishi-motors-philippines-holds-grand-launch-for-2013-mirage.html"> hatchback</a> in grand style. The <strong>momentous occasion</strong> marked Mitsubishi's renewed assault on the Passenger Car (PC) segment, and was even graced by the presence of <strong>Mitsubishi Motors Corporation's president, Mr. Osamu Masuko.</strong></p>
<p>However, there's <strong>more to the story with the Mirage</strong>. If you've heard of rumors that a sedan version is coming, well, it's true. <strong>We can confirm through a source that MMPC's hot selling hatchback will be joined by a sedan version later this year</strong>.</p>
<p>With the hatchback version selling flying off dealerships at rapid rates (they did have a 2000+ waiting list),<strong> a sedan version will only strengthen the little car's position in the market</strong>. Expect more of the same technologies such as the <strong>1.2 liter MIVEC Inline-3 engine</strong>, <strong>CVT transmissions</strong>, <strong>DVD Navigation</strong> and other niceties to be packaged with the Mirage sedan, as well as <strong>the trunk space associated with 3-box designs</strong>.</p>
<p>The Mitsubishi Mirage sedan will reportedly be r<strong>evealed at the 2013 New York Auto Show in March</strong>.</p>
<p>Currently, the Toyota Vios (in production since late 2006) is the dominant force in the B-Car segment. Could the Mirage sedan challenge the class sales leader as well as industry sales leader? We'll find out later this year.</p></div></article>
