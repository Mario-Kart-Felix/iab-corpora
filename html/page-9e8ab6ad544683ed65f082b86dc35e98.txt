		<h2 class="article-title">
			<a href="http://lexusenthusiast.com/2009/08/03/a-lexus-hatchback-rendering-by-auto-express/" title="Permalink to Lexus Hatchback Rendering by Auto Express" rel="bookmark">Lexus Hatchback Rendering by Auto Express</a>
		</h2>
				<div class="article-info">
			<small>Posted by <a href="/about">krew</a> on August 3rd, 2009</small>
		</div>
				<hr />	
	</div>

	<div class="article-content">
		<p>Using the Lexus HS &#38; RX as inspiration, Auto Express <a href="http://www.autoexpress.co.uk/news/autoexpressnews/239416/luxury_lexus_baby_hatches_out.html">has put together</a> a rendering of what the upcoming Lexus CT compact hatchback may look like:</p>
<p><img src="http://lexusenthusiast.com/images/weblog/09-08-03-lexus-ct200h-rendering-1.jpg" alt="Lexus CT200h Rendering Front View" class="borderc" /></p>
<p><img src="http://lexusenthusiast.com/images/weblog/09-08-03-lexus-ct200h-rendering-2.jpg" alt="Lexus CT200h Rendering Rear View" class="borderc" /></p>
<p>I&#8217;m really impressed by the side-panel creasing, the Z jog in the sheet metal is quite stylish. Both the front and rear look too small for the body, but there are some nice design cues. In particular, I like how the rakish RX rear window is incorporated.</p>
<p>Very nicely done.</p>
<p>[Source: <a href="http://www.autoexpress.co.uk/news/autoexpressnews/239416/luxury_lexus_baby_hatches_out.html">Auto Express</a>]</p>
