		<h2 class="article-title">
			<a href="http://lexusenthusiast.com/2013/01/11/next-generation-lexus-is-wagon-coming-to-detroit-auto-show/" title="Permalink to Next-Generation Lexus IS Wagon Coming to Detroit Auto Show?" rel="bookmark">Next-Generation Lexus IS Wagon Coming to Detroit Auto Show?</a>
		</h2>
				<div class="article-info">
			<small>Posted by <a href="/about">krew</a> on January 11th, 2013</small>
		</div>
				<hr />	
	</div>

	<div class="article-content">
		<p><img src="http://lexusenthusiast.com/images/weblog/13-01-11-2014-lexus-is-wagon.jpg" alt="2014 Lexus IS Wagon" class="borderc" /></p>

<p>Is the 2014 Lexus IS going to be offered as a wagon? A post <a href="https://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=10151381325773534&amp;set=a.116982348533.100875.90671958533&amp;type=1&amp;theater">on the Lexus USA Facebook page</a> suggests the IS Sport Cross might be making a comeback:</p>

<p><img src="http://lexusenthusiast.com/images/weblog/13-01-11-lexus-is-sport-cross.jpg" alt="Lexus IS Sport Cross" class="borderc" /></p>

<blockquote>
  <p>Do you remember the ‘02 IS 300 Sport Cross with its style, utility and performance? Will we bring it back? Watch the 2014 IS premiere on Facebook in 6 days to find out!</p>
</blockquote>

<p>As exciting as an IS wagon might be, it seems unlikely Lexus would announce it so casually on Facebook &#8212; another strike against the idea is that a photo of the old Sport Cross is used in the post, and not an actual teaser image.</p>

<p>What do you think? Will Lexus bring out a new Sport Cross at the Detroit Auto Show? If not, should they?</p>

<p>(The above image is not the IS wagon, but <a href="https://twitter.com/shane_pinnell/status/289766322861129728">a photoshop by Shane Pinnell</a> <a href="http://jalopnik.com/5975142/here-is-a-2014-lexus-is-wagon">posted on Jalopnik</a>.)</p>

<p>[Source: <a href="https://www.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=10151381325773534&amp;set=a.116982348533.100875.90671958533&amp;type=1&amp;theater">Lexus USA</a>]</p>
