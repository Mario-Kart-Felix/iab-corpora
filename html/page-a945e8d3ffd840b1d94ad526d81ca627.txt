<a name="About AA Breakdown Cover"></a>
<h3 title="About AA Breakdown Cover">
<div class="displayInline">About AA Breakdown Cover</div>
</h3>
<h4 class="faqsQuestion">How does the AA compare with other breakdown organisations?</h4>
<p>As the UK's largest breakdown organisation, we fix more breakdowns by the roadside than anyone else. There's no charge for labour at the roadside and 95% of our members would recommend us to friends.
As a member you're also entitled to AA Rewards, which could save you more than the cost of your membership.
    </p>
<h4 class="faqsQuestion">What are the chances of you repairing my car at the roadside?</h4>
<p>We're able to help over 3.5 million people a year. We fix 4 out of 5 cars at the roadside using unique on-board computers to help diagnose faults when you've broken down. </p>
<h4 class="faqsQuestion">Will you come out if I'm the passenger rather than the driver?</h4>
<p>Yes, with AA Breakdown Cover you're always covered as a driver or a passenger. If you've got Personal membership (cover for you) this applies for any vehicle. With Vehicle membership (cover for your car), you're covered in the vehicle you registered when you bought your breakdown cover.</p>
<h4 class="faqsQuestion">How many times can I break down?</h4>
<p>You can call us out five times in one membership year (seven times with Joint membership or eight with Family membership) without incurring any call-out charges.</p>
<h4 class="faqsQuestion">How quickly can you reach me?</h4>
<p>If you break down, we've got the latest satellite technology to help find the shortest route to get to you. We also give priority to people in vulnerable situations. If you <a href="/motoring_advice/news/the-aa-in-your-pocket-free-iphone-and-android-app.html">download our app</a> we can locate you by using your mobile phone's GPS signal.
</p>
<h4 class="faqsQuestion">What's Silver and Gold membership?</h4>
<p>These are membership upgrades that provide breakdown cover enhancements to our loyal breakdown members. Please see <a href="/breakdown-cover/membership-benefits/index.html">Silver and Gold membership</a> for more information.</p>

<h3 title="Buying breakdown cover">
<div class="displayInline">Buying breakdown cover</div>
</h3>
<h4 class="faqsQuestion">How soon will I be covered if I apply online?</h4>
<p>Your Roadside Assistance begins immediately after your online application has been successfully processed. If you provide your email address we'll email you confirmation of your cover. Your documents and membership card(s) will be posted to you within 28 days.

Please note: Home Start, Relay and Stay Mobile benefits only start 24&nbsp;hours after your membership begins. Breakdown Repair Cover (parts and garage cover) begins 14&nbsp;days after your cover has started. </p>
<h4 class="faqsQuestion">Can I buy AA Breakdown Cover as a present for someone else?</h4>
<p>Yes you can, as long as you know their name and address. Please see <a href="/services/breakdowncover/giftmembership/index.jsp">Breakdown cover gift membership</a> for more information.</p>
<h4 class="faqsQuestion">Is my online payment secure?</h4>
<p>Yes. We use the highest form of encryption generally available to make sure your payment is safe.

Our secure-server software scrambles all the details you input before they are sent, so that only we can understand them. </p>

<a name="Existing breakdown customers"></a>
<h3 title="Existing breakdown customers">
<div class="displayInline">Existing breakdown customers</div>
</h3>
<h4 class="faqsQuestion">I've broken down but haven't received my membership card yet. What should I do?</h4>
<p>Don't worry, just call us on 0800 887766 if you break down. If you bought your breakdown cover online, please remember to quote the membership number that was emailed to you at the time. </p>
<h4 class="faqsQuestion">Can I change the way I pay for AA Breakdown Cover?</h4>
<p>Yes. We provide a choice of easy payment methods. When you renew you can choose to set up a direct debit or a continuous payment by credit or debit card. These options will save you time in the future as we'll renew your cover automatically at the end of the year. You can also make a one-off payment with either a credit or debit card when renewing.

We accept Visa and MasterCard. Alternatively, you can pay for your cover monthly directly from your bank account. Just call us on 0800 085 2721.</p>
<h4 class="faqsQuestion">Can I make changes to my breakdown cover?</h4>
<p>Yes. If you'd like to talk to someone about changing your cover, please call us on 0843&nbsp;316&nbsp;4444 or 0161&nbsp;332&nbsp;1789. To amend your Breakdown Repair Cover, please call 0800&nbsp;587&nbsp;5856.</p>
<h4 class="faqsQuestion">How do I complain about AA products or services?</h4>
<p>If you're unhappy with our service for any reason, we really want to hear from you. Please see the information on <a href="/aboutaa/complaints.html">what you need to do next</a>.</p>

<h3 title="Renewing your cover">
<div class="displayInline">Renewing your cover</div>
</h3>
<h4 class="faqsQuestion">How can I renew my breakdown cover?</h4>
<p>We'll send you a renewal invitation up to 10 weeks before your cover is due to expire. This will include details of your renewal, based on your existing level of cover, and the various ways to renew. If you're not already paying on a continuous credit card or annual direct debit basis,  you can choose a payment method to suit you. You'll soon be able to renew your cover online, but until then call us on 0800 085 2721.</p>
<h4 class="faqsQuestion">Will I keep my existing membership number when I renew my breakdown cover?</h4>
<p>Yes, your membership number will stay the same even if you've made changes to your cover.</p>
