<h2>Interior</h2>
<p><strong>2014 Ford Transit Connect Wagon</strong> is another family car debuted at LA auto Show 2012 that people can get from the American automaker, Ford. The new vehicle offers you with fun driving for the family during the holiday. This fuel efficient car can bring much comfort when spending time inside the cabin. You can take ranging from five you to seven passengers on the convenient compartment. There are three levels of trim served y the manufacturer. You can choose the XL, XLT and Level Titanium edition. The vehicle comes in two wheelbase lengths so that the driver and passengers can get ample of room on the cabin.</p>
<p><a href="http://autosupercars.com/2014-ford-transit-connect-wagon-review/2014-ford-transit-connect-wagon-dashboard" rel="attachment wp-att-313"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-313" alt="2014 Ford Transit Connect Wagon Dashboard" src="http://autosupercars.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/2014-Ford-Transit-Connect-Wagon-Dashboard.jpg" width="1024" height="640" /></a></p>
<p><a href="http://autosupercars.com/2014-ford-transit-connect-wagon-review/2014-ford-transit-connect-wagon-interior-design" rel="attachment wp-att-310"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-310" alt="2014 Ford Transit Connect Wagon Interior Design" src="http://autosupercars.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/2014-Ford-Transit-Connect-Wagon-Interior-Design.jpg" width="900" height="540" /></a></p>
<h2>Exterior</h2>
<p>The manufacturer claims that 2014 Ford Transit Connect Wagon is constructed based on the durability, agility and reliability. The innovative automobile is created by the compact Transit Connect van. There are three row seating arrangement when you take a sneak peek inside the compartment. The people who live in cold region will be benefited when riding Transit connect wagon since it is offered in electric glass defroster. The new car looks big and large from the outside since it can accommodate seven passengers. But it looks rugged and aggressive with the fabulous front and rear end.<a href="http://autosupercars.com/2014-ford-transit-connect-wagon-review/2014-ford-transit-connect-wagon-front-lamp" rel="attachment wp-att-314"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-314" alt="2014 Ford Transit Connect Wagon Front Lamp" src="http://autosupercars.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/2014-Ford-Transit-Connect-Wagon-Front-Lamp.jpg" width="1024" height="640" /></a></p>
<p><a href="http://autosupercars.com/2014-ford-transit-connect-wagon-review/new-2014-ford-transit-connect-wagon" rel="attachment wp-att-312"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-312" alt="New 2014 Ford Transit Connect Wagon" src="http://autosupercars.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/New-2014-Ford-Transit-Connect-Wagon.jpg" width="1024" height="640" /></a></p>
<p><a href="http://autosupercars.com/2014-ford-transit-connect-wagon-review/2014-ford-transit-connect-wagon-rims" rel="attachment wp-att-304"><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-304" alt="2014 Ford Transit Connect Wagon Rims" src="http://autosupercars.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/12/2014-Ford-Transit-Connect-Wagon-Rims.jpg" width="640" height="1024" /></a></p>
<h2>Engine</h2>
<p><em>2014 Ford Transit Connect Wagon</em> is available in two engine options. Both offer you with impressive fuel economy. The first engine comes at four cylinder option. It comes in the displacement of 2.5 liter. If you love with Ecoboost engine you can have it in 1.6 liter displacement. The latest one comes in the fuel economy rate at 30 miles per gallon when driving the car in the highway.</p>
<h2>Price</h2>
<p>2014 Ford Transit Connect Wagon is planned to go on sale in North American market in the 4th quarter of 2013. The official pricing has not been officially stated by the Ford Company.</p>
