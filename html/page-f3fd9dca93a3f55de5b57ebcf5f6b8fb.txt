<div class="PostContent">
<h1>Full Restoration Services</h1>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>A full <strong>auto restoration</strong> entails completely disassembling the vehicle and restoring each and every part and system.  This process limits ongoing maintenance and future restoration situations.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.precisioncarrestoration.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/06/DSC01822.jpg"><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-12275" style="margin: 10px; border: black 5px solid;" title="1966 Chevy II Nova" src="http://www.precisioncarrestoration.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/06/DSC01822-300x225.jpg" alt="1966 Chevy II Nova" width="300" height="225" /></a><a href="http://www.precisioncarrestoration.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/06/DSC05067.jpg"><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-12277" style="margin: 10px; border: black 5px solid;" title="1966 Chevy II Nova" src="http://www.precisioncarrestoration.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/06/DSC05067-300x225.jpg" alt="1966 Chevy II Nova" width="300" height="225" /></a><a href="http://www.precisioncarrestoration.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/06/DSC05060.jpg"><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-12276" style="margin: 10px; border: black 5px solid;" title="1966 Chevy II Nova" src="http://www.precisioncarrestoration.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/06/DSC05060-300x225.jpg" alt="1966 Chevy II Nova" width="300" height="225" /></a><a href="http://www.precisioncarrestoration.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/06/DSC05073.jpg"><img class="alignnone size-medium wp-image-12278" style="margin: 10px; border: black 5px solid;" title="1966 Chevy II Nova" src="http://www.precisioncarrestoration.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/06/DSC05073-300x225.jpg" alt="1966 Chevy II Nova" width="300" height="225" /></a></p>
<p>A full auto restoration primarily includes:</p>
<p><span><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Body</span><br style="text-decoration: underline;" /></span></p>
<ul>
<li>Disassemble and catalog every piece on vehicle</li>
<li>Pull body off frame (optional)</li>
<li>Media blast and restore frame (optional)</li>
<li>Media blast or mechanically/chemically strip vehicle</li>
<li>Metal repairs of all rust</li>
<li>Replace or replate all trim</li>
<li>Completely smooth out body of all waves and dents</li>
</ul>
<p><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Paint</span></p>
<ul>
