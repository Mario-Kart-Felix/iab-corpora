                                                                              <h1>Allied <em>On Your Side</em><sup><em>®</em></sup> Rewards – Roadside Assistance</h1>
        <p>When you've got roadside assistance coverage on your Allied auto insurance policy, you don't need to worry if you get stranded on the road. You're covered in any car – whether it's insured on your policy or not.</p>
        <p>Allied Insurance roadside assistance covers people, not cars. Every relative that lives with you* is covered, no matter what they're driving – and even when they're not driving. If a covered member of your household is a passenger in someone else's car, they can still use the Allied roadside assistance benefit.</p>
        <p>We offer two tiers of Roadside Assistance:</p>
        <h2>Roadside Assistance - Basic</h2>
        <ul>
          <li>Towing up to 15 miles</li>
          <li>Fuel delivery</li>
          <li>Lockout coverage</li>
          <li>Jump starts</li>
          <li>Flat tires</li>
          <li>Cross Country Perks</li>
          <li>Cost: About $10 per 6 month policy period</li>
        </ul>
        <h2>Roadside Assistance - Plus</h2>
        <ul>
          <li>Towing up to 100 miles</li>
          <li>Trip interruption coverage</li>
          <li>Turn-by-turn directions</li>
          <li>Pre-trip map routing </li>
          <li>Fuel delivery</li>
          <li>Lockout coverage</li>
          <li>Jump starts</li>
          <li>Flat tires</li>
          <li>Cross Country Perks</li>
          <li>Cost: About $20 per 6 month policy period</li>
          
        </ul>
        <p>*Excluded drivers are not covered.</p>
