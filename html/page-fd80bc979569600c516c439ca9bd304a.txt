            <p>At the New York Automotive and Diesel Institute, located in Queens County, New York, you can train to become a master certified Automotive, Truck, and Diesel Technician or a master certified Collision Repair Technician. The institute is one of the only Automotive Service Excellence certified auto body programs in all of New York City.<br />
 <br />
You can be admitted to the institute if above the age of 18, above 17 with parental consent, or above 16 ½ with a release letter from your high school. If you’re not yet a high school graduate or you lack a GED, the institute offers an independently administered standardized exam.<br />
 <br />
The institute provides several programs that range from 10 courses to 20, with the option of a full-time or part-time load. They include: <br />
*Master Automotive &amp; Diesel Technician<br />
*Master Automotive Technician<br />
*Certified Automotive Technician<br />
*Master Truck &amp; Diesel Technician<br />
*Certified Truck &amp; Diesel Technician<br />
*Master Certified Collision Repair Technician  <br />
<br />
All of these programs are designed to give you theoretical instruction and first-hand experience with automotive technology, as well as the skills to diagnose and service the automotive, truck, and diesel systems.<br />
 <br />
Once graduated from the institute, its Placement Department will aid you in your job search. Graduates have found work with companies like H.O. Penn, Mercedes Benz, Infiniti, U-Haul, New York Bus Services, and more.<br />
 <br />
Financial aid is available to those who qualify.</p>
