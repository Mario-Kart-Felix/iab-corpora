<h2 style="text-align:center;"><font size="6"><strong>Auto Body Technician: <br /><span></span>Hybrid Vehicle Repair &amp; Service</strong></font><br /></h2>
<div class="paragraph" style="text-align:left;display:block;"><font size="2">As the number of electric drive vehicles on the road grows, so will the need for auto body repairs to accident-damaged vehicles. Understanding how to evaluate and repair damaged components in the high voltage and propulsion systems will be increasingly critical skills for technicians in the shop. <br /><span></span><br /><span></span>  This one-day training includes:<br /><span></span>  </font><ul><li><font size="2">A brief overview of      electric drive vehicle identification, design, and operation.</font></li><li><font size="2">A hands-on      laboratory portion to cover component identification, location, service,      repair, removal, and reinstallation. </font></li><li><font size="2">Diagnostic testing      for determining when components need to be serviced or replaced. </font></li></ul>      </div>
