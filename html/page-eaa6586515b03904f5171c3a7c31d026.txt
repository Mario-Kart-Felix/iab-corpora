They come from the barns, garages and shops of off-road enthusiasts across the land where modifications have been done by hand to make vehicles capable of taming virtually any spot thats not paved. One thing serious off-roaders know is being prepared for the trails allows you to get back in one piece, and lets you slip out of jams that are hard to avoid. Because serious off-roading requires serious lighting, CARiD offers high-quality off-road lights that let you see farther than standard headlights allow, and in almost any direction.</p></div><div class="mobile_hide"><ul class="gallery"><li class="gallery_li"><a class="gallery_a" rel="gallery1565" href="/images/pages/off-road-lights/kc-rally-800-lights.jpg" title="KC HiLiTES® - Rally 800 Off-Road Lights"><img src="/images/pages/off-road-lights/kc-rally-800-lights_t.jpg" alt="KC HiLiTES® - Rally 800 Off-Road Lights" /><i></i></a></li><li class="gallery_li"><a class="gallery_a" rel="gallery1565" href="/images/pages/off-road-lights/lumen-off-road-light-bars.jpg" title="LUMEN® - Off-Road Light Bars"><img src="/images/pages/off-road-lights/lumen-off-road-light-bars_t.jpg" alt="LUMEN® - Off-Road Light Bars" /><i></i></a></li><li class="gallery_li"><a class="gallery_a" rel="gallery1565" href="/images/pages/off-road-lights/lumen-off-road-lights.jpg" title="LUMEN® - Off-Road Driving Lights"><img src="/images/pages/off-road-lights/lumen-off-road-lights_t.jpg" alt="LUMEN® - Off-Road Driving Lights" /><i></i></a></li><li class="gallery_li"><a class="gallery_a" rel="gallery1565" href="/images/pages/off-road-lights/westin-off-road-lights.jpg" title="WESTIN® - Off-Road Driving Lights"><img src="/images/pages/off-road-lights/westin-off-road-lights_t.jpg" alt="WESTIN® - Off-Road Driving Lights" /><i></i></a></li></ul><div class="clear"></div></div><div class="mobile_hide"><p>Unlike factory vehicle lighting, off-road lights can be pivoted in any direction you choose to see down a sudden drop off, to the sides to see around switchbacks, or when you need to look up from a dip.
