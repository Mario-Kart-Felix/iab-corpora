        <h1><font size="5">Antique, Classic &amp; Collector Car Photo Galleries</font></h1>
        <p><font size="4"><b>Automotive History<br>
          </b></font>With photos of over 1,000 collector cars it all adds up to 
          a brief, look at automotive history.</p>
        <p>It is a kind of fun look at some of the cars of the past. From the 
          the brass era, to the muscle cars of the 60s and beyond, we've got the 
          picture.</p>
        <p>We even throw in a few facts, figures, and specifications to make it 
          all more interesting. </p>
        <p>You can find photos of Mopar muscle cars, a Chrysler Airflow, the Dodge 
          Charger, Plymouth Barracudas and DeSotos.<br>
          <br>
          Or how about a picture of the antique Ford Model T, the Model A, the 
          Boss Mustang or the classic Lincoln Continental.<br>
          <br>
          From antique Chevrolet cars and pickup trucks to the classic 1959 Cadillac 
          and beyond, we've got snapshots of lots of GM cars and trucks for you. 
          Of course there is more than one picture of the All American Sports 
          Car, the Corvette.<br>
          <br>
          No collection of classic car pictures would be complete without a picture 
          of a Vintage Volkswagen. Beetle, Pickup, or Camper. And there is a VW 
          Bus or two, as well as some Karmann Ghias and custom VW's.<br>
          <br>
          There are are some really wonderful cars, like the classic <a href="http://www.hubcapcafe.com//ocs/pages01/kiss2501.htm">Kissel 
          Gold Bug</a>, the beautiful <a href="http://www.hubcapcafe.com/ocs/pages01/aubr3501.htm">Auburn 
          851 Speedster</a>, and a whole crowd of classic <a href="http://www.hubcapcafe.com/ocs/packard.htm">Packard</a> 
          and <a href="http://www.hubcapcafe.com/ocs/studebaker.htm">Studebaker</a> 
          automobiles.</p>
        <p>We even have photos of a few magnificent <a href="http://www.hubcapcafe.com/ocs/om_00001.htm">Rolls 
          Royce</a>, <a href="http://www.hubcapcafe.com/ocs/om_00001.htm">Bentley</a> 
          and <a href="http://www.hubcapcafe.com/ocs/acd.htm">Duesenberg</a> automobiles.
