	<h2>Find Diesel mechanic training schools</h2>
<p>From the trucks that transport goods to stores around the country to the buses that ferry passengers on a city's streets to oil platforms that drill wells to extract oil, diesel trucks and industrial equipment depend on diesel technology and the expert knowledge of diesel technicians to keep these vehicles and equipment up and running.<br /><br /> Diesel technology is undeniably powerful stuff and allows today's heavy trucks, tractors, and industrial equipment to do the heavy lifting in the commercial, agricultural, and industrial sectors. In addition, diesel technology is used in the transport sector in buses and trains. Increasingly, diesel technology is also used in passenger cars for the advantages that diesel engines have over gasoline-powered engines.<br /><br />To get into this exciting field, diesel mechanics and technology training programs will provide you with the knowledge and skills you will need to adequately service, diagnose, and repair a variety of diesel vehicles and specialized equipment that the economy and the world of industry need.</p>
