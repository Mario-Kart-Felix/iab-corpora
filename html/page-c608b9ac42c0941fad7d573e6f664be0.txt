if you've got a flat tire, or even accidentally lock your keys in the 
car.</b><br/><br/><br/>Do any of those situations sound familiar?  I've found myself in that scenario on more than one occasion!<br/><br/><br/>When

<p>The older we get, quite often the less resilient we are to being stuck 
on the side of the road for hours on end, waiting for help to arrive.  
It can really be a source of fear and anxiety for older drivers.<br/><br/><br/>AARP
 Hartford has partnered with Allstate to provide drivers 50 and over 
with a comprehensive roadside assistance package that covers all the 
bases for a small annual fee.<br/><br/><br/><b>Here's a list of the benefits that the AARP Roadside Assistance program has to offer you: </b><br/><br/></p><ul><li>The company provides 24/7 customer service to help you at once.<br/><br/></li><li>If
 you have a flat tire, AARP will send a team to change it. The team will
 also tow your car if necessary and boost or replace your dead battery. <br/><br/></li><li>Running
 out of fuel will give you headaches especially if the gasoline station 
is kilometers/miles away. The AARP team will offer emergency fuel supply
 delivered right to you. <br/><br/></li><li>AARP also provide 
services on emergency lockout.  They will replace your lock or your key 
if necessary (within the agreed upon limits).<br/><br/></li><li>The AARP roadside assistance coverage can extend to include any car whether you are the driver or the passenger. <br/><br/></li><li>A family member in your household can also be included in the AARP coverage for only $10 per year more. Excellent value!</li></ul><p><br/>In
 addition to the AARP Roadside Assistance Program, AARP Hartford is a 
company that offers insurance programs for your home and automobiles, 
specifically catering to older Americans. <br/><br/><br/>When 50 or older, 
unexpected expenses that may occur due to car accidents or home-related 
damages can cause a lot of unnecessary stress.  As such, it is the AARP 
Hartford's goal to help American seniors to be knowledgeable of some of 
the latest ways and trends that may help them ensure quality life. <br/><br/><br/></p><p>The
 AARP Hartford insurance company has had good standing in terms of

 Bridge and in 1937 were provided by the company. <br/><br/><br/></p><p>AARP Hartford is quick to help in times of tragedy.
