							<div class="title">About Hybrid Auto Cast</div>
							Hybrid Auto Cast (HAC) is an ISO 9001 : 2000 certified company. Within a span of 6 years, since its inception in 1998, HAC has earned a reputation as manufacturer of quality die-castings. HAC unit has reputed horizontal cold champer die casting machines of capacities 80 T, 120 T and 400 T
. All the machines have electrical for efficient and pollution free melting. HAC is conscious of environment safeguards.<br><br>
							HAC is investing in development of intricate castings conforming to international quality standards with the experienced and expertise acquired. HAC is proud of its technical man power and their depth of managerial and technical expertise that inspires confidence amongst the customers. HAC is always geared to meet the challenges posed by growing market with the spirit of enterprise. <br><br>
							<b class="blu">Core Competence</b><br>
							High pressure die casting in the weight range from 5gms to 1.2 Kgs, with a minimum wall thick of 1.3mm, with good surface finish and a high dimentional accuracy (ISO IT 11-14) with machining and powder coating.<br>
