Students will receive training in components such as transmissions, electrical systems, brakes, heating and air conditioning, general maintenance and more. Training in electronics is also a valuable part of the auto mechanic training program. With an accredited education you will obtain the knowledge and skills to work in a variety of setting including repair shops, automotive dealerships, manufacturers, and more.<br /><h3>Diesel Mechanic Online</h3>Studying for a degree in diesel technology will teach you to work on large <a href="http://www.EducationPETAP.org/categories/OSD/online-transportation-schools-colleges.cfm">transportation</a> equipment, such as buses, heavy trucks, locomotives and construction equipment. Course work will cover subjects like jacks and hoists, computerized testing equipment, welding, and electrical and engine testing equipment. With a degree in automotive technology you can become a diesel technician for public transit companies, freight trucking companies, repair shops or even the government. Hands on training through an <a href="http://www.EducationPETAP.org/categories/VSD/automotive-diesel-schools-colleges.cfm">automotive vocational school</a> or community college, is also available through some online schools to provide you the practice and experience needed to become an automotive service technician.<br /><h3>Motorcycle Mechanic Online</h3>Motorcycle mechanic programs give students the technique and knowledge needed to repair, diagnose, service, and overhaul motorcycles, dirt bikes and street bikes. Online training programs for a motorcycle mechanic include engine <a href="http://www.EducationPETAP.org/categories/OSD/online-electronics-systems-technology-schools-colleges.cfm">electrical systems</a>, power transfer systems, hydraulics, and gasoline engines. Motorcycle technicians, parts salesmen, and service managers are just a few of the employment opportunities available to those with an automotive service technician certification.
