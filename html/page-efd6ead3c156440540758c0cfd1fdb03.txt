So exhaust systems dumped straight out and did not have any form of H or X pipe. However, racers eventually realized the tremendous benefits of having a balance tube between the pipes of a dual exhaust system. With tremendous gains in power, especially in the low end, S10 crossover pipes are one of the most cost effective ways to boost horsepower and torque for you ride. Whether you are an all-out racer or simply a performance enthusiast, crossover pipes are a must have item to maximize your machine.
