<h3>Service Center Details and Benefits</h3>
		<p><font color="#008000"><b>=&gt;</b></font> <b>Vehicle Plans</b> - follow 
		the vehicle regardless of who is driving.</p>
		<p><br>
		<font color="#008000"><b>=&gt;</b></font> <b>Individual Plans</b> - follow 
		the person regardless of the vehicle they are in. <br>
&nbsp;</p>
		<p><strong>Customer Satisfaction</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li>All calls answered in the United states - you will never be transferred overseas nor will you ever get voicemail.</li>
  <li>We maintain an average 30 minute response time nationwide from when you call the dispatch center to when help arrives.</li>
  <li>All customer service professionals have a minimum of 3 years' experience.</li>
	<li>We support English, Spanish and French speaking customers.</li>
	<li>Our services are available 24 hours per day, 365 days a year.</li>
	<li>We donât use your address ( email or personal) to send you junk mail like other companies do.</li>
</ul>
<p align="justify"><strong>24 Hour Emergency Towing Service: </strong> Best Roadside Service operates a 24 Hour emergency tow service when your vehicle breaks down or if you are stranded on the road due to a mechanical issue. If you have the individual plan, our tow service will cover you in any vehicle you are driving or riding in. If you have the vehicle plan, our tow service will cover the vehicle or motorcycle regardless of who is driving.</p>
<p align="justify">&nbsp;</p>
<p align="justify">Towing is for roadside assistance emergencies only. The plan tows up to 75 miles, to the nearest repair facility. Service does not apply to previously disable vehicles and there is only one tow per disablement.</p>
<p align="justify">&nbsp;</p>
<p align="justify"> <strong>24 Hour Battery Jumpstart Assistance:</strong> If your battery goes dead or fails, we will send a technician to jumpstart the covered vehicle.</p>
<p align="justify">&nbsp;</p>
<p align="justify"><strong>24 Hours Flat Tire Repair Service:</strong> Best Roadside Service provides urgent roadside flat tire service for covered vehicles so you are never stranded by the side of the road no matter day or night. Just one phone call and we will send a technician to install a drivable spare on the vehicle, get you back on the road again and then the member can drive safely to get the flat tire repaired. It is subscribers responsibility to have a properly inflated, working spare with their vehicle at all times. </p>
<p align="justify">&nbsp;</p>
<p align="justify"><strong>24 Hour Lockout Service:</strong> Should you get locked out of your vehicle, we will send a technician with manufacturer approved tools to get you back into your vehicle and on the road again. Lock out service is 24 hours a day and the coverage is up to $100 per occurrence.</p>
<p align="justify">&nbsp;</p>
<p align="justify"><strong> </strong><strong>24 Hour Lost Key Service:</strong> If you lose your keys to the covered vehicle, Best Roadside Service allows up to $100 reimbursement for the fabrication of a temporary key. This lost key service is only available for covered vehicles that have a key system that can be duplicated by a certified locksmith.</p>
<p align="justify">&nbsp;</p>
<p align="justify"><strong>24 Hour Fuel Delivery:</strong> If your covered vehicle should run out of gasoline, we will dispatch a service technician to deliver fuel to get you back on the road again. Our 24 hour fuel delivery service includes 3 gallons of fuel delivered to you free of cost.</p>
<p align="justify">&nbsp;</p>
<p align="justify"><b>24 Hour Winching/Extrication:</b> Entitles you up to $50.00 maximum benefit to be used if your vehicle needs to be extracted/winched out. 
Vehicle must not be more than 15 feet from a regularly maintained roadway.</p>
<p align="justify">&nbsp;</p>
<p align="justify"><strong>24 Hour Emergency Personal Assistance:</strong> Our customer service and support team, based in the United States, is always here to assist you no matter what your needs are.  If you need messages relayed to loved ones during any weather related or roadside emergency, our staff will send and receive messages for you. We can make calls for you on your behalf and take the stress away from any roadside emergency. If you need us, we will be there for you-24 hours, 365 days a year!</p>
<p align="justify">&nbsp;</p>
<p align="justify"> <strong>24 Hour Trip and Routing Service:</strong> Your membership includes customized trip planning and routing services. We can help you get where you want to go to reach your destination safely and efficiently.  If you ever get lost, just call our emergency roadside assistance hotline and we can give you turn âby- turn directions, 24 hours a day, any holiday and 365 days a year!</p>
<p align="justify">&nbsp;</p>
<p align="justify"><strong>24 Hours Concierge Service:</strong> When you are traveling, it can be difficult to manage car rental service or book a hotel room. We offer 24 hour concierge service and make sure that all hotel and rent-a-car arrangements are made in time.Whatever you need, you can call us. Thatâs why we are hereâto help you.</p>
<p align="justify">&nbsp;</p>
<p align="justify"><strong>Discounts for Travel and Hotel: </strong>Save on travel arrangements and trips! We offer discount programs and attractive travel discounts for our members.  We also offer daily discount packages for vacations.</p>
		<p>&nbsp;</p>
		<p><strong>Service Limitations</strong></p>
<ul>
	<li>Individual plans have 4 calls per plan; all others have 6 calls per plan.</li>
	<li>On couple/family plans, members must reside at the same address.</li>
	<li>Subscriber must be with the disabled vehicle in order to receive service. (However if you cannot remain with the vehicle for safety reasons, we will attempt to provide service).</li>
	<li>Service plan may be transferred to a different vehicle in the case of the sale of the vehicle or the salvage of the vehicle.  In both cases proper documentation is required in order to transfer service to a newly purchased vehicle.  Member of record must send email to 
	<a href="mailto:info@bestroadside.com">info@bestroadside.com</a> and advise us of new  and old vehicle information. All transfers require a 7 calendar day waiting period before service is once again active.</li>
	<li>Service cannot be guaranteed on roads that are not regularly maintained including private property.</li>
	<li>Service does not cover installation or removal of snow tires, chains nor dismounting, rotating or repairing tires. Flat service covers swapping out flat tire for drivable spare tire only. Subscriber must have a drivable spare with vehicle or trailer to receive the flat tire service.</li>
	<li>Service does not cover vehicles greater than 12,500 LBS Gross Vehicle Weight (GVW).</li>
	<li>Service does not cover vehicle storage charges, cost of parts, installation, products, materials, impounding and additional labor related to towing.</li>
	<li>Service cannot be provided to vehicles with expired safety inspection, license plate and or emission sticker where required by law OR to vehicles not legally registered.</li>
	<li>Couple/Family plans share the available service calls.</li>
	<li>This plan is for consumer/personal use only. No commercial vehicles are covered under these plans. If member/members are/is riding and/or driving a commercial vehicle (any vehicle used for company/business/commercial use-service will be denied).</li>
	<li>Service cannot be provided to impounded vehicles.</li>
	<li>Service will be refused if the vehicle is not in a safe condition to be towed.</li>
	<li>Service cannot be provided in areas not regularly traveled such as vacant lots, beaches, open fields or other areas that would be hazardous for service.</li>
	<li>Delivery of repaired tires is not included in service.</li>
	<li>Service does not include installation of ANY parts other than replacing a flat tire with a usable spare.</li>
	<li>Towing vehicles to a junkyard or location of disposal is not included in service.</li>
	<li>Towing service is for emergency roadside occurrences only.</li>
	<li>Towing or service while at an auto repair shop or service station, to another location.</li>
	<li>Service when a vehicle is snowbound. We do not hoist, winch or shovel vehicles from unplowed areas, snow banks, snowbound driveways or curbside parking.</li>
	<li>Towing to home is not covered.</li>
	<li>Accident towing is not a covered benefit.<strong></strong></li>
	<li>Refunds given on a pro-rata basis on unused, full months only, less a $10 administrative fee.  No refunds will be given if any service call was used and expenses incurred.<strong></strong></li>
</ul>
