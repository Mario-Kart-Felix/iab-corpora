<p style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0"><b>
<span style="font-family: Arial">How Hybrids Get Such Great 
Gas Mileage</span></b><span style="font-size:9.0pt;font-family:
Arial"> </span></p>
<p style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0">
<span style="font-size:9.0pt;font-family:Arial">It is no 
accident that the most fuel efficient vehicles in some classes 
for the 2006 model year are hybrid-electric vehicles (HEVs). 
Hybrids can be configured in many different ways to achieve 
a variety of different objectives. They combine the best 
features of the internal combustion engine with an electric 
motor and can significantly improve fuel economy without 
sacrificing performance or driving range. HEVs may also 
be configured to provide electrical power to auxiliary loads 
such as power tools.</span></p>
<p style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0">
<span style="font-size:9.0pt;font-family:Arial">HEVs are 
primarily propelled by an internal combustion engine, just 
like conventional vehicles. However, they also convert energy 
normally wasted during coasting and braking into electricity, 
which is stored in a battery until needed by the electric 
motor. The electric motor is used to assist the engine when 
accelerating or hill climbing and in low-speed driving conditions</span></p>
<p style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0">
<span style="font-size:9.0pt;font-family:Arial">&nbsp;where 
internal combustion engines are least efficient. Some HEVs 
also automatically shut off the engine when the vehicle 
comes to a stop and 
restart it when the accelerator is pressed. This prevents 
wasted energy from idling. Unlike all-electric vehicles, 
HEVs now being offered do not need to be plugged into an 
external source of electricity to be recharged; conventional 
gasoline and regenerative braking provide all the energy 
the vehicle needs. You can learn more about HEVs in the
<a class="body" target="_self" href="technology/technology.html">
<span style="font-size: 9pt">technology</span></a> section.</span></p>
<p style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0">
<span style="font-size:9.0pt;font-family:Arial">Potential 
buyers should also be aware that the federal government 
is currently offering tax incentives for HEVs and other 
alternative fuel vehicles. Some states also offer incentives.
</span></p>
