<h1 class="bigger_featured_article_title">2007 Nissan Versa Hatchback Review</h1>

<p><a href=/Nissan-Versa-pricequotes.html"><img src="/wp-content/themes/bad/images/reviews/NISVER01.JPG" border="0" align="right" hspace="3" alt="2007 Nissan Versa Hatchback" />
 </a>

The Nissan Versa is a compact hatchback with the attitude of its bigger brothers. It&#8217;s got one of the longest wheelbases in its class, and offers an interior fit and finish that is at the top of the heap. It&#8217;s powered by a 122-horsepower engine and set up on the platform of the European carmaker Renault&#8217;s Modus and Clio. Based on the Nissan Tiida sold in Australia and Japan, the Versa is all new to the U.S. and available either as a hatchback or sedan.</p>

<p><br/><strong>The BuyingAdvice Team Says:</strong></p>
<p>The Versa is one of Nissan&#8217;s freshest models in quite some time. Aimed at the fast growing subcompact market, the Versa offers a level of comfort and interior space to match any of its competitors. Its 4-cylinder engine has more than enough power to give this hip compact some zip while still delivering excellent fuel economy. The hatchback design maximizes cargo capacity, so vital in a small car. The Versa is the only model in its class with optional Bluetooth wireless capability. An excellent choice for young drivers or anyone on a tight budget.</p>

<p><br/><strong>What&#8217;s New For 2007:</strong></p>
Everything on the Versa is new for its first model year, 2007. It&#8217;s been built from the ground up to offer a comfortable, capable, and fuel-sipping option to the U.S. market, which has faced chaotic gasoline prices. It&#8217;s available in two trims, both powered by a 122-horsepower in-line 4-cylinder engine. The Versa is a front-wheel-drive vehicle and has a wealth of useful interior features available, such as Bluetooth wireless system, a Rockford Fosgate sound system with subwoofer, and Nissan&#8217;s Intelligent keyless entry. Upon introduction, the 1.8S trim has seating for five, AC, tilt steering column, a CD player, and 60/40 folding rear seats. The 1.8SL trim adds to these features cruise control, an optional leather steering wheel, audio controls mounted in the steering wheel, power accessories, and an upgraded sound system with 6-disc changer.<br /><br />

<p><strong>What is the Predicted Reliability:</strong></p>
<p>Our research indicates that the Nissan Versa will continue in Nissan&#8217;s long-established trend of average to above average mechanical integrity and reliability. But as it is a new model, the true long-term reliability of the Nissan Versa is unknown. However, drivers have been fairly happy so far. Each Nissan Versa is offered with a 3-year, 36,000 mile basic warranty.</p>

<p><br/><strong>Analysis Of Safety Ratings And Features:</strong></p>
<p>Safety is excellent in the Versa. In government crash tests, it received four out of five stars in front impact tests, and five out of five stars in side impact tests.  The Insurance Institute for Highway Safety gave the Versa its best &#8220;Good&#8221; rating for front, side, and rear collisions, which is almost unheard of.  Standard safety equipment on the Versa includes a tire pressure monitoring system, front and side airbags, and head-curtain airbags.  Anti-lock brakes are optional.  It is a compact car, so it is at risk if involved in a collision with larger vehicles. There are currently no major recalls for the 2007 Nissan Versa Hatchback.</p>

<p><br/><strong>Pros and Cons:</strong></p>
<p>Pros<br/>
+ It&#8217;s a fuel sipper<br/>
+ Convenient hatchback design<br/>
+ Interior fit and finish are excellent<br/>

<br/>
Cons<br/>
- Unknown reliability<br/>
- ABS not standard<br/>
- Road noise
</p>

<p><br/><strong>Head-To-Head Competition:</strong></p>
<p>Other fuel-thrifty hatchbacks include the <a href=/Toyota-Matrix-pricequotes.html">Toyota Matrix</a>, the <a href=/Scion-xB-pricequotes.html">Scion xB</a>, and the <a href=/Chevrolet-Aveo5-pricequotes.html">Chevrolet Aveo5</a>. The Matrix offers slightly more cargo capacity than the Versa, but costs almost $3,000 more and lacks the Versa&#8217;s side airbags. The Scion has ABS and traction control and power accessories standard, but costs about $1,500 more and doesn&#8217;t have the Versa&#8217;s side airbags either. Finally, the Aveo5 has a price tag just slightly less expensive than the Versa&#8217;s, but lacks the CD player and cargo capacity of Nissan&#8217;s newest hatchback.</p>

<p><br/><strong>What Others Are Saying:</strong></p>
<p>&#8220;The interior is simple in design, yet attractive and ergonomic; the dash cluster is easy to read and sharply displayed. The radio controls on the steering wheel are among the most useful I have seen. Unlike the hard plastic found within many of its competitors, the dash, door panels, and arm rests are softly padded and well-fitted.&#8221;<em> &#8211; <a href="http://www.freshalloy.com/site/cars/nissan/2007/versa/roadtest/default.asp" target="_blank">Fresh Alloy</a></em></p>

<p>&#8220;On the road, what you notice first about the Versa is that it feels large, &#8220;like a real car,&#8221; noted one driver. It&#8217;s as tall as the Dodge, in fact, with a commanding view forward&#8230;The light steering was informative, and the wheel was fat and soft. And it was the lone contestant here to offer six gears, making the most of the power band.&#8221;<em> &#8211; <a href="http://www.caranddriver.com/comparisons/10982/2007-nissan-versa-18sl.html" target="_blank">Car and Driver</a></em></p>

<p>&#8220;The Versa, a name intended to reflect the car&#8217;s versatility, will have a front-wheel-drive powertrain as new to the U.S. as the car itself&#8230;The standard transmission will be a six-speed manual. Optional will be a four-speed automatic and the latest evolution of Nissan&#8217;s continuously variable transmission.&#8221;<em> &#8211; <a href="http://www.newcartestdrive.com/sneakpreview.cfm?Vehicle=2007_Nissan_Versa&#038;ReviewID=89" target="_blank">New Car Test Drive</a></em></p><br/>

<p>Read more about the 2007 Nissan Versa Hatchback at the <a href="http://www.nissanusa.com/versa/" target="_blank">Nissan</a> manufacturer web site.</p></div>

function chkInt(field)
{
  if ((window.event.keyCode >= 48 && window.event.keyCode <= 57) || (window.event.keyCode == 8) || (window.event.keyCode == 9) || (window.event.keyCode == 63234) || (window.event.keyCode == 63235))
  {
    if (window.event.keyCode == 48 && field.value == "0")
     return false;
    else
     return true;
  } else
    return false;
}
