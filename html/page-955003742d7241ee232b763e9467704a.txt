H3R Performance brings aviation style Halotron&#174; 1 fire extinguishers to the automotive
enthusiast with HalGuard&#153; premium clean agent fire protection. <br><br>

				
HalGuard&#153; is no ordinary automotive fire extinguisher. It contains Halotron&#174; 1, electrically
non-conductive "clean" extinguishing agent that rapidly turns into a gas. Halotron&#174; 1 has passed extensive testing by Underwriters Laboratories, and meets FAA requirements. It can be found on commercial
aircraft, and is used by airport fire fighters and on the flight line.<br><br>
