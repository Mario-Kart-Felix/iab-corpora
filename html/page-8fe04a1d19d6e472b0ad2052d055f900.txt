<h2>Definition</h2>
</div>
<div>An <a onclick="pageTracker._trackPageview(&#39;/outgoing/links-inside-definition.com&#39;)" href="http://www.businessdictionary.com/definition/organization.html">organization</a> or <a onclick="pageTracker._trackPageview(&#39;/outgoing/links-inside-definition.com&#39;)" href="http://www.businessdictionary.com/definition/economic-system.html">economic system</a> where <a onclick="pageTracker._trackPageview(&#39;/outgoing/links-inside-definition.com&#39;)" href="http://www.businessdictionary.com/definition/goods-and-services.html">goods and services</a> are exchanged for one another or for <a onclick="pageTracker._trackPageview(&#39;/outgoing/links-inside-definition.com&#39;)" href="http://www.businessdictionary.com/definition/money.html">money</a>. Every business requires some <a onclick="pageTracker._trackPageview(&#39;/outgoing/links-inside-definition.com&#39;)" href="http://www.businessdictionary.com/definition/form.html">form</a> of <a onclick="pageTracker._trackPageview(&#39;/outgoing/links-inside-definition.com&#39;)" href="http://www.businessdictionary.com/definition/investment.html">investment</a> and enough <a onclick="pageTracker._trackPageview(&#39;/outgoing/links-inside-definition.com&#39;)" href="http://www.businessdictionary.com/definition/customer.html">customers</a> to whom its <a onclick="pageTracker._trackPageview(&#39;/outgoing/links-inside-definition.com&#39;)" href="http://www.businessdictionary.com/definition/output.html">output</a> can be sold on a <a onclick="pageTracker._trackPageview(&#39;/outgoing/links-inside-definition.com&#39;)" href="http://www.businessdictionary.com/definition/consistent.html">consistent</a> basis in <a onclick="pageTracker._trackPageview(&#39;/outgoing/links-inside-definition.com&#39;)" href="http://www.businessdictionary.com/definition/order.html">order</a> to make a <a onclick="pageTracker._trackPageview(&#39;/outgoing/links-inside-definition.com&#39;)" href="http://www.businessdictionary.com/definition/profit.html">profit</a>. Businesses can be privately owned, not-for-profit or state-owned. An example of a corporate business is PepsiCo, while a mom-and-pop catering business is a <a onclick="pageTracker._trackPageview(&#39;/outgoing/links-inside-definition.com&#39;)" href="http://www.businessdictionary.com/definition/private-enterprise.html">private enterprise</a>.</div>
