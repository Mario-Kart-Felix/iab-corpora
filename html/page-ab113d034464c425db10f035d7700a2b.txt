				<strong>Every day, we give you photos and videos that show how much a set of custom shoes, and sometimes an aftermarket suspension, can change a car&rsquo;s identity, so for today we wanted to bring you something that&rsquo;s truly unusual - a minivan.</strong><br />
<br />
The first vehicle that came to our minds was the Toyota Sienna, since the automotive producer itself showed us the custom face of this model at <a href="/news/2010-sema-toyota-sienna-swagger-wagon-supreme-25932.html" title="2010 SEMA: Toyota Sienna Swagger Wagon Supreme">SEMA</a>. We came across the adjacent video, which shows us a Sienna that had been fitted with an air suspension, which allows the driver to cater to both family hauling and car show attendance needs.<br />
<br />
This Swagger Wagon (this is how Toyota called the aforementioned custom Sienna) rides on a set of Vossen VVS-CV3 wheels, which use a concave, five-spoke design to grab attention. Both axles use 20x10.50inch rims, which come in a Matte Silver Machined finish.				<!-- google_ad_section_end -->
