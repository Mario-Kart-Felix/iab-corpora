  <h1>
    <a href="index.shtml">SIUE School of Business</a>
  </h1>
</div>
<div> </div>

                <div class="bodytext">
                        <p ></p>
<table align="right" border="0" cellpadding="7" cellspacing="7" width="1%">
  <tr>
    <td valign="top">
      <img alt="business" height="218" src="img/business.jpg" width="328" />
    </td>

<hr />
<p>Take charge of your future with an SIUE business degree. With so many exciting programs, you can explore opportunities in</p>
<ul>
  <li> 
  <a href="http://www.siue.edu/business/accounting/">Accounting</a></li>
  <li>
    <a href="http://www.siue.edu/business/cmis/">Computer Management and Information Systems</a>
  </li>
  <li>
    <a href="http://www.siue.edu/business/econfin/">Economics and Finance</a>
  </li>
  <li>
    <a href="http://www.siue.edu/business/mgtmkt/">Management and Marketing</a>
  </li>
</ul>
<p>We also offer 
<a href="http://www.siue.edu/mba">MBA</a> and graduate programs in business tailored to fit your busy schedule.</p>
<p>
<a href="http://www.siue.edu/business/alumni/">School of Business alumni</a> lead corporations in the St. Louis region, nationally and around the world. Our graduates tell us that the School of Business prepared them to be leaders. In turn, many of our alumni give their time and expertise to act as mentors for you, our students.</p>
<p>Your SIUE degree stands out, with 
<a href="http://www.siue.edu/business/about/accreditation.shtml">AACSB International-accredited</a> programs in the School of Business and our accountancy program. Only 10 percent of business schools in the world have achieved this prestigious accreditation.</p>
<p>Quality faculty and academic programs mean high standards in the classroom which means you leave SIUE prepared to tackle a rewarding career.  Also, your SIUE education comes at a cost you can afford.</p>
<p>SIUE's 
<a href="http://www.siue.edu/apply/tuition/" target="_blank">tuition</a> is the lowest among Illinois four-year universities.</p>
<p>Don't take our word for it. Check us out for yourself.</p>
<ul>
  <li>Learn more about 
  <a href="prospective/pdf/UGAdmissionInformationBulletin2012.pdf" target="_blank">admission to a School of Business undergraduate program</a>.  (pdf)</li>
  <li>Come to our Fall 
  <a href="OpenHouse.shtml" target="_blank">2013 Explore Academic Excellence Day</a> (our annual Visit Day) on Saturday, November 2, 2013 to learn more about our Undergraduate programs. </li>
  <li>Apply for undergraduate or graduate 
  <a href="http://www.siue.edu/apply" target="_blank">admission to SIUE</a>. </li>
</ul>
