									<p>The Honda Civic was launched in mid-1972 for the 1973 model year as a two-door coupe; the hatchback followed by the end of the year. The hatchback was sold in foreign markets as the Ballade, the Domani and the Acura EL. Initial engine displacement was 1169cc. The car had front-wheel drive. The Civic remained a subcompact car until 2001, when it was enlarged to compact dimensions. Through the 2000s the Civic was consistently upgraded with up-market appointments and luxury-style options. For the 2006 model year, the hatchback was dropped, according to Edmunds.com and Automotive.com.</p>								</div>

The move elevated the Civic hatchback from subcompact to compact status while still maintaining its tidy, efficient exterior size.</p>								</div>
