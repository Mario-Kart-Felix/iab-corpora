<p align="left" style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0"><font color="#FFFFFF" size="4">The Northstar
Gallery
presents a collection of beautiful fine art
photographs exploring the beauty, sensuality,
form and
design of&nbsp; classic motor cars and vintage automobiles.&nbsp;The automobile
is a powerful symbol that has stirred the dreams and ambitions of men and women
for 100 years. Cars created of passion, remain even more alluring today. They
call us to understand their hold on us and their important contribution to the
human experience. &quot;The Art of the Car&quot; is an exploration of the
multiple and complex roles that classic automobiles have played in our lives and
is a celebration of the automobiles enduring beauty.</font> &nbsp;</p>

<p align="left" style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0">&nbsp;</p>

<p align="left" style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0"><font color="#FFFFFF" size="4">Individual
fine art prints may be
produced from any of the images in the Northstar Galleries. Automotive art, especially in mural size
makes a stunning presentation that adds beauty and style to your home, business or office environment.&nbsp;For
information on purchasing automotive art please click <a href="index%20automotive%20art.htm">here</a>.</font></p>

<p align="left" style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0">&nbsp;</p>

<p align="left" style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0"><font color="#FFFFFF" size="4">Signed and beautifully framed print sets are also available for business applications.
All color prints are the finest quality fine art photographs available
and are printed on Fuji Crystal Archive
photographic paper. Additional
print options and fees may be viewed by clicking <a href="../pages/Order.htm">here</a></font></p>

<p align="left" style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0">&nbsp;</p>

<p align="left" style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0"><font color="#FFFFFF" size="4">The artist also offers
commissioned, commercial and editorial automotive
photography. Please call to discuss your project. Information on commissioned
fine art photography of your classic car at your site is available by clicking <a href="index%20commissioned%20photography.htm">here</a>.</font></p>

<p align="left" style="margin-top: 0; margin-bottom: 0">&nbsp;</p>
