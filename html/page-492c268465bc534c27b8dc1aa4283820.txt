<p>Chinese carmaker JAC (Jianghuai Automobile Co Ltd) will be bringing three new cars to <a href="http://paultan.org/2013/04/11/citroen-ds-wild-rubis/" target="_blank">Auto Shanghai 2013</a>, which will be happening later this week. One is an MPV, one a SUV, and the other is a compact sedan. Honestly, the only reason we&#8217;re interested is because they all look VERY familiar.</p>
<p>The JAC Refine M6 MPV is rumoured to be powered by a 2.0 turbo engine, with a 1.9 diesel also on offer. Six speed manuals are the norm, but the gasoline engine can be had with a five-speed auto, too. The sedan, tentatively named HeYue A20, has a 2,490 mm wheelbase and 1.3L/1.5L VVT options. A CVT gearbox is available. Lastly, the HeYue S30 SUV (2,560 mm wheelbase) can be had with 1.5T and 1.8L engines.</p>
<p>But who cares, just look at them! I&#8217;m pretty sure the <a href="http://paultan.org/2011/05/06/interview-casey-hyun-creative-design-manager-at-hyundai-motor-company-and-designer-of-the-sonata-yf/" target="_blank">folks behind Fluidic Sculpture</a> will be having a great laugh seeing this &#8211; we did! Sorry, no prizes for naming the right inspiration :)</p>
