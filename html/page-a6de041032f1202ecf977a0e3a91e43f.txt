<h2 class="thethe-tabs-group-title">Specifications</h2><div id="thethe-tabs-1" class=" thethe-tabs-group"><ul><li class="thethe-tab"><a href="#thethe-tabs-1-1">Interior</a></li><li class="thethe-tab"><a href="#thethe-tabs-1-2">Ride and comfort</a></li><li class="thethe-tab"><a href="#thethe-tabs-1-3">The Bottom Line</a></li></ul><div id="thethe-tabs-1-1"></p>
<p>The interior boasts useful features compared to the later variants; the futuristic digital tachometer with blue light gradient still sports this model.  The two-tier dashboard display is unfriendly to look at as the driver would have to look up or down to monitor speed.  The side cover of the door are replaced with plastics (a lighter material compared to the soft leather-like material on the previous models.)  Plastic sidings tend to become brittle as it ages and prone to light scratches, plus the feeling of luxury has been diminished.</p>

</a></p>
<p></div><div id="thethe-tabs-1-2"></p>
<p>
The front suspension is still equipped with McPherson Strut-Type with Stabilizer; this gives a rough and noisy ride because it transmits road noise to the body shell due to its engineering design compared to the comfort ride of a double wishbone suspension.  Even with the poor ride handling of the McPherson Strut, it has become ubiquitous among car manufacturers because of its low cost.</p>
<p><div id="attachment_1270" class="wp-caption aligncenter" style="width: 210px"><a href="http://www.manilaautohub.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/images-2.jpeg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-1270" title="McPherson Strut" src="http://www.manilaautohub.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/images-2-200x150.jpg" alt="McPherson Strut" width="200" height="150" /></a><p class="wp-caption-text">McPherson Strut</p></div></p>
<p><a href="http://www.manilaautohub.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/02/img-drive-smart02.jpg"><br />
</a>
</p>
<p></div><div id="thethe-tabs-1-3"><br />
Honda civic lineups have been tested to be much better than the competition, but for the last few years, other automakers introduced some impressive models that outclass this unit (competing strongly in this class are Toyota Altis, Chevy Cruze and Hyundai Elantra.)</p>
<p>While this latest 2012 model is a good choice, I wonder if the redesign can sustain its class leadership among the latest models of other automakers.   Consumers are becoming practical and Honda Civic would need to convince the buyers as other automakers offers same comfort ride, powertrains, advanced safety features, and better interior designs and technology options.</p>
