            </div><h2>Verify Safety, Mitigate Failure, and Obtain ETL Certification for Your EV-Related Battery Systems</h2><p>Bring your automotive battery technologies to market quickly and cost-effectively. When adapting your battery packs, cells, chargers, service disconnects, and wires harnesses for EV and HEV applications, we can help you verify all related parts and products for distribution via global supply chains. With battery testing laboratories located throughout the world*, we help you secure <a href="/marks/etl/" title="ETL Certification">ETL Certification</a> in accordance with all major OEM and industry standards, as well as requirements from the National Electrical Code (NEC) and custom test standards. </p>
<ul>
<li>Battery Testing to UL 1642, UL 2054</li>
<li>Electric Vehicle Testing to <a href="/automotive/electric-vehicle/evse/sae-j1772/" title="SAE J1772">SAE J1772</a>, SAE J2464</li>
<li>Electric Vehicle Battery Testing to IEC 60086-1, 60086-2, 60086-3</li>
<li>CTIA Accredited Battery Testing to IEEE 1725, IEEE 1625</li>
<li>Failure Analysis and Battery Safety Investigations</li>
<li>FreedomCAR Electrical Energy Storage System Abuse Test Manual for Electric and Hybrid Electric Vehicle Applications</li>
<li>Nordic Ecolabel Testing (White Swan)</li>
<li>Sandia National Laboratories Electrochemical Storage System Abuse Test Procedure Manual Requirements (SAND 99-0497)</li>
<li>UN Transportation Testing for Lithium Batteries (<a href="/energy-storage/un-transportation-testing/" title="UN/DOT 38.3">UN/DOT 38.3</a>)</li>
<li>USABC Electric Vehicle Battery Test Procedure Manual Requirements</li>
</ul>
<p>Learn about our <a href="/automotive/electric-vehicle/battery-performance-testing/" title="battery performance testing and validation services">battery performance testing and validation services</a>.</p>
