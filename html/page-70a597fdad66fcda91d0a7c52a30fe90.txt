			<h1 class="main_title">Green Technology &amp; Alternative Fuels</h1>
			
			<div class="tech_cat dotted flc" style="margin: 10px 0px 0px 0px;">
			The desire to lessen environmental impacts and reduce oil dependence is spurring interest in alternative fuels and green technologies. Explore Green Car's <strong>Green Technology &amp; Alternative Fuels</strong> section to learn more about green vehicles and the alternative fuels and technologies that power them.
			</div>
			
			<div class="tech_cat dotted flc">							
				<a href="/tech-hybrids.php" class="tech_left " rel="NOFOLLOW"><img src="http://cdn-www.greencar.com/layout_images/tech/hybrids.jpg" rel="nofollow" alt="Hybrids"  /></a>				
				<div class="tech_right ">
					<div class="tech_title">
						<h2><a href="/tech-hybrids.php">Hybrids</a></h2>
					</div>
					<p>Hybrids use two or more power systems, such as a gasoline engine and an electric drive motor, to provide more efficient propulsion. Learn more about hybrids and what distinguishes them from other vehicles in Green Car</p>					
					<a href="/tech-hybrids.php"> More on Hybrids</a>
				</div>
			</div>
			<div class="tech_cat dotted flc">							
				<a href="/tech-electric-cars.php" class="tech_left " rel="NOFOLLOW"><img src="http://cdn-www.greencar.com/layout_images/tech/electric-cars.jpg" rel="nofollow" alt="Electric Cars"  /></a>				
				<div class="tech_right ">
					<div class="tech_title">
						<h2><a href="/tech-electric-cars.php">Electric Cars</a></h2>
					</div>
					<p>Electric cars use one or more electric drive motors, powered by batteries, for zero-emission motoring. Electric cars are recharged by plugging into the grid.</p>					
					<a href="/tech-electric-cars.php"> More on Electric Cars</a>
				</div>
			</div>
			<div class="tech_cat dotted flc">							
				<a href="/tech-ethanol.php" class="tech_left " rel="NOFOLLOW"><img src="http://cdn-www.greencar.com/layout_images/tech/ethanol.jpg" rel="nofollow" alt="Ethanol"  /></a>				
				<div class="tech_right ">
					<div class="tech_title">
						<h2><a href="/tech-ethanol.php">Ethanol</a></h2>
					</div>
					<p>Ethanol is an alcohol-based alternative fuel made from biomass. As a popular alternative fuel, ethanol is typically used in the form of E85 to power flex fuel cars outfitted specifically to run on this blend of 85% ethanol and 15% gasoline.</p>					
					<a href="/tech-ethanol.php"> More on Ethanol</a>
				</div>
			</div>
			<div class="tech_cat dotted flc">							
				<a href="/tech-hydrogen-cars-fuel-cells.php" class="tech_left " rel="NOFOLLOW"><img src="http://cdn-www.greencar.com/layout_images/tech/hydrogen-cars-fuel-cells.jpg" rel="nofollow" alt="Hydrogen Cars & Hydrogen Fuel Cells"  /></a>				
				<div class="tech_right ">
					<div class="tech_title">
						<h2><a href="/tech-hydrogen-cars-fuel-cells.php">Hydrogen Cars & Hydrogen Fuel Cells</a></h2>
					</div>
					<p>Hydrogen cars are among the cleanest cars on the road, emitting oxygen and water vapor only.  Hydrogen is the cleanest burning of all liquid and gaseous alternative fuels.</p>					
					<a href="/tech-hydrogen-cars-fuel-cells.php"> More on Hydrogen Cars & Hydrogen Fuel Cells</a>
				</div>
			</div>
			<div class="tech_cat dotted flc">							
				<a href="/tech-natural-gas.php" class="tech_left " rel="NOFOLLOW"><img src="http://cdn-www.greencar.com/layout_images/tech/natural-gas.jpg" rel="nofollow" alt="Natural Gas"  /></a>				
				<div class="tech_right ">
					<div class="tech_title">
						<h2><a href="/tech-natural-gas.php">Natural Gas</a></h2>
					</div>
					<p>Natural gas is a clean-burning alternative fossil fuel that can easily power internal combustion engine vehicles. The domestic abundance of natural gas makes it a highly attractive alternative fuel option.</p>					
					<a href="/tech-natural-gas.php"> More on Natural Gas</a>
				</div>
			</div>
			<div class="tech_cat dotted flc">							
				<a href="/tech-plug-in-hybrids.php" class="tech_left " rel="NOFOLLOW"><img src="http://cdn-www.greencar.com/layout_images/tech/plug-in-hybrids.jpg" rel="nofollow" alt="Plug In Hybrids"  /></a>				
				<div class="tech_right ">
					<div class="tech_title">
						<h2><a href="/tech-plug-in-hybrids.php">Plug In Hybrids</a></h2>
					</div>
					<p>Plug in hybrids get high MPG, cover many miles on battery power alone, and include a gasoline engine to provide greater range as needed. Plug in hybrids are mostly recharged from the grid, but some plug-in hybrid models can generate electricity when using</p>					
					<a href="/tech-plug-in-hybrids.php"> More on Plug In Hybrids</a>
				</div>
			</div>
			<div class="tech_cat dotted flc">							
				<a href="/tech-biodiesel.php" class="tech_left " rel="NOFOLLOW"><img src="http://cdn-www.greencar.com/layout_images/tech/biodiesel.jpg" rel="nofollow" alt="Biodiesel"  /></a>				
				<div class="tech_right ">
					<div class="tech_title">
						<h2><a href="/tech-biodiesel.php">Biodiesel</a></h2>
					</div>
					<p>As a leading alternative fuel, biodiesel can be made from various sources including soybeans and biomass. Biodiesel can be used in most diesel powered cars without modification. </p>					
					<a href="/tech-biodiesel.php"> More on Biodiesel</a>
				</div>
			</div>
			<div class="tech_cat dotted flc">							
				<a href="/tech-air-powered-cars.php" class="tech_left " rel="NOFOLLOW"><img src="http://cdn-www.greencar.com/layout_images/tech/air-powered-cars.jpg" rel="nofollow" alt="Air Powered Cars"  /></a>				
				<div class="tech_right ">
					<div class="tech_title">
						<h2><a href="/tech-air-powered-cars.php">Air Powered Cars</a></h2>
					</div>
					<p>Air powered cars are relatively new to the green car scene. Compressed air is currently being explored as a viable 'alternative fuel' to efficiently power car engines with little or no environmental impact.</p>					
					<a href="/tech-air-powered-cars.php"> More on Air Powered Cars</a>
				</div>
			</div>
			<div class="tech_cat dotted flc">							
				<a href="/tech-future-cars-technology.php" class="tech_left " rel="NOFOLLOW"><img src="http://cdn-www.greencar.com/layout_images/tech/future-cars-technology.jpg" rel="nofollow" alt="Future Cars & Future Technology"  /></a>				
				<div class="tech_right ">
					<div class="tech_title">
						<h2><a href="/tech-future-cars-technology.php">Future Cars & Future Technology</a></h2>
					</div>
					<p>Find out more about future cars and the technology that powers them. What alternative fuel or new electric engine will power cars in the future? Read Green Car</p>					
					<a href="/tech-future-cars-technology.php"> More on Future Cars & Future Technology</a>
				</div>
