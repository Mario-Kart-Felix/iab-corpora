<p>	 

Whether you're on the road or in your own driveway, help is only a phone call away. When you add Westfield Insurance Roadside Assistance to your car insurance policy, you have towing and roadside assistance for covered vehicles, 24 hours a day, 365 days a year, and are covered throughout the United States and Canada. Help is just a toll-free phone call away. One of our roadside service professionals will quickly arrive to offer assistance, whether you lock yourself out of your car, have a flat tire or just run out of gas. </p>
 
<h2>Roadside assistance coverage provides*: </h2>
<ul>

	<li>Towing </li>
	<li>Winching</li>
	<li>Jump starts</li>
	<li>Lockout services</li>
	<li>Gas delivery</li>
	<li>Tire changes</li>

</ul>

<h2>Help is quick and easy</h2>
<p>For help, just call 877-787-9078. You don't need to worry about filling out forms or having cash, checks or credit cards on hand. No payment is needed at the scene, and in most cases no out-of-pocket expense is incurred. After a service professional provides assistance, all that's left to do is sign the bill.
</p>

<p>*See your policy for actual coverage amounts and details. </p>
