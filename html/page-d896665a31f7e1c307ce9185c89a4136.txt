<h1>32oz zMAX Multi-Purpose Formula</h1>
<p>The 32oz zMAX Multi-Purpose Formula is clearly labeled for easy installation and can also be installed by your mechanic at your next oil change.</p>
<p>1. ENGINE:&nbsp; Add 12 oz. to engine oil.<br />2. TRANSMISSION: (automatic or manual) Add 6 oz. to transmission fluid.<br />3. POWER STEERING:&nbsp; Add 2 oz. to power steering fluid.<br />4. FUEL SYSTEM:&nbsp; Add 12 oz. to fuel tank, gasoline or diesel.</p>
<p>zMAX does not replace your regular oil. Using zMAX will not overfill your normal oil level, because it soaks into metal. The 32oz zMAX Multi-Purpose Formula treatment is recommended every 6 months or 6,000 miles, whichever comes first. By properly replenishing zMAX, your engine will receive nonstop protection.</p>
<p><strong>zMAX does not void manufacturer's warranties.</strong></p>
<p><em><strong>Some vehicles have &ldquo;Sealed Transmissions&rdquo; (No place to fill or check the fluid level) which also means no place to add zMAX Transmission Formula. </strong></em><em><strong>Verify with your Owner&rsquo;s Manual if this applies to your vehicle.</strong></em></p>
<h1>For use in place of zMAX Heavy Duty Engine Formula</h1>
<p><strong><strong>This diesel fuel additive complies with the federal low sulfur content requirements for use in diesel motor vehicles and nonroad engines.</strong></strong></p>
<p><strong>Class 6, 7, &amp; 8 Heavy Duty Over the Road Vehicles:</strong>&nbsp;Up to 32 quart capacity, add 16 oz. (1/2 quart) of zMAX. For more than 32 quart capacity, add 32 oz. (1 quart) of zMAX. Retreat every 30,000 miles or every other oil change with a minimum of every 4 months.</p>
<p><strong>"Off-Highway" Vehicles &amp; Stationary Engines:</strong>&nbsp;Up to 32 quart capacity, add 16 oz. (1/2 quart) of zMAX. For more than 32 quart capacity, add 32 oz. (1 quart) of zMAX. Retreat every 250 hours or a minimum of every 4 months.</p>
<p><strong>Class 1-5 vehicles:</strong>&nbsp;Add 16 oz. of zMAX to engine oil. Retreat every 6,000 miles or every oil change with a minimum of every 4 months.</p>
<p><em><strong>Some vehicles have &ldquo;Sealed Transmissions&rdquo; (no place to fill or check the fluid level) which also means no place to add zMAX Transmission Formula.</strong></em>&nbsp;<em><strong>Verify with your Owner&rsquo;s Manual if this applies to your vehicle.</strong></em></p>
<h1>For use in place of zMAX Heavy Duty Fuel Formula</h1>
<p><strong><strong>This diesel fuel additive complies with the federal low sulfur content requirements for use in diesel motor vehicles and nonroad engines.</strong></strong></p>
<p><strong>Class 6, 7, &amp; 8 Heavy Duty Over the Road Trucks:</strong>&nbsp;For best results and for fuel capacities from 50 to 150 gallons per tank, add 1 qt. (32 oz.) of zMAX to each fuel tank in use. Retreat every 20,000 miles or a minimum of every 4 months.</p>
<p><strong>Class 1-5 Trucks:</strong>&nbsp;For best results, add 16 oz. (1/2 quart) of zMAX to the fuel tank in use. Retreat every 250 hours or a minimum of every 4 months.</p>
<p><strong>Off-Highway Vehicles &amp; Stationary Engines:&nbsp;</strong>From 10 to 30 gallons, add 16 oz. (1/2 quart). Over 30 gallons, add 32 oz. (1 quart) . Retreat every 6,000 miles or a minimum of every 4 months.&nbsp;<em>Caution: For diesel, do not treat less than 10 gallons.</em></p>
