	    <h1 style="font-size: 16px; margin: 5px 0 10px 0;">Hyundai Automotive South Africa launches roadside assistance offering to local market</h1>
	    <div style="margin: 10px 0 20px">
		    <table border="0" cellspacing="5">
			    <tr>
				    <td><strong>Date:</strong> <span id="ctl00_EngineContent_lblDate">22 Jun 2010</span></td>
				    <td style="padding: 0 0 0 20px;"><strong>Author Type:</strong> <span id="ctl00_EngineContent_lblAuthorType">Press Release</span></td>
			    </tr>
			    <tr>
				    <td colspan="2"><strong>Author:</strong> <span id="ctl00_EngineContent_lblAuthor">Hyundai</span></td>
			    </tr>
			    <tr>
				    <td colspan="2"><strong>Source:</strong> <span id="ctl00_EngineContent_lblSource">Hyundai</span></td>
			    </tr>
		    </table>
	    </div>    
	    <p style="text-align: justify; line-height: 150%" align="justify"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: Tahoma; font-size: 10pt">As of the 1</span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: Tahoma; font-size: 10pt"><sup>st</sup> of June 2010, Hyundai Automotive South Africa (HASA) have launched a full 24-hour roadside assistance pledge to all its customers on the back of its industry first 5 year/150 000km warranty. In line with HASA&#8217;s leading manufacturer warranty, this new roadside assistance programme has been extended to all local customers who purchase a new Hyundai. This means that when a customer acquires a new vehicle, they will receive a 5 year/150 000km manufacturer warranty, with 24 hour roadside assistance included in the package.&nbsp; </span></p>
<p style="text-align: justify; line-height: 150%" align="justify"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: Tahoma; font-size: 10pt">This comprehensive and carefully considered warranty structure has been put in place as customer value and satisfaction is a key cornerstone of the brand&#8217;s success in South Africa. The Hyundai Assist &#8211; Roadside Assistance warranty includes out-of-fuel services, the changing of flat tyres, key lockout services, jump-start/home start services, breakdown towing, 24-hour car hire and 24-hour overnight accommodation to name just a few of the benefits. For customers to take advantage of this assistance they merely need to contact the Hyundai call centre to be assisted either directly or via a credible towing agency.</span></p>
<p style="text-align: justify; line-height: 150%" align="justify"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: Tahoma; font-size: 10pt">For all customers who purchased their vehicles prior to 1 May 2010, they need to contact the following number (xxx) if they would like to upgrade their current package to include 24-hour roadside assistance. </span></p>
<p style="text-align: justify; line-height: 150%" align="justify"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: Tahoma; font-size: 10pt">Previously, HASA offered a 1-year roadside assistance to vehicles under R200 000 and a 3-year roadside assistance package for vehicles that retailed over R200 000. However, this new offering applies to all Hyundai vehicles - regardless of retail price or model. </span></p>
<p style="text-align: justify; line-height: 150%" align="justify"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: Tahoma; font-size: 10pt">&#8220;To this end, the moving of our towing service from the AA to our own personal Hyundai Assist Agent has enabled us to provide our valued customers with a comprehensive and extended roadside assistance warranty, aligned specifically to our brand. We feel this will serve as a strong value-add for existing and potential customers when considering or choosing a Hyundai vehicle,&#8221; says Michael Rohde, <span style="font-family: Tahoma; color: black; font-size: 10pt">Director of After Sales and Service at Hyundai South Africa</span></span><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: Tahoma; font-size: 10pt">.</span></p>
<p style="text-align: justify; line-height: 150%" align="justify"><span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: Tahoma; font-size: 10pt">&#8220;It is important to HASA that our customers are offered first hand customer care and quality service, not only when buying a vehicle &#8211; through a reputable dealer and brand - but also through a solid after sales service offering that extends far beyond the usual, market related, call of duty.&nbsp; As a result, we are excited about this extension on our roadside warranty and believe that this is evident of our commitment to customer service and loyalty, once again presenting Hyundai as the superior, all-rounded vehicle partner, that we pride ourselves on,&#8221; concludes Rohde.</span></p>
<p align="justify"><strong><span style="font-family: Tahoma; font-size: 10pt">About Hyundai Automotive South Africa</span></strong></p>
<p align="justify"><span style="font-family: Tahoma; font-size: 10pt">Hyundai Automotive South Africa, an entity of the Hyundai Motor Company, was selected as the Global distributor of the Year in 2005 and forms part of the Associated Motor Holdings group locally. Since inception, Hyundai SA has established a network of over 80 dealerships in Southern Africa, including Namibia, Swaziland and Botswana. Hyundai has a wide range of products and have a particularly good name for maintaining high customer service standards. Hyundai is the fastest growing automotive brand in the world, and have developed a strong brand amongst the South African motoring public. Our mission is to grow our brand on a reputation built on integrity; recognizable style, reliability and superior build quality.</span></p><!-- Parsing Time: 0ms-->
