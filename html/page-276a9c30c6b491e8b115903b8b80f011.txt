            						<h3>Strength &amp; Tower Mobility Combined.</h3>
            						<h3>&nbsp;</h3>
            						<p>
            						<h4> Our Signature Hybrid Frame Machines</h4>
</p>
                                    <p>were created with versatility and power at top of mind. They have 3 pivoting pulling towers at one end of the bed and 2 (or 3) movable towers that ride on a track around the other end. The pivoting towers provide strength, stability and repair width while the movable towers make 360-degree pulling access possible. </p>
            <p>&nbsp;</p>
                                    <p>This  combination gives auto body technicians 10-ton hydraulics at each tower plus 360-degree pulling access to  the vehicle frame. That makes the Hybrid a great frame rack with less &quot;dead zones&quot;.  The towers glide into position with smooth, one-handed motion around the track making tower group pulls easier than ever.</p>

                                    <h4>Why Upgrade To Signature Hybrid Frame Machines?</h4>
                                    <p>&nbsp;</p>
                                    <p>Some frame machines offer strength but insufficient tower mobility. Others have movable towers but not enough pulling power or the ability to work on wider vehicles.The Signature Hybrid has solved these challenges with an auto body frame rack that combines power, precision and all-around access. So you and your team no longer have to sacrifice one for the other.</p>
