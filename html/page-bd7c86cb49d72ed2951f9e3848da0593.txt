<h2>America&#8217;s Chrome Shop</h2>
<p>We are the leader in handling custom plating and polishing for industrial, commercial and decorative applications.</p>
<p>We have years of experience in chrome plating parts and accessories for classic cars, hot rods, trucks, motorcycles, boats, antiques. and decorative items.</p>
<p>Typical items are bumpers, wheels, grilles, radio knobs, hardware, nuts and bolts.<br />
We chrome plate almost everything, including appliances, automotive, marine items, medical, dental, jewelry, architectural hardware, plumbing, fire engine parts and accessories.</p>
<p>All parts are triple plated: copper, nickle and chrome. Starbright can plate steel, stainless steel and aluminum.<br />
We do repair and plate die cast, buffing, straightening and polishing.</p>
