                    <dt>Product Description</dt>
                    <dd><p class="FontNormal">
	BAW has introduced multipurpose off-road Luba into the market after careful examinations of the real driving conditions in China. Luba includes the following series: 3400, 3000, 2700 and 2400 etc. Supported by BAW&#39;s own off-road-vehicle production technology, the advanced design and imported core technologies, Luba means another hot point of the multipurpose off-road vehicles in the competitive market.</p>
<p class="FontNormal">
	The performance of Luba 3400 can compete against any world famous peer. It is outstanding for its pure off-road chassis, Japanese engine, advanced V 6-cylinder, double roof camshaft and 24 effective fuel-burning valves. It provides 294 N.M torque while running 4000r/m and 136 KW while running 4800r/m. Thus its low speed with big torque and high speed with big power make it easier for the vehicle to start, climb and overtake.</p>
<p class="FontNormal">
	The suspension system is also unique: double front suspension arms, coil spring with a stabilizator, rear coil spring with independent suspension of 4-rod connection. 4-rod connection system is especially advanced nowadays as it makes vehicles run smoothly with agility and coziness.</p>
<p>
	Another factor to Luba&rsquo;s popularity is its massive and royal body. Recently, BAW has introduced many advanced processing techniques and materials to perfect the surface coating, assembly and interior decoration. Humanized technology is utilized in control system, driving systems and safety system. For example, steel plate placed in the roof, wave-shaped steel beams in each door, front &amp; rear braking discs, ABS with load sensing pressure proportioning valve, and so on. All those advanced and complete safety devices will guarantee an easy and peaceful journey.</p>
