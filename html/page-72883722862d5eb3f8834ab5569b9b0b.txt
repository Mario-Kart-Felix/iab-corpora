    $.fn.s3Slider = function(vars) {

        var timeOut     = (vars.timeOut != undefined) ? vars.timeOut : 4000;
        var current     = null;
        var timeOutFn   = null;
        var faderStat   = true;
        var mOver       = false;
        var items       = $("#" + element[0].id + "Content ." + element[0].id + "Image");

            $(items[i]).mouseover(function() {
               mOver = true;
            });
            
            $(items[i]).mouseout(function() {
                mOver   = false;
                fadeElement(true);
            });
            
        });
        
        var fadeElement = function(isMouseOut) {
            var thisTimeOut = (isMouseOut) ? (timeOut/2) : timeOut;
            thisTimeOut = (faderStat) ? 10 : thisTimeOut;
            if(items.length > 0) {
                timeOutFn = setTimeout(makeSlider, thisTimeOut);
            } else {
                console.log("Poof..");
            }
        }
        
        var makeSlider = function() {
            current = (current != null) ? current : items[(items.length-1)];
            var currNo      = jQuery.inArray(current, items) + 1
            currNo = (currNo == items.length) ? 0 : (currNo - 1);
            var newMargin   = $(element).width() * currNo;
            if(faderStat == true) {
                if(!mOver) {
                    $(items[currNo]).fadeIn((timeOut/6), function() {
                        if($(itemsSpan[currNo]).css('bottom') == 0) {
                            $(itemsSpan[currNo]).slideUp((timeOut/6), function() {
                                faderStat = false;
                                current = items[currNo];
                                if(!mOver) {
                                    fadeElement(false);
                                }
                            });
                        } else {
                            $(itemsSpan[currNo]).slideDown((timeOut/6), function() {
                                faderStat = false;
                                current = items[currNo];
                                if(!mOver) {
                                    fadeElement(false);
                                }
                            });
                        }
                    });
                }
            } else {
                if(!mOver) {
                    if($(itemsSpan[currNo]).css('bottom') == 0) {
                        $(itemsSpan[currNo]).slideDown((timeOut/6), function() {
                            $(items[currNo]).fadeOut((timeOut/6), function() {
                                faderStat = true;
                                current = items[(currNo+1)];

                                    fadeElement(false);
                                }

<h3 class='post-title entry-title'>
<a href='http://luxurycarsnewz.blogspot.com/2011/11/luxury-car-brands-in-america-2011.html'>Luxury Car Brands In America 2011</a>
</h3>
<div class='post-header'>
<div class='post-header-line-1'></div>
</div>
<div class='post-body entry-content'>
<div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"><a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-HwYSgfMFlZA/Tr1VE2FfPPI/AAAAAAAAAEQ/XzHsFBBVXdA/s1600/luxury+car+brands+in+America+in+2011.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="clear: left; float: left; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" height="204" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-HwYSgfMFlZA/Tr1VE2FfPPI/AAAAAAAAAEQ/XzHsFBBVXdA/s320/luxury+car+brands+in+America+in+2011.jpg" width="320" /></a></div><div style="text-align: justify;"><a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-Bv0D2-L9_As/Tr1VYlqBzqI/AAAAAAAAAEY/B4lQUgjRMxE/s1600/luxury+car+in+America+in+2011.png" imageanchor="1" style="clear: right; float: right; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-left: 1em;"></a><a href="http://luxurycarsnewz.blogspot.com/2011/11/in-conditions-of-luxury-cars.html">Luxury car</a> is the expression used for the car that puts up nice and fascinating features at increased outgoings.<br />
<br />
The luxury car is a vehicle that sends you great copiousness of comfort and coziness. The luxury car has greater performance, building preciseness, design resourcefulness, greater equipments, comfort, technological invention, and other discretional features.<br />
<br />
The content reports <u>the luxury car brands in America</u>.<br />
<br />
Luxury cars include sedan, coupe, hatchback, beach wagon and transformable body style vehicles.<br />
Luxury cars are available in any size and any price bounds.<br />
<br />
The luxury cars underscore more on ease, visual aspect and agreeablenesses in the car rather than saving, performance and usefulness.<br />
&nbsp;</div><div style="text-align: justify;">Today cars have not just remained a substance of transport, but have raised a luxurious, comfy and swift medium of traveling.<br />
<br />
There is rattling betterment in the car technology with every passing day. The demand of comfort and luxury has turned up growth of luxury cars in America.<a href="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-Bv0D2-L9_As/Tr1VYlqBzqI/AAAAAAAAAEY/B4lQUgjRMxE/s1600/luxury+car+in+America+in+2011.png" style="clear: right; float: right; margin-bottom: 1em; margin-left: 1em;"><img border="0" height="227" src="http://3.bp.blogspot.com/-Bv0D2-L9_As/Tr1VYlqBzqI/AAAAAAAAAEY/B4lQUgjRMxE/s320/luxury+car+in+America+in+2011.png" width="320" /></a><br />
<br />
Today there are number of luxury cars seen on the road of America manufactured by different car maker.<br />
<br />
<u><b>Luxury Car Brands in America:</b></u><br />
Below is the list of top-selling<a href="http://luxurycarsnewz.blogspot.com/p/luxury-cars-brands.html"> luxury car brands</a> in America for the year 2011.</div><div style="text-align: justify;"></div><div style="text-align: justify;"></div><div style="text-align: justify;"></div><div style="text-align: justify;"><br />
</div><div style="text-align: justify;"><br />
</div><div style="text-align: justify;"><b style="color: blue;"><u>Mercedes-Benz.</u></b><br />
Mercedes-Benz followed BMW and was listed second in the list of luxury  car brands. The sales of Mercedes increased to 25.4 % therein particular  year. The C-class Mercedes-Benz model was the best selling model of  Mercedes in May 2011. The total sale of this brand in 2011 was around  19,976.<br />
<br />
<b><u style="color: red;">BMW.</u></b><br />
BMW topped the list of luxury car brands in America in the year 2011. The sale of this brand increased to 14.3 % in the year 2011. BMW 3-series model was the best selling model of BMW in the year 2010. The total sale of this luxury car in May 2010 was around 17859.<br />
<br />
<b style="color: orange;"><u>Lexus.</u></b><br />
The best selling model of this brand was Lexus RX. In the month of May 2011, the total sales of Lexus reached to about 22,216.<br />
<br />
<u style="color: lime;"><b>Acura.</b></u><br />
Acura MDX was the highest selling car of this brand in the year 2011. In May 2011, about 11,766 cars of this brand were sold in the market.<br />
<br />
<u style="color: #351c75;"><b>Audi.</b></u><br />
The sales of Audi rose to 14.3 % in May 2011. Audi A4 was the highest selling car therein year. About 9,205 cars of this brand were sold in May 2011.</div><div style="text-align: justify;"><br />
</div><div style="text-align: justify;"><u style="color: #660000;"><b>Cadillac.</b></u><br />
Cadillac CTS remained the best selling model of this brand in 2011. The total sale of this brand in May 2011 was around 12,328.<br />
<br />
<u style="color: blue;"><b>Infiniti.</b></u><br />
The sale of this brand increased to 31.7 % in 2011. The highest selling car of this brand was Infiniti G37. A total of 8091 cars of this brand were sold in the economic year 2011.<br />
<br />
<u style="color: lime;"><b>Lincoln.</b></u><br />
Though the sale of this brand reduced to 11.5 %, still it managed to be the 8th ranked car brand in the list of highest selling luxury car brands in May 2011. Lincoln MKZ was the best selling car of this brand and the total of 7755 cars of this brand were sold in the year 2011.</div><div style="text-align: justify;"><br />
</div><div style="text-align: justify;"><u style="color: red;"><b>Jaguar and Land Rover.</b></u><br />
Jaguar and Land Rover brands were listed 10th in the list of best  selling luxury car brand in the year 2011. The sales of this brand  increased by 53.3 % in 2011 and about 3671 cars were sold in 2011 of  this brand.<br />
<br />
<u style="color: #274e13;"><b>Volvo.</b></u><br />
Volvo experienced tremendous reduction in the sales rate by 29 %. It was ranked 9th in the list of best selling luxury car brands in America in 2011. The Volvo XC60 was the most selling car of this brand in May 2011. The total of 4659 cars of this brand was sold in the whole year.<br />
<br />
<br />
<br />
The above listing affords you the information about the topmost<i> luxury car brands in America in 2011</i>.<br />
</div>
