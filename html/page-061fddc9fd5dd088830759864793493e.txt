<blockquote><p class='BodyText'><b>What is a Classic Car?</b><BR>
There are so many different definitions of what a classic car is.  Some would put specific age limits on the definitions, and others would say that only special variants would qualify for classic status.  In our search for the “perfect” definition, we believe a classic car is “a car that retains its appeal after it is no longer produced.”  Without question “appeal” of a car is determined by the individual.  To us a classic car is one that stops depreciating in value and either levels off or begins to appreciate over time.  So why not invest in a classic car, enjoy and have some fun!</p></blockquote></p><p></p>
