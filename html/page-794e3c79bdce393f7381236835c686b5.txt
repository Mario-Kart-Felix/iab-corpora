			<div class="post-4930 post type-post status-publish format-standard hentry category-auto-news tag-luxury-cars" id="post-4930">
			    <div class="entry">
<p><a href="http://budgetedmonton.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/Mercedes-Benz_SLR_McLaren_2_cropped.png" rel="wp-prettyPhoto[4930]"><img class="alignleft size-full wp-image-4932" title="Mercedes-Benz_SLR_McLaren_2_cropped" src="http://budgetedmonton.com/wp-content/uploads/2012/03/Mercedes-Benz_SLR_McLaren_2_cropped.png" alt="" width="156" height="117" /></a>Most of the time when we see a fancy car we find ourselves staring for quite a long time and thinking to ourselves how great it would be to drive one of those vehicles. There are some car rental companies in the United States and Canada that allow people to rent these one of a kind cars, but it does come with a hefty price tag.</p>
<p>Here’s a look at some of the world’s top notch vehicles that are available from manufacturer&#8217;s local dealerships.</p>
<p><strong>Lotus:</strong> $249/day or $399/day on weekends.<br />
<strong>Aston Martin V8:</strong> $695/day<br />
<strong>Mercedes Benz SLR McLaren Roadster:</strong> $1450/ day (US Currency)<br />
<strong>Lamborghini Gallardo:</strong> $1,299/day or $3,599 for the weekend). Keep in mind that only people who can give a $10,000 security deposit are allowed to rent.<br />
<strong>Vintage Rolls Royce:</strong> $350/ 3 hours and $95.00 for each additional hour<br />
<strong>Rolls Royce Phantom:</strong> $2,000 each way for a tour of Niagara Falls</p>
<p>If you want to see more vehicles along with pictures visit <a title="MSN Canada Autos" href="http://autos.ca.msn.com/" target="_blank">autos.ca.msn.com</a>.</p>
<p>If you had the money, would you rent one of these vehicles? Tell us your thoughts!</p>
<p><strong>*Please not that Budget Edmonton does not currently have these vehicles as part of our fleet.</strong></p>
