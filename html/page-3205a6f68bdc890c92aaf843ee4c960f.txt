                    <div class="post-content clearfix"><div class="aligncenter rtp-margin-bottom-10"><img width="620" height="465" src="http://cdn.hautetime.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/2014-Panamera-S-E-Hybrid-_3_-620x465.jpg" class="rtp-margin-0 colorbox-26956  wp-post-image" alt="2014-Panamera-S-E-Hybrid-_3_" /></div><p>Today we’re going green, luxury style! We’re taking a look at a hybrid version of Porsche’s iconic four-door saloon Panamera. The Panamera S E-Hybrid was unveiled their year at Auto Shanghai, and it’s the first plug-in hybrid offering from the German automaker.</p>
<p><a href="http://cdn.hautetime.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/2014-Porsche-Panamera-Interior-_2_1.jpg" ><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-26964 colorbox-26956" alt="2014-Porsche-Panamera-Interior-_2_1" src="http://cdn.hautetime.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/2014-Porsche-Panamera-Interior-_2_1.jpg" width="1000" height="560" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><span style="font-family: arial; color: #cc0033;"><em>See more on this car at <a href="http://www.hauteauto.com/" onclick="javascript:_gaq.push(['_trackEvent','outbound-article','http://www.hauteauto.com']);" target="_blank">Haute Auto</a>.</em></span></p>
<p>The Panamera S E-Hybrid is a lean, green machine. It’s got a powerful electric motor and a high-performance battery that can be recharged either at home or at public charging stations. The result is an electric drive that produces 95 hp (more than double the power of the previous model’s 47 hp electric motor) and can do zero to sixty in 5.2 seconds on its way to a top track speed of 167 mph.</p>
<p><a href="http://cdn.hautetime.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/2014-Porsche-Panamera-Interior-_1_1.jpg" ><img src="http://cdn.hautetime.com/wp-content/uploads/2013/07/2014-Porsche-Panamera-Interior-_1_1-620x465.jpg" alt="2014-Porsche-Panamera-Interior-_1_1" width="620" height="465" class="aligncenter size-medium wp-image-26963 colorbox-26956" /></a></p>
<p>The new Panamera will be available in the U.S. starting later this year, and will start at $99,000.</p>
