<TD>
<STRONG>
<FONT color=#000000 face="serif,Garamond" size=6>A</FONT>
<FONT color=#2222ff face=Garamond size=4>lthough there 
are museums and attractions throughout the state that contain antique 
cars, there is not one true public antique automobile museum in all of New 
Jersey. All museums are a necessity, for they play a vital role in the 
nation's educational system. They add a more tangible dimension to what 
can be learned from sitting in a classroom, or from watching TV. Our 
goals are to not only bring antique cars to a central viewing place, but 
to also preserve history, share that history, and take pride in a history 
that truly encouraged and shaped growth in New Jersey and this 
country.</P>
</TD>
</TR>
</Table>



</FONT>

<P align=left>
<FONT color=#000000 face="serif,Garamond" size=6>P</FONT>
<FONT color=#2222ff face=Garamond size=4>lans for the museum include programs 
 to enrich the lives of students of all grade levels. The automobile is 
 an extraordinarily rich object, embracing science, mathematics, history, 
 foreign languages, aesthetics, and civil and social issues. Students, 
 scouts, community groups, and special audiences will benefit from our 
 programs. Hands on exhibits will be developed for all age 
 groups.</FONT></P></BLOCKQUOTE>
      <BLOCKQUOTE>
<P align=left>
 <FONT color=#000000 face="serif,Garamond" size=6>F</FONT>
 <FONT color=#2222ff face=Garamond size=4>or the adult population we will have 
 lecture series, such as the history of the automobile, car collecting, 
 racing, and the impact of the automobile in New Jersey, the country,
and our society.&nbsp; We will also 
 sponsor workshops that will provide fundamental knowledge about the 
 parts of a car, how it works, and basic maintenance 
 techniques.</FONT></P></BLOCKQUOTE>
      <BLOCKQUOTE>
 <P align=left>
 <FONT color=#000000 face="serif,Garamond" size=6>T</FONT><FONT 
 color=#2222ff face=Garamond size=4>he Vintage Automobile Museum of New Jersey
was granted  501 ©3 status in 2002 as a non profit organization
and will be run as an educational and community minded entity. 
We are not simply planning to build an old car parking lot. 
Our goal is to put together a total program preserving our automotive heritage 
and its impact in New Jersey, the country, and on our society.
</FONT></P></BLOCKQUOTE>
