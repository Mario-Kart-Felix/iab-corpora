<h2 class="entry-title">Welcome to Bowler</h2>
<div class="entry-content">
<p>Bowler is a market leader in the production of racing and high performance all terrain vehicles, and has been since 1985. The UK pioneers in Rally Raid cars, we have never stopped competing at the highest levels and during this time we have designed, developed and produced some of the most innovative, durable and exciting all terrain vehicles ever seen.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>The Bowler Wildcat, Nemesis and now the EXR have all been proven in the toughest race in the world – the Dakar. Each evolution of Bowler has refined the knowledge and experience gained from the previous one, and we are now proud to offer the EXR – the ultimate all terrain supercar.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Based in Derbyshire in the UK, we use both technology and craftsmanship to deliver vehicles with style, strength, performance and an unrivalled spirit of adventure.</p>
