<p style="background: white"><strong><span style="font-size:18.0pt;color:green">
<a name="Molds For Sale:">Molds For Sale:</a></span></strong><b><span style="font-size:18.0pt;color:green"><br>
</span></b>The original molds for the car pictured&nbsp;are for sale.&nbsp;<br>
Not the car, not the bodies; just the molds.&nbsp;<br>
The molds were built with the highest quality materials and craftsmanship.&nbsp;&nbsp;</p>
<p style="background: white"><span style="font-size:13.5pt">THE ELECTRIC CAR:<br>
</span>The Doran EV is one of the most famous electric cars which won many races 
and electric vehicle range contests and rallys in the 1990s.</p>
<p style="background: white"><span style="font-size:13.5pt">THE GAS POWERED CAR:<br>
</span>was powered by a Subaru flat-4 cylinder engine.&nbsp; It was fast, handled 
very well, and was still very economical and efficient&nbsp;with its aerodynamic 
shape, light weight, and low rolling resistance.</p>
<p style="background: white">The electric car&nbsp;was owned by the <i>Petersen 
Automotive Museum</i> in Los Angeles <span style="font-size:10.0pt">(a LA County 
Museum) from 1996 to 2004</span>.&nbsp; It was acquired for its beauty and 
technology.&nbsp;</p>
