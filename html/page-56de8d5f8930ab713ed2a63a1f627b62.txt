         <h1 style="font-size:15px; margin-top:10px; margin-bottom:10px"><strong>Hybrid Vehicle Service Gloves</strong></h1> 
                        
        
        <p><strong>Engineered to protect you from high voltage danger when servicing today&rsquo;s hybrid cars.</strong> </p>
<p><strong>Rubber Insulating Gloves:</strong></p>
<ul>
    <li>Natural rubber gives glove superior protection </li>
    <li>Maximum use 1000 volts AC / 1500 volts DC </li>
    <li>12&quot; long with Rolled Cuff </li>
    <li>Rolled cuff gives extra grip for easy pull-on and pull-off </li>
    <li>Meets ASTM D 120 Standard </li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Protective Over Gloves: </strong>Wear over Hybrid Service Gloves to protect the rubber insulating gloves from punctures and abrasion </p>
