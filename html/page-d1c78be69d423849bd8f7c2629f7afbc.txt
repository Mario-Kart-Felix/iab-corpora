		            		<div class="entry-title"><h1 class="epi-fontLg bwalignc">
      <b>Curry’s Auto Service Announces the Launch of The Hybrid Shop Dealer 
      Program to Partners in the Hybrid Vehicle Market, Maximizing Business 
      Opportunities and Growth</b>
    </h1></div>
							
							<meta itemprop="headline" content="Curry&rsquo;s Auto Service Announces the Launch of The Hybrid Shop Dealer Program to Partners in the Hybrid Vehicle Market, Maximizing Business Opportunities and Growth"/>
		            
		    		
		    		<div id="story_subheadline">
			        	
    <p class="bwalignc">
      <i>The area’s leading auto service provider partners with Automotive 
      Research and Design to bring expanded growth, ROI to shop owners and 
      dealer partners through exclusive dealer programs</i>
    </p>
  
		    		</div>
					
					
					
						
                    <div class="story_dateline updated" title="2013-05-09T15:09:00Z">
                    	<time itemprop="dateModified" datetime="2013-05-09T15:09:00Z">
                    	May 09, 2013 11:09 AM&nbsp;Eastern Daylight Time&nbsp;
                    	</time>
                    </div>
					
					<!-- start story body -->
					
					<div class="entry-content" itemprop="articleBody">
					
					<p>STERLING, Va.--(<span itemprop="provider publisher copyrightHolder" itemscope="itemscope" itemtype="http://schema.org/Organization" itemid="http://www.businesswire.com" class="author source-org vcard"><span itemprop="name" class="org fn"><a itemprop="url" href="http://www.businesswire.com/">BUSINESS WIRE</a></span></span>)--Curry’s Auto Service, one of the largest independent auto service and 
      repair shops in the Washington, DC, area, announces today that it has 
      partnered with <a target="_blank" href="http://cts.businesswire.com/ct/CT?id=smartlink&amp;url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.autoresearchanddesign.com%2Findex.php&amp;esheet=50629245&amp;lan=en-US&amp;anchor=Automotive+Research+and+Design+%28AR%26D%29&amp;index=1&amp;md5=05dbaa19d48cf57f6ea06de2c5e6d9f5">Automotive 
      Research and Design (AR&amp;D)</a> to form a new joint venture 
      exclusively for Hybrid vehicles, called <a target="_blank" href="http://cts.businesswire.com/ct/CT?id=smartlink&amp;url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.currysauto.com%2Fthe-hybrid-shop.aspx&amp;esheet=50629245&amp;lan=en-US&amp;anchor=The+Hybrid+Shop+%28THS%29&amp;index=2&amp;md5=0695613830b37db9150e0b40299fc674">The 
      Hybrid Shop (THS)</a>. This new service will specialize in serving the 
      Hybrid segment of the automotive aftermarket, and will bring an 
      environmentally-sound alternative to battery replacement to restore <a target="_blank" href="http://cts.businesswire.com/ct/CT?id=smartlink&amp;url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.thehybridshop.com%2Fservices%2Fhybrid-specific-services%2Fbattery-conditioning%2F&amp;esheet=50629245&amp;lan=en-US&amp;anchor=Hybrid+battery&amp;index=3&amp;md5=45f8030bd8aa0805d7a56f17e060ef28">Hybrid 
      battery</a> power for greater fuel economy, cost savings, and vehicle 
      performance. This new service offering brings unprecedented 
      opportunities for revenue growth and innovation through a complete 
      dealer package now available.
    </p>
<blockquote><p>&#8220;Our new partnership with AR&amp;D as well as the exclusive technology and 
      service offerings THS provides to the growing Hybrid market represent a 
      tremendous business opportunity to shop owners and dealer partners&#8221;</p></blockquote>

    <p>
      The Hybrid vehicle market has experienced a continued upward growth 
      trajectory in recent years. In 2013, there are:
    </p>
    <ul>
      <li class="bwlistitemmargb">
        Over 2.7 million Hybrids on the road;
      </li>
      <li class="bwlistitemmargb">
        57 new models available for purchase by various automobile 
        manufacturers; and.
      </li>
      <li class="bwlistitemmargb">
        Hybrid vehicles account for approximately 4% of the total new car 
        market.
      </li>
    </ul>
    <p>
      The U.S. Department of Energy forecasts that the Hybrid vehicle market 
      will grow more than 10% by 2015, with the number of new models produced 
      expected to double, and even triple over the next ten years.
    </p>
    <p>
      <a target="_blank" href="http://cts.businesswire.com/ct/CT?id=smartlink&amp;url=http%3A%2F%2Ffirstlandingautocare.com%2F&amp;esheet=50629245&amp;lan=en-US&amp;anchor=First+Landing+Auto+Care%2C+Virginia+Beach%2C+VA&amp;index=4&amp;md5=e91f9b45bc74cba3932edb15a5189c44">First 
      Landing Auto Care, Virginia Beach, VA</a>; <a target="_blank" href="http://cts.businesswire.com/ct/CT?id=smartlink&amp;url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.lloydstireservice.com%2F&amp;esheet=50629245&amp;lan=en-US&amp;anchor=Lloyd%27s+Tire+and+Auto%2C+Santa+Cruz%2C+CA&amp;index=5&amp;md5=68418e5d928f6293ace5cdb8d0aa8d02">Lloyd’s 
      Tire and Auto, Santa Cruz, CA</a>; <a target="_blank" href="http://cts.businesswire.com/ct/CT?id=smartlink&amp;url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.fifthgear.biz%2F&amp;esheet=50629245&amp;lan=en-US&amp;anchor=Fifth+Gear+Automotive%2C+Lewisville%2C+TX&amp;index=6&amp;md5=48824379f72530651b8abf3d6d74b9ff">Fifth 
      Gear Automotive, Lewisville, TX</a>; and <a target="_blank" href="http://cts.businesswire.com/ct/CT?id=smartlink&amp;url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.goodworksautorepair.com%2F&amp;esheet=50629245&amp;lan=en-US&amp;anchor=Good+Works+Auto+Repair%2C+Tempe%2C+AZ&amp;index=7&amp;md5=fbef6c1434535d9028e6620206faddea">Good 
      Works Auto Repair, Tempe, AZ</a> are the first dealers to have gone 
      through The Hybrid Shop’s 4-day on site technician training, Hybrid 
      Electric Vehicle Maintenance, Service, and Diagnostics Course. They are 
      now all fully certified to provide the <a target="_blank" href="http://cts.businesswire.com/ct/CT?id=smartlink&amp;url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.thehybridshop.com%2Fservices%2Fhybrid-specific-services%2Fbattery-conditioning%2F&amp;esheet=50629245&amp;lan=en-US&amp;anchor=Battery+Conditioning+Service&amp;index=8&amp;md5=7bbdb4ad2a24430411a3e28e3b358ff6">Battery 
      Conditioning Service</a>.
    </p>
    <p>
      The Hybrid Shop dealer opportunities available also provide shop owners 
      and dealer partners with a variety of new revenue streams and areas for 
      continued business growth in the expanding Hybrid market, including:
    </p>
    <ul>
      <li class="bwlistitemmargb">
        Battery Conditioning and Rebuilding;
      </li>
      <li class="bwlistitemmargb">
        Motor-Generator (Electronic Machine) testing, diagnosis and 
        replacement;
      </li>
      <li class="bwlistitemmargb">
        Power Inverter &amp; Control System Testing;
      </li>
      <li class="bwlistitemmargb">
        dc-dc Converter Testing;
      </li>
      <li class="bwlistitemmargb">
        Other Hybrid Specific Maintenance; and
      </li>
      <li class="bwlistitemmargb">
        Normal Vehicle Maintenance.
      </li>
    </ul>
    <p>
      Dealer opportunities are available to existing shop owners that desire 
      to add Hybrid vehicle services to their current offerings. THS offers 
      two participation options, Dealer or Affiliate Partner. A Dealer can 
      expect to:
    </p>
    <ul>
      <li class="bwlistitemmargb">
        Bring a complementary business to their existing business;
      </li>
      <li class="bwlistitemmargb">
        Become a leading expert on hybrid repair and technology;
      </li>
      <li class="bwlistitemmargb">
        Tap into the fastest– growing segment of the auto repair market and 
        deliver a green, environmental friendly service that will translate 
        into as much as 85% gross profit for their dealerships; and
      </li>
      <li class="bwlistitemmargb">
        Receive area exclusivity, one conditioning machine, a five-day 
        training course, and marketing support from THS.
      </li>
    </ul>
    <p>
      “Our new partnership with AR&amp;D as well as the exclusive technology and 
      service offerings THS provides to the growing Hybrid market represent a 
      tremendous business opportunity to shop owners and dealer partners,” 
      Curry’s Auto Service Co-Founder and CEO, Matt Curry states. “As the 
      Hybrid vehicle market continues to expand, Area Dealers and Affiliate 
      Dealers have a unique opportunity to serve Hybrid customers in this 
      growing area, to help them maximize their green footprint and improve 
      the performance and economy of their vehicle in a cost-effective manner, 
      while setting their organizations on a path to continued revenue growth 
      and expansion.”
    </p>
    <p>
      <b>About Curry’s Auto Service</b>
    </p>
    <p>
      Founded by Matt and Judy Curry in 1997, Curry’s Auto Service was named 
      “2010 Top Shop” by Motor Age Magazine. In June 2010, Curry’s Auto 
      Service was chosen by the readers of Northern Virginia Magazine as “Best 
      Auto Repair Shop” in Northern Virginia. In addition, Curry’s has 
      received the highest AAA ratings in the country for customer 
      satisfaction. Headquartered in Gainesville, Virginia, Curry’s Auto 
      Service is one of the largest independent auto service and repair shops 
      in the Washington, DC, area, with eight locations in Northern Virginia 
      and one in Maryland. Curry’s services more than 4500 customers each 
      month at its shops in Alexandria, Arlington, Chantilly, Dulles, Fairfax 
      City, Falls Church, Gainesville, Gaithersburg and Great Falls/Reston. As 
      part of its growth strategy, Curry’s opened three new stores in 2011. 
      They plan to open a shop in Leesburg, Virginia, later this year. For 
      more information, please visit <a target="_blank" href="http://cts.businesswire.com/ct/CT?id=smartlink&amp;url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.currysauto.com&amp;esheet=50629245&amp;lan=en-US&amp;anchor=www.currysauto.com&amp;index=9&amp;md5=13b317a882a85b49e007ef51198fda81">www.currysauto.com</a> 
      or follow us on <a target="_blank" href="http://cts.businesswire.com/ct/CT?id=smartlink&amp;url=https%3A%2F%2Ftwitter.com%2FTheHybridShop&amp;esheet=50629245&amp;lan=en-US&amp;anchor=Twitter&amp;index=10&amp;md5=f51e24659f025005cf60e5bef851727b">Twitter</a> 
      and <a target="_blank" href="http://cts.businesswire.com/ct/CT?id=smartlink&amp;url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.facebook.com%2Fpages%2FThe-Hybrid-Shop%2F538683679511535&amp;esheet=50629245&amp;lan=en-US&amp;anchor=Facebook&amp;index=11&amp;md5=1b0b8121afee6c1207e51a2e447b4c8b">Facebook</a>.
    </p>
    <p>
      <b>About Automotive Research and Design (AR&amp;D)</b>
    </p>
    <p>
      Automotive Research and Design was founded in 1987 as an Engineering and 
      Technology company focused on developing and manufacturing diagnostic 
      equipment, Hybrid and Electric Vehicle Training, Curriculum Solutions, 
      Special Projects, and Consultations. For more information, please visit <a target="_blank" href="http://cts.businesswire.com/ct/CT?id=smartlink&amp;url=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.go2hev.com&amp;esheet=50629245&amp;lan=en-US&amp;anchor=www.go2hev.com&amp;index=12&amp;md5=6d3153e7adb95927c90a81e118139eb4">www.go2hev.com</a>.
    </p>
