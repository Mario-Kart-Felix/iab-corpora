<div class="post"><div class="posttop"><div class="username">MagicRat</div><div class="date">06-18-2009, 09:24 AM</div></div><div class="posttext">The SRX is a 'crossover' which makes it half - minivan. IMO a true minivan has a lower, car-like floor, and usually more room than a cross-over.<br />
<br />
Despite the fact that minivans are hugely practical, they are a declining market segment in North America. Car makers are pushing the cross-over because their vaguely sporty rugged image seems to have more appeal than a plebian minivan. This appeal translates to more sales and a higher per-vehicle profit margin. <br />
<br />
Personally, I wanted a Caddy minivan or wagon a few years ago..... before the Escalades became affordable on the used market. But my wife refused to let me buy a hearse.  :(</div></div><hr />
