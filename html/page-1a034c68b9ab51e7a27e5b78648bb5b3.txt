                    <b>Honda Accord Wagon:</b>
                        <p align="justify">
In the year 1991 the Honda Accord Wagon was finally introduced to the market after a long wait. This was the vehicle that was introduced for those that needed a vehicle that offered more in terms of luggage space and that was able to provide much in terms of the cargo that could be transported. There was as much as double the capacity of the typical sedan trunk and this made for a vehicle that could cater to the need for cargo space. There was the inclusion in this model of the side air bags as well that was an added bonus that was not included in the sedans of that time. These vehicles were all fuel injected and the engines were more powerful than the typical engines.
</p><p align="justify">
The Honda Accord Wagon is a vehicle that also had the luxury that was typical of the Accords. This has the style of the Accord and certainly lived up to its reputation. Although there were many changes in the interior and exterior styling of the vehicles over the years they always conformed to the need to provide safety and protection to the passenger with the additional perks of luxury. The station wagon version was however dropped from the Honda line up in the year 1998 but this does not mean that parts for these vehicles are not available. There are still many of these durable vehicles on the roads and parts are available.
