<h1 class="pageHeading">Crossover X and H-Style Exhaust Pipes</h1>
<div id="category_main_top">
</div>
<div class="catDesc">
<table width="100%" border="0" bgcolor="#CCCCCC">
<tr>
<td>X-pipe and H-pipes synchronize the balance of the exhaust pulses from each bank of cylinders based on firing order which increases velocity and scavenging from each cylinder. Most people agree that X-pipes are better at improving performance than H-pipes, especially at the top end. However, H-pipes tend to have a deeper sound than x-pipe equipped vehicles and generally build power down low. These are general claims and your engine and exhaust combination could have different results.
