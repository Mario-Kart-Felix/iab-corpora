  <p><img src="files/anim2.gif" alt="Image: Classic car details" title="1967 Eldorado, 1962 Imperial, and 1976 Mark IV" width="116" height="111" hspace="4" vspace="4" align="left" />Automotive Mileposts provides authenticity, restoration data, and history
    for classic car and vintage auto enthusiasts. We specialize in American-made,
    traditional and personal luxury cars of the 1950s through the 1970s, with
    some overlap into other decades and types of vehicles, as necessary to
    tell the complete story of the classic cars we feature on the site.</p>
  <p>Restoration data helpful to restorers and collectors is thoroughly researched
    and authenticated, and includes introduction dates, correct paint color
    names and codes for each year, make, and model, a complete list of standard
    equipment and optional features, including pricing and option or accessory
    codes when available, with any changes for the year noted. Mechanical specifications
    include engine availability, displacement, and horsepower; transmissions
    used; rear axle ratios; weight and height specifications; width and length
    measurements; and any factory codes that allow identification of these
    parts. Production numbers and original manufacturer's suggested retail
    prices are broken down by body style, and include mid-year price changes
    if applicable.</p>
  <p>Complete interior trim descriptions, including materials, colors, seating
    configurations, and trim codes are provided, plus vinyl roof and convertible
    top colors, textures, and codes, plus paint stripe data. Additional attention
    is given to rare trim packages and special edition models. Factory show
    cars, concept cars, and prototypes are covered, as are changes made to
    the cars during the production run. Photographs are used to provide details
    that are difficult to describe, and to serve as a visual reference. Technical
    tips, service bulletins, and other information is provided in informative
    articles, and through our experts on the AUTOPOSTS Classic Car Forum.<img src="files/anim1.gif" alt="Image: Classic car details" title="1969 Thunderbird, 1969 Mark III, and 1967 Imperial" width="116" height="111" hspace="4" vspace="4" align="right" /></p>
  <p>Find  rare, NOS, or obsolete parts with our unique auction keyword search
    pages that allow visitors to check multiple search terms all at the same
    time&#8212;by vehicle make, model and year&#8212;all on the same page.
    We also have a keyword and part number search for items  not listed by a make or model. Results are shown in real time, and are    updated constantly. MILEPOSTS Garage is our very own online classic car magazine with helpful information on
    buying or selling a classic car, articles on how to restore your vintage
    car, automotive humor and insight, and the old car hobby in general. Auto
    Brevity gives you brief insights into the automotive world.</p>
  <p>We welcome the opportunity to provide you with classic car information,
    history, and entertainment.</p>
