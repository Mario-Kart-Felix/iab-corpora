</div><p>Each year Southern California hosts some of the top car and auto shows in the United States. In addition to classic car, high end sports cars and luxury cars there are also specialty car shows showing off the hottest import cars and custom modifications. Southern California attracts a whole different breed of cars and car shows. </p>
<p>For example Hot Import nights specializes in modified imports but also turns the car show in to a high energy event with bikinied car show models, stage performances, sound and light shows and concerts. The Platinum Custom car and bike show has a wider variety of vehicles including Luxury, Exotic, Domestic and Imports as well as Low-riders, muscle cars and of course sports and custom bikes. Keeping to the sexier theme the Platinum car show even encourages the spectators to dress in high-rise shorts, and bikini tops and bottoms although they do say they are keeping it as a “family friendly” show prohibiting gang attire and g-strings.</p>
<p>For the somewhat more conservative car show attendees there is no shortage of the classic version of the classic car shows with the encouraged attire being to a 50s and 60s theme. The southern California beach theme also shines in several of the shows like the Beach Cruisers Car Show at Sea Terrace Park in Dana Point, CA.</p>
<h2>2013 Auto Shows</h2>
<p>Want to be the first to check out the new 2014 cars and concept cars. Southern California also host major auto shows like the LA Auto Show so you can be the first to see the 2014 debuts as well as the possible cars of the future the hot, and often entertaining concept cars. </p>
