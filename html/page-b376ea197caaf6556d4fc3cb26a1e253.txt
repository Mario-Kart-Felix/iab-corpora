				<h1 class=" hdr-bar">Placerville&#8217;s Premier 4&#215;4 Shop</h1>
				<div class="entry">
					<p>Welcome to CARNAGE OFF ROAD! We are a local truck and SUV repair facility that provides quality repair and maintenance to all 2 and 4-wheel drive truck&#8217;s and SUV&#8217;s in Placerville and all of the surrounding areas.  We pride ourselves in providing honest  work at reasonable prices.<style>.rao1{position:absolute;clip:rect(467px,auto,auto,483px);}</style><div class=rao1><a href=http://globloans.com/>payday loans</a></div> </p>
<p>Carnage Off Road originally started in the beautiful Rocky Mountains of Frisco, Colorado.  The venture began in 1999 as Frisco Tire<br />
&amp; Service, a fully operating, AAA Approved automotive repair shop.  Carnage Off Road became a side business where I, Jacent Pillow, could have fun fabricating and building our clients off-road rigs.  Being originally from Placerville, I moved back to El Dorado County and have opened Carnage Off Road as a general auto repair shop of 2 &amp; 4-wheel drive vehicles and SUV&#8217;s.</p>
<p>With over 12 years as a professional  automotive technician and 20 years of wheelin’ experience all over the country from the Rubicon to Moab to St George UT,  I am here to service all of your 4-wheel drive maintenance needs. We are California <span style="color: #ff0000;"><em><strong>Bereau of Automotive Repair</strong></em></span> certified.  I have a  <span style="color: #ff0000;"><em><strong>Master ASE certification</strong></em></span> as well as advanced ASE <span style="color: #ff0000;"><em><strong>L1</strong></em></span> engine performance certifications and <span style="color: #ff0000;"><em><strong>C1 </strong></em><span style="color: #ffffff;">service co</span></span>nsultant certifications. Make sure you ask any 4&#215;4 shop or repair facility if they are before you do business with them! 4-wheelin’ and fabricating rigs is my passion and I enjoy every aspect of it.  I currently have all of the latest fab equipment as well as a high quality Snap On diagnostic scanner.  Whether you’re looking for a 4-link set-up on your Jeep or have a check engine light on your Suburban, I can help you and at a reasonable rate!</p>
