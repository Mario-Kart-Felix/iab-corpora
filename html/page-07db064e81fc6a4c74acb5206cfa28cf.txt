<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;"> </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;">2.1 - Remove the rear wheel</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;"> </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;">2.2 - Disconnect the taillight/turn connector under the seat.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;"> </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;">2.4 - Extract the three pins from the male half (fender side) of the connector for the taillight (blue, red/yellow, black). Make note of which wires are in which positions in the connector.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;"> </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;">2.5 - Pull the wire bundle for the taillight out of the clips on the underside of the fender. Push the grommet and wire bundle through the hole in the fender.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;"> </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;">2.6 - Remove the taillight assembly from the fender.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;"> </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;">2.7 - Remove the taillight housing/lenses from the taillight assembly.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;"> </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;">2.8 - Remove the two bulbs from their sockets.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;"> </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;">2.9 - Drill out the rivet holding the two sockets to the plastic frame and remove the sockets from the frame. Be careful not to damage the ground wire terminal. See Figure 1 and 2.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;"> </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;">2.10 - Install the two sockets and the ground wire terminal onto the replacement aluminum piece using a 10-24 x 1/4” pan head machine screw. Use red loctite on the screw threads. &nbsp;See Figure 3.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;"> </span></p>
<p><i><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;">NOTE (author’s opinion, use your own judgement here) - The license lamp is no longer needed since light from the taillight bulb is no longer blocked from reaching the license plate. The license lamp can be omitted by cutting the gray wire at the wiring grommet. </span></i></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;"> </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;">2.11 - Mount the replacement aluminum assembly onto the fender. Tighten the three mounting nuts finger tight. Install the taillight bulb and license bulb (if needed). Route the wiring bundle as shown in the figures. Push the wire bundle and grommet through the hole in the fender. See Figure 4 and 5.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;"> </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;">2.12 - Place the taillight wiring bundle into the clips on the underside of the fender.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;"> </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;">2.13 - Reinsert the three pins into the proper positions in the taillight/turn connector. Reconnect the connector. Check that the tail, brake, and turn signals operate properly.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;"> </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;">2.14 - Install the tailight gasket and housing/lenses onto the aluminum assembly. Use anti-sieze on the three housing screws. Reinstall the license plate mounts/brackets at this time as needed. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;"> </span></p>
<p><i><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;">NOTE - Due to manufacturing variations with the taillight housing, taillight lens, and the replacement piece, you may have use a 3/16” drill to drill out the three housing and lens holes to achieve alignment. </span></i><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;">&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;"> </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;">2.15 - Adjust the position of the taillight assembly as needed. Tighten all bolts and nuts. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;"> </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;">2.16 - Reinstall the rear wheel. </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;"> </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;"> </span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 10pt; font-family: Arial;">ALL DONE!</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 12pt; font-family: Arial;"><span style="font-size: 8pt;">Credits: Install Instructions were independetly written and submitted by Gordon Malone, after he bravely agreed to be our 1st betaa-tester and install one on his beautiful Harley Springer.&nbsp; Thank you Gordon, for all your help and feedback!</span></span></p>
