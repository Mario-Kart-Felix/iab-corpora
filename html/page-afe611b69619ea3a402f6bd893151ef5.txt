	<h2 class="sf_blog_posttitle" id="post-123">Farmers Auto Insurance Towing and Roadside Assistance Coverage</h2>
	<div class="sf_blog_entry">
		<P style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt" face=arial><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 11px"></FONT>Most people in Southern California do not realize that their Farmers auto insurance policy has an optional&nbsp;coverage for towing and roadside assistance.&nbsp;&nbsp;People often purchase additional coverage for these items through AAA or Auto Club. I thought I'd take this time to make sure that my current and potential future customers know about a great value that Farmers Insurance has with their towing/roadside assistance coverage on the auto insurance policy.<BR><BR></FONT></P>
<P style="MARGIN: 0in 0in 0pt"><FONT style="FONT-SIZE: 10pt" face=arial><IMG style="BORDER-BOTTOM: 1px solid; BORDER-LEFT: 1px solid; WIDTH: 200px; FLOAT: left; HEIGHT: 150px; BORDER-TOP: 1px solid; BORDER-RIGHT: 1px solid" alt="roadside assisance Farmers insurance towing" src="http://images.quickblogcast.com/8/6/0/1/6/268735-261068/roadside.jpg?a=92">When I receive a request for an auto insurance quote from a new customer, I almost always include coverage for the towing/roadside assistance in the quote. Farmers Insurance provides for 3 tows per year and up to $150 per incident, with the additional premium for most cars running about $40-$50 per year. This is a significant bargan, considering that typically, without coverage, a tow costs several hundred dollars.&nbsp; It's also important to note that using this coverage up to 3 times per year will in no way not cause your auto insurance rates to increase. This coverage also is also available in case you need roadside assistance for any sort of mechanical failure or weather-related problem with your car. Make sure you ask your Farmers Insurance agent about our towing &amp; roadside assistance program.</FONT></P>
