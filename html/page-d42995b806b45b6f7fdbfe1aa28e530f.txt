		<p class="hide-while-editing" itemprop="description">The stunning Fisker Karma is a four-door coupe featuring plug-in hybrid technology that made its first public appearance at the Detroit Show.

This Karma will begin production in the 4th quarter of 2009 with annual production projected to reach 15,000 cars. The first 99 cars off the assembly line will be individually numbered and signed by Henrik Fisker, the former Design Director for Aston Martin.  Deliveries will begin in May or June 2010.
