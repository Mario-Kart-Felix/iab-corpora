            <h1 class="title">Peugeot 2008 crossover here in 2013</h1>
            <p>Wednesday 12 September 2012</p>
        </div>
        <div class="articleBody">
            <div class="mpu">
                <span id="placeholderMPU"></span>
            </div>
            <div id="articleMainContent">
        <p><strong><img class="alignleft size-full wp-image-37130" style="margin-bottom: 11px;" title="peugeot-2008-380249" src="http://www.autotrader.co.uk/articleresources/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/peugeot-2008-380249.jpg" alt="Peugeot 2008" width="380" height="249"/>• Peugeot 2008 to rival the Nissan Juke<br />
• 1.2-litre turbo petrol engine in development<br />
• On sale next year</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Peugeot 2008 will take on the <a href="http://www.autotrader.co.uk/articles/2011/01/cars/nissan/juke/nissan-juke-hatchback-2010-expert-review">Nissan Juke</a>, <a href="http://www.autotrader.co.uk/articles/2009/12/cars/skoda/yeti/skoda-yeti-se-car-review">Skoda Yeti</a> and upcoming <a href="http://www.autotrader.co.uk/new-cars/generation/56571">Vauxhall Mokka</a>, when it goes on sale next year.</strong></p>
<p>Peugeot’s latest pictures show the 2008 Concept which is set to be unveiled at the Paris Motor Show on September 27. It will be hard to miss, thanks to fluorescent yellow paintwork, finished with a matt lacquer, which is said to be “full of energy”.</p>
<div style="margin-left: -48px;"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-37131" title="peugeot-2008-side-654297" src="http://www.autotrader.co.uk/articleresources/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/peugeot-2008-side-654297.jpg" alt="Peugeot 2008" width="654" height="297"/></div>
<p>It features Peugeot’s latest grille, with ‘Peugeot’ script at its brow and a large Lion badge set back on top of the bonnet. The heavily blistered and scalloped lines which feature around the vehicle add to its appearance of chiselled solidarity.</p>
<p>Large alloy wheels and a high ride height point towards a penchant for a spot of light off roading, while the headlights are based on “the sharp pupil of a cat”.</p>
<p>A new 1.2-litre three-cylinder engine is being developed for the 2008, with turbocharging and direct fuel injection bringing power up to 110bhp.</p>
<div style="margin-left: -48px;"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-37132" title="peugeot-2008-rear-654297" src="http://www.autotrader.co.uk/articleresources/wp-content/uploads/2012/09/peugeot-2008-rear-654297.jpg" alt="" width="654" height="297"/></div>
<p>Aimed at a world audience, the 2008 has been a joint collaboration between Peugeot stylists in Paris, Shanghai and Sao Paulo and will be built in France, China and Brazil.</p>
<p>According to Peugeot it has been designed for “young-minded city dwellers who love the city, while also loving to escape from it.”</p>
<p><em>By Andy Goodwin, senior road tester</em></p>
