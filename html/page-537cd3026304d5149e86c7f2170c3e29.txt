                  <div class="viewarticle padtop10px padbottom10px">At a time when consumers are giving the cold shoulder to diesel cars and wagons, BMW is toying with the idea of launching two such versions of the 2012 3 Series in North America.<br />
<br />
This highly surprising information does, however, fly in the face of what BMW&rsquo;s sales and marketing director, Ian Robertson, is saying. <br />
<br />
<table width="1" cellspacing="0" cellpadding="0" border="0" align="">

<br />
He stated yesterday that only a major shift in consumer behaviour would ensure the commercial success of the Touring.<br />
<br />
It seems more likely that the automaker will follow the evolution of the market before taking such a plunge. Logically, Europe (where the versions will first be released) will serve as a testing ground to get a feel for North American interest.<br />
<br />
So no one can say for sure when or if these variants of the 3 Series will ever grace our dealerships.<br />
<br />
We&rsquo;ll keep you posted!<br />
