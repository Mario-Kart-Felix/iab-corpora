<h2><a href='/PANELS/DODGE-CHARGER-REPAIR-PANELS.HTM'>Fix Your Rusted Vehicle Today!</a></h2>
<p>Mill Supply has a large inventory of rust repair panels and parts. We carry rocker panels, rear quarter skins, partial rear quarter panels, front fenders, partial front fenders, floor pans, floor supports, trunk floor pans, wheelhouses, inner rear quarter sections, outer door skins, inner door bottoms, radiator supports, cab mounts and and other body panels.</p>
<p>We carry body panels for BMW, Buick, Cadillac, Chevrolet, Chrysler, Datsun Nissan, Dodge, Ford, Honda, International, Jeep, Mack Truck, Mazda, Mercedes, Mercury, Oldsmobile, Plymouth, Pontiac, Sprinter Vans, Subaru, Suzuki, Toyota, Volkswagen, Volvo, and more!</p>
<p>Here at Mill Supply we carry quality parts so your car or truck can look like it did the day it came off of the lot whether that was 3 years ago or 50. Mill Supply began to sell to body shops in 1944 and we now sell to both shops and individuals looking to repair automobiles. Some of our most popular parts include weather stripping, dash tops that fit over broken dashboards, rocker panels, wheel arches, door handles, lights, mirrors, quarter panels, Auveco fasteners, tools, and floor mats. </p>
<p>Popular models that we carry replacement panels for include; Ford Bronco, Plymouth Roadrunner, Ford Mustang, Chevrolet Camaro, Chevrolet Cavalier, Toyota Camry, Chevy Chevelle, Minivans, Full-size Vans, Volkswagen Beetle, Honda Civic, Dodge Charger, Dodge Challenger, Pontiac Firebird, Jeep CJ5, Dodge Neon, Plymouth Barracuda, Pontiac GTO, Jeep Wrangler, Toyota Corolla, and all major pickup trucks e.g., Chevy Silverado, Ford F series, and Dodge Ram.</p>
