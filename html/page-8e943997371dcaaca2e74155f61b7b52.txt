<P>
Dear Members,
<BR>

Welcome August! I'll bet it won't be any hotter than July. Still lots to do out
there in our fair(?) weather months. The show at Lakehurst was well attended
and nicely run (Thanks John). Our Club picnic was also well handled, thanks to
all, especially Gary & Ann, and Stan also for being around that grill on another
very hot day, to the Ross family for securing our spot, Richie for our music and
all of the other helpers!. Everyone in attendance seemed to enjoy the day, and
it did not go without acknowledging Walter.<P>
Lots to do again this month, starting with what is always a great day at the
Rose Garden Show, featuring the Cameos. The following day there is a pancake
breakfast at Green Island Clubhouse that Gary & Ann can fill us in on, and our
important August meeting on the 7th. It will be important to help set the details
for our own car show on Sunday September 8th. Please keep in mind that we
will not have some of our busiest hands to help this year. The Cusacks will be
away, so we need some help from our other members. I'm sure John will tell us
all at the meeting what help he'll need and where, but parking and judging are
on the list I'm certain. Kitchen help will be needed too without Marge being
available, so please come and help out your club!<P>
I'd be remiss if I didn't mention my fav,"Lead East"! I'll be up there for 4 days
with my new ride and I'd love to see some club members there, if only for a
day. It is a lot of fun! and they play Connie Francis too!
Looking forward to seeing everyone at the August meeting, & don't forget
bring a friend!


<P>Don't forget to check 
<A HREF="http://www.DowntownTomsRiver.com">DowntownTomsRiver.com</A> for 
information about local shows & events in our area.
