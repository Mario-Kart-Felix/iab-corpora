    <h2>Station Wagon Cover FAQ's</h2>
    <br />
    <strong>What if my antenna doesn't retract or remove?</strong>
    <p>Your cover should come with a small patch made of the same material, with an adhesive backing and a perforated hole in the center. This will allow you to create a reinforced hole in your cover for your antenna to come through. <a href="/articles/ResourceGuide.aspx?scrollto=sectiond">More About Antenna Patches</a></p>

    <strong>Do your covers have mirror pockets?</strong>
    <p>No, our covers will accommodate your mirrors without pocketing them. This ensures that you will get maximum durability from your cover, as mirror pockets are unnecessary and are usually the first part of a car cover to deteriorate or rip. <a href="/articles/ResourceGuide.aspx?scrollto=sectionm">More On Mirror Pockets</a></p>

    <strong>Will my cover last in extreme weather?</strong>
    <p>We offer a variety of outdoor covers that excel in different environments so that you're covered no matter what the weather. Your cover should withstand just about anything Mother Nature can dish out, however there are a few instances where you should be more alert and take extra precautions. Speak with an Empire Sales Representative, or visit the <a href="ProtectionHome.aspx">Shop By Protection Level Page</a> to learn about which cover would be right for you.</p>

    <strong>How long does it take to receive my package?</strong>
    <p>Orders ship within two (2) business days of being received. Actual transit time with the shipment carrier varies depending on the service selected. We offer FedEx Ground shipping (3-7 business days), 2nd Business Day, and Next Business day shipping. Please note that expedited shipping times do not include order fulfillment time. Please allow 1-2 business days for us to process and ship out your order.</p>
