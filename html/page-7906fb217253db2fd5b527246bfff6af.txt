<h2>Why performance auto insurance premiums are higher</h2>


<p>Sports  cars, which are high performance cars, are usually placed by insurers in a high  risk category due to certain reasons. Firstly, sports cars have a high accident  risk. Secondly, they use many modified components which are costly to repair or  replace. Thirdly, insurance companies decide on the premiums by taking into  consideration certain statistics, which show a history of high claims for high  performance cars.</p>


<p>Insurance  companies also take into consideration the driver&rsquo;s age, the driver&rsquo;s driving  record, how the car is used, the modifications to the engine or other parts and  the type and make of the engine. A clean driving history can attract low auto  insurance rates, among other factors.</p>


<p>If  you own a high performance car, it need not mean that you have money to throw  away. Although most insurers charge highly for such cars, this need not be the  case for you.</p>

<h2>Ways to lower your performance auto insurance premium</h2>


<ul>
  <li>Get  performance auto insurance that charges for limited mileage. For this, you will  have to provide the insurance company with an estimate of your annual mileage. </li>
  <li>Buy  and insure a sports car after the age of thirty, and combine it with a clean  driving history.</li>
  <li>Disclose  every modification. This will ensure that these modifications are also covered,  and there will be no conflicts in the event of a claim.</li>
  <li>Protect  your car against theft by installing the latest security system, and also by  parking it in a garage.</li>
</ul>
