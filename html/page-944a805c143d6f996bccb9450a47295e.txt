	    <h1 class="al"><a name="Feb 6 - 10, 2013 - Stewart’s Auto Sales delivered with off-road performance vehicles" class="dealsanchor">Feb 6 - 10, 2013 - Stewart’s Auto Sales delivered with off-road performance vehicles</a></h1>
	  </div>
	  <p><img src="http://www.suzukicaribbean.com/assets/images/news/Feb6-10-2013-Stewarts-Auto-Sales1.jpg" alt="Feb 6 - 10, 2013 - Stewart’s Auto Sales delivered with off-road performance vehicles"></p>

<p> KINGSTON, JA  -Thursday, February 7, 2012  - Day two of the 4x4 Off-Road Challenge presented by Stewart's Auto Sales took place on a modified, purpose-built course in Barbican Circle today. Suzuki ... vehicles, provided exclusively to Jamaica by Stewart's, were put to the test in a closed course which featured among other hurdles a 38-degree angled obstacle, water pit, and steep hill climb.</p>

<p>Members of the media were given an exclusive opportunity to sample the range of vehicles available in a private test drive session. From Suzuki, the recently upgraded Grand Vitara; SX4; and Jimny models were provided...</p>

<p>For the next four days, the public is encouraged to visit Barbican Circle to get behind the wheel at no cost. Suzuki's Sales & Marketing Manager, said `We wanted to further highlight what the Stewart's Automotive Group has to offer with this event. Suzukis not only perform well in Rally and Dover, they have great off-road performance as well...</p>

<p>The Stewart's Automotive Group, the largest automotive group in Jamaica, is the first company to host what will be their second test drive event of this kind in Kingston's corporate area. The 4x4 Off-Road Challenge runs from 12 noon to 6 P.M. every day until Sunday, February 10, 2012</p>
