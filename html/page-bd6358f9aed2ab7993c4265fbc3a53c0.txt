		<p>Diesel-engine vehicles are seen in <a href="http://www.autospecialtyoflafayette.com/lafayette" ><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Lafayette</span></a> and <a href="http://www.autospecialtyoflafayette.com/west-lafayette" ><span style="text-decoration: underline;">West Lafayette</span></a> regularly, and we are the area&#8217;s trusted <strong>diesel mechanic </strong>shop. Auto Specialty of Lafayette is a complete petrol and <strong>diesel engine repair</strong> shop that services both foreign and domestic diesel-engine cars and lightweight trucks. Diesel engines are more complex than standard petrol engines, which is why rebuilding and repairing diesel engines requires expert eyes, hands, and knowledge. If you own a vehicle that runs on diesel fuel and has problems with a sluggish or non-starting engine, <a href="http://www.autospecialtyoflafayette.com/contact" ><span style="text-decoration: underline;">contact us</span></a> at <strong>(765) 477-7300</strong> to make an appointment, or drop by our <strong>Lafayette IN</strong> location today.</p>
<p><img src="/wp-content/images/diesel-mechanic.jpg" alt="Diesel Mechanic Lafayette IN" />   <img src="/wp-content/images/diesel-technician.jpg" alt="West Lafayette Diesel Technician" /></p>
<h3>Lafayette Indiana Diesel Mechanic</h3>
<p>Auto Specialty of Lafayette is one of the few auto repair shops in the area to provide both petrol and <strong>diesel auto repair</strong>. As certified <strong>diesel mechanics</strong>, we understand the intricacies of each diesel engine, including:</p>
<ul>
<li>Fuel Injectors</li>
<li>Fuel Injector Pumps</li>
<li>Turbos</li>
<li>Fuel Lines</li>
<li>Glowplugs</li>
</ul>
<p>As the most complex part of a diesel engine, the fuel injector requires expertise to repair, remove, or replace. Frequently, fuel injectors are located in different places when compared to other diesel engines. This can make locating and replacing a diesel fuel injector more difficult and time-consuming. Most imported and domestic diesel-engine vehicles also require fuel injectors and parts that are specifically manufactured for their respective make and model. Comprehensive understanding of how a diesel engine works is the key to repairing them correctly.</p>
<p><img src="/wp-content/images/diesel-mechanic-contact.jpg" alt="Diesel Repair Indiana" /></p>
<h3>Benefits of a Diesel Engine</h3>
<p>Diesel engines have numerous benefits over standard gasoline engines. Unlike their gasoline-counterparts, diesel engines burn fuel more slowly, resulting in better gas mileage and a longer lasting engine. Diesel engines, on average, last several years longer than a standard engine that runs off gasoline, and some have even been known to last over 300,000 miles. Of course, Auto Specialty of Lafayette recommends that you perform routine truck and <a href="http://www.autospecialtyoflafayette.com/oil-change" ><span style="text-decoration: underline;">car maintenance</span></a> of your diesel-engine vehicle to extend its life.</p>
<p>Complement your diesel-engine car or lightweight truck with an expert <strong>diesel mechanic</strong> shop that won’t take advantage of you. We stand by our motto that <em>Service + Honesty = Trust</em> and make it our personal goal to complete each <strong>diesel engine repair</strong> with care and dedication, because your safety and satisfaction is important to us. If you are a resident of <strong>Lafayette</strong>, <strong>West</strong> <strong>Lafayette</strong>, <strong>Attica</strong>, <strong>Fowler</strong>, <strong>Delphi</strong>, <strong>Crawfordsville</strong>, or any surrounding area and need <strong>diesel truck repair </strong>or <strong>diesel engine repair </strong>services<strong>, </strong><a href="http://www.autospecialtyoflafayette.com/contact" ><span style="text-decoration: underline;">contact</span></a> the <strong>diesel specialists</strong> of Auto Specialty of Lafayette at <strong>(765) 477-7300.</strong> We also repair standard engines, <a href="http://www.autospecialtyoflafayette.com/brakes" ><span style="text-decoration: underline;">brakes</span></a>, <a href="http://www.autospecialtyoflafayette.com/tires" ><span style="text-decoration: underline;">suspension</span></a> systems, <a href="http://www.autospecialtyoflafayette.com/catalytic-converter" ><span style="text-decoration: underline;">exhaust systems</span></a>, and perform oil changes and regular <a href="http://www.autospecialtyoflafayette.com/auto-repair" ><span style="text-decoration: underline;">auto repairs</span></a>.</p>
