     The main features of a Sedan cars include bigger chassis dimensions,
    seating space and luggage space included in single body shell. Sedan are the
    mid size cars having more row seats with passenger capacity of four or more.
    A sedan model has more than four doors with separate boot or luggage space
    at the rear. Basically sedan car means the cars with more boot space and
    entirely different section from the passenger's cabin. In cars sedan is
    synonymous to luxury. Sedan cars are the blend of comfort, luxury, elegance
    and stunning interior with high end safety features. There are endless high
    end luxury cars in the same segment. The demand of sedan cars are increasing
    rapidly in India which is the main reason many foreign car manufactures
    target India as a launching pad for these luxury cars. The major players in
    this particular segment are Honda, Skoda, Volkswagen, Ford and Hyundai.<BR>
