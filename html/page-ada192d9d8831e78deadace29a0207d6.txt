If you change insurers down the road, they could be counted against you and result in a higher premium!  It's a nasty little secret that very few consumers know about until it's too late.  <br><br>I'm an insurance agent and I have an AAA membership for roadside assistance.  It's worth $25 or so more per year and the AAA membership also entitles you to a lot of discounts for travel, rental cars, hotels, restaurants, etc.<br><br><br mce_bogus="1"></p>
