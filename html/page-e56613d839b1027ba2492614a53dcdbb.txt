<p>If you&#8217;re thinking about replacing your vehicle&#8217;s current exhaust system, let the <strong>performance experts</strong> at Automotive Concepts help you choose the perfect exhaust to achieve the performance and sound you are looking for out of your vehicle. We carry the top name brands including: Magnaflow, SLP Performance, Borla, Corsa, Dynatech and more! We install hundreds of exhaust kits every year, so you can trust your vehicle with our pros!</p>

<p><strong>Performance exhaust systems</strong> offer many benefits compared to stock exhaust systems including:</p>
<ul class="list-icon write">
<li>Increased Horsepower by improving exhaust flow</li>
<li>Improved Fuel Economy</li>
<li>Longer-lasting High Grade Steel</li>
<li>And of course, the Performance Sound!</li>
</ul>

<h3>Custom Exhaust Systems &amp; Tips</h3>
<p><a href="exhaust-custom" class="imgFloatLeft rt-image"><img src="http://www.ac-mn.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/01/custom_menu.jpg" alt="Custom Exhaust by Automotive Concepts of MN" /></a>Go totally custom with your exhaust system! <br />
ant two pipes, four pipes, black or chrome tips? Automotive Concepts has a variety of options to give the look, power and sound to match your style. <a href="exhaust-custom">More Info &gt;</a></p>
<div class="clear" style="padding-bottom:50px;"></div>


<h3>QTP Electric Cutout</h3>
<p><a href="exhaust-qtp-cutout" class="imgFloatLeft rt-image"><img src="http://www.ac-mn.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/01/qtp-menu.jpg" alt="QTP Electric Cutouts" /></a>The QTP (Quick Time Performance) Electric Cutout is an electronically controlled valve that bolts to standard cutouts (OE &amp; Custom exhausts) that opens and closes from inside your vehicle, controlling volume and exhaust flow. <a href="exhaust-qtp-cutout">More Info &gt;</a></p>
<div class="clear" style="padding-bottom:50px;"></div>


<h3>Magnaflow Performance Exhaust</h3>
<p><a href="exhaust-magnaflow" class="imgFloatLeft rt-image"><img src="http://www.ac-mn.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/01/magnaflow-menu.jpg" /></a>Whether you drive a street, off-road or high performance vehicle, MagnaFlow has dyno-proven exhaust systems that are rugged enough to survive grueling Baja racing and provide years of trouble-free performance.</p>
<p>Magnaflow systems provide a smooth, deep tone that is suitable for grocery-getting SUV&#8217;s with casual tow duties to full blown modded drag racing. <a href="exhaust-magnaflow">More Info &gt;</a></p>
<div class="clear" style="padding-bottom:50px;"></div>


<h3>Borla Performance Exhaust</h3>
<p><a href="exhaust-borla" class="imgFloatLeft rt-image"><img src="http://www.ac-mn.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/01/borla-menu.jpg" /></a>BORLAÂ® doesn&#8217;t stop with unsurpassed technology. They engineer and manufacture all  exhaust products in the USA of high quality austenitic stainless steel and back their street exhaust systems with a Million-Mile Warranty.  <a href="exhaust-borla">More Info &gt;</a></p>
<div class="clear" style="padding-bottom:50px;"></div>


<h3>Corsa Performance Exhaust</h3>
<p><a href="exhaust-corsa" class="imgFloatLeft rt-image"><img src="http://www.ac-mn.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/01/corsa-menu.jpg" /></a>Corsa Performance Exhaust manufactures world-class exhaust systems that deliver  great sound during acceleration and are whisper quiet when cruising thanks to RSC (Reflective Sound Cancellation)  noise-suppression technology. <a href="exhaust-corsa">More Info &gt;</a></p>
