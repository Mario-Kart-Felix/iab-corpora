                    <p>The sensible and versatile new 2002 Volkswagen Jetta Wagon 
                      boasts a new four-cylinder engine with more power this year. 
                      And as usual, the well-appointed interior absolutely brims 
                      with creature comforts and practical amenities that make 
                      driving a pleasure for passengers and drivers alike. </p>
                    <p><b><font color="#336699">Available Models<br>
                      </font></b>Whether you need a terrific-looking vehicle to 
                      commute to work in or a perfect family car, there is a Jetta 
                      Wagon model that will suit your individual and lifestyle 
                      requirements. A wide array of models is available - 2002 
                      Volkswagen Jetta GL 2.0L Wagon, Jetta GL TDI Wagon , Jetta 
                      GLS 2.0L Wagon, Jetta GLS TDI Wagon , Jetta 1.8T Wagon, 
                      Jetta GLS VR6 Wagon, and the 2002 Volkswagen Jetta GLX VR6 
                      Wagon.</p>
                    <p><b><font color="#336699">Interior Features<br>
                      </font></b>The impressive Jetta Wagon is equipped with standard 
                      features such as an automatic door locking feature, power 
                      door locks with central power locking system, CFC-free air 
                      conditioning, two folding keys, valet key, chrome interior 
                      door handles, AM/FM stereo with cassette player, 60/40 split 
                      folding rear seat for extra cargo space, and a sporty four-spoke 
                      padded steering wheel.</p>
                    <p>Some of the higher end models are equipped with a leather-wrapped 
                      steering wheel with cruise and audio controls, heated front 
                      seats, power windows, eight-way power driver's and passenger's 
                      seats, luxurious leather seating surfaces, premium AM/FM 
                      stereo with CD player, cruise control, and electronic climate 
                      control to keep you comfortable.</p>
                    <p><b><font color="#336699">Exterior Features<br>
                      </font></b>Each four-door Volkswagen Jetta Wagon is accented 
                      with body color side moldings, body color mirror housings, 
                      body color grille with center logo, body color door handles, 
                      body color bumpers with black lower section, standard fifteen-inch 
                      steel wheels with full wheel covers (alloy on the GLX VR6 
                      model), and a roof-mounted flexible black whip antenna. 
                      The rear hatch certainly comes in handy, and makes loading 
                      and unloading cargo - or the family dog - quick and easy.</p>
                    <p>The five top-end Jetta Wagon models are equipped with heated 
                      dual power outside mirrors and available heated windshield 
                      washer nozzles.</p>
                    <p><b><font color="#336699">Performance<br>
                      </font></b>The base model VW Jetta GL 2.0L Wagon and GLS 
                      2.0 Wagon are both built with a 2.0-litre, four-cylinder 
                      engine that exacts 115 horsepower and 122 lb-ft of torque. 
                      The standard transmission on these and all other models 
                      is five-speed manual, with optional four-speed automatic 
                      transmission available as an option.</p>
                    <p>The Jetta GL TDI Wagon and GLS TDI Wagon come with a 1.9-litre, 
                      turbo-charged four-cylinder engine with an output of 90 
                      horsepower and 149 lb-ft of torque. The Jetta GLS 1.8T Wagon 
                      features a 1.8-litre, four-cylinder, turbo-charged engine 
                      that cranks out 180 horsepower and 174 lb-ft of torque. 
                      At the highest end, the Jetta GLS VR6 Wagon and GLX VR6 
                      Wagon are equipped with a 2.8-litre, six-cylinder engine 
                      that produces 174 horsepower and 180 lb-ft of torque.</p>
                    <p><b><font color="#336699">Safety and Security<br>
                      </font></b>In the safety department, each Jetta Wagon is 
                      armed with an immobilizer theft deterrent system, and the 
                      top five models are equipped with a keyless entry system. 
                      The list of standard fare continues with three-point seatbelts 
                      at all seating positions, child safety locks, child seat 
                      tethers and anchors, side impact door beams, front and rear 
                      crumple zones, driver air bag, passenger air bag, side impact 
                      air bags, and anti-lock brakes.<br>
                    </p>
