<h1>Motorcycle Engine Rebuilders Vancouver BC</h1>
<p>Alec&#8217;s Automotive does precision machine work on all make and models of motorcycle engines from Harley’s to Honda’s.</p>
<p><strong>Bike Engine Machining and Rebuilding Services Include:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Cylinder Boring</li>
<li>Cylinder head rebuilding</li>
<li>Machine crankshafts</li>
<li>Complete motorcycle engine rebuilding</li>
</ul>
<p>We replace sunken valve seats with new hardened satellite inserts, repair broken fins and castings, and much more.</p>
<p>Modern multi-valve cylinder heads can benefit from our complete valve operations, which include replacing the valve guides, machining the valves, cutting the seats with our precision Serdi valve seat machine, and re-assembly with new valve stem seals.</p>
<p>We&#8217;d be happy to help you with your upcoming motorcycle engine rebuilding project.</p>
<p><strong>Give us a call at 604-876-7111 for a quote.<br /> </strong></p>
