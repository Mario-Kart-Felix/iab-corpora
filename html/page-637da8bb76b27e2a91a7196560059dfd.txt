			<h1 class="articleTitle">Online Auto Repair Classes and Training Programs</h1>

			<p class="date">
				
			</p>
			<p class="wikiDescription">Auto repair classes are available in fully online and hybrid formats; however, fully online auto repair classes are not very common. Students who choose fully online courses may be required to gain hands-on experience through internships or entry-level auto repair jobs.

			
			

			</p>

						<h2 id="section---OverviewOfOnlineAutoRepairCourses">Overview of Online <a class="external" href="http://education-portal.com/auto_collision_repair_courses_in_ca.html">Auto Repair</a> Courses</h2>
<p>Because auto repair technicians need to be familiar with the inner workings of cars and other vehicles, students typically can't complete an entire educational program online. However, online auto repair courses are available as part of certificate and associate degree programs. Most graduates work as auto repair technicians in mechanic shops or dealership service centers. Some seek certification from the National Institute for Automotive Service Excellence, which offers many different specializations.
</p>
<h3 id="section---RequirementsForOnlineCourses">Requirements for Online Courses</h3>
<p>Online courses are taught via the Internet, so students need a computer with Internet access. They may also need a word-processing program and other software to complete class assignments. Because online students don't work in a school auto shop using school-provided tools, they may need to purchase their own auto repair tools, which can include assorted pliers, gauges, screwdrivers and wrenches. Some online auto repair classes may not provide academic credit that transfers to other colleges.
</p>
<h3 id="section---ListOfOnlineAutoRepairCoursesAndPrograms">List of Online Auto Repair Courses and Programs</h3>
<h4 id="section---AutomotiveTechnicianTechniquesOnlineCourse">Automotive Technician Techniques Online Course</h4>
<p>This class covers multiple aspects of automotive technology, including auto repair. Lessons focus on areas such as engines, electrical systems, brakes and batteries. Students may want to apply the techniques they learn by working at automotive repair shops.
</p>
<h4 id="section---RepairForAutoBodySpecialistsOnlineProgram">Repair for Auto Body Specialists Online Program</h4>
<p>Students interested in <a class="external" href="http://Education-Portal.com/articles/Auto_Body_Repair_Technician%3A_Online_Diploma.html">auto body repair</a> can enroll in certificate programs that consist of several courses. These courses cover basic materials, refinishing, cost estimating and metalworking. As in other online auto repair courses, students may choose to supplement their virtual studies with hands-on work.
</p>
<h4 id="section---AutoRepairForSpecificAutoBrandsOnlineCourse">Auto Repair for Specific Auto Brands Online Course</h4>
<p>Students hoping to work in the service center of a dealership often receive training in specific car brands. These courses are usually offered in a hybrid format. Students complete some of their work online and visit campus for hands-on practice.
</p>
<h4 id="section---AutoElectronicsRepairOnlineCourse">Auto Electronics Repair Online Course</h4>
<p>Also available as a hybrid course, an automotive electronics course covers wire repair, diagnostics and circuits. During the hands-on component, students work with real auto electrical systems and circuit boards.</p>
