<blockquote align="justify" style="margin:0pt 0pt 0pt 140px;border:medium none;padding:0px"><i>
Kacal’s  Auto and Truck Service was founded in 1949 by George Kacal and began as  a Phillips 66 station and small neighborhood repair shop.  The shop  helped George and his wife, Billie support their family of 12 children.   George’s youngest son, Mike took a special interest in his father’s  business from early childhood.  He grew up helping his dad around the  shop and entertaining customers.  When Mike was old enough, George began  teaching his son about the mechanics of car repair.  Everyday after  school, Mike would help with the day to day responsibilities of running  the family business.  After high school, he obtained ASE Master  Certifications in cars and trucks and became the lead technician at the  shop.  <br />
<br />
In 1982 George decided that he wanted to spend more  time with his wife and grandchildren.  He wanted to retire and began  looking into selling the business.  Naturally, Mike stepped in to make his father an offer.  George happily accepted and was thrilled that  his legacy would continue through his son who had displayed a life long  passion for the family business.</i></blockquote>
