<p class="post-info"><a href="http://www.findgascards.com/blog/top-5-ranked-minivans/" rel="bookmark" title="Permanent Link: Top 5 Ranked Minivans for 2008">Top 5 Ranked Minivans for 2008</a></p>
<em>posted by FindGasCards.com</em>
<div class="post-content">
	<p>Looking for a new minivan?  Whether you have a huge family or small, minivans are a great choice.  There is a minivan designed for everyones lifestyle.  Minivans have come along way over the years, most have GPS Navigation systems, folding down seats, more space, and more luxurious items.  Check out the top ranked minivans from minivan drivers below: </p>
<ol>
<li><strong>The 2008 Honda Odyssey:  </strong>The Honda Odyssey is number one because it reached the highest rank on it&#8217;s overall performance.  This minivan reached a 9 out of 10&#8242;s for both its safety and performance.  What better could you ask for?  The minivan is known for it&#8217;s reliability and great gas mileage.   This vehicle averages 17 mpg city and 25 mpg highway. </li>
<li><strong>The 2008 Toyota Sienna:  </strong>The Sienna ranks highly because it delivers one of the best combinations of power, comfort, and interior space throughout all minivans.  The Sienna is a very reliable, comfortable and safe vehicle.  This minivan averages 17 mpg city and 23 mpg highway.  Nothing beats a starting price of $21,000!</li>
<li><strong>The 2008 Chrysler Town and Country:  </strong>The town and country reached an 8.9 out of 10 on it&#8217;s overall performance.  This vehicle is known for its safety.  This Chrysler reached an 9.8 out of 10!  Want a minivan that is safe for you and your children?  There is no better vehicle!  Town and Country&#8217;s start at $21,000 which is a great buy!  They average 19 mpg city and 25 mpg highway.</li>
<li><strong>The 2008 Kia Sedona:  </strong>The Sedona is ranked #4 out of it&#8217;s class with still a 8.8 out of 10 overall performance.  This Kia minivan is once again known for it&#8217;s safety.  Nothing is better than having your children ride in a vehicle with a 9.8 out of 10 crash test rating!  To add to the perks, the Sedona has a reliability of 8.0!  This great minivan starts at just $19,000 and averages 16 mpg city and 23 mpg highway.</li>
<li><strong>The 2008 Dodge Caravan:  </strong>This minivan is ranked #5 out of its class with an overall performance of 8.8 out of 10!  That says a lot on how well the automotive companies are doing.  This vehicle starts at $21,000 and averages 17 mpg city and 24 mpg highway.  This vehicle is known for its performance and well passed scored of safety at 9.8 out of 10.</li>
</ol>
<p>There are many minivans on the market today, but it may be hard to find better than these.  All five average a score of 8.8 or higher out of 10!  They are mostly known for their performance, affordability, safety and gas mileage.  What else could you ask for?</p>
