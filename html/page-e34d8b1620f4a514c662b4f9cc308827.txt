			var nameEQ = name + "=";
			var ca = document.cookie.split(';');

				<h1>Hatchback</h1>

				<p class="module-description">
					Hatchback is a car body style with shared passenger and cargo volume. It is characterized by rearmost accessibility via a rear third or fifth door, typically a top-hinged lift-gate. It features fold-down rear seats to enable flexibility within the shared passenger/cargo volume. As a two-box design, the body style typically includes an A, B &#038; C-pillar, and may include a D pillar. A hatchback has a sloping back with a hinged rear door that opens upward.				</p>
