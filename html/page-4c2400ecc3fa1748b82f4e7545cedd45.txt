<div><h1>Troy, MO Truck Accessories</h1>
	<p class="intro">Auto Authority</p>

	
		
		<p><strong>Auto Authority was founded in 1996 as a transportation company and was met with immediate adversity when our truck caught fire on our very first load. </strong>With my life savings spent on the start up and a long battle between the truck manufacturer and the insurance company, we were left without any income. That is when Auto Authority became a used car lot. I know pre-owned sounds much classier, but in the beginning there was no class about it. We were retailing cars for under $500, so you get the picture! The word spread fast about our customer service and integrity and we soon had over 30 cars on the lot from $1,500-$25,000. Then another transition happened when we took a bunch of truck accessories on a trade in. Right away we knew there was a big need for an accessory shop with installation in the area. We now offer thousands of vehicle accessories, tires, wheels, lift kits, performance parts, camper shells, running boards, and interior items. All are top brand names with no name prices and we offer installation on everything we sell.</p>
<p><br />
In 2009, the automotive industry was struggling so, we were looking to diversify our business. We thought that a top brand zero turn mower would be a good fit for us, after all almost all the landscape companies in the area were already customers for their tow vehicles. That is when we signed a deal with Scag and have had the same explosive growth we experienced with the accessories. We have a full time shop to service most brands of 4 and 2 stroke equipment and are a warranty center for Briggs, Kohler, and Kawasaki engines. This side of our business is known as Turftastic, but currently operates out of the same building as Auto Authority.&nbsp;</p>
<p>I'd like to thank all those that have been with us since the start and welcome the rest of you to come in and let us serve you for all your vehicle and power equipment needs.</p>
<p>We also own and operate Turftastic from our location. That branch specializes in lawn and power equipment sales and repairs.</p>
