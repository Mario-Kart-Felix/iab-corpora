    <span class="headline2"><br />
    Introducing C3 Hybrid by Auto Page</span>
    <p>The latest and most advanced vehicle security and remote start solution, C3 is a cell phone-based vehicle communication system. The C3 Hybrid telematics module integrates cellular and GPS tracking to provide the user with a cell phone remote commander for security, remote start and GPS tracking from anywhere in the world.</p>
    <p><span class="headline1">The Next Revolution</span></p>
    <p><img src="images/phoneapp.jpg" width="300" height="200" align="right" style="margin-left:20px;" />It all starts with your Smart Phone. No matter where you are or who your current cellular carrier is* the power of C3 is at your fingertips. With our simple intuitive icon based application you can navigate all the of the features of your remote start and vehicle security system. Track your teenage driver, warm your vehicle at the airport as soon as you land, or get text alerts** if your car alarm is triggered. C3 is your all-in-one solution, putting the power right in your mobile phone.</p>
    <p class="headline1">Hardware and Service Options</p>
    <p>C3 can either be installed as a integrated system with any C3 compatible remote start and/or security system by Auto Page, installed to operate an existing aftermarket installed alarm/remote start system, or installed to operate the Factory O.E.M. keyless entry and security system. Your dealer can help you decide which features are most important to you. Auto Page has a complete line of C3 compatible solutions that will fit your vehicle security or remote start needs, all controlled through an intuitive interface on your cellular phone or internet connected computer.</p>
    <p>C3 is a subscription-based service, first year unlimited service is included with your purchase of the C3 Hybrid system. Annual renewal fee of $99 is due 1 year after date of activation.</p>
    <p><strong>C3 standalone features:</strong></p>
    <ul>
      <li>Track Your Vehicle on an interactive map, anywhere in North America</li>
      <li> Get Speed Notifications to your email or cell phone</li>
      <li>Set Geo Fence boundaries and get alerted if your vehicle goes outside of the boundary</li>
    </ul>
<p><strong>C3 Hybrid features with optional compatible Auto Page systems:</strong></p>
<ul>
  <li>Lock/Unlock Your Doors without your keys<br />
  </li>
  <li>Start/ Stop Engine with unlimited range<br />
    </li>
  <li>Get Security Notifications if your vehicle is tampered with<br />
    </li>
  <li>Trunk Release control (vehicles equipped with power trunk release only)</li>
  <li>Auxiliary Control for an added on feature<br />
    </li>
  <li>GPS Locate your vehicle. Get boundary alerts and speed alerts</li>
  </ul>
