<p>Luxury vehicles are ideal for renters seeking a vehicle with superb performance, precision construction and superior design. If you want to make your mark with style, choose a luxury vehicle for your next trip.</p>

<h2>Types of Luxury Rental Cars Provided by CarRentalExpress.com</h2>

<h2>How to Find Luxury Cars Using CarRentalExpress.com</h2>

<p>1) Navigate to the rate search box at the top of this page.<br />
2) Enter the city you wish to rent a vehicle in.<br />
3) Select the dates you wish to rent the vehicle.<br />
4) Click on "Search for Rates."</p>

<p>You will be taken to a search results page showing all of the luxury vehicles available for rent. Here you can compare prices and vehicles and choose a location where you would like to pick up the vehicle. You can sort these listings by price, distance from downtown or airport, ratings and agency name. You can also change currency on that page to compare prices easier. It is currently defaulted to US dollars but that is easily changed. The search results page also has information about the car rental agencies as well as ratings and reviews that are one click away. You can also check map results to see where the agencies are located so that you can make sure the car you need is close to where you will need to pick it up. In many cases our agencies are located near or on the lot of major airports or in the downtown area of larger cities. Some agencies offer free pickup service but this is not always possible. Check the agency profile page to see what free services they might offer to their customers.</p>

<h2>Specializing in Luxury Cars</h2>

<p>Some of our agencies, such as <a href="/revolution/exclusive-car-rental/profile">Exclusive Car Rental</a>, specialize in luxury and exotic rentals. This means that in states such as <a href="/places/states/california">California</a>, <a href="/places/states/nevada">Nevada</a> and <a href="/places/states/arizona">Arizona</a> you can find some of the hottest cars by renting with Exclusive. We have exotic rentals in cities all over the world too. They offer some pretty fine automobiles, if it's in your budget.</p> 

<p>A few of the cars that Exclusive Car Rental has in stock include the Aston Martin V8 Vantage, Bentley Continental GT, Bentley Flying Spur, BMW M3 Convertible, Cadillac Escalade ESV, Chevrolet Corvette, Ferrari 360 Convertible, Jaguar XKR Convertible, and a huge selection of other models including cars made by Porsche, Range Rover and Mercedes Benz. For most people these cars won't be rented for long vacations but they might be perfect for special events, weddings and other occasions requiring a fancy vehicle.</p>
