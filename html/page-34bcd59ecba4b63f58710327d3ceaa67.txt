<h1>24-Hour Roadside Assistance</h1>
<p>Our Motor Club has thousands of service stations and garages under contract to protect
you - whether you need a tow or a jumpstart, if you run out of gas or have a tire go flat, if your battery dies, or you lock yourself out of your car. Simply make a phone call. We will send help quickly.

<p>Don't be left stranded anywhere in the continental U.S.!

<h2>Emergency Road Services</h2>
<p>Members are entitled to emergency road service which includes jump starts, change of tires, delivery of gasoline, and mechanical assistance.

<h2>Emergency Towing Service</h2>
<p>Members are entitled to a tow if their car cannot be started. Members may be towed to the garage of their choice 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. There are limits to towing coverage, please read your service agreement.

<h2>Toll-free Emergency Service Lines</h2>
<p>Members are entitled to call United Auto Motor Club's toll-free 24-hour emergency road service when their vehicle becomes disabled on the road.

<h2>Emergency Lockout Service</h2>
<p>If keys are locked inside member's vehicle, member can call toll-free 24-hour hotline to receive locksmith service.

<h2>Auto Accident Towing</h2>
<p>In the event of a reportable accident with another vehicle, memberd are entitled to towing service.</p>
