		<h2><img src="images/h_img.jpg" alt="Automotive Equipment" /></h2>
        <div id="busi_lede">
			<p class="pg">Mitsubishi Electric's automotive equipment technologies are at the core of many general, hybrid and next-generation clean-energy vehicles made by the world's major automobile manufacturers. In addition to supplying CPUs (the brain center of automotive electronics), alternators, starters, electric power-steering systems and car multimedia systems, Mitsubishi Electric delivers a wide variety of cutting-edge and high quality automobile components for greater amenity, safety and ecology.</p>
			<p class="txt10b">PLEASE NOTE: Automotive equipment from Mitsubishi Electric is sold as original equipment mainly to automobile manufacturers. If you have an inquiry regarding non-aftermarket parts, please contact your car manufacturer or local service dealer.</p>
		</div>
		
        <div class="clearfix ies_mg">
            <div class="cont_l_wrap">
                <div class="heightLine-group1">
                    <h3><a href="car_components/index.html"><strong>Automotive Electrical Components</strong></a></h3>
                    <p>Every component in Mitsubishi Electric's lineup of automotive equipment is meticulously designed to provide superior performance while reducing part size and weight to a minimum. In addition to increasing vehicle reliability and performance, these products were created with the environment in mind, contributing to improved fuel efficiency.</p>
                </div>
            </div>
            <div class="cont_r_wrap">
                <div class="heightLine-group1">
                    <h3><a href="car_mechatronics/index.html"><strong>Car Electronics/Mechatronics</strong></a></h3>
                    <p>Mitsubishi Electric manufactures a variety of intelligent power control devices and systems for tomorrow's vehicles, including ECUs, electric power steering components, millimeter-wave radar and other car electronic/mechatronic components. These products are designed with concern for both safety and ecology.</p>
                </div>
