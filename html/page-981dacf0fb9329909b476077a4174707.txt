            <h1>Mitsubishi reveals G4 Compact Sedan Concept ahead of Auto Shanghai debut this week</h1>

            <a href="http://content.worldcarfans.co/2013/4/22/medium/10740438731915566612.jpg"><img width="580" src="http://content.worldcarfans.co/2013/4/22/big/600235680288251500.jpg" alt=" Mitsubishi reveals G4 Compact Sedan Concept ahead of Auto Shanghai debut this week" /></a>

            <div class="articlead2" id="Worldcarfans_ArticlePages_Middle_BTF_Center_300x250"></div><script type="text/javascript">googletag.cmd.push(function() { googletag.display('Worldcarfans_ArticlePages_Middle_BTF_Center_300x250'); });</script> <h2 class="abstract">Previews budget sedan</h2>
            <div class="articlecontent">
               <!-- google_ad_section_start -->
               <p>Mitsubishi has released fully-revealing photos of the G4 Compact Sedan Concept scheduled to make its debut later this week at 2013 Auto Shanghai.</p>
<p><a href="http://www.worldcarfans.com/113030454573/mitsubishi-g4-compact-sedan-concept-teased-for-bangkok" target="_blank">Teased</a> more than a month ago, Mitsubishi's latest concept serves as a preview for an upcoming affordable small sedan. It adopts new styling ideas of the Japanese marque such as the brilliant-cut, high-clarity diamond motif for the front grille, headlamps and taillights. It also has a short nose as well as "graceful and flowing side proportions".</p>
<p>The G4 Compact Sedan boasts a Reinforced Impact Safety Evolution (RISE) body which Mitsubishi says it's the lightest in its class thanks to an extensive use of high-tensile steel. Power comes from a compact and lightweight 1.2-liter MIVEC engine connected to a CVT.</p>

                <div class="articlecontent"><div class="body">The&nbsp;<em>MITSUBISHI Concept G4</em>&nbsp;is a vision for a new compact sedan which MMC plans to roll out globally. The&nbsp;<em>MITSUBISHI Concept G4</em>&nbsp;uses a high-clarity brilliant-cut diamond motif in the design of the front grille, headlamps and rear combination lamps to create a frontal visage with a commanding presence and a tail end which leaves a sharp impression as it drives off into the distance.</div>
<div class="body">&nbsp;</div>
<div class="body">The&nbsp;<em>MITSUBISHI Concept G4</em>&nbsp;carries next-generation compact sedan styling featuring</div>
<div>
<ul>
<li>a short nose which delivers an outstanding forward field of view and superior maneuverability,</li>
<li>graceful and flowing side proportions which balances interior space among the most generous in its class with very sleek aerodynamics, and</li>
<li>a dynamic character line which kicks up towards the rear.</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div class="body">The&nbsp;<em>MITSUBISHI Concept G4</em>&nbsp;achieves fuel efficiency that is among the best in its class as its RISE (Reinforced Impact Safety Evolution) body - the lightest in its class due to the extensive use of high-tensile steel - is powered by a lightweight and compact 1.2-liter MIVEC engine mated to a CVT with sub-geartrain in addition to uncompromising weight reduction throughout.</div>
<div class="body">&nbsp;</div>
<div class="body">The&nbsp;<em>MITSUBISHI Concept G4</em>&nbsp;delivers driving pleasure thanks to the agile and pleasing driving dynamics stemming from its lighter weight as well as its reassuring handling/stability and comfortable ride. Passenger comfort is enhanced by the high hip point and large door openings which facilitate entry and exit as well as by its generous rear seat leg space.</div></div>
