	            <h1 class="art-postheader">ROADSIDE ASSISTANCE</h1>	                <div class="art-postcontent">
	                    <!-- article-content -->
	                    <div class="none"><g:plusone href="http://precisionautoservice.com/services/roadside-assistance/" size="standard" count="true"></g:plusone></div><p><strong>Our FREE roadside Assistance Program Just Got Better!</strong></p>
<p>Call us for details.</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Mechanical First Aid:</strong> Any service requiring a minor adjustment, exclusive of parts; to enable a disabled vehicle to proceed under its own power (where available)</li>
<li><strong>Tire Service:</strong> The changing of a flat tire with an inflated spare</li>
<li><strong>Battery</strong><strong> Service:</strong> Attempting to start a vehicle with a booster battery</li>
<li><strong>Delivery Service:</strong> Delivering needed fuel or fluid to the disablement location (the customer is responsible for paying the actual cost of the goods delivered)</li>
