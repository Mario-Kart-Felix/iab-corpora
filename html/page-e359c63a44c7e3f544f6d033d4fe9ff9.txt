            <h3>Degrees and Certificates</h3>
            <p>Our program is designed to prepare you for entry-level positions in diesel service
                technology. Program training is broad enough to give you a background in different
                disciplines of the diesel service industry. In addition, the program offers several
                industry-upgrade courses.</p>
            <p>In this program you can earn the following:</p>

              <h3>Transfer Opportunity: Continue on to Earn a Bachelor's Degree</h3>
               <p>The PCC Diesel Tech  program has an articulation agreement with Montana State University Northern. Articulation agreements make it easy for  you to transition to a four-year school and complete  your bachelor's degree. Our AAS degree in Diesel Service  Technology articulates with MSUN as a direct articulation, which means they accept  allÂ of our credits and students can transfer straight in as a junior leading to a Bachelor of Science Degree in Diesel Technology.Â Students  graduating from MSUN are highly sought after by many large diesel  related companies.</p>
            <p>You can get more information and advisor contact info at <a href="http://www.msun.edu/academics/cots/program-Diesel.htm">Montana State University: Diesel Technology</a>.Â  </p>
            <h3>Industry Affiliations, Flexible Careers </h3>
            <p>Our program enjoys a variety of industry affiliations, corporate partnerships, and
                vast resources. Corporate manufacturers as well as local dealers and repair facilities
                support our program with new products, training, service literature, engines, heavy
                equipment, technical support and computer software. The widespread use of diesel
                equipment allows diesel service technicians to be able to work in a shop or in field
                service. As a diesel technician, you could work on a construction job, in the woods
                with a logging operation or at sea on a tug or fishing boat. The diesel engine is
                used whenever a rugged, dependable source of power is needed.</p>
