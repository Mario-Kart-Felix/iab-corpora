            <h1>
              Roadside Assistance            </h1>
          </div>
        </div>
        <!--/post-title -->
        <p>Roadside Assistance is available 24 hours a day, 365 days a year to assist members when owned or leased vehicles are disabled as a result of unavoidable circumstances. Members will only have to pay for any non-covered expenses or covered costs in excess of the 15 miles per occurrence maximum (up to $80 retail value). Coverage is extended to the member, spouse, and dependent children up to 21 years of age permanently residing at registered address when driving any vehicles that they own or lease for 12 months or longer.</p>
<p><strong>Highlights</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>• Available 24 hours a day, 365 days a year</li>
<li>• Up to 15 miles towing per occurrence maximum for covered charges</li>
<li>• Covers Member, spouse and dependent children up to age 21 permanently residing at registered address when driving any vehicles they own or lease (12 months or longer)</li>
<li>• Towing Assistance</li>
<li>• Flat Tire Assistance</li>
<li>• Fuel, Oil, Fluid and Water Delivery Service</li>
<li>• Lock-out Assistance</li>
<li>• Battery Assistance</li>
<li>• Collision Assistance</li>
</ul>
