<div id="main"><h1 id="start" name="start">Marine/Vehicles/Heavy Equipment</h1>
<div class="bodytext"><p>Pre-production pressure test measurement of vital product parameters is top priority in the automotive and off-road equipment industries. Controlling hydraulic pressure in such difficult and rugged production and operation applications as die cast shot monitoring and automotive stamping process is a specific&nbsp;pressure transmitter&nbsp;strength matched by few competitors.</p><p>Viatran invites you to learn about an application to determine weight and load as well as steering and break fluid measurement.&nbsp;</p><h2>Pressure Measurement for Off-Road Equipment</h2><p><img style="float: left; border: 0; margin: 5px;" src="/stuff/contentmgr/files/1/d56edaf8ef0b3e7205e9a4546a4ba793/files/presure_measurement_for_off_road_equipment_resized.jpg" alt="Pressure Measurement for Off-Road Equipment" width="160" height="106" />Pressure sensors serve many critical functions in off-road equipment, such as weight and load determination, power steering and brake fluid measurement, and monitoring engine condition. Extreme temperature, corrosion, shock and vibration shorten the life of on-board sensors found in off-road equipment. For pressure sensors to be useful, they must be able to endure the rigors of this environment. A truck manufacturer for strip mining operations needed transmitters for three applications. The first application measures the load of the truck. To be cost effective, the trucks have to run at full capacity without being overloaded. The second application uses a transmitter to measure accumulator pressures in the power steering system. The third transmitter application provides pressure data to a microprocessor controlled braking system. Curious? Please click on <a href="/contentmgr/showdetails.php/id/13900" target="_blank">Pressure Measurement for Off-Road Equipment</a>.</p>
