        <h1>Manufacturer of Diesel Tanks and Oil Tanks</h1>
	<div class="singleLine"></div>

	<h2>
	Viraj Engineering Company is a manufacturer of <b>diesel tanks</b> and <b>oil tanks</b> based in India.
	<b>Diesel tanks</b> are fitted on Jeeps, Light Commercial Vehicles(LCV), Buses etc. 
	<b>Oil tanks</b> are fitted on hydraulic equipment such as earth movers, trucks, 
	tippers (tipper trucks), dumpers (dump trucks) and other heavy equipment. 
	<b>Diesel tanks</b> and <b>oil tanks</b> are available in sizes ranging from 3 liters to 450 liters.
	<br/><br/>
	Viraj Engineering Company started operations in 1983 and has grown at a consistent and steady pace.
	Viraj Engineering Company supplies <b>diesel tanks</b> and <b>oil tanks</b> to customers all over the world. Primary customers:
