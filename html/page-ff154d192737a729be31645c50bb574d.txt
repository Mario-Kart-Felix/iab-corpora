<p>This award makes the Mercedes-Benz G-Class as a superstar because it is the degree to-8 achieved G-Class the longest series models produced by Mercedes-Benz.</p>
<p>G-Class Family currently has three variants, namely the short-wheelbase Station Wagon, long-wheelbase Station Wagon and Convertible. Each variant combined with tech V6 BlueTEC diesel engine 210 hp (155 kW) and 540 Nm of torque or a 5.5 liter V8 engine with the legendary power of 388 hp (285 kW) with torque of 530 Nm.</p>
<p>While the supreme model, the G 55 AMG, is only available as a four-door long-wheelbase Station Wagon. This model uses a supercharged V8 engine capable of generating power up to 507 hp (373 kW) and 700 Nm of torque.</p>
<p>Mercedes-Benz G-Class uses a variety of superior features like electronic traction control system 4ETS, the Electronic Stability Program (ESP), and three differential locks, all-wheel drive system with a permanent off-road ratio, and the transmission system 7-speed 7G Tronic</p>
