					<div class="entry">
						<p><strong>Electric Cars For Sale</strong></p>
<p>This decade (2010-2019) will someday be known as the starting point when a deluge of electric cars (electric vehicles) made news headlines. Today, almost every major car manufacturer has announcing proposed release dates for new models of EVs in 2013 and beyond. This includes Ford, Chevrolet, Toyota, Mitsubishi, Smart and many more. For those of you researching electric vehicles and buyers in the market for an all-electric model - these are electrifying times.</p>
<p>Plans by the federal government and some states to improve the required infrastructure and invest quite heavily into the electric car industry have been unveiled and these are likely to lead to even greater options for the electric vehicle buyer. Below is a selection of electric cars for sale now and in coming months.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Smart Fortwo Electric Drive</strong></p>
<p><a href="http://www.electriccarsforsale.org/featured/smart-fortwo-ed"><img class="alignleft size-full wp-image-211" alt="smart-ed" src="http://www.electriccarsforsale.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/smart-ed.jpg" width="101" height="100" /></a>Smart has created a new version of the smallest production car in the U.S., the <a href="http://www.electriccarsforsale.org/featured/smart-fortwo-ed">Smart Fortwo Electric Drive</a>. Merging economy and efficiency, this electric car is the ideal vehicle for small and congested cities where you don’t get the space to drive around freely. As the name clearly states, the car is a two-seater with not much space apart from that.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Nissan Leaf</strong></p>
<p><a href="http://www.electriccarsforsale.org/featured/nissan-leaf"><img class="alignleft size-full wp-image-85" title="Leaf From Nissan" alt="Leaf From Nissan" src="http://www.electriccarsforsale.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/12/leaf2.jpg" width="100" height="100" /></a>The Nissan Leaf is a 4 door hatchback with a 100 mile range on a single charge. A full charge will take less than 8 hours. A top speed of 90mph is achieved using an 80kW electric motor and an Li-ion battery. Prices will start from $28k and limited numbers of the <a href="http://www.electriccarsforsale.org/featured/nissan-leaf">Nissan Leaf</a> electric car will be for sale in areas of the country in 2010.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Chevrolet Volt</strong></p>
<p><a href="http://www.electriccarsforsale.org/featured/chevy-volt"><img class="alignleft size-full wp-image-94" title="Volt From Chevrolet" alt="Volt From Chevrolet" src="http://www.electriccarsforsale.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/12/Volt-Preview.jpg" width="100" height="100" /></a>Slated for release in November 2010 the <a href="http://www.electriccarsforsale.org/featured/chevy-volt">Chevrolet Volt</a> is a 4 door hatchback with an impressive 400 mile range from a single charge and generates a top speed of 100mph. Up to 10,000 units are expected to be made in the first year and it is expected to cost around $40k.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Mitsubishi iMiEV</strong></p>
<p><a href="http://www.electriccarsforsale.org/featured/mitsubishi-imiev"><img class="alignleft size-full wp-image-78" title="imiev From Mitsubishi" alt="imiev From Mitsubishi" src="http://www.electriccarsforsale.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/11/imiev21.jpg" width="100" height="100" /></a>The <a href="http://www.electriccarsforsale.org/featured/mitsubishi-imiev" target="_blank">Mitsubishi iMiEV</a> has a 100 mile range, 80mph top speed, and both on-board and off-board chargers. 5,000 units are set for sale in the US and EU although the price tag of nearly $50,000 makes this version of the existing gas model seem a little pricey at the moment. Read more <a href="http://www.electriccarsforsale.org/featured/mitsubishi-imiev" target="_self">here</a>.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Peugeot iON</strong></p>
