            <h3 style="text-align: center;">&nbsp;</h3>
            <h3 style="text-align: center;">&nbsp;<b><a target="_blank" href="http://web.archive.org/web/20120823121509/http://www.lehrauto.com/images/lehrpursuit-lores.pdf">Press Release 9/1/2010</a></b></h3>

            <div><strong>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Lehr Auto</strong>&nbsp;is proud to inform it's clients about the purchase agreement of&nbsp;&nbsp;<strong>Pursuit North</strong>, located in Martinez, Ca.&nbsp; The aquisition will allow Lehr Auto and Pursuit North to maximize purchasing power and better serve the client base in the Bay Area.&nbsp; All existing employees will remain in place at both locations.</div>

            <p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Established in 1945, Lehr Auto Electric is known as Sacramento&rsquo;s elite auto electric repair facility. Please visit our service department for more information. In the late 1980&rsquo;s Lehr Auto Electric expanded its product line with Emergency Vehicle Equipment. The industry needed a supplier that could offer reliable customer service, speedy delivery and competitive prices. Lehr stepped up to become one of Northern California&rsquo;s largest supplier of emergency equipment for police cars and many other public safety vehicles. Frank, Jeff, Steve, Doug, Mark or Jim can assist you with all your equipment needs. Lehr warehouses thousands of products from well known suppliers such as Whelen, Code 3, Federal Signal, Sound-Off, Gamber Johnson, Troy, Havis-Shields, Panasonic, Setina, ProGard, Unitrol, Able II, Kussmaul, Unity, Ecco and many more. Our parts department carries many traditional auto electric lines such as Cole Hersee, Pollack, Hella, K-4 switches, Deka, Little Fuse, Datcon, Stewart Warner and many other hard to find auto electric components you might need.</p>
            <p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Lehr remains as one of the few remaining shops to offer Starter and Alternator repair and rebuilding. Rebuilding starters and alternators is often a more economic solution for many of the larger fleet vehicles and the hard to find replacement units. Expedite service is available on request. Sean our rebuilder has over 20 years of experience, so you can rest assured the job will be done correctly the first time.</p>
            <p>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; <img hspace="10" alt="Certified Installer" vspace="2" align="right" src="http://lehrauto.com/images/l_certified.gif" /> Did you know that Lehr is the Sacramento valley Cablecraft assembler? Yes we offer most any Push-Pull type cables available. We have the ability to manufacture almost any cable, emergency brake style or push-pull you might need. Whether it&rsquo;s a 173 series out of the book or duplicating your sample, Lehr will find a solution.</p>
