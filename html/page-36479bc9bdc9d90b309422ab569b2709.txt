      Canton Classic Car Museum was established by the late Marshall Belden, Sr
      in 1978. The museum currently exhibits forty rare and unusual classic and
      special interest automobiles. The gorgeous vehicles are complemented by
      the thousands of pieces of historical memorabilia displayed throughout the
      museum.
</p>
<p>The Canton Classic Car Museum has something for everyone. Automobilia.
      Vintage toys. Steam engines. Movie posters. Historic photography. Oriental
      rugs.
      Meyer's Lake Park and Canton historical displays. The list goes on and on.</p>
<p>Please take the time to explore our web site and get a taste of what
      the museum is all about. Be sure to take a closer look at our featured
      vehicles on the right. They are all truly fantastic machines and the phrase<em>'poetry in motion'</em> certainly applies.</p>
<p>We are open seven days a week from 10:00 am until 5:00 pm and we are
      handicap accessible. Admission rates: Adults $7.50 (18 and up); Seniors (60 and up)
      $6.00; Youth (5-17) $5.00; Children (4 &amp; under) $3.00. Discount group rates available. Closed New Years, Easter, Thanksgiving and Christmas. <a href="contactus.htm">Contact us</a> for
  more information.</p>
<p>Our extensive gift shop has something for everybody including model cars, T-shirts, jacket, puzzles, ties, jewelry and more! The gift shop is open during
      museum hours. </p>
