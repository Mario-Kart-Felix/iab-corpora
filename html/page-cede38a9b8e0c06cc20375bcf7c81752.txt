<h1>Unearthing The New Lexus ‘Premium Compact’ Concept</h1>

							<div class="entry clearfix" align="justify">
								<p style="text-align: justify;">There were rumors since the last one year that <a title=" 2009 Lexus GS 450H Review" href="http://www.automotivecraze.com/2009-lexus-gs-450h-luxury-sedan-review/">Lexus</a><strong> </strong>is going to launch a <strong>new hatchback</strong> which will offer tough competition to<strong> </strong><a title="2009 BMW 1 Series Review" href="http://www.automotivecraze.com/2009-bmw-1-series-review/">BMW 1 series</a> and <strong>Audi A3</strong>.  We have official news to confirm this rumor. The car is expected to be unveiled at next months Frankfurt Auto Show, however previews reveals the first indication of design and direction  for the up coming model.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">The only official information that we have received is that this model will be a perfect blend of high tech innovations and ground breaking designs. They have promised to raise the bench mark for the existing <strong>luxury car</strong> segment.  The company boasts of many new innovative features but sadly we may not know as the cars are likely to be sold in overseas market only. The main reason that we can conclude  for <strong>Lexus </strong>entering into the Overseas market is that the <strong>new hatchback</strong> will be an entry level model and will definitely priced below the IS range – a segment that is unlikely to enter in North America.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;"><a href="http://www.automotivecraze.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/08/Lexus-premium-compact.jpg"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1128" title="Lexus premium compact" src="http://www.automotivecraze.com/wp-content/uploads/2009/08/Lexus-premium-compact.jpg" alt="Lexus premium compact" width="512" height="211" /></a></p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">The company has not disclosed many facts about this version but earlier reports claim that this model will be equipped with a <strong>Hybrid power train</strong> and will share some of its internal features with<strong> Toyota Auris</strong> which is also commonly known as <strong>Toyota Corolla</strong> in some areas.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">It is expected that the production version will be debuted at next years<strong> Geneva Motor Show</strong> which will be held in March before going on sale in <strong>Europe</strong>. The newer concepts will be known by September 15 so all you automotive aficionados stayed tuned to automotivecraze and we shall provide you up to date information about this <strong>luxury car</strong>.</p>
