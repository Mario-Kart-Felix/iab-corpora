<p><strong> <img height="230" width="356" src="/images/stories/omvic_cai_certified.png" alt="omvic_cai_certified" title="bill_and_mark_infront_of_caa_car_centre_web_size.jpg" style="margin: 5px; float: left;" /></strong></p>
<p><strong>Worry-free luxury vehicle acquisition and service – all at an affordable price.</strong></p>
<p align="justify">Located at 135 Dieppe Road in St. Catharines, Wiley Luxury Brand Auto offers their clients the highest quality pre-owned luxury brand vehicles available on today’s market. Whether it’s late or classic models, Mark Wiley, president of Wiley Luxury Brand Auto, sources vehicles from all over North America including special auctions, dealer trade-ins and private sales as well as from private individuals who have maintained their cars in pristine condition.</p>
<p align="justify">If you’re looking to purchase a late model Mercedes-Benz SL Roadster or a classic 1963 Bentley S3 Continental, you don’t have to contact your financial advisor. You just have to call Mark Wiley or one of his automotive associates.</p>
<p align="justify">A lifelong Niagara native, Mark has spent the past 23 years in the automotive retail industry including 10 years in various senior sales management positions with both Mercedes-Benz and General Motors. During his tenure with General Motors, he experienced incredible success – ranking in the top 1% in auto sales right across Canada. <br /><br />“I always look back fondly on the first six years I spent in the industry,” Mark says, “That’s the time I really built the foundation of my career in the service department and my appreciation for the business.”</p>
<p align="justify">If you’re looking for a truly open and unbiased car buying experience when purchasing a luxury auto, it’s always important to choose an experienced independent dealer. Brand popularity, brand quality ranking, brand resale value, brand loyalty – all of these are major factors within the automotive industry that can somewhat change from model year to model year. But the major dealerships will tend to only focus on the current product lines offered through their franchises, rather than sourcing an outside vehicle that will best suit their customers’ needs and pocketbook.</p>
<p align="justify">Refreshingly with Wiley Luxury Brand Auto, the customer relationship doesn’t end after the final sale is made. We’re always striving to support our clients and enhance their vehicle ownership experience. We offer extensive warranty coverage choices, financial assistance, repair and restoration services, and various other automotive services – using partner companies that we’ve carefully chosen who share our passion for an exceptional customer experience.  </p></td>
