<b><a href="http://topautoreview.blogspot.com/2013/06/honda-odyssey-2014.html">2014 Honda</a> Accord Plug-In Hybrid</b> launches in USA. Honda offers the Accord in North America from now on also in a plug-in hybrid version.<br />
<br />

<br />
The <i>Honda Accord</i> is one of North America to the best-selling cars, the Japanese are now expanding the popular series about a plug-in hybrid version. Is driven by the combination of fuel-efficient Accord a 2.0 liter 4-cylinder petrol engine with 141 hp and a 55 hp electric motor.<br />
<br />
The large 6.7 kWH battery supplies the electric motor with energy and allows a pure electric range of around 22 kilometers before the batteries need to be recharged via a plug socket or engine power. To recognize the plug-in hybrid model on a modified front end with LED daytime running lights and the LED tail lights.<br />
<br />
In the facilities of nearly $ 40,000 expensive Accord Plug-in Hybrid will also lack nothing. Also on board are, among other things heated seats front and rear, 2-zone automatic climate control, cruise control, a navigation system, a 180-watt sound system and much more.<br />
<br />
For Europe, the introduction of the <u>Accord <a href="http://topautoreview.blogspot.com/2012/10/2013-volvo-v60-plug-in-hybrid.html">Plug-In Hybrid</a></u> is not provided, as the U.S. Accord is fundamentally different from the European version.
