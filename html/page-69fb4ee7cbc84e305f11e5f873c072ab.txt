I am not sure if you guys have seen the TV commercial for the Toyota Swagger Wagon, or seen it on Youtube but its kinda funny. Does not make me want to run out and buy a new Toyota, but made me laugh.</p>
<p>Here are a few pictures that I took on the Limo <span style="text-decoration: underline;">Swagger Wagon</span>, which I had not seen before. Its pretty cool&#8230;.if you are a hip hop star <img src='http://www.myhonestmechanic.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/icon_smile.gif' alt=':)' class='wp-smiley' />   This car is very cool, but how in the heck can you park this thing?</p>
<p>I think my two young daughters would love to play inside, but I KNOW my wife would not want to try and find a parking place at the grocery store with it. I can say, its a cool concept and I do have to admit that the commercials for the Swagger Wagon are kinda cute, but I am truck guy. <img src='http://www.myhonestmechanic.com/wp-includes/images/smilies/icon_sad.gif' alt=':(' class='wp-smiley' /> </p>
<p>See if you can picture yourself in this vehicle?  I wonder what the gas mileage is?  Could I get<a target="_blank" title=" auto insurance" href=" http://www.myhonestmechanic.com/compare-auto-insurance"> auto insurance</a> coverage for it?</p>
