    <div class="field field-name-body field-type-text-with-summary field-label-hidden"><div class="field-items"><div class="field-item even" property="content:encoded"> <p>What separates a local auto accessories or electronics store from the retail chain ones like Auto Zone, Best Buy or Napa Auto Parts? For starters a family business is one that provides a personal touch. We feel you are more likely to return to a store that has courteous staff, and specialized knowledge of accessories. Products ranging from auto security systems, navigation, back up cameras and remote car starters to pickup truck covers, nerf bars, and accessories. It doesn't matter if the store offers advanced auto protection or promises to take you into the auto zone. It's more important to have the right parts, and to have people who have the knowledge to correctly outfit your car, because they have done this before. Our store has all of these elements, surely we can help you restyle your vehicle.</p>
