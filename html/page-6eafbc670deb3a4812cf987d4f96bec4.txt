<h3><a class="underscored"  href="../auto_products/roadside_assist.php">Towing</a></h3> 
<p class="first">If your vehicle cannot be started after attempting any of the services described above, or because of an accident or when in the service facility&#39;s judgment the vehicle cannot be placed in a safe driving condition, it will be towed at no charge to the responding or nearest AAA service facility, or to any location within 3 miles.</p>
<p>If the member desires to have the vehicle towed to a destination other than as stated above, it is not considered emergency towing, but a “tow of convenience.” Basic level members will not be charged for the first three miles, and will be charged $4.00 per mile after the first three miles.</p>

<p>Plus level members will not be charged for the first 100 miles, and Premier level members receive the first 200 miles at no charge. Additional miles over these mileage allowances will be charged at $4.00 per mile.</p>

<p>Rates may differ from Club to Club, and members may be asked to pay whatever rate is in effect in that territory. Any subsequent towing for the same breakdown will be at the member’s expense. Tow rates in certain states may be subject to tax. </p>

Tows to a member&#39;s choice of destination may have to be delayed until the peak demand on our staff and equipment has subsided.</p>

<li>In the event that you ever require more than four road service calls &#40;or five, if applicable&#41; in any single membership year, you will be required to pay &#36;35 for light service and/or tows back to the closest responding facility&#59; tows to other destinations will be at prevailing rates for towing service.</li>
