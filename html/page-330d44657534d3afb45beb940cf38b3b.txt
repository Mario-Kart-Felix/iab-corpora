<p>Here is a 2002 Subaru WRX Wagon in World Rally Blue For Sale.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>This vehicle has been treated well by the previous owner and has had a full safety inspection and repairs to include Cv axles, transmission repairs and seal replacement.  We have performed an alignment, balanced the tires and replaced all fluids as well.  Its ready to go and drives excellent.  The miles are 160k but if you didn&#8217;t see the odometer when driving you would truly think it had less.  This is a great Ski car with the added bonus of the legendary performance of the WRX.  It has had a full detail and shows very well paint is glossy and no major dents.</p>
