<p>Directory of technologies and resources relating to plug-in hybrid electric vehicles (PHEV)
</p><p>Some hybrid owners are doing plug-in conversions by adding batteries or replacing them with larger capacity units. Add an inverter and a plug to make a plug-in hybrid. The control system can also be altered so that the gas engine kicks in at a higher speed.
</p>
