            <h6 style="text-align: center">
            <font SIZE="2">Off-Road Motorcycles, and Snowmobiles<br>
                Source: Ohio DMV<br>
            &nbsp;</h6>
            <p class="secondP" style="text-align: center">
            <a href="autotitle.htm">Auto Title Home 
                Page</a> | <a href="autotitlefaq.htm">Auto 
                Title FAQ</a> | <a href="autotitlegeneralinfo.htm">Auto Title 
                General Information </a><br>
                <a href="autotitleusedvehicles.htm">Used Vehicles</a> |
            <a href="autotitleoffroadvehicles.htm">Off-road Vehicles</a> |
            <a href="autotitleforms.htm">Auto Title Forms </a> </p>
            <hr>
      <p>Listed below are acceptable types of evidence that may be used by
    the Clerk of Courts of Common Pleas Title Offices for title issuance on APV's and Off-Road
    Motorcycles.</p>
      <h6>One of the following:<o:p><a href="#Top of page"><img border="0" src="images/toparrow.gif" align="right" width="22" height="41"></a></h6>
            <ul>
              <li>Manufacturer's
        Certificate of Origin or Importer's Certificate of Origin;</li>
              <li>Another jurisdiction's certificate of
        title in applicant's name, or assigned to Ohio resident;</li>
              <li>A current or expired registration issued
        by Ohio or any other jurisdiction;</li>
              <li>A notarized bill of sale;</li>
              <li>A current insurance policy or declaration
        page that describes the vehicle by vehicle identification number (VIN), year, make, model,
        and issued to the applicant in this or another state;</li>
              <li>A warranty order in applicant's name that describes the
        vehicle by VIN, year, make, and model;</li>
              <li>A security agreement that lists the owner, lender, VIN,
        year, and make.</li>
            </ul>
      <h6>Two or more of the 
      following:</h6>
            <ul>
              <li>An out-of-state inspection obtained from&nbsp; 
        a deputy registrar license agency;</li>
              <li>A sworn,
        notarized affidavit declaring ownership of the vehicle by the applicant;</li>
              <li>A sworn, notarized affidavit by other 
        persons attesting to applicant's ownership;</li>
              <li>Receipts in applicant's name from the
        purchase of parts or components;</li>
              <li>Photographs and or pencil tracings of the
        VIN.</li>
            </ul>
      <h6>
      APV, Off-Road Motorcycle, and Snowmobile
Facts:</h6>
            <ul>
              <li>Legislation
        requiring the registration of APV's, Off-Road Motorcycles, and Snowmobiles became
        effective July 1, 1999.</li>
              <li>Dealers are required to provide a title
        for all APV's and Off-Road Motorcycles within 30 days from the date of 
      purchase by a customer.<o:p><a href="#Top of page"><img border="0" src="images/toparrow.gif" align="right" width="22" height="41"></a></li>
              <li>Snowmobiles will continue to be untitled
        vehicles.</li>
              <li>Before an APV or Off-Road Motorcycle may
        be registered, it must be titled.</li>
              <li>First time renewal of an APV or Off-Road
        Motorcycle registration will require the presentation of an Ohio title.</li>
              <li>A new 30-day tag will be made available
        for customers to purchase if they wish to use their vehicle while waiting for a title to
        be secured.</li>
              <li>The APV/Off-Road Motorcycle 30-day tag
        will be different from the current 30-day tag that is issued for other motor
        vehicles.&nbsp; The APV/Off-Road Motorcycle 30-day tag will be constructed of material
        that will enable it to remain legible and relatively intact despite the environmental
        conditions to which it is likely to be exposed during the 30-day period for which it is
        valid.</li>
              <li>The fee for the 30-day tag will be $4.75
        ($2 state fee plus $2.75 deputy registrar fee).</li>
              <li>30-day tags will be available for
        purchase from dealerships licensed to sell APV's and Off-Road Motorcycles.</li>
              <li>30-day tags will not be issued for
        Snowmobiles (Snowmobiles are not titled).</li>
              <li>Ohio will offer non-residents a 15-day
        registration for $7.75 ($5 state fee plus $2.75 deputy registrar fee).</li>
            </ul>
