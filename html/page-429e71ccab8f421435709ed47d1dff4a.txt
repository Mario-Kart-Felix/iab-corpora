        <p align="left"><strong>Ford's 2001 Windstar Minivan</strong></p>
        <font size="3"><p>NEW YORK, April 19, 2000 &#150; Ford&#146; 2001 Windstar minivan,
        unveiled today at the New York International Auto Show, raises the safety stakes in the
        family transportation market by adding a new standard passive Personal Safety System and
        optional AdvanceTracTM interactive vehicle dynamics control system to its long list of
        safety firsts.</p>
        <p align="center"><img src="Windstar_intro-msm.jpg" alt="Jac Nasser introducing the Windstr at the New York Auto Show" WIDTH="302" HEIGHT="202"></p>
        <p align="center"></font><font size="2"><strong>Jac Nasser introducing the Windstar at the
        New York Auto Show</strong></font><font size="3"></p>
        <p align="right"></font><font size="1">Photos: Ford</font><font size="3"></p>
        <p>Windstar is the only minivan to earn five stars, the government&#146;s highest
        front-crash test ratings, since 1997. Starting in 1999, Windstar &#150; with optional side
        airbags - became the first vehicle in the industry to earn quadruple five-star status when
        new side-impact ratings were established.</p>
        <p>These ratings - combined with Ford&#146;s advanced Personal Safety System,
        AdvanceTracTM interactive vehicle dynamics system and LATCH child safety-seat anchoring -
        will give 2001 Windstar customers peace of mind anytime and anywhere.</p>
        <p>SAFETY/SECURITY FEATURES</p>
        <p align="center"><img src="wind_pers_saf.jpg" alt="SAFETY/SECURITY FEATURES" WIDTH="491" HEIGHT="379"></p>
        <p>The 2001 Ford Windstar continues its &quot;Safety First, Family Driven&quot; philosophy
        with significant new as well as familiar carryover safety and security features:<ul>
          <li>New Personal Safety System standard</li>
          <li>Dual-stage front inflating airbags</li>
          <li>Crash severity sensor, integrated safety-belt pretensioners, energy management
            retractors, safety-belt usage sensors and driver&#146;s-seat position sensor</li>
          <li>New AdvanceTracTM interactive vehicle dynamics system available (minivan first, mid-year
            introduction)</li>
          <li>New low-tire warning system standard</li>
          <li>New heated signal mirrors available (minivan first)</li>
          <li>Industry-first strobe warning for sliding door</li>
          <li>Self-sealing tires now available in 16-inch tires (previously available on 15-inch
            sizes)</li>
          <li>Improved head restraints on front-row bucket seats</li>
          <li>Child seat restraint anchorage system standard</li>
          <li>ISO-Fix-style Lower Anchors and Tethers for Children, or LATCH (minivan first when
            introduced in August 1999)</li>
          <li>Structural safety</li>
          <li>Meets or exceeds all federal regulatory safety standards</li>
          <li>Side airbags available</li>
          <li>SecuriLockTM passive anti-theft system standard; remote keyless entry standard on all
            four-door models</font></li>
        </ul>
