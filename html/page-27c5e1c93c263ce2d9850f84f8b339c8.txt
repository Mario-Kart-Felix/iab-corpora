		<h2 id="single-title" class="post-title page-title">
			Off Road Vehicles		</h2>
				<p><strong><a href="http://www.bestwayinsurance.ca/auto-insurance/off-road-vehicles/offroad-300x211/" rel="attachment wp-att-343"><img class="alignleft size-full wp-image-343" title="offroad-300x211" src="http://www.bestwayinsurance.ca/wp-content/uploads/2011/05/offroad-300x211.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="211" /></a></strong></p>
<p><strong></strong> </p>
<p><strong></strong> </p>
<p><strong>RECREATIONAL VEHICLES</strong></p>
<p><strong>DID YOU KNOW WE CAN INSURE TOYS?</strong></p>
<p align="center"> </p>
<p align="center"> </p>
<p style="text-align: left;" align="center">We have gained access to several new and exciting programs to insure your toys.  What kind of toys, you ask? </p>
<p>We can now offer competitive pricing for: </p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Motorcycles      </strong></li>
<li><strong>Watercraft         </strong></li>
<li><strong>Trailers         </strong></li>
<li><strong>Motorhomes</strong></li>
<li><strong>Snowmobiles</strong></li>
<li><strong>ATVs</strong></li>
</ul>
<p>For some items, such as ATVs, UTVs, snowmobiles and motorcycles, insurance coverage is usually required by law. Other fun objects, such as boats, personal watercraft or trailers are often not considered when it comes to insurance. The fact that insurance coverage is not required by law does not mean it is not necessary. A boat, for example, can be an expensive asset and a liability concern.  When considering the number of boats out there on our lakes and waters, it can also be a significant liability concern.</p>
<p>Many people say “My ATV never leaves my property so I don’t need liability.”  You can still hurt someone or they can be hurt while driving your ATV and you would need coverage.  Currently, the government does not require coverage if you remain on your own property, however, this does not mean <strong><em>you</em></strong> do not need it.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p align="center"><strong>Contact us today – we offer several coverage options at great rates.</strong></p>
