			<p>The minivan of the future  or at least Chryslers future  is on display at the 2012 North American International Auto Show.<br><p>Dubbed the 700 C for now, the silver minivan with chrome accents appears to be lower to the ground and more aerodynamic than the current Dodge Caravan or the Chrysler Town & Country. <br><P>Fiat and Chrysler CEO Sergio Marchionne, said today the company is trying to decide whether or not it wants to make two minivans or just one.
