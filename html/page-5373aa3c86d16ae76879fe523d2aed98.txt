									<h3 class="fakeh3">3+33 Toyota PRIUS contract hire leasing deals</h3>
									<p>Our Toyota PRIUS leasing packages allow you to spread the cost of your car lease monthly - subject to an agreed mileage allowance. Most of our business customers prefer to spread the cost of their 36-month rentals over the full term of the lease. By taking a 3+35 rental profile, they therefore reduce the monthly rental. This payment profile is referred to as a "spread rental". However, some customers prefer a 3+33 payment profile, which is often referred to as a "terminal pause" contract. This type of leasing contract is where there is a "payment holiday" at the end of the car lease.</p>
