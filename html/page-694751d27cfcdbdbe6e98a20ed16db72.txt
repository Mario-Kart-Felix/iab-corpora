<h1><a name="aol-photo-gallery" href="/photos/top-10-luxury-cars/">Top 10 Luxury Cars</a></h1>
<ul class="photos"><li class="knot-slide-data">
<div class="description">
<p>
Luxury comes in many shapes, sizes and prices &ndash; especially when dealing with luxury cars.<br/>
<br/>
Some high-end vehicles look great, ride great or provide that right mix of performance and luxury. For AOL Autos readers, the mix is just as eclectic as one might think.<br/>
<br/>
During September, readers looked at nearly everything in the luxury market, but here are the top 10 luxury cars, researched by AOL Autos readers.</p>
</div>
<a class="knot-anchor" href="http://www.blogcdn.com/autos.aol.com/media/2012/09/audi-a6-1347998582.jpg" data-media-id="47497" data-photo-src="http://www.blogcdn.com/autos.aol.com/media/2012/09/audi-a6-1347998582.jpg" data-credit="Audi"></a>
</li><li class="knot-slide-data">
<div class="description">
<p>
<strong>MSRP: </strong>$37,850 - $45,45<br/>
<strong>Invoice: </strong>$35,201 - $42,269<br/>
<strong>Fuel Economy: </strong>22 mpg City, 32 mpg Highway<br/>
<br/>
This coupe is just the right mix of sportiness and luxury, though it may likely be a vehicle designed for single people or DINKs -- dual income, no kids -- couples. The front is spacious, wrapped in leather, and it&rsquo;s a dream to drive. The second row is great for your computer bag. Powered by a 2-liter turbocharged I4, the A5 is great for road trips or commutes to work. It&rsquo;s available in two-wheel or all-wheel drive.<br/>
<br/>
<strong>Research the Audi A5</strong><br/>
<a href="http://autos.aol.com/cars-Audi-A5-2013/overview/">Audi A5 Photos &amp; Information</a><br/>
<a href="http://autos.aol.com/cars-Audi-A5-2013/specs/">Audi A5 Specs</a><br/>
<a href="http://autos.aol.com/getaquote">Audi A5 Local Dealer Price Quote</a></p>
</div>
<a class="knot-anchor" href="http://www.blogcdn.com/autos.aol.com/media/2012/09/a5.jpg" data-media-id="47498" data-photo-src="http://www.blogcdn.com/autos.aol.com/media/2012/09/a5.jpg" data-credit="Audi">10. Audi A5</a>
</li><li class="knot-slide-data">
<div class="description">
<p>
<strong>MSRP: </strong>$34,200 - $46,800<br/>
<strong>Invoice: </strong>$32,070 - $43,320<br/>
<strong>Fuel Economy: </strong>19 mpg City, 29 mpg Highway<br/>
<br/>
Dollar for dollar, the Genesis sedan offers the most luxury for the lowest price. The Genesis continues to impress, first with its luxurious appointments inside the cabin and then with it&rsquo;s optional 429-horsepower 5-liter V8.<br/>
<br/>
It may ride like a big sedan, but it&rsquo;s a really nice big luxury sedan that adds to Hyundai&rsquo;s reputation. The Genesis has put Mercedes and Lexus on notice.<br/>
<br/>
<strong>Research the Hyundai Genesis</strong><br/>
<a href="http://autos.aol.com/cars-Hyundai-Genesis-2013/overview/">Hyundai Genesis Photos &amp; Information</a><br/>
<a href="http://autos.aol.com/cars-Hyundai-Genesis-2013/specs/">Hyundai Genesis Specs</a><br/>
<a href="http://autos.aol.com/getaquote">Hyundai Genesis Local Dealer Price Quote</a></p>
</div>
<a class="knot-anchor" href="http://www.blogcdn.com/autos.aol.com/media/2012/09/genesis.jpg" data-media-id="47499" data-photo-src="http://www.blogcdn.com/autos.aol.com/media/2012/09/genesis.jpg" data-credit="Hyundai">9. Hyundai Genesis</a>
</li><li class="knot-slide-data">
<div class="description">
<p>
<strong>Consumer Reports says: </strong>Redesigned for 2012, the A6 is agile, quick, and a joy to drive, surrounding drivers and passengers with a sumptuous, impeccably finished cabin that&rsquo;s brimming with the latest high-tech features. The A6&rsquo;s potent, supercharged V6 engine, super-smooth eight-speed automatic transmission, and comfortable ride add to the business-class feel.<br/>
<br/>
It returns respectable gas mileage, even with all-wheel drive.</p>
</div>
<a class="knot-anchor" href="http://www.blogcdn.com/autos.aol.com/media/2012/09/a6.jpg" data-media-id="47500" data-photo-src="http://www.blogcdn.com/autos.aol.com/media/2012/09/a6.jpg" data-credit="Audi">LUXURY CAR: Audi A6</a>
</li><li class="knot-slide-data">
<div class="description">
<p>
<strong>MSRP: </strong>$25,900 - $29,200<br/>
<strong>Invoice: </strong>$24,452 - $27,507<br/>
<strong>Fuel Economy: </strong>24 mpg City, 35 mpg Highway<br/>
<br/>
All new to the Acura lineup, the ILX creates a new starting point for Honda&rsquo;s luxury brand. The ILX promises a sporty ride, solid on-road performance and slew of standard luxury features. It&rsquo;s also affordable luxury with a starting price under $26,000. That low price tag could explain why so many people are checking out this compact luxury sedan.<br/>
<br/>
However, it still hangs onto some of Acura&rsquo;s polarizing front end, which people who like it call it modern, and others call it hideous.<br/>
<br/>
<strong>Research the Acura ILX</strong><br/>
<a href="http://autos.aol.com/cars-Acura-ILX-2013/overview/">Acura ILX Photos &amp; Information</a><br/>
<a href="http://autos.aol.com/cars-Acura-ILX-2013/specs/">Acura ILX Specs</a><br/>
<a href="http://autos.aol.com/getaquote">Acura ILX Local Dealer Price Quote</a></p>
</div>
<a class="knot-anchor" href="http://www.blogcdn.com/autos.aol.com/media/2012/09/ilx.jpg" data-media-id="47501" data-photo-src="http://www.blogcdn.com/autos.aol.com/media/2012/09/ilx.jpg" data-credit="Honda">8. Acura ILX</a>
</li><li class="knot-slide-data">
<div class="description">
<p>
<strong>MSRP: </strong>$36,100<br/>
<strong>Invoice: </strong>$33,815<br/>
<strong>Fuel Economy: </strong>21 mpg City, 31 mpg Highway<br/>
<br/>
The Lexus ES 350 is one of those luxury cars that&rsquo;s a safe bet. It&rsquo;s soft and quiet ride make it easy to appreciate and allows any driver to relax on the commute home from a long day in the office. Lexus has begun to attempt to make it more sporty, but, still, it simply wraps its passengers in a cocoon of luxury.<br/>
<br/>
It&rsquo;s 3.5-liter V6 provides plenty of speed on the highway and the ride is so compliant it&rsquo;s easier to get moving much faster than you think. It may be a safe bet, but it&rsquo;s also a good one.<br/>
<br/>
<strong>Research the Lexus ES 350</strong><br/>
<a href="http://autos.aol.com/cars-Lexus-ES+350-2013/overview/">Lexus ES 350 Photos &amp; Information</a><br/>
<a href="http://autos.aol.com/cars-Lexus-ES+350-2013/specs/">Lexus ES 350 Specs</a><br/>
<a href="http://autos.aol.com/getaquote">Lexus ES 350 Local Dealer Price Quote</a></p>
</div>
<a class="knot-anchor" href="http://www.blogcdn.com/autos.aol.com/media/2012/09/es350.jpg" data-media-id="47502" data-photo-src="http://www.blogcdn.com/autos.aol.com/media/2012/09/es350.jpg" data-credit="Toyota">7. Lexus ES 350</a>
</li><li class="knot-slide-data">
<div class="description">
<p>
<strong>MSRP: </strong>$32,500 - $33,400<br/>
<strong>Invoice: </strong>$30,227 - $31,062<br/>
<strong>Fuel Economy: </strong>24 mpg City, 31 mpg Highway<br/>
<br/>
As Audis continue to grow in popularity, it&rsquo;s likely we&rsquo;re going to see more and more A4s on America&rsquo;s roadways. This entry level Audi provides everything from precision handling to Audi&rsquo;s legendary Quattro all-wheel drive system. The A4 may look less aggressive than the BMW 3 Series, but it&rsquo;s appeal continues to grow.<br/>
<br/>
It includes Audi&rsquo;s 211 horsepower 2-liter engine mated to an eight-speed transmission that helps it achieve 31 mpg on the highway and 24 mpg in the city. It can easily convert into a small family hauler and proves the point how luxury can arrive in small packages.<br/>
<br/>
