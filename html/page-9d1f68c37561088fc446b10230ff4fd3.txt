		<div style="clear:both;"></div><h2>24/7 Emergency Towing &amp; Auto Assistance Services</h2>
<p><strong>24 Hour Towing Inc.  1-800-518-8687 gives its utmost priority to accidents. We dispatch tow vehicles to give immediate relief to the persons and vehicles involved in any accident</strong></p>
<p>Our trucks feature GPS locators for the fastest response times possible. We deal with all light-duty and heavy-duty vehicles caught in the accidents. We have variety of sophisticated tow trucks such as flatbed tow trucks which can lift your car without damage. Our trained technicians can retrieve vehicles from a ditch, deep valleys or deep water.</p>
<p>We are available 24 hour a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. Anytime or anywhere you need us we will be there in 30 minutes or less. Our services are available in San Francisco, San Jose and Oakland.<br />
24 Hour Towing Inc. providers are legally bonded, approved and licensed. We are recognized and approved for payment by major insurance companies.</p>
<p><strong>We provide all kinds of Road Side Assistance Roadside Services such:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Auto Assistance</li>
<li>Accident removal</li>
<li>On/off-road winching</li>
<li>Luxury and exotic vehicles</li>
<li>Lost car key replacement</li>
<li>Broken key extraction</li>
<li>High security key manufacture</li>
<li>Car lockout Services</li>
<li>Jump start</li>
<li>Battery installation</li>
<li>Flat tire change (if customer has spare)</li>
<li>Out of gas service</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>We also provide many types of towing options, such as:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Local towing service</li>
<li><a title="Long distance towing" href="http://theautotowing.com/long-distance-towing/">Long distance towing</a></li>
<li><a title="Heavy duty towing" href="http://theautotowing.com/heavy-duty-towing/">Heavy duty towing</a></li>
<li>Light duty towing</li>
<li>Flat bed towing</li>
</ul>
<p>24 Hour Towing Inc. manages on/off road winching in a tactical with an evenly distributed lifting force. When a tractor or trailer needs to be lifted to an upright position, we use the most high-tech air cushion recovery technology to put it upright.</p>
<p><strong>24 Hour Towing Inc. service</strong> can handle all your towing requirements such as out of gas service, flat tire replacement, jump start, on/off road winching, <strong>lost car key solutions</strong> plus much more. Even if you car needs to be towed when you are out with all your family members, it is no problem. With our large 4 door trucks that seat six campers or a five wheel camper trailer we can tow your car and your family so you get home safe and secure.</p>
<h3>24 Hour Towing Inc. service providers are located in: San Francisco, San Jose and Oakland</h3>
<p><em><strong>Call us today on 1-800-518-8687 for all your auto tow needs. You can also register as our member.</strong> </em></p>
