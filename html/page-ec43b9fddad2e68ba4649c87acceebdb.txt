    <h1>Your Guide to Automotive, Diesel, Motorcycle, Aircraft and Marine Mechanic Training</h1>

    <div id="intro"><p>Are you good with your hands? Do you like to check under the hood and see how everything's working? You might be suited for a lucrative career as a mechanic. At MechanicSchools.com, we list a wide variety of accredited auto mechanic schools and colleges, as well as aircraft, marine and motorcycle technician training programs. This informative guide offers career and salary information as well as lists of diverse mechanic training programs nationwide.</p>
<h2>What Kind of Mechanic Do You Want to Be?</h2>
<p>For the nation's future auto mechanics, we provide links to schools offering auto body repair, diesel technician programs and information on the NASCAR Technical Institute. Future aircraft mechanics can find links to airframe and power plant schools and avionics programs. For motorcycle and marine technicians, we have technical schools offering accredited manufacturer-specific training to give your career a start. We shall help you discover various mechanic schools &amp; programs!</p></div>
