							I just did this nasty job. The fuse is not in the fuse block, but in a seperate fuse position near the relays. It's hard to see and even harder to reach. I had to remove two relays that were in the way just to get access. The relays are located behind the dashboard and can be reached through the opening above the fuse panel. Please note: There are two fuses in the relay area, both with retaining clips. I replaced the one in the light blue holder. The one in the red holder controls other things; not sure what because there is no documentation in the manual covering it. Pretty stupid on VWs part to put a fuse in such a location and not document it anywhere. Guess they figured it's good for a $120 service visit. Best of luck! &nbsp;
