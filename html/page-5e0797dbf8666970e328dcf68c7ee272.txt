		<h1>Career Training in Automotive Mechanics</h1><p>Online and distance education training programs in automotive mechanics, motorcycle repair, auto body repair technician, small engine repair, auto detailer, and more.</p><br /><div id="content_intro"><h2>Automotive Mechanics & Repair Training Courses</h2><p>According to the U. S. Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS),
automotive service technicians and mechanics had median hourly
earnings between $11 and $26 in 2004. Career opportunities are
expected to show steady growth over the next several years.</p></div><div id="content_body">

<h4>Automotive Mechanic Career Training</h4>
<p>Although there are no standard educational requirements for entry-level automotive
mechanics, most employers seek applicants who have a high school
diploma as well as some form of formal training. Career education
programs are available through online high schools and automotive
service training programs. Career training and college degree
programs, which are often sponsored by automotive manufacturers,
are available through community colleges, vocational schools, and
online colleges and universities.</p> 

<p>According to the BLS, courses in automotive repair, electronics, physics, chemistry, English,
computers, and mathematics provide a good educational foundation
for an automotive technician or mechanic. On-the-job (OTJ) training
usually supplements formal training for these increasingly
high-tech jobs. Depending on education and experience,
comprehensive OTJ training can last between four and seven years.</p>

<p>Automotive Mechanic Continuing Education Automotive mechanics need
to stay on top of the constantly evolving technological trends in
the automotive industry. Some mechanics choose a specialization,
such as front-end or brake mechanic, tune-up technician, or
transmission tech or rebuilder. If you have a knack for problem
solving and a mechanical aptitude, you may want to gear up for an
automotive mechanic career.</p></div><div id="read_more_container"><a id="read_more">Read More</a></div><div id='content_left' class='span-14'><link rel="stylesheet" type="text/css" href="/hqx/min/?b=css&f=school_listing_ajax.css" /><script type="text/javascript" src="/hqx/min/?b=js&f=school_listing_ajax.js,degree-guide.js">
