    $('ul.tabs').each(function () {

        // For each set of tabs, we want to keep track of
        // which tab is active and it's associated content
        var $active, $content, $links = $(this).find('a');

        // If the location.hash matches one of the links, use that as the active tab.
        // If no match is found, use the first link as the initial active tab.
        $active = $($links.filter('[href="' + location.hash + '"]')[0] || $links[0]);
        $active.addClass('active');
        $content = $($active.attr('href'));

        // Hide the remaining content
        $links.not($active).each(function () {
            $($(this).attr('href')).hide();
        });

        // Bind the click event handler
        $(this).on('click', 'a', function (e) {
            // Make the old tab inactive.
            $active.parent().removeClass('active');
            $content.hide();

            // Update the variables with the new link and content
            $active = $(this);
            $content = $($(this).attr('href'));

            // Make the tab active.
            $active.parent().addClass('active');
            $content.show();

            // Prevent the anchor's default click action
            e.preventDefault();
        });
    });</script>

    <h2 class="font18"><a href="/Blog/post/SPIED-Pre-Production-BMW-i8-Hybrid-Electric-Supercar-on-Secret-Test-Track.aspx" data-role="ajax-nav">SPIED: Pre-Production BMW i8 Hybrid Electric Supercar on Secret Test Track</a></h2>

    <span class="author"><a href="/Blog/author/Brenda-Priddy.aspx">Brenda Priddy</a></span> on <span class="pubDate" itemprop="datePublished">05.01.2012 11:42</span>
    <div class="text">
        <p style="margin: 0in 0in 0.0001pt;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 0.0001pt;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 0.0001pt;">Nearly a year ago we spied what almost seemed more like a publicity stunt than car- testing&mdash;a near clone to&nbsp;the BMW i8 Frankfurt Motor Show Concept: <a title="BMW i8 Vision EfficientDynamics" href="http://www.bmw-i-usa.com/en_us/bmw-i8/" target="_blank">Vision EfficientDynamics</a>, playing in the snow in northern Sweden.</p>
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 0.0001pt;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 0.0001pt;">Stunning, but more &ldquo;concept&rdquo; than production.</p>
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 0.0001pt;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 0.0001pt;">Now, for the first time ever, we&rsquo;ve got close up to the real thing! We caught a pre-production BMW i8 driving on a secret test track, and we were still able to snap a few photos before it went into a spy-proof garage.</p>
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 0.0001pt;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 0.0001pt;">In the words of our shooter in Europe:</p>
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 0.0001pt;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 0.0001pt;">&ldquo;The machine you see will be the reigning monarch of the Bavarian automaker's <a title="BMW Launches New Sub-Brand" href="http://www.automedia.com/Blog/Post/Born-Electric-BMW-Launches-New-i-Sub-Brand.aspx" target="_blank">new sub-brand</a> i once it debuts.</p>
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 0.0001pt; text-align: justify;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin: 0in 0in 0.0001pt; text-align: justify;">&ldquo;Based on the Vision EfficientDynamics concept that starred at the 2009 Frankfurt Motor Show, the vehicle is said to pack 2+2 seating and a 0-100 km/h time in the 4.8- second range, along with a supercar price tag. Look to fork around 150,000 Euros when this beastie touches down.</p>
<p>&ldquo;There's been some indication that <a title="BMW new cars 2012" href="http://www.automedia.com/New_Cars/bmw">BMW</a> will forgo the thrifty diesel engine of the concept in favor of a turbocharged four-cylinder gasoline unit to work in coordination with a hybrid system. While that's bad news for diesel fans, the fact that BMW is gearing the platform toward gasoline means that this vehicle will more than likely be very powerful. With its lightweight structure and the plug-in hybrid, it will, nonetheless, remain a very fuel-efficient car.&rdquo;</p>

    <h3 class="font18"><a href="/Blog/post/SNEAK-PEEK-FISKER-NINA-Concept-Spied-Before-2012-New-York-Auto-Show.aspx" data-role="ajax-nav">UPDATE: FISKER NINA (Atlantic) Concept Spied Before 2012 New York Auto Show</a></h3>

    <span class="author"><a href="/Blog/author/Brenda-Priddy.aspx">Brenda Priddy</a></span> on <span class="pubDate" itemprop="datePublished">03.29.2012 13:30</span>
    <div class="text">
        <p style="margin-bottom: 0.0001pt;">Last week Fisker provided media outlets with an extremely simple and not-too-revealing line-sketch of the NINA concept car: a plug-in hybrid model smaller than the <a title="403-hp Fisker Karma plug-in hybrid sports car" href="http://www.automedia.com/Blog/post/403-hp-fisker-karma-plug-in-hybrid-to-get-67-mpg.aspx">Fisker Karma</a>. Today we spotted the new Fisker Nina as it was being loaded onto a transporter heading to Manhattan for its debut at the 2012 <a title="new auto show photo gallery" href="http://www.automedia.com/Auto-Shows/New_York_International_Auto_Show">New York Auto Show</a> April April 6-15.&nbsp; </p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0.0001pt;">Although we should have full details at the press preview days April 4-5, we already know that the Fisker Nina will be built in Wilmington, Delaware at the former <a title="Fisker buys GM factory" href="http://www.automedia.com/Blog/post/fisker-buys-gm-factory-to-build-plug-in-hybrids.aspx">GM/Solstice plant</a>. We also know that Fisker has an agreement with BMW for a turbocharged four-cylinder engine.</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0.0001pt;"><em>Brenda Priddy &amp; Company</em></p>
<p>UPDATE 4/4/12 - Fisker has officially announced today at the NY auto show the production model name of the Nina Concept Project car - Fisker Atlantic - and promises the car will definitely go into production in 2013.</p>

    <h4 class="font18"><a href="/Blog/post/Volvo-Debuts-XC60-Plug-In-Hybrid-Concept-SUV-at-2012-Detroit-Auto-Show.aspx" data-role="ajax-nav">Volvo Debuts XC60 Plug-In Hybrid Concept SUV at 2012 Detroit Auto Show  </a></h4>

    <span class="author"><a href="/Blog/author/James-M-Flammang.aspx">James M. Flammang</a></span> on <span class="pubDate" itemprop="datePublished">01.08.2012 07:12</span>
    <div class="text">
        <p class="MsoNormal">Volvo, the Swedish automaker that&rsquo;s now owned by a Chinese organization, hasn&rsquo;t focused much on <a title="hybrid cars" href="http://www.automedia.com/Hybrid_Cars_All_About_Hybrids/dsm20101101h1/1">hybrid</a> or <a title="electric cars" href="http://www.automedia.com/Hybrid_Cars_Electric_Vehicle_Buying_Guide/dsm20110301h3/1">electric cars</a>. Now, the company is making a move in that direction. At Detroit&rsquo;s North American International Auto Show, Volvo is unveiling a concept version of its XC60 crossover SUV&ndash;&ndash;the Volvo XC60 Plug-In Hybrid Concept&ndash;&ndash;with <a title="plug in hybrid" href="http://www.automedia.com/Plug-In_Hybrids_Ready_For_Prime_Time/dsm20081101ph/1">plug-in hybrid</a> gasoline technology.</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">Volvo claims that its XC60 Concept is the first electrically-powered automobile that can be plugged into a conventional wall outlet without a special adapter. As described by Volvo, the XC60 Concept is a &ldquo;pure electric&rdquo; car, a thrifty hybrid, and &ldquo;a powerful high-performance car all rolled into one.&rdquo;</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">The ability to plug into any 110-volt electrical outlet makes this &ldquo;a viable option at home or on the move,&rdquo; Volvo asserts. Charging might take place just about anywhere, though some patience would be required. Charging at 110 volts takes a claimed 7.5 hours. Find a 220-volt outlet and the charge time for the lithium-ion battery pack dips to 3.5 hours.</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">Unlike current hybrid and electric cars, the XC60 Plug-in Hybrid Concept lets the driver decide how to make the best use of available power. Three buttons on the center stack allow selection from three Modes: Pure, Hybrid, or Power.  <a href="/Blog/post/Volvo-Debuts-XC60-Plug-In-Hybrid-Concept-SUV-at-2012-Detroit-Auto-Show.aspx" >more</a>
