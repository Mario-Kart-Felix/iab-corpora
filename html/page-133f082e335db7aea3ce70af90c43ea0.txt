					<h2>Roadside Assistance</h2>

        <h3>National Auto Division offers the most comprehensive road-side assistance program for the entire life of your policy.</h3>
      <p>We provide customers of our vehicle service agreement program a membership into <strong>Nation Safe Drivers - Roadside Assistance</strong>, at no additional cost, for the entire term of the policy. Even though we hope you are never faced with a roadside emergency, we are extending this benefit to provide additional peace 
        of mind to our consumers and their families.</p>
      <h2>Benefits of Nation Safe Drivers - Roadside Assistance</h2>
      <p><strong>Emergency Roadside Assistance 24 hours a day/365 days 
        a year</strong>- Upon enrollment in an extended service contract with 
        National Auto Division, you will be provided with a toll-free number which 
        will allow access to our motor club network throughout the United States 
        and Canada. Covered emergencies are subject to a $50.00 
        per occurrence limitation.</p>

        <p><strong>Towing Assistance</strong>- If your car cannot be started 
        or driven, a contracted service provider will tow your vehicle to a service 
        facility.</p>
        <p><strong>Battery Assistance</strong>- If a battery failure occurs, 
        a jump start will be applied by a service technician to start the covered 
        vehicle.</p>
        <p><strong>Flat Tire Assistance</strong>- A technician's service 
        consists of removal of the flat tire and its replacement with a customer 
        provided spare tire.</p>
        <p><strong>Fuel, Oil, Fluid, and Water Delivery Service</strong>- 
        A service vehicle will be dispatched to deliver an emergency supply of 
        fuel, oil, fluid and water if you are in immediate need.</p>

        <p><strong>Emergency Lock-Out Assistance</strong>- In the event that 
        your keys are locked inside the vehicle, our service technician will provide 
        assistance in gaining entry to the vehicle.</p>
      
      <h3>Additional Policy Benefits</h3>
      <p><strong>Rental</strong>- In the event of a covered mechanical 
        failure, there will be reimbursement for actual expenses occurred for 
        substitute transportation at the maximum daily rate of $35.00 per day, 
        not to exceed 5 days.</p>
        <p><strong>Travel Expenses</strong>- In the event of a covered mechanical 
        failure, there will be reimbursement for up to $75 per day for a period 
        not to exceed 3 days for restaurant, meals, and lodging that you incur 
        for staying at a hotel/motel when your vehicle can not be operated and 
      is more than 100 miles from your home.</p>

      <p>For more information and further details on the program benefits, please 
        contact <strong>800.430.4428</strong>.</p>
