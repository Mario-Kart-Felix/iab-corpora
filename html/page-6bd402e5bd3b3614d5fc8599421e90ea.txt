                    <p>The Auto/Diesel Institute offers courses designed to teach you specifically what you need to get a job in the <a href="/programs/automotive-courses/">Automotive Services</a> or <a href="/programs/diesel/">Diesel Service</a> industries. Taught by a <a href="/programs/faculty/">full-time and adjunct faculty</a> comprised of master mechanics with decades of real-world experience, in labs and bays outfitted with the latest high-tech equipment, <span class="caps">ADI</span> has everything you need to start &#8212; or build &#8212; a great career in the Auto or Diesel industry.</p>

 <div id="automotive">

	<h3>Automotive Services Technology</h3>

	<p>Jobs for auto technicians are growing 40% faster than most jobs. And every course is designed to prepare you for those jobs &#8211; by giving you state-of-the art skills to handle vehicle systems from bumper to bumper.</p>

	<p><a href="/programs/automotive-courses/">Learn More &raquo;</a>
 </div>
 <div id="diesel"></p>

	<h3>Diesel Service Technology</h3>

	<p>Diesel technology is changing faster than ever before &#8211; so demand is booming for technicians with up-to-date abilities. And at our brand-new Diesel Technology Center, you&#8217;ll learn the most advanced skills using the latest equipment.</p>

	<p><a href="/programs/diesel">Learn More &raquo;</a><br />
</div></p>
